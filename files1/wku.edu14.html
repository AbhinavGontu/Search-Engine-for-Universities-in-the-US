Join The WKU Alumni Association
When you join the WKU Alumni Association you make things happen
for yourself, other alumni and friends of WKU. You embody The Spirit Makes The Master.

Membership Makes it Happen

The WKU Alumni Association is dependent on the membership of its alumni to be able to provide the programs and events that you enjoy. When you join the alumni association you are helping to make things happen for yourself and your fellow alumni.

The alumni association produces many programs and events throughout the year.  Your membership helps us produce these events and keep alumni connected to WKU in fun and exciting ways.
