Apply Today

At Cameron University, we believe every person deserves an affordable, top-quality education provided by a caring faculty and staff. Cameron offers associate, bachelor’s and master's degree programs, which allows us to serve the higher education needs of southwest Oklahoma. Cameron University wants to be a part of your educational journey, so apply today!

Schedule A Tour

We invite you to book a personalized tour and let us show you our beautiful campus. The best way to learn what all the talk is about, is to join a small group of others on a fun, interactive tour. The tour group will learn a little bit of Cameron University history, sightsee our student living quarters and several other campus facilities, and find out how we can help make your higher education decision easier. Lastly, you will get a chance to ask important questions throughout the tour and even apply for admissions afterwards.

Why Cameron?

Cameron University provides a diverse and dynamic student body access to quality educational opportunities; fosters a student-centered academic environment that combines innovative classroom teaching with experiential learning; prepares students for professional success, responsible citizenship, life-long learning, and meaningful contributions to a rapidly changing world; and is a driving force in the cultural life and economic development of the region.

Find A Major

Cameron University offers degrees at the associate, bachelor’s and master’s degree levels. With more than 50 degree options, as well as a large number of pre-professional programs, there’s a great chance that you’ll find the CU degree that’s perfect for you. Your success is as important to us as it is to you!
