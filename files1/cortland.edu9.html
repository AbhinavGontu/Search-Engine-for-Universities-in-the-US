Register Your Account

The Red Dragon Network is the alumni gateway to SUNY Cortland! It is the one-stop-shop for information and getting involved with the college. Features include: a real-time events calendar, an online alumni directory, news feeds and social media networks. Alumni can update their profile to stay connected with each other as well as the things they love at the college.

Register your account today, to gain full benefits! Your member ID number is required to authenticate your alumni account.   Please use the Help feature located at the top of this page if you have any questions.

Enjoy this alumni benefit and be sure to share this web page on your social networks with fellow Red Dragons.

Red Dragon Network. Connect, Rediscover and Explore.
