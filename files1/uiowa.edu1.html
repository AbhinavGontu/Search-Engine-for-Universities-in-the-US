HawkID Login for Employee Self Service

Forgot your HawkID password?

Access to Employee Self Service requires a HawkID and Password. New Employees receive a HawkID and Password shortly after starting employment. Former Employees may access Employee Self Service for 18 months after leaving the University.

Two-Step Login is required for Employee Self Service. Visit http://its.uiowa.edu/two-step for more information and to enroll your devices in Two-Step.

Please contact the ITS Help Desk for assistance, questions, or concerns.
