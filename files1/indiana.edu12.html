Baseball Group Packages
Single Game Tickets can be purchased at $7.00 for adults and $5.00 for youth. 

Group Tickets are available for all home games. Group tickets are only $4.00 each for groups of 25 or more! 
For more information, click here.

Four Pack Tickets are four or more tickets purchased at $10.00 per ticket that includes an entre and a drink. This offer is only valid for weekday games. Offer not valid on game day.

Reserve the Group Patio for one of our home games and enjoy a unique and private experience. Group Patio amenities include:
    20x30 Frame Tent with industrial heaters for cold weather games
    Group seating
    Food options available
    Potential group experiences 

Located in the right field corner of Bart Kaufman Field, the Group Patio is great for businesses, community organizations, birthday parties, youth leagues and other group outings. To find out more information on how to reserve the Group Patio, please contact Kurt Pangborn at (812) 855-9370 or via e-mail kpangbor@indiana.edu.
