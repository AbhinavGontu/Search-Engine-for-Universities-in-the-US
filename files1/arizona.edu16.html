FUEL THE RESPONSE FOR ARIZONA STUDENTS

Many students affected by COVID-19’s disruptions are experiencing financial hardship and are struggling to cover rent, utilities & other basic necessities. Help fuel the response for Arizona students by making a gift to the Arizona Student Emergency Fund.
