Basketball Camps

High School Boys Team Camp

Date: CANCELLED 

Cost: $200 per team

Grades: rising freshmen through seniors

Schedule to be released closer to the date.

Description:  The Team Camp provides an excellent opportunity to build chemistry and momentum for your team as you prepare for upcoming tournaments! Coached by the MSOE Staff each team will received college level coaching and development through high level training.

Register

 

Girls Middle School Basketball Team Camp

Date: June 28, 2020

Cost: $150 per team

Grades: rising 5th through 8th graders

Time: 9am until 5pm

Description:  One day team camp shootout. Team Camp provides an excellent opportunity to build chemistry and momentum for your team as you prepare for upcoming tournaments! It is also a great chance for your players to hone their on-court skills as they play against competitive opponents while being coached by the MSOE Coaching Staff. The cost includes guarantee of 3 games and officials.
