Graduating Soon? Here’s what to do with your TigerMail (9/27/2018) - Please be aware your access to TigerMail and other Brenau systems will end approximately two full terms after graduation.  Before that happens, we recommend you transfer your TigerMail emails and documents to your Gmail account at https://takeout.google.com/transfer or (if you don’t have a Gmail account) export everything at https://takeout.google.com
