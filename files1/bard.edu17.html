Disrupting Systemic Oppression

Equity & Inclusion
On June 1, 2020, the Fisher Center issued a statement in solidarity with Black Lives Matter, which represented our commitment to addressing issues of racial inequity in our organization. Since then, working groups comprised of Fisher Center staff members have formed to address matters of equity, diversity, and inclusion. The Equity & Inclusion Working Groups will share regular updates on the Fisher Center’s Equity & Inclusion webpage.
