Welcome!

The Center for Career and Professional Development serves all Clarion University students and alumni. Our mission is to inform and empower students and alumni by linking them with resources and opportunities so they can develop and achieve their career goals.

We collaborate with academic departments, student organizations and other university offices to provide a learning environment where students can acquire the knowledge, skills, and experiences required to successfully transition to employment or graduate education.

We create and maintain partnerships with employers and community agencies to develop internships and employment opportunities for students and alumni.
