Spring Cultural Calendar

Spring 2021 Cultural Events

Elon University presents a broad offering of academic and special programs every semester. The cultural calendar provides a month by month overview of musical and theatrical performances, guest and faculty lectures, religious and ethnic observances, recitals, art exhibitions, special entertainment and other events.

For information about how the university is handling campus events this semester in response to COVID-19, please visit the Ready & Resilient website.

Events are subject to change or cancellation.
