Connect

Connect with other alumni through class notes, events, regional clubs, Reunions, affinity networks, alumni news, alumni travel and more. You can also find the alumni directory and order transcripts (Voyager), including class notes, here.

Engage

Engage with Lawrence by volunteering as an Alumni Ambassador. Interview prospective students, staff college fairs, join your Regional Club committee, nominate a classmate for an award and more.

Pay It Forward

Pay it forward to future Lawrentians by giving back to Lawrence in time or treasure. Learn about others who have done so, donate to the Lawrence Fund or learn more about planned giving.

Learn more about the Lawrence University Alumni Association

If you attended Lawrence University or Milwaukee-Downer College, you are one of the 18,000+ members of the Lawrence University Alumni Association (LUAA), which is led by the 35 member Board of Directors. The board works with the Lawrence staff, faculty and administration to advance the mission and purposes of the university.
