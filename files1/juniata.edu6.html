Limited on campus tours and counselor meetings are open to prospective students who will be entering college in the Fall or Spring of 2021; registration for these visits closes 48 business hours in advance and is required. Please review our campus visit policies below prior to scheduling an on campus visit.

Please note all visit and event times are in the Eastern Time Zone (ET).

Spring Open House Events

Get to know Juniata by participating in one of our on-campus open house events. This spring, every on-campus event will offer a morning and afternoon session. While events may vary in size and duration, each session will be limited to no more than 50 students and one guest per student. When registering to participate in one of these sessions, please plan to spend a minimum of 3.5 hours with us. Prior to submitting your registration, please ensure you are registering for your desired session, as we will be unable to change registrations once our capacity is met.

Juniata is committed to providing a safe visit experience for all of our guests and campus community, so we ask that you please review our on-campus visit policies prior to registering. All guests and staff will practice social distancing measures and be required to complete a pre-screening questionnaire. Masks will also be required of visitors and staff. No exceptions can be made. We will be happy to provide a face covering should you need one and must require that it be worn at all times while on campus.

While we hope to host all spring events on-campus, we must remain flexible, keeping in mind that our events may need to transition to a virtual format. If necessary, virtual events will be hosted from an event webpage with live engagement on Zoom and will include at least one (1) five-minute break.

For event details and to register online, please click on the event of your choice below. Please be mindful that registration typically closes six days before an on-campus event.
