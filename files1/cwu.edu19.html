Equipment Checkout

Location: Equipment Checkout Desk located in Samuelson Hall Lobby 

Hours: Monday - Friday: 9 am - 5 pm

The Multimodal Education Center offers a variety of equipment ranging from laptops and GoPros to projectors and lavalier microphones. Checkout what equipment we rent out. Equipment checkout is free to all members of the CWU community with a current CWU Connection Card. Please read our equipment checkout policy.

We will not be handling any long-term loans for students, faculty or staff. If you need a laptop for long term use, you can complete this equipment checkout form or consult with your department heads to purchase equipment need for your work.

  • 7 days per checkout -Please read our equipment checkout policy to avoid any fines and/or late fees. 
  • Equipment cannot be renewed. Equipment must be returned with all of its components. A late fee charge of $45 per day will apply to any equipment not returned after the seventh day. Additionally, users assume responsibility for any damaged, stolen, or missing equipment, and will be charged replacement fees. 
  • Equipment can be reserved ahead of time. Reservations are filled on a first come, first served basis and are subject to equipment availability. Indicating a needed-by date does not guarantee that the equipment will be available at that time. 
  • If returned late, request for the fine to be appealed. For specific instances where returning equipment on time was beyond your control you can request the fine to be removed from your account. The MEC adheres to a strict late fee policy and maintains the discretion to refuse any appeal lacking adequate justification.
