Keeping Our Community Safe

As Buffs together, we all play an important role in taking care of each other.
Here are a few things we each must do to help keep our community safe: 

Daily Buff Pass

Use the Buff Pass to:

  • Fill out the daily health form
  • Obtain your daily pass for being on campus
  • Find assigned monitoring testing days
  • Monitoring test results
  • Occupancy information for campus buildings

Access Buff Pass

 

Weekly Monitoring Testing

Weekly monitoring tests are expected for all faculty, staff and students who are on campus at least once a week. Check the Buff Pass to find your weekly testing day.

Monitoring testing is used to reduce community spread of COVID-19 by proactively monitoring, identifying and referring possible cases to the public health clinic for diagnostic testing.
