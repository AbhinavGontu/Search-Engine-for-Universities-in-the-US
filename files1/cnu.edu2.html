COVID Precautions

  • Visitor Guidelines

All on-campus visitors must follow the safety protocols listed in the visitor guidelines. This includes wearing a cloth face covering at all times, observing six feet of physical distancing from others and submitting to all check-in procedures. We ask that students who are traveling with a parent or guardian limit guests to one individual when possible.
