The e-Learning Services (e-LS) department at LC State facilitates a variety of technology-enhanced instruction, including fully online, hybrid, and web-enhanced lecture courses through the Canvas learning management system (LMS).

What Happens at e-Learning Services?

  • Support, advisory and outreach:
    • Developing, sharing, and ensuring good practice in e-learning by working with departments and individuals. e-LS runs courses and workshops, and encourages and supports LC State faculty and associates.
  • Running and enhancing e-learning services:
    • Improving the usability and functionality of our campus Learning Management System (LMS) of Canvas.
  • Innovation and development:
    • Assessing student and teacher needs, evaluating new technologies, pedagogies, and services, and guiding the future of online learning.
