Challengeyour Mind

Broaden your knowledge of the historical, scientific, cultural, artistic, and spiritual heritages that have shaped modern society with Lyons EPIC core curriculum.

Discover Your Future

Explore the World

Travel to unbelievable locations, explore new cultures, and create memories that will last a lifetime through the endowed Nichols International Studies Program. 

Take the Journey

Take the Leap

Explore Lyon, the Ozarks, and beyond with the Lyon Education and Adventure Program. Zip-line across campus, kayak through the Spring River, or plumb the depths of caverns in Blanchard Springs.

Academic Programs

Go Beyond Boundaries

Fine Arts

Develop practical, creative, and conceptual skills in various art forms, including visual arts, music, and theatre.

Humanities

Learn to critically reason, skillfully analyze, and effectively communicate across broad fields of knowledge.

Math & Science

Build a critical understanding of math and science, from eigenvalues and particle theory to ecology and biochemistry.

Social Science

Explore human and animal behavior through various comparative lenses.

International Programs

Make the world your classroomdeepen your global knowledge while examining transnational cultures.
