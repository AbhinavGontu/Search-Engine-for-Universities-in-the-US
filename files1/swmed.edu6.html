COVID-19 Vaccination Update

As a state-designated COVID-19 vaccination hub, UT Southwestern is continuously working to improve access to vaccines and has multiple initiatives to further those efforts. First is our online, public registration portal for eligible individuals to schedule COVID-19 vaccinations at UT Southwestern. Second, we are now offering vaccines, by appointment only, at The University of Texas at Dallas in Richardson and RedBird Mall in southern Dallas. Eligible individuals can choose to be vaccinated at either of those two sites or at our Market Hall or West Campus Building 3 locations in Dallas.

Existing patients who meet the necessary criteria – now expanded to include those 50 years of age and up – should continue to schedule vaccinations through their UTSW MyChart accounts. More detailed information on the process and eligibility are available on our COVID-19 Vaccination site, along with tip sheets and answers to frequently asked questions.

Patient Notification

UT Southwestern Medical Center inappropriately disclosed the names and email addresses of patients previously seen in the UTSW Kidney Cancer Program through to a third-party vendor. The vendor was used to send invitations to an event for the Kidney Cancer Program. No other information was shared with the third-party vendor and the patients’ email accounts were not compromised in any way. UT Southwestern considers the protection of our patients’ privacy of utmost importance, and we deeply regret the occurrence of this incident and any worry, distress, or difficulty that it may cause. If you are a current or previous patient of the Kidney Cancer Program and have questions about whether your name or email address was affected, you can contact the UT Southwestern Privacy Office at (214) 648-6080.
