Center for Pre-College Programs Bulletin:
            
Our Spring, Summer and Fall 2020 programs were all virtual while featuring active participation opportunities for everyone.  They were a rousing success in unique circumstances.  While Spring 2021 programs are underway, we are busy planning Summer 2021 offerings which also will be virtual.  We look forward to welcoming everyone back to campus in Fall 2021.  Our top priority will always be to provide students with rich, culturally relevant, academically authentic and safe learning experiences.  We remain committed to supporting educators and families in providing high quality STEM learning.  We view our programming opportunities as a worthwhile challenge and a privilege in these highly unusual times.
            
Learn more about our upcoming events and how to register.
