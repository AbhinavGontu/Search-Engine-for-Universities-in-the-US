Be a part of the action this season! Purchase tickets through the Panthers Ticket Office and cheer on the Panthers. The Panthers Ticket Office is located in the lobby of the Petersen Events Center. Business hours are Monday - Friday from 9:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. View the information below to learn more about our ticketed sports and find the best ticket option for you.

Students, select the sport you are interested in for student ticket information or view the student ticket page.

GENERAL POLICIES

The Panthers Ticket Office accepts cash, checks, debit cards and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover and American Express) for season ticket purchases. Please make checks payable to The University of Pittsburgh. The Panthers Ticket Office accepts cash, debit cards and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover and American Express) for single-game ticket purchases. All sales are final and refunds or exchanges are not permitted.

WILL CALL
A valid photo ID is required to pick up tickets at will call for all sports.
