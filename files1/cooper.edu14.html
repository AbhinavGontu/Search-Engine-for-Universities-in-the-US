Election 2021

February 10, 2021
Election

The 2021 CUAA Election will be held during the months of March and April 2021. It will begin on March 15th and continue through April 30th, 2021.

See the slate of candidates

All the officer positions are on the ballot this year and so is the Alumni Trustee positon.

The slate of Candidates for these positions was announced at the February Council Meeting held on February 18, 2021 via zoom.

If you are a candidate, you will be given a page on this site that you can use as part of your campaign. Send your headshot photo, campaign statement, and biographical material to contact@cooperalumni.org with the subject heading, Campaign materials.

Each alumna and alumnus who is registered with the CUAA will be sent a ballot from our Survey Monkey account on the first day of voting. You can register on this website

If you would like to update your registration information do so here. You must be logged in to the website for the update process.
