Scholar's Mentorship Program +

The Scholar's Mentorship Program (SMP) is a multicultural program, founded by the Black Studies Department, that addresses the educational disparities of the historically underrepresented students of SUNY New Paltz.
Visit our website for more information.

Communities at New Paltz +

Communities at New Paltz is a specifically designed Living/Learning community program for First-Year students. This program offers four distinct living experiences featuring specific inter-disciplinary themes: Leadership, Health & Wellness, Career Exploration, and Service Learning. It's the perfect way to immediately find other people with your shared interests.

Academic Schools & Tours

School of Business - an accredited member of the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB), a distinction reserved for just the top 5 percent of business schools worldwide.

School of Education - State-of-the-art classrooms, an expanded Curriculum Materials Center, and remodeled Literacy Center.

School of Fine & Performing Arts - Nationally accredited programs in art education, art history, art studio, music, and theatre arts.

College of Liberal Arts & Sciences - Home to 17 departments and interdisciplinary programs, offers eleven interdisciplinary minor curricula from all five schools.

School of Science & Engineering - we provide a rigorous education in math, science and engineering with an emphasis on problem-solving, creative thinking and teamwork.
