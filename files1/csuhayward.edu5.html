Semester Conversion

Semester Conversion was a 4.5 year initiative at Cal State East Bay that ended with the successful launch to the semester in Fall 2018. All departments and divisions, faculty, staff, students, and administrators participated in this work. The Semester Conversion website was the epicenter for communication for the initiative.
