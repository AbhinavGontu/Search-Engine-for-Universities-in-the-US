Law School Admissions

At GGU Law, we have been educating lawyers for more than 115 years, with successful alumni all over the world. We seek diverse students who share a passion for an unmatched legal education focused on practical skills and hands-on training in a supportive and collaborative environment.

JD Program

GGU Law offers an innovative and personalized learning experience that gives students the practical skills, judgment, and knowledge to become exceptional lawyers for today’s truly global market.

Learn More
 

Master of Laws (LLM)

At GGU, we offer six award-winning LLM programs. Our programs offer great flexibility by accepting entering students in the fall, spring , or summer semesters, and allowing students to complete the program on a full-time or part-time basis.

Learn More
 

SJD in International Legal Studies

GGU also offers a terminal law degree, equivalent to a PhD, called an SJD. The SJD in International Legal Studies is designed to provide in-depth coverage of specialized areas of international and comparative law.
