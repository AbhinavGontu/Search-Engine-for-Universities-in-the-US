YOU ARE VITAL TO UNH’S SUCCESS

 

Support from our Wildcat family and friends makes extraordinary things possible at UNH, which aspires to be among the top 25 public universities in the country in the most important measures of student success.

Your gift to an area of the university that is meaningful to you makes a difference.

Choose where you would like to make your gift.
