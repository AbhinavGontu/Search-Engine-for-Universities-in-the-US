Keeazhia Thompson | Business

What’s it like to be a college student? What’s it like to find the right school that puts you on the path to success?

In this video, Keeazhia Thompson, a business administration major here at Daemen, reflects on her journey as a college student.

If you’re interested in learning more about our business administration program, click this link! ➡️ https://www.daemen.edu/academics/areas-study/business-administration

Do you drink coffee?

Occasionally, when I came to college, I started drinking coffee, so.

(upbeat music)

Before I was a college student, I thought it was going to be like very, very stressful, and your professors were gonna not know you by name or they were gonna scare you off with the amount of homework.

Because seeing movies and like not having like somebody there to guide you through college because I’m a first generation college student, so it’s even more scary, because it’s like, I don’t have the person in my ear telling me how college is I just gotta face the facts.

When I was a kid, I was kind of confused on what I wanted to be when I grow up.

I wanted to be a lawyer, I wanted to be a doctor, I wanted to be a veterinarian, but when I finally got into like basketball, football, sports, I knew I wanted to do Sports Management.

And then, on the other hand, I want to do Criminal Justice because like I’m the person that always likes to solve stuff.

I love mysteries, I watch First 48 before I watch any other kid show when I was a kid.

I feel like Daemen changed me when I came to college, when I came down to time management, when it came down to having more responsibility.

Now I live Off-campus so I have to also factoring me driving into school. You don’t have your parents and your back in your back pocket anymore.

When you did in high school, all my teachers are very friendly. Whenever you go if you have any questions, I talk to them.

I love the stress relievers that we have on campus like Spa Night, I like to do more activities, which I feel like that broke me out of my show.

My freshman year actually coming to college, I’m not as stressed though, as I thought I would be.

Hi, my name is Keeazhia Thompson.

I’m a Business Administration major with a specialization in Sports Management at Daemen College, and you should definitely experience Daemen for yourself.
