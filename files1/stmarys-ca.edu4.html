    Drive-Through Campus Tour

    We look forward to welcoming you to Saint Mary's College for a drive-through campus tour! Please fill out the form below to receive your visit confirmation and self-guided tour map.
    • You and your party are allowed to be on your drive-through campus tour for 1 hour.
    • Upon arrival, please check in with Public Safety at the campus entry gates.
    • Show the officer at the gate your confirmation email.
    • Use this Google Maps tour to guide yourself through campus and read about each area as you go. You can also download a PDF map (which will be attached in your confirmation email). Please note that the bookstore, Oliver Dining Hall, and Cafe Louis are closed to the general public.
    • Stay in your car at all times and please wear your mask.
    • Pose for an in-car selfie in front of Chapel Lawn or in front of the campus.
    • Drive home safe!
