Welcome, Future Students

Fall 2021 applicants: With the exciting news about the anticipated return to primarily in-person instruction in the fall, our campus community remains hopeful in our pursuit to reduce the impact of COVID-19 on your ability to attend SF State in the fall.

Visit our FAQ page for more information. Undergraduate admissions counselors are available to guide you through the admission process. If you still need help, please email ugadmit@sfsu.edu or call (415) 338-6486.
