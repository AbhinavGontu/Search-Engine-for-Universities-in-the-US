Job Search

Welcome and thank you for visiting the Oklahoma State University career site. 

You may search and review jobs without creating a profile. When you are ready to apply, creating your profile only takes a few minutes.Your online profile allows you to apply for jobs and upload your resume.

  • To create a profile, click the Create a Profile link from any page in the career site.
  • Already have a profile? Click the Log In link and log in to view your profile and review the status of applications you have submitted.
  • OSU Employees (Faculty and Full-Time Staff): You may apply for positions on the Talent Management System. Go to talent.okstate.edu to log in with your Okey credentials.

Search Jobs to view a list of all job openings. To narrow your search by campus or employment type, select your preference in the drop down fields and refresh your search results.
