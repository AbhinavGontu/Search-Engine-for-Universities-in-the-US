REGISTER FOR THE COVID-19 VACCINATION AT THE Y

In an effort to help senior and vulnerable populations sign up to receive the COVID-19 vaccine, the YMCA of Metropolitan Dallas is teaming up with Dallas County Health and Human Services (DCHHS) to host free vaccine registration clinics at select YMCA branches beginning February 17 and continuing weekly through March 24.

READY. SET. SUMMER!

Summer at the Y is a special time!  Children experience new adventures, learn to swim, make new friends, and shape memories that last a lifetime.  Join us for summer camps, swim lessons, and sports and make this summer one to remember.

Read here about what to expect when you visit the Y, the measures we have taken to help create and maintain a safe environment, and to make your reservations for Group Exercise classes, Childwatch, and Lap Swim.

YMCA DAY OF GIVING

YMCA Day of Giving is a one-day opportunity to support the Y as we support the community.  Every dollar you give helps drowning prevention, families negatively affected by the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, children in our preschool and childcare programs, and so much more!

/ydog

YMCA AFTERSCHOOL

YMCA Afterschool offers intentional programming, from the time the bell rings to 6:30 pm - right on your child's school campus.  Join us!
