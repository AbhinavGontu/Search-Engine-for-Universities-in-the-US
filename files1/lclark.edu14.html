Campus Living

Campus Living Gallery
  

The Office of Campus Living engages students in the residential community, and designs programs and processes that encourage student learning. As part of the Division of Student Life, we empower you to learn, lead, and engage with one another.

Campus Living Philosophy

  • Celebrate diversity and inclusion;
  • Promote engagement and citizenship;
  • Foster self-advocacy and personal responsibility;
  • Champion holistic student development and the betterment of the collective.
