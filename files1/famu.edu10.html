HBCU Excellence

Founded on October 3, 1887, Florida A&M University is the highest-ranked public HBCU in the U.S. according to News & World's Report's list of national public universities. Learn more about what program may help you excel and accomplish your dreams.

WHAT TYPE OF STUDENT ARE YOU?

Freshman

3.00 GPA

You're a junior or senior in high school with your sights set on going to FAMU.

REQUIREMENTS

Transfer

2.50 GPA

You've earned twelve transferable semester college credits and you're ready to take your academic career to the next level!

REQUIREMENTS

International

Good Academic Standing

You're a Non-US citizen seeking an undergraduate degree.

REQUIREMENTS

Returning

Good Academic Standing

Did you take a break for two or more consecutive semesters? Are you ready to get back in the swing of things? This option is for you!

REQUIREMENTS

Graduate

3.00 GPA

You've already earned a baccalaureate degree from an accredited college or university and you're looking for a challenging next step.

REQUIREMENTS

Law

Average GPA 3.07

You have a bachelor's degree from an accredited college or university and you're ready to hit the classroom running.

HOW TO APPLY

We're thrilled that you're applying to Florida A&M University! We offer 54 bachelor's degrees, 29 master's degrees, three professional degrees, and twelve doctoral degrees. Find one that helps you accomplish your goals.
 

Apply Now
 

Don't Forget To:

  • Submit all official high school and/or college/university transcript(s)
  • Submit official scores for the Redesigned SAT (code: 5215 or ACT (code: 0726), if applicable
  • Submit official GED transcript or TOEFL/IELTS score, if applicable
