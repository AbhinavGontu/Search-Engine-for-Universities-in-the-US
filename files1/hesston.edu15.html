Visit Hesston College

Schedule your visit today.

Home » Admissions & Financial Aid » Visit Hesston College

Come visit Hesston College in person. Contact your admissions counselor or admissions@hesston.edu to arrange a visit.

What to Expect

At Hesston College, we believe that a campus visit is the best way to imagine yourself as a student here. Sit in on classes, meet with profs in your major, eat in the dining hall, take a campus tour with one of our Student Ambassadors and ask them for the scoop on what campus life is really like (no one knows better than a student, right?). You can do almost everything our students do except spend a night in the dorms and you will experience the genuinely warm Hesston College welcome.

Contact your admissions counselor or fill out the form to arrange an in-person visit at least four days in advance of your desired visit date.

Virtual visits remain an option for those who prefer it. Fortunately, social distancing is only physical distancing and we can still share our unmatched community with you remotely. These visits can include virtual meetings with your admissions counselor, professors, coaches, financial aid and going on a campus tour. Register for a virtual visit.
