Jobs at Weber

Weber State University seeks to create a work environment and organizational culture that reflects a diverse society and a climate for the success of every employee by appreciating the uniqueness that each one brings to the workplace. Weber State University is an equal opportunity employer and actively seeks diverse and highly qualified candidates.

Weber State University was recently named by Forbes and Statista to the 2nd annual list of America's best-in-state employers. To view the article and list of employers please click here.

Weber State University utilizes an online employment system for its application process. Applicants will search job postings and submit application materials for faculty and staff openings through an online application system. This is the only way that we will accept applications.

Note for Current Salaried Employees / Internal Users: If you are a current salaried WSU employee, do not create a new applicant account. Instead, close this webpage, log into the eWeber portal and click on the PeopleAdmin app. While logged into PeopleAdmin, return to this site. You will be logged in with your employee user account and will be able to search and apply for positions.

Benefits

WSU offers a generous benefits package to salaried employees. The benefits include medical, dental, long-term disability, life insurance, retirement, a wellness program with release time and paid incentives, tuition benefits, free tickets to athletic and performing arts events, and paid holidays, vacation, and sick leave.

Detailed information is available on the Human Resources website.
