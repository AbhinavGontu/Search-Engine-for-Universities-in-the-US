The Arts at UMaine

At the University of Maine, the arts are a way of life. Here, you can catch a Broadway show, see Grammy-winning artists live, enjoy student-run productions, and visit world-renown museums. You also can learn the arts through the wealth of our program offerings. Interested in dance? Take a class. Curious about painting? Pick up a brush. Or how about taking classes in a hot field like — filmmaking, digital music, or computer design. With our state-of-the-art facilities, we can take care of that, too. Consider this site a portal into the arts at UMaine.
