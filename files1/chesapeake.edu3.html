Do you have a question? Ask Us    FAQ Answers

Built in 2002, the Chesapeake College Library serves as the academic center of the college. Also known as the LRC, the library contains over 34,000 books, CDs, and DVDs as well as over 80,000 eBooks, streaming videos, and electronic journals. The library features an online catalog, friendly librarians, wireless network, public computers, and group study rooms. In house materials are supplemented by access to over fifty online databases.
