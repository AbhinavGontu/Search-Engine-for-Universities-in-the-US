At Jefferson, our mission is We Improve Lives. This mission guides the work that each of our employees does, every single day. Whether providing patient care, educating future leaders or collaborating behind the scenes to run the organization seamlessly, we strive to bring our mission to life through our work.

Our employees are our most important asset. Our dedicated and inspired workforce allows us to achieve success in reimagining health, education and discovery to create unparalleled value for our communities. We are committed to living our values to provide the best experience possible for our students and colleagues:

  • Put People First
  • Be Bold & Think Differently
  • Do the Right Thing
As the 2nd largest employer in the Philadelphia area, Jefferson offers competitive salaries and a generous benefits program to meet employees' needs.
