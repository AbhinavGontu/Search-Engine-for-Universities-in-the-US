Welcome New & Transfer Students!

Welcome and congratulations on your acceptance to the Haskell Indian Nations University. We are thrilled that you will be joining our Haskell family!

As a new student at Haskell, you are bound to have questions. New Student Orientation is designed around you, helping to uncover the answers to the questions you have, and providing you information about campus resources, programs and services.  By the time you leave orientation, you’ll be on your way to becoming a successful student.
