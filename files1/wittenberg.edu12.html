NCAC "Homegrown" Series - Anne Moelk, DePauw

March 3, 2021
Women's Lacrosse

DePauw second-year womens head lacrosse coach Anne Moelk has a wealth of experience in the athletics field and in higher education. She has traveled all across the country working with coaches and athletes, while serving in a variety of different roles including work as an assistant coach, a recruiting coordinator for a law practice, an assistant director of student affairs, an assistant dean, a coaching education trainer and most recently she was appointed to the World Lacrosse's newly-formed Women in Sport Commission. Moelk has also taken her talents to the Division I level, working with teams in both the Ivy League and the Big 10. She certainly has an incredible resume; however, her roots remain grounded in the North Coast Athletic Conference.
