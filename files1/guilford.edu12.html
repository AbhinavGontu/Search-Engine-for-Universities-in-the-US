We don't want to be good. We want to be great.


We are constantly setting goals we want to exceed. Our training is focusing on strengthening our abilities and making sure our team is balanced in its drive towards excellence. Our student-athletes have been training hard throughout the fall and are looking forward to returning to competition in the spring. We have several key returners as well as a talented group of freshmen who are ready to make contributions to the team’s success.

Our future success also depends on you. As a valued supporter of the baseball team, you are integral to how well we do. You can help us address funding needs for uniforms, equipment and travel expenses.
