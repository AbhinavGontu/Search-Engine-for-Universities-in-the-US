First-Year Students

You are here

  1. Home ›
  2. Admission ›
  3. First-Year Students

First-Year Students enjoy a world of opportunity in classrooms, on campus.

We offer over 35 undergraduate majors, several pre-professional programs, more than 50 clubs and organizations, 25 NCAA Division III Athletic Teams, intramural sports and scores of activities—from campus ministry and service to multicultural events.

The first step to take advantage of the value of a King’s education is to apply.

Our admission guidelines are easy to follow. If you want to take advantage of the Monarch life, get started today.
