  • Zoom in and out quickly by using the scroll wheel on your mouse.
  • Click and drag the map to pan.
  • Expand the Legend and Map Display panel to show additional information on the map.
  • Click a building on the map to select it. The Building Information panel will display related details.
  • These layers will display an info window when clicked:
    • Accessible Parking: Number of spaces
    • Bike Racks: Total capacity
    • Construction Projects: Project information
    • Road Closings: Closing information
    • Battery Collection Containers: Room number or location information
