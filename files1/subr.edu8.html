About Business Cluster

About Southern University Business and Industry Cluster

More than 1,200 companies and 80 colleges and universities nationwide participate in Cluster. Initiated by the National Alliance of Business, the Cluster program enhances the capability of historically minority colleges and universities to prepare graduates who are ready to assume professional roles in both public and private industries.

Helping strengthen educational opportunities, participating companies pool resources to assist universities. Moreover, company and college representatives work together to establish priorities in response to challenges confronting today's higher education institutions.
