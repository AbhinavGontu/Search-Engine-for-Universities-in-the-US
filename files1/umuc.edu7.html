VET TEC, a pilot program offering training for high-tech careers to Veterans, is now taking applications from potential training providers.

Education Service Yellow Ribbon Program Open Season

March 16, 2021 1:15 PM

From March 15 to May 15, 2021, we are accepting new Yellow Ribbon Program agreements, modifications to existing open-ended agreements, or agreements to withdraw from participation. See our Yellow Ribbon Program Schools’ web page for instructions and forms.

The recent passage of Public Law 116-315, Section 1008, “Technical Correction to Clarify Eligibility for Participation in Yellow Ribbon Program of Department of Veterans Affairs,” allows foreign schools to participate in the Yellow Ribbon Program. This law amends Section 3317(a) of title 38 USC. VA is working to implement this section of the law and will post information specifically for foreign schools in the near future in order to provide information on the application process for academic year 2021-2022.

January 21, 2021 11:00 AM

With the start of Spring enrollment season, the U.S. Department of Veteran Affairs (VA) is experiencing an increase in claims pending. All claims are worked in the order they are received. Currently VA is processing the majority of original enrollments within 24 days and the majority of re-enrollments within 12 days.  You can contact our call center at 1-888-442-4551 or ask a question pertaining to benefits or your claim. See how VA is responding to COVID-19 and supporting GI Bill beneficiaries.

Effective 11/5/20 STEM Scholarship Beneficiaries are Eligible for Work Study Benefits

December 2, 2020 10:45 AM

Work Study applications and contracts are currently being accepted for STEM Scholarship recipients. Rogers STEM Scholarship beneficiaries interested in Work Study may apply at VA.gov.

Verify STEM Attendance via Text

November 6, 2020 4:00 PM

Beginning this month, Rogers STEM Scholars will receive an initial opt-in text message to their primary phone number asking if they would like to participate in STEMText. If Rogers STEM Scholars opt-in to the program, they will receive text messages each month to verify their attendance.

Watch this video to learn more about the STEMText program!
