Bishop Bubbles

Levels FAQ

Bishop Bubbles provides the Delaware community with an established and well-tested learn-to-swim program. Knowledgeable and experienced Ohio Wesleyan swimmers and staff who have passion for aquatics and childhood education teach lessons.

COVID-19 Announcement

Due to COVID 19 there will be no Bishop Bubbles for the fall of 2020 and spring of 2021.Our hope is we will be back in the fall of 2021.

How Much Does It Cost?

  • First child: $75/session ($60/session for OWU employees)
  • Each additional child: $35/session.

Note: There is an applicable 10% discount off total price for signing up for all three sessions when registration and payment is received and post marked before October 4, 2019.

Registration & Payment

We have returned to “snail mail” registration to cut down on complications with communication and payment. We will be sending an email with the registration form attached. Be aware, you will not be registered until the form and payment is received. Once registered you will receive a confirmation email. No refunds for scheduling issues. If you are new and have not received an email and would like to register, please contact us at bishopbubbles@owu.edu.

Note: Please check this website to be sure that the session and track that you are registering for are open. The website will be updated as session and tracks become full.

Return payment (make checks payable to “Ohio Wesleyan Swimming & Diving”) and registration form to:
