College of Business

College of Business

The College of Business, ranked among the top 50 in the U.S., is accredited by AACSB International. Our innovative graduate programs, including the nationally recognized MBA, MPAc,  and the Graduate Certificate in Business Analytics, offer a distinctive educational experience provided by acclaimed faculty who are committed to your success.

School of Health Sciences

School of Health Sciences

Our accredited Physician Assistant Studies program offers exceptional medical education through exceptional hands-on training – first in our high-tech labs and classrooms, and then with patients in an unprecedented 12 clinical specialty rotations alongside preeminent doctors affiliated with our key partners.
