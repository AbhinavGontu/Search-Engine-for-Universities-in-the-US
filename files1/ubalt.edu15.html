Apply to the University of Baltimore

  • Home
  • Admission
  • Apply Now

To apply, complete our online application.


  • First-time Undergraduate, Transfer and Graduate Online Application
  • To Send Us Documents:
    • Students who need to send us admission-related documents, such as letters of recommendation, resumes, or official transcripts or test scores, should use documents@ubalt.edu. Please note: to be considered official, transcripts or test scores (SAT, AP, GMAT, etc.) must be sent directly from the issuing school or organization.
    • Students who need to send us information that contains Personal-Identifiable-Information should use our secured upload system (UB Log in required). Please DO NOT use this system to send admission documents.
  • How to begin your application
    1. Visit our online application and sign up.
    2. Enter your first name, last name, email and click submit.


      Please note these directions do not apply to the School of Law application.
  • How to resume a previously saved application
    1. Visit our online application and sign in.
    2. You'll be prompted to enter the email and password you created when saving your application.


      Please note these directions do not apply to the School of Law application.
  • How to reset your password
    1. Visit our online application and click on the forgot password link.
    2. Enter the email you used to sign up to request a new password


      Please note these directions do not apply to the School of Law application.

Applying online saves you time, but if you must fill out a paper form, we also have downloadable applications.
