See the Difference You Can Make

UM-Dearborn Difference Makers are at the forefront of their fields. They’re improving the lives of others and making an impact on campus and in our local communities. Nominated by faculty and staff, these individuals aren’t just outstanding students and alumni, they embody academic and professional achievement, integrity and leadership.

Being a Difference Maker doesn't end at graduation. See updates from our Difference Maker alumni.

Unfortunately, due to the COVID-19 pandemic we were unable to conduct the photo and video shoot with all 2020 Difference Makers. As you look through the website, you will see some student profiles without these assets. Our hope is to complete these elements once it is safe to return to campus.
