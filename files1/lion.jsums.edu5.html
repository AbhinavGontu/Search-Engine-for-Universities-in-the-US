Thank you for visiting the H.T. Sampson Library. We apologize for any inconvenience. Due to health concerns associated with COVID-19, please practice being safe: wear masks, maintain social distancing, and frequently wash hands.

If you need reference assistance, please call 601-979-2386 or email library@jsums.edu

Access to Resources:

  • The library homepage provides access to online resources, including databases and journal articles.
  • Interlibrary loans of physical items will be discontinued at this time until they can be safely resumed.
  • Interlibrary loans of online articles will continue to the extent libraries can supply them.

As always we thank you for your patience and ask that you remain flexible as we deal with this evolving pandemic.
