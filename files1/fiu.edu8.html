Same World-Class University, Online.

With 20 years’ experience in online education, FIU Online provides you with the opportunity to earn more than a degree. It’s your future.

Global Impact

With a student body of nearly 54,000, we are among the top 10 largest universities in the nation and have collectively graduated more than 200,000 alumni in 33 countries.

Game Changers

FIU is a South Florida anchor institution and a solutions center for the world’s toughest challenges. Our teaching is delivered by experts and shaped by our students.

Distinctions

FIU’s drive to solve global problems earns us a distinction few other universities can match, R1 Carnegie ranking — the highest research classification offered by the Carnegie Foundation for the Advancement of Teaching.

Innovation

By merging forward-thinking advances with technology and modern learning practices, we make the thinkable possible to ensure our students stand out from the competition with experiential learning.
