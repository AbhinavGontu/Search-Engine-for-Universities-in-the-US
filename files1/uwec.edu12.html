2020-21 Division III Compliance Forms

Due to Proposal NC-2020-5, institutions are no longer required to execute the following forms: “Certification of Compliance for Institutions” and “Certification of Compliance for Staff Members of Athletics Departments”. Rather, beginning August 1st and no later than October 15th, an institution’s President/Chancellor and Athletics Director will be required to attest to certain compliance obligations via the NCAA Learning Portal. Information regarding this attestation and how to access the NCAA learning portal will be forthcoming.

  • 2020-21 Division III Summary of Regulations
  • Form 20-3c: Student-Athlete Statement
  • Form 20-3f: Drug Testing Consent
  • Form 20-3c and 20-3f Combined (letter size)
  • Form 20-3c and 20-3f Combined (legal size)
  • Form 20-4c: Student-Athlete Affirmation of Eligibility
  • Form 20-10c: General Amateurism and Eligibility Form for International and Select Student-Athletes
  • 2020-21 Permission to Contact: Self-Release Form
  • 2020-21 NCAA Banned Drugs List

 Note:  Information will be lost if completed file is not saved as a separate file.
