Subscription

The National University Online Library is a popular resource for alumni and includes one of the largest collections of electronic books in the nation.

As a student, you had access to a great collection of print and online resources through the National University Library System, including eBooks on EBSCOhost (formerly NetLibrary), one of the largest collections of electronic books; EBSCO's Academic Search Alumni Version, a collection of academic online journals; and borrowing privileges from over 200,000 titles in the National University Library.

With Alumni Online Library privileges, you can continue to have access to the following key resources and services:

  • Borrowing books (free delivery outside of the San Diego area)
  • Using online journals with the alumni version of Academic Search Premier (EBSCO)
  • Access to the e-Book collection
Secure My Membership
  

*You must be registered and signed in to the Alumni Online Community to secure your membership.

The annual membership fee for alumni is $50. Sign up today and a username and password will be forwarded to you within two business days. You must have a National University ID number and an ACCESS password. This information will be e-mailed to you upon payment.

Visit www.alumni.nu.edu/librarysubscriptionpayment to secure your payment and membership online.

The Alumni Library subscription fee can also be paid by check by mailing to:

Books Direct for Alumni

What is Alumni Books Direct? 

  • A free book requesting service for remote alumni with an Online Library Membership.
  • Alumni living in the San Diego area should visit the Spectrum Library to check out books, which will be held for you at the help desk.
  • You may check out a total of 5 books for two weeks (14 days + shipping time) with one renewal. Books mailed to your home (outside of San Diego) are sent using USPS with return postage included.

Who qualifies for Alumni Books Direct?

  • Remote National University alumni with a current membership to the Online Library.
  • You must live in either the continental United States or Hawaii.

How do I request books?

  • Search the Library Catalog to find what you need.
  • Fill out and submit the online Books Direct Request Form using the ID number you received with your Alumni Online Library membership.

Alumni Library Circulation Policy. If you have further questions, please call (858) 541-7900 or send an e-mail to circdesk@nu.edu.
