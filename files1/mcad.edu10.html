elkeating:

Unfortunately this year I wasn’t able to do the full 24 hours of 24 hour comic day, but in the spirit of it we did a smaller assignment, 4 pages in 4 hours, for my Comic Media and Concepts class. The comics I made over the summer for my mini-anthology Venti really prepared me for this because it helped teach me what I can do with straight black and white and how to create a finished-looking piece in a short amount of time. 

This piece is really personal, I actually made this the day before my consultation for HRT, so I think my anxiety over that sort of influenced the subject. I also get asked why I picked the name Delta a lot, so I figured it would make as good a story as any for this. 

Be sure to keep an eye out for this in Volume 2 of Delta Church Semi-Annual! I’m slowly but surely building up content for it, so it’s on its way!

Part 1 of a “Work” series in Concepts and Metaphors.
Job - Grade School Teacher
Descriptive Word - Growing
Concept - All of the children have a plant sprouting from their heads - an indication of their mind / brain / comprehension level and growth! Done in a mid-century modern style. The words on the chalkboard are “dog” in different languages - which will relate to the second half of the project, a Hollywood Animal Trainer!
