Welcome to HRM L&OD

HRM L&OD at Mississippi State University is committed to the professional development of our employees. With the agreement of your supervisor, your participation in learning and development programs and services is welcomed and encouraged. Employee training courses are designed to improve your efficiency, effectiveness, and enjoyment on the job.
