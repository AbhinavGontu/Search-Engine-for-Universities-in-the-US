Affordable + Amazing

We hear you: no student wants high debt at the start of their career. Nearly half of all EWU students graduate without student loans.

Flexibility + Safety

When EWU makes decisions about COVID-19, your health and investment in EWU are protected. There will be no bait and switch. We follow CDC guidelines and have established a mix of online classes and in-person labs.

Diversity + Inclusion

EWU has six cultural centers. Students attend from 43 states and 70 countries. More than 1 in 3 students come from diverse backgrounds. Nearly 50 percent are first in their families to attend college. EWU thrives on all kinds of diversity, and so will you.
