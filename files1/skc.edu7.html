SKC Undergraduate Research Symposium

August 1-2, 2020

The Salish Kootenai College Life Sciences Department is hosting the second annual Undergraduate Research Symposium at the Robert DePoe III building this summer of 2020. Undergraduate research highlights the next generation of scientists and their dedicated mentors. This scientific symposium will be from August 1-2, 2020 at the Salish Kootenai College and will include poster and oral presentations, a keynote speaker, and activities on the Flathead lake and reservation in Montana.
