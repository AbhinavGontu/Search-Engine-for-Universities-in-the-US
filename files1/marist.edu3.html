COVID-19 Guest Self-Reporting Instructions

The New York State "New York Forward" Reopening Plan requires visitors to complete the Self-Reporting Survey below before coming to campus.

Each member of your group will need to fill out this form individually.  If a member of the group is under 18, the parent or legal guardian of the person listed can fill out the form.

For more information regarding visiting campus click here.

 

Enter the name of the person this form is being completed for

Are you in violation of the New York State COVID-19 travel advisory guidelines?

Have you experienced any of the following symptoms of COVID-19 in the past 14 days?

Fever, chills, cough, shortness of breath or difficulty breathing, fatigue, muscle or body aches, headache, new loss of taste or smell, sore throat, congestion or runny nose, nausea or vomiting, diarrhea.

Have you tested positive for COVID-19 in the past 14 days?
