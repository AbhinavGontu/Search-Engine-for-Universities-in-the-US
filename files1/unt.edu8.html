Tours

  • CORONAVIRUS (COVID-19) UPDATES

    In the interest of the health and safety of our campus tour visitors and university community, we are offering limited capacity in-person walking tours and guided virtual tours only at this time. UNT requires the use of face coverings by all campus tour visitors and community members inside buildings and in public settings outside, as well as maintaining social distancing measures. Appropriate use of face coverings is critical in minimizing risks to others near you.

    As tour capacity is limited, tours fill quickly. If you are unable to find a tour that meets your scheduling needs, please contact us at 940-565-4104 to be placed on a list to be contacted when additional tours or times are available.

    For current information on the university response to COVID-19 please visit healthalerts.unt.edu.
