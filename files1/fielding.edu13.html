MA in Media Psychology On Demand Info Session

Register below to receive a direct link to the Recorded Webinar for an in-depth look at earning your MA in Media Psychology at Fielding.

Be a Pioneer in an Emerging Field! Media Psychology applies the psychology of human behavior and emotion to media and technology impact, design and distribution. Our students come from a variety of professional experiences and academic backgrounds with the common goal of applying psychological science to the media and technology landscape.

In this recoreded webinar, we’ll identify key attributes of our programs and what distinguishes our program from the rest. We will outline our program components, as well as discuss our educational model.
 
Learning Objectives:
Develop an understanding of the components of the program
Identify the distinctive features of Fielding’s program
Understand the educational model
Learn the admission requirements and eligibility
