Freshmen & Transfer Scholarships

FHSU offers first-time, full-time, on-campus freshman and on-campus transfer students the opportunity to receive automatic scholarships. Deadline is June 30, 2021. Apply now.

 

Free Unofficial Transcript Evaluations

Now is the time to complete your degree. Request your free unofficial transcript evaluation and connect with a transfer success coach.

 

Academic Programs for Service Members and Veterans

FHSU offers three academic programs that are specifically designed for active military and veterans. Learn more about our AAS in Technology and Leadership, Officer Candidate School, and Air University (AU-ABC).

 

FHSU's Covid-19 Response

Fort Hays State University is continuing to evaluate and monitor the situation surrounding the spread of the 2019 novel coronavirus (COVID-19) and communicating continuously with state and local health care agencies. For current updates, resources and information visit
