Group Sales for Women's Basketball, Soccer, Volleyball and Football

Print

Butler Athletics offers a variety of opportunities for groups at experiences for your group at all ticketed athletic events.

For more information on group opportunities at men's basketball, please click HERE.

Football

High-Five Tunnels
Groups wishing to participate in the tunnel program should contact Brady Collier, Director of Sales, at (317) 940-6118 or blcollie@butler.edu

Volleyball

High-Five Tunnels
Groups wishing to participate in the starting lineup tunnel program should contact the ticket office at tickets@butler.edu or 317-940-8919.

The maximum tunnel size is 20. All participants must be between the ages of 5 and 12.

National Anthem Performances
Groups wishing to sing the national anthem should contact Lindsay Martin at lmartin@butler.edu

Video Board Recognition for Groups
Groups purchasing 50 tickets or more will be listed on the video board during the 1/2 Set Break. Groups with fewer than 50 tickets may purchase recognition by completing the form here.


Women's Basketball

High-Five Tunnels
Groups wishing to participate in the starting lineup tunnel program should contact the ticket office at tickets@butler.edu or 317-940-8919.

The maximum tunnel size is 25. All participants must be between the ages of 5 and 12.

National Anthem or Halftime Performances
Groups wishing to sing the national anthem or perform during halftime at women's basketball should contact athleticsintern@butler.edu or 317-940-8934

Video Board Recognition for Groups
Groups purchasing 50 tickets or more will be listed on the video board during the 2nd quarter media timeout. Groups with fewer than 50 tickets may purchase recognition by completing the form here.


Men's & Women's Soccer

Lineup Mascot Program
Groups wishing to participate in the starting lineup mascot program where youth accompany both the home and visiting team onto the field for starting lineup announcements should contact the ticket office at tickets@butler.edu or 317-940-8919.

The maximum participant number is 25. All participants must be between the ages of 5 and 12.

Other Group Opportunities

Birthday parties
Church or community organization outings
Employee social events
Youth Sports Nights

Tickets can be purchased in bulk or each group member can order online using a promotional code provided by the Butler ticket office.
