Salina, Kansas -- Our Home

Kansas Wesleyan's home is Salina, KS, a thriving city of 50,000 located at the crossroads of I-70 and I-135. Whether it's community support like Purple Friday, proximity to metro areas such as Kansas City (three hours), Wichita (one hour) or Denver (six hours), or local restaurants and hot spots, Salina has it! Read on to learn more about our great home.
