Accessibility @ Hampshire

Accessibility & Inclusion: A Campus-Wide Collaboration

Hampshire College strives to create a Universally Designed and barrier-free environment for all College community members and visitors to campus to enjoy the education, employment, services, programs, and activities, regardless of ability. Where barriers still exist, College community members work together to create solutions for and accommodate individuals with disabilities.

If you have a specific question or concern about accessibility at Hampshire and would like to share it for members of CORAL (Community Outreach and Resources for Accessible Learning) to address, please complete the anonymous accessibility feedback form.
