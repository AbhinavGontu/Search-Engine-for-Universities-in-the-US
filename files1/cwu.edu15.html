Academic Advising - DECLARED MAJORS

Updates for Fall 2020 Quarter.


All staff in the Professional Advising office are currently working remotely. Here is how you can contact your advisor or our office:

 

Current CWU students: Email your assigned advisor using your CWU Outlook email account or go to your advising canvas course and schedule a Zoom appointment. When scheduling a Zoom appointment please note that the page times out quickly and we recommend having your schedule in front of you.

 

Prospective and new incoming summer or fall students: Click on the major advising link below to bring up a list of advisors along with the majors that they support. You can click on the advisors name to find their email address.

 

 

The advisors and support staff are working diligently to respond to emails as quickly as possible. Please allow 2 business days for us to reply.
