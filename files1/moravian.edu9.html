Welcome to AMOS
Access Moravian Online Services
 
AMOS is the web portal for prospective students, current students, faculty, and staff of the College and Seminary. You'll need your NetID and password to log in. If you've applied to Moravian, log in using your personal email address and the password you set in the Account Activation process.  Your NetID will be delivered to you through AMOS once it has been created.
 
If you need technical help, contact the IT Help Desk at help@moravian.edu or click on the IT Help tab for a link to our page with more help resources.
