Graduate Programs

Explore Colorado State University Graduate School programs with our finder below.

World-class teaching and research work together to produce a top-ranked educational experience for Colorado State University graduate students. CSU is consistently ranked as one of the nation’s top academic institutions in a wide variety of categories – from teaching and learning to research and student achievement in studios, laboratories, and classrooms. CSU is recognized among doctoral universities with very high research activity, commonly referred to as “R1” by Carnegie. Our distinguished faculty are not only creating the latest knowledge, they are sharing it with their students.
