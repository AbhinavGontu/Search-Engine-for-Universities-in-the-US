Welcome!

You are Not Logged In

You are currently not logged in to My Graceland so you have only the most generic information available to you.

 
If you are a current user, please log in using the section above.
Welcome to My Graceland. This website is the place for people to come for information tailored to their specific relationship with Graceland University. 

Current Students

Students currently taking classes can access the site with their normal network username and password. Usernames look something like jwd1 and are not your full email address. Students who forgot their network passwords should read more here.

Past Students

Students who leave Graceland continue to have access to My Graceland but not to Graceland's full network. Past students who don't know their password should read more here.

Current Employees

Faculty and staff can currently access the site with their normal network username and password. Employees who do not know their network information should speak with their supervisors. Employees who forget their network passwords should read more here.

Past Employees

Employees who leave Graceland continue to have access to My Graceland (but not to Graceland's full network) for 7 years. Past Employees who don't know their password should read more here. 

Prospective Students

We are now providing early access to My Graceland for accepted students. You will have received an email about your account.  You will have some custom content available here. For general program information please go to www.graceland.edu. 

Alumni and Friends

The site is now available for alumni and friends who do not already have access. Please click on the Alumni and Friends tab  and fill out the form to request access.  You may also click on www.graceland.edu to visit our publicly accessible site.

Parents and Spouses

Parents and spouses of current students will also be given My Graceland access. For now visit The GUest Access FAQ for more information about access to our older web site.

General Website

The My Graceland site is designed for people with an existing relationship with Graceland. If you're looking for our public site, please continue to www.graceland.edu.
