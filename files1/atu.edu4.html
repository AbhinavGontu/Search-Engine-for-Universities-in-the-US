Spirit Squads

Mission

Our mission is to emphasize and teach the importance of safety, discipline, school spirit, unity, and pride. Spirit Squads must represent the school to the highest degree and set an example of good behavior and sportsmanship at all times (in uniform or not). Within the spirit organization the two programs must support the athletics of the university and uphold an image of professionalism and spirit for the university and community.

Learning Outcomes
Students participating as members of the ATU spirit squads will:

  1. Gain a greater understanding of effective teambuilding strategies
  2. Improve their time management skills
  3. Develop a stronger sense of belonging to the Arkansas Tech University community

Our mission is to emphasize and teach the importance of safety, discipline, school spirit, unity, and pride. Spirit Squads must represent the school to the highest degree and set an example of good behavior and sportsmanship at all times (in uniform or not). Within the spirit organization the two programs must support the athletics of the university and uphold an image of professionalism and spirit for the university and community.

TECH CHEER

Tech Cheer is composed of up to sixteen members. Tech Cheer participates in all home conference games for football, basketball, and volleyball and select away games for football and basketball. 

 GOLDEN GIRLS

The Golden Girls are composed of up to fourteen members. The Golden Girls participate and perform at all home football and conference basketball games.

Learn more about Spirit Squads tryouts

 Both squads participate in pep rallies throughout the year and various campus and community related events.
