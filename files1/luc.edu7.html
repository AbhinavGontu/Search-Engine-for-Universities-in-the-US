    Ask Kayhan Parsi, JD, PhD, to reflect on the proudest moment of his career working in bioethics and he won’t cite his own publications or accolades—he’ll tell you about the success of his students.READ MORE
  • Video

    Online Learning in Bioethics

    The Master of Arts in Bioethics and Health Policy is one of three online graduate degree programs offered through the Neiswanger Institute for Bioethics, which is housed at Loyola's Stritch School of Medicine. This fully online program was started in 2002, having graduated over 170 students. We also offer an online certificate program and a doctoral program that includes some blended courses.
  • Video

    Health Care Mission Leadership in Loyola's Institute of Pastoral Studies

    The MA in Health Care Mission Leadership offers professional preparation for those in senior leadership positions in a health care environment who are charged with developing, enhancing, cultivating, and protecting the identity and mission of their organizations.
  • Video

    Online Learning through Loyola's Institute of Pastoral Studies

    Learn about online learning options with Loyola University Chicago's Institute of Pastoral Studies. Several Loyola degree programs may be completed entirely online.

J-Term 2021

J-Term Online at Loyola

Online classes during J-Term give students the opportunity to get ahead in their studies while living off-campus. Over 100 online courses will be offered during J-Term 2021, many of which are available to visiting, non-degree students as well as Loyola students. Registration will open in fall 2020 for Loyola students. Register through LOCUS. Visiting students should apply for J-term courses in fall 2020.
