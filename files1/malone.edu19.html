2020-21 Season Tickets
Print
8/10/2016 12:00:00 AM
In response to the ever-changing landscape surrounding the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, the Malone University Athletic Department has announced updates to its ticket policy beginning with the basketball and volleyball competitions January 28-30.
 
A limited number of spectators will be permitted to attend each game played inside Osborne Hall. Right now, this access is limited to families of Malone University players and coaches. There is also a ticket allotment given to the visiting team. These tickets will continue to be pre-sale only, and must be redeemed ahead of time through our online ticketing partner Eventbrite. There are still no walk-up transactions permitted at the gate.
 
For all doubleheader basketball games, attendance will be limited to these groups only.
 
However, for all volleyball matches, and any potential future single basketball games, there will be an allotment of tickets available to the Malone University student body. These tickets must be claimed ahead of time through the Student Athlete Advisory Committee (SAAC).
 
The policy is constantly being reevaluated throughout the ongoing pandemic.
 
As has been the case all season long, all home basketball and volleyball competitions will continue to be broadcast live on the Great Midwest Digital Network.
