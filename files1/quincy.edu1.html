Get ready for ... 2021 Commencement

The 2021 Spring Commencement Ceremony is scheduled for Saturday, May 8th – rain or shine.  In the event of severe weather, the in-person ceremonies will be moved to Sunday, May 9th. The current plan is to hold two morning ceremonies at QU Stadium, times to be determined.  Graduates will be able to sign up for a specific ceremony until available slots are filled. Due to current COVID-19 restrictions, the number of guests will be limited. All plans are subject to change based on state and/or local guidelines.  Further plans will be announced.

 

Any questions regarding Commencement can be directed to:

Registrar’s Office, 217-228-5432, ext. 3970, or email: registrar@quincy.edu
