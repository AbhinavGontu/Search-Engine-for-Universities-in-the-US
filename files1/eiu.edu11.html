Undocumented Students

Eastern Illinois University welcomes all applicants regardless of citizenship status. Diversity and inclusion are core values of our institution, and we support all students in their pursuit of higher education at EIU.

Undocumented students who apply to EIU, with or without Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals (DACA), are treated identically to any U.S. citizen or permanent resident.
