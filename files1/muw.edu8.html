MUW Foundation

Welcome to the MUW Foundations website!  Thank you for visiting us to make an online gift or to learn more about the MUW Foundation, our mission, those who serve on our 2020 Board of Directors and ways to support The W.

The MUW Foundation is a 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to securing private funding to support the mission of Mississippi University for Women. While the State of Mississippi provides a portion of the funds necessary for The W to operate, private funding from alumni, friends, businesses, foundations, and other organizations is essential to The Ws continued success.

Alumni from all walks of life all share in the unique educational experiences The W has given us. Friends of the university recognize the tremendous asset The W is in the community, the state, and the region. Our contributions greatly enhance the lives of W students and the efforts of outstanding faculty and staff committed to preparing our students for brighter futures and success beyond the classroom.

We invite you to be a part of transforming students lives at The W. Please join me in giving to the MUW Foundation and in being part of what makes The W such an extraordinary University.

 

Sincerely,

Jayne Perkins-Brown, Class of 1977

Chair

MUW Foundation Board of Directors
