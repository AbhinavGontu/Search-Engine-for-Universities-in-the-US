CampusAlerts



Every student is automatically registered to receive an email from CampusAlerts when there is an emergency situation in which you need to take immediate action (tornado is approaching, fire in the building, suspicious or armed person on or near campus).  We recommend that in order to receive a text message in an emergency situation, you go to the CampusAlerts KWU secure page and enter your cell phone number. You may also enter the phone number or email of a parent or family member. Events such as campus closings due to inclement weather will be communicated via the front page of the KWU website in a RED banner as well as through a campus email.

Stay active and healthy

Students, faculty, staff and alumni have access to The Everett Morgan Strength Training Center, a state-of-the-art facility that includes numerous types of exercise equipment and fitness programs.

Explore your new hometown, a thriving community of 50,000 that offers many unique partnership opportunities. Students participate in Salina Community Theater, the Salina Symphony and Chorale and other local businesses.
