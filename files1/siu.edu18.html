6:30pm
Alumni Reunion 2021 is cancelled.
SIU School of Medicine

After much consideration, due to COVID-19 and the uncertainty of large group gatherings in June, Reunion 2021 will not take place. Based on feedback from class reunion committees and discussion with Dean & Provost Dr. Kruse, the feelings are that it would be too soon for people to feel comfortable traveling, and it’s just too early. We know this is disappointing news. The Office of Alumni Affairs will work with each reunion class committee to develop virtual or other class connections if they choose to do so and when it is safe.
