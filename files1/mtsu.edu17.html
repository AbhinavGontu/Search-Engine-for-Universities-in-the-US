MTSU Cheerleading

Welcome to the official site of MTSU Cheerleading! Join us at all football games and home basketball games as we cheer the Raiders to victory! You may also see us representing MTSU at pep rallies, university and alumni functions, and community and charity events. Keep up with our squad by liking us on Facebook and following us on Instagram.
 
 

Appearance Requests
