Ticket Information

INDIVIDUAL GAME PRICING
 
VOLLEYBALL/SOCCER/BASKETBALL
  • General Admission: $10
  • Youth (12 & Under): $5
  • Seniors (55 ): $5
  • Visiting Students (w/college ID): $5
  • Kids (2 & Under): Free
  • CSUEB Students/Staff/Faculty: Free (with ID)

All other athletic home events are free for all viewers to attend.

Tickets are available at the Pioneer Gym ticket window (basketball & volleyball) and the Pioneer Stadium front gate (soccer) beginning one hour before game time. Only cash is accepted at this time.


GROUP TICKETS

Group outings are a great way to get co-workers, family or friends together for a fun-filled day or night of Pioneer Athletics! Group rates are available for home games in men's basketball, women's basketball, volleyball, men's soccer, and women's soccer.

For more information on purchasing group tickets, please contact the Facilities and Events Coordinator at (510) 885-3088.
