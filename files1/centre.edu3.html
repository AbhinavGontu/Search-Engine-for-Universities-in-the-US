New CTL Collaborative Space

 

The Center for Teaching and Learning has a new office and collaborative space on the third floor of Crounse hall.  We are excited to welcome faculty to this space, once COVID restrictions are lifted. 

Browse the photo slideshow of our new common area.  We are thrilled to have a space that faculty can use to collaborate with CTL staff and one another; engage in conversations related to teaching, instructional technology, and experiential learning; relax and browse reading materials related to teaching and learning and more. 

Check out our page with images and videos of the new CTL space.
