APPLY FOR FREE THIS MARCH!

Now through March 31 (EST).

Submit the Messiah University Application with NO APPLICATION FEE! Save $50 by using the promo code SPRING. If you are a first-year, domestic student you may also choose to submit The Common Application for free—just be sure to select the school-specific fee waiver for SPRING on Messiah's member page.

FIRST-YEAR APPLICANTS

  • Students in their senior year of high school.
  • Have earned a high school diploma and have earned less than 12 college credits after high school
  • Have earned a high school diploma and have never attended college.
  • Start your Messiah University application
  • The Common Application
  • Login to continue your Messiah University application
  • Admissions status page

TRANSFER APPLICANTS

Students that have earned a high school diploma and more than 12 college credits after high school.
  • Start your application
  • Login to continue your application
  • Admissions status page

Dual Enrollment Applicants

Students that are a junior or senior in high school who wish to take college courses through the Messiah University dual enrollment program.
  • Start your application
  • Login to continue your application
  • Admissions status page

*To submit your application, we do not need to have your transcripts or test scores. Those may be sent after the submission of the application.

Note: Students who have already attended Messiah and are seeking to re-enroll can access the form on the Registrar's Office website.
