Disability Services Registration

Accommodations

Accommodations will be provided on-campus for in-person classes, housing and research laboratories and through Zoom and other University-sponsored platforms for virtual events, classes and experiential learning (e.g. social work field placements). Learn more about available accommodations in our list of Services below.

Contact Us

Students & Faculty can contact us at any time by phone at 212-854-2388 or by email at disability@columbia.edu for more information and answers to questions regarding our services.

    Additional Resources

    Disability Services Liaisons

    Disability Services liaisons are designated faculty or staff from each school, academic program, or campus service who work with Disability Services to coordinate accommodations for students with disabilities.

    Notice of Non Discrimination

    Columbia University is committed to providing a working, learning and living environment free from discrimination and harassment and to fostering a nurturing and vibrant community founded upon the fundamental dignity and worth of all of its members. For more information, please contact the office of Equal Opportunity and Affirmative Action (EOAA). 

    Columbia Health Annual Report

    Read highlights from the Columbia Health Annual Report.

    Disability Services administered and provided oversight for 12,630 accommodated exams to undergraduate students on behalf of faculty, an increase of 21% over the previous year.

    Over 3,000 students received accommodations from Disability Services, an increase of 13.5% over the prior year.

    Due to COVID-19, demand for e-text increased by more than 110%, partly as a result of independent asynchronous learning, with over 700,000 pages of electronic text provided by Disability Services.
