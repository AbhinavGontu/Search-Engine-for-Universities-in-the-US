Athletic Communications

The athletic communications office at the University of Wisconsin-Parkside is led by Sean Daniels along with Dylan Derousseau, Graduate Assistant for Athletic Communications. The office is responsible for the following:
* Statistical compilation and dissemination.
* Handling of all printed matter including media guides, game-day programs and game notes.
* Managing the Parkside Athletics website (www.parksiderangers.com).
* GLIAC and NCAA reporting.
* Fulfilling requests of local, regional and national media.
* Compile and distribute releases both to the local media and hometowns of appropriate athletes.
* Maintain school records and archives.
* Coordinate radio broadcasts of all home and road basketball games. Serve as a host for visiting radio.
* Social media, including Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, SnapChat and Youtube.

Working media who wish to interview a Parkside student-athlete, coach, or administrator should contact Sean Daniels (danielss@uwp.edu) 262-595-2045) to make arrangements. Game credentials and practice schedules may also be available through the Sports Media Relations Office.
