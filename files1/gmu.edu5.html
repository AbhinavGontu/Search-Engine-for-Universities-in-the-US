New Student and Family Programs (NSFP) recognizes the important role families play in their student’s development and success at Mason. NSFP strives to develop relationships and support families throughout their student’s college experience. Serving as a centralized resource, NSFP is a liaison between the university and families, and provides an outlet for families to become active members of the Mason community through programming and outreach.

If you have additional questions, or if we can help in any way, please contact us at (703) 993-2475 or email at families@gmu.edu.
