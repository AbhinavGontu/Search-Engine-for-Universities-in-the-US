Voted #1 Public Golf Course in Arkansas Three Years in a Row

Cooper’s Hawk Golf Club in Melbourne Arkansas has been voted #1 Public Golf Course in Arkansas for 3 years in a row. This poll is held by Arkansas Business – Executive Golfer Magazine. Cooper’s Hawk received 6,732 votes and the 2nd place course received 3,841 votes. A clear victory!

Cooper’s Hawk Golf Course has 18-championship holes, a large practice green, a practice range, Champion G-12 Ultradwarf Bermudagrass, and zoysia fairways. We have a fully stocked Pro Shop with great prices. We also have a full-service restaurant with great food, from burgers to prime rib and a full-service bar. You’ve played the rest now come and experience the best.
