    COLLEGES & SCHOOLS

    St. Thomas is a Catholic university with rich cultural and international diversity committed to the academic and professional success of its students who become ethical leaders in our global community

    BUSINESS

    The Gus Machado College of Business at St. Thomas University is committed to educating tomorrow’s global leaders. Our expert faculty prepares students for the evolving realities of the global marketplace.

    BISCAYNE COLLEGE

    Biscayne College’s various majors provide background and knowledge for careers or further professional studies based on the experiences and research of an outstanding and creative faculty.

    LAW

    St. Thomas Law trains and graduates lawyers capable of applying legal principles to address and solve problems of an increasingly complex and changing society.

    SCIENCE

    The College of Science, Technology, Engineering & Health will provide an education of excellence, rigor, and relevance in the natural sciences, health, and technology that will prepare you to seek your future.
