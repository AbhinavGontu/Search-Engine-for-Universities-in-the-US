A Cornerstone of the Fort Lauderdale Community

Beaux Arts is a volunteer organization whose mission is to encourage an understanding and appreciation of the arts in Fort Lauderdale. Beaux Arts proudly supports Museum on the Move, an innovative new education program that brings Broward County sixth graders to NSU Art Museum Fort Lauderdale for an interactive tour and learning experience. Beaux Arts also hosts an annual Spring Honoree Luncheon to help garner support for future art education initiatives at NSU Art Museum.  Finally, Beaux Arts provides support to the NSU Art Museum by contributing to the museum’s ongoing operations & enhancing its educational programming and outreach.
