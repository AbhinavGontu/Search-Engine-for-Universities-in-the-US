Monday, April 12, 2021

Dallas / Fort Worth Tiger Virtual Mixer

Wednesday, April 14, 2021

Young Alumni Leaders: Champions For Change
Please join us for our first-ever virtual discussion with some of Oxy’s young alumni leaders as they share their inspiring career journeys and how their paths have been shaped by Oxy’s four pillars: excellence, equity, community, and service.

Saturday, April 17, 2021

“Finnish Lessons 2.0” ALOED’s Spring Book Club
Join ALOED’s spring book club for a discussion of Finnish Lessons 2.0 by Pasi Sahlberg. The book details the complexity of meaningful change by illustrating Finland’s educational performance in light of the most recent international assessment data.
