    		Bridges

     

    The Department of Instructional Design’s Spring Semester 2021 office hours for Bridges, Panopto, or other instructional technologies are Monday - Friday from 8:00am - 5:00pm, EST.  *For virtual office hours consultation via Zoom, please email us at id@rwu.edu for an appointment, or view our on-campus hours.

    *Days/times are subject to change without notice. 

    Please make sure you are directing all of your communications to id@rwu.edu and not to individual team members so we can better manage your needs and support you more efficiently.

     

    • Talking about Teaching - Tablets Recording - Thursday, February 18, 2021

    • Keynote Speaker Jose Bowen Recording - Thursday, January 28, 2021

     

    Important Information and Additional Resources:

    • For Login/Password issues, please contact the IT MediaTech Desk, mediatech@rwu.edu or call (401) 254.6363.
    • For general Bridges support, please email id@rwu.edu or call us at (401) 254.3187. For online support, go here.
    • Supported browsers for Bridges on all platforms are Chrome or Firefox only.
    • To Refresh or Clear Browser Caching for Chrome or Firefox, click here.
    • Profile Photos in Bridges are pulled in and refreshed from another source every Thursday. For more information on this, please click here.
    • All Bridges course sites are Unpublished after 60 days end-of-term policy (when final grades are due). Students that need access to prior or past courses need to contact the respective instructor. In the event the instructor has left the institution, please contact the Dean's Office.
    • Bridges restarts every Sunday morning at 3:00AM EDT and is unavailable for 10-15 minutes.
