OFFICIAL CAMP WEBSITE OF | SMC Running Camps

 

SMC Running Camps & Clinics are dedicated to all aspects of the sport including skill development, strategy, competition and mechanics. Athletes will learn leading edge running drills, core and strength development and flexibility activation. They will have their running form both analyzed and critiqued for optimum efficiency. There will be  discussions with professional distance runners on how to be the best, as well as discussing modern training theories and the components needed to maximize their potential.

Saint Mary's College is very fortunate to have some of the finest coaches in Northern California. Head Coach Marty Kinsey is proud to bring aboard Associate Head Coach, Tom Kloos, who has competed professionally and coached multiple national champions including multiple Olympians and world medalist. All camp instructors have been chosen for their extensive knowledge of the sport and their enthusiasm to work with children and young athletes.
