SPRING 2021 COMMENCEMENT

We are pleased to honor our 2020 and spring 2021 graduates this May through two different, optional commencement ceremony formats. We are working diligently to plan safe and memorable commencement ceremonies, both virtually and in-person at Globe Life Field.
