Our personal attention is matched by our commitment to academic excellence. At SSU, you will be challenged to explore new worlds, ideas, and ways of thinking. Our
Community

We welcome your point of view. No matter where you come from, or where you're going. Whether you want to make a stand, build a bridge or just take a hike. When you join our family, you become part of a diverse community — on and off campus — that's focused on always making Shawnee State and Portsmouth an even better place to work, live and discover.

Because when it comes down to it, our story is still being written every day — just like yours.
