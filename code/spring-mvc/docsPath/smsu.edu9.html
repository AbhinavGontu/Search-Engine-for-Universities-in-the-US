Coronavirus (COVID-19)

Last updated 3/8/2021

Southwest Minnesota State University is closely monitoring the emerging public health concern involving the 2019 Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19).

If you have traveled abroad to an area affected by COVID-19 or have had close contact with a confirmed case of COVID-19, health officials encourage you to seek further guidance from your preferred health professional.

For information on the status of events at SMSU, visit our news page at www.SMSU.edu/today or visit our Facebook page at  http://www.facebook.com/smsutoday for the latest information.

Minnesota State has developed a dashboard for reporting COVID-19 case data impacting the colleges and universities and the system office. The dashboard will serve as a tool to inform campus communities and aid decision-making stemming from changes in COVID-19 cases and changes in community spread.
