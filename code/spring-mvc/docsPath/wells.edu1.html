  • Give to Wells

Your support makes a profound difference; please consider a donation to the Wells Fund today

Your gift plays an essential role in ensuring students today are able to complete their education while having the best experience possible. Together, we can provide each student with the resources they need to get the degree they deserve so they too can make a difference in their communities.

 You can provide your support for Wells College in several ways.

  • Make a gift online using the giving button on this page. 
  • Make a credit card gift by phone or to make arrangements for a gift of stocks, bonds or mutual funds. Please contact Lori Rook in the Advancement Office at 315.364.3476. Download Transfer Instructions.
  • Send a gift by personal check. Checks should be made out to "Wells College," and mailed to: Office of Advancement, Wells College, 170 Main Street, Aurora, NY 13026 
  • You can direct distributions in the amount of your choosing from your traditional or Roth IRA. For more information, click here.
  • Check to see if your employer will match your gift to Wells. 

For information on making a planned gift or including Wells in your estate planning, contact us at 315.364.3260.

HELPFUL INFORMATION FOR 2020 YEAR-END GIVING: 

For credit card gifts: Wells College will be processing gifts made online using credit cards up to 11:59 pm on Thursday, December 31. According to IRS guidelines, in order to deduct your gift in tax year 2020, the gift should be reflected on your credit card statement with a 12/31/20 transaction date.

To give a credit card gift by phone please call the Office of Advancement at 315.364.3476 by 4:00 PM EST on Tuesday, December 22. We will be accepting calls on Thursday, December 31 from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM EST to assist you with any last-minute donations or questions.

For checks: If there are any concerns about a gift counting towards 2020 we recommend a check dated and post-marked no later than December 31 be mailed to the Wells College Advancement Office, 170 Main Street, Aurora, NY 13026.

For gifts of securities: Gifts of stock, bonds or mutual funds to the College must be completed by 12/31/20.

CARES Act 2020: Legislation known as the CARES Act, designed to rescue the economy from the effects of the coronavirus pandemic, was passed by Congress and signed into law by the president on March 27, 2020. Click here for a summary of provisions applicable to charitable giving included in the Act, officially named the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act.

Your gift makes a difference. Thank you for your support of Wells.
