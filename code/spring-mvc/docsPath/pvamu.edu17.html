Founded in 1987, the PVAMU Sports Hall of Fame is dedicated to recognizing those outstanding individuals that have represented Prairie View A&M University athletically with pride, excellence, and determination.  
 
THIS IS ONLY THE BEGINNING!
With the launching of PVPanthers.com, the Prairie View A&M Sports Hall Of Fame now has its own dedicated area to honor the greats of Prairie View A&M Athletics.  The PV Sports Hall Of Fame Committee would like to properly enshrine its members so if you have a picture and/or bio of one of an honoree, please submit the information electronically to Laura Collins at ljcollins@pvamu.edu.
