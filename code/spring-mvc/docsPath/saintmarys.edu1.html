Spirituality Hour

Join the Alumnae Association Board of Directors as they kick off their monthly "Spirituality Hour." These events will be an informal and fun way to connect with fellow Belles on a variety of topics centered in spirituality through both presentations and small group discussions. Every alumna is welcome to attend, regardless of religious background or level of "expertise." Click here for events and registration!

Introducing SMC Talks
The Alumnae Association Board of Directors invites you to join SMC Talks. SMC Talks is a virtual seminar modeled after TED Talks, whose slogan is “ideas worth spreading.” Each short program will feature an alumna who shares insights on professional topics, personal growth, spiritual inspiration, or distinctive talents. Click here to learn more!
Meet our New President, Dr. Katie Conboy!
This fall, President Katie Conboy will be participating in ten virtual Coffee and Conversation events hosted by several of our outstanding alumnae clubs! Keep an eye on our events section below to see where she is headed next.

Welcome Alumnae!

To all Saint Mary’s College alumnae and friends, welcome home! Our alumnae are a group of women with a wealth of talent, experience, know-how, access to innumerable resources, and they can do much to perpetuate the legacy of Saint Mary’s College.

Saint Mary’s College honors the rich tradition that has established her as a nationally recognized Catholic women’s college dedicated to empowering women to make a difference in the world. All alumnae are called to support and advance the mission of Saint Mary’s College.
