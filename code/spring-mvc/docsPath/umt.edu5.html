2020-21 Academic Calendar

The 2021-2022 academic calendar is under review and may be adjusted given the status of COVID-19. UM is working collaboratively with university administrators, faculty and other constituent groups, along with our colleagues within the Montana University System to finalize an academic calendar and will provide an update as soon as possible.

Dates listed below are subject to change without notice; dates appearing in registration instructions take precedence over those listed below.

NOTE: UM students taking coursework at campuses other than UM's Mountain, Missoula College, or Bitterroot College Program campuses should consult that campus' academic calendar.

The Alexander Blewett III School of Law academic calendar is available here.

Summer Semester 2021

Summer sessions are offered in 3-week, 6-week and 12-week formats.

All formats have a session beginning in May. See below for the start dates of all summer sessions. For more information, visit the UM Summer website.

12-week Summer Sessions
Date Description
Monday, May 10 Classes Begin for 12-week Session
Monday, May 31 Memorial Day - No Classes, Offices Closed
Monday, July 5 Independence Day Observed - No Classes, Offices Closed
Friday, July 30 Last Day of Classes for 12-week Session
