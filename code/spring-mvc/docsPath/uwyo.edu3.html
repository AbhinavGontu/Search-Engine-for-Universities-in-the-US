Visit the University of Wyoming

Tour Campus — From Anywhere.


From our in-person visits, Cowboy Webinars and a virtual campus tour — we're ready to show you all that UW has to offer!

The World Needs More Cowboys. The world needs you.

 

 

Take an On-Campus Tour Today

Future students can sign up for a daily tour on our beautiful campus in Laramie, Wyoming. We have designed these tours at limited capacity for the safety of our campus community. Each day is available on a first-come, first-served basis and are subject to change.

 

Schedule a Tour

 

Join a Daily Ambassador Session

Attend a weekday Zoom admissions sessions at 4 p.m. Monday - Friday to view campus and interact with UW Student Ambassadors.

  • Learn more about UW and the application process
  • Get a glimpse of campus from wherever you are
  • Hear about what life is like as a Cowboy
  • Ask any question you have about UW

Register Here

 

Take a Virtual Tour of Campus

We understand that a trip to Laramie may not be in the cards right now. That's why we've created a self-guided tour of the UW campus that will give you an up-close look at what we have to offer along with real student videos. For the best experience, we recommend watching the tour with sound.

 

Start My Tour

 

Important reminders about in-person visits

  • Tours are available on a limited basis to promote physical distancing.

  • Tours are offered Monday - Friday.

  • Visits currently only include a campus tour and an admissions session.

  • Students will be connected with their academic area of interest through their confirmation email.

  • All participants are required to wear a face covering while on campus per university policy.

  • To help protect our students living on campus, a tour of the residence halls are not currently permitted. Check out our White Hall tour video!

  • At this time, tours will not be able to go through Half Acre Recreation Center. However, we encourage you to take a video tour the Half Acre facility here.

  • Due to safety procedures, lunch at the Washakie Dining Center will not be offered. Retail options are available on campus along with several restaurants around Laramie.

  • If you're in the area but didn't have a chance to schedule a tour, we encourage you to stop by for our drop-in tour at 2 p.m.

  • Tours are subject to temporary suspension at the university's discretion according to its COVID-19 policy.
