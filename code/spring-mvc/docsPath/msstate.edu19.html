Mississippi State University
Welcome Center

The mission of the Welcome Center is to ensure all visitors leave Mississippi State University with a positive lasting impression of the entire University. The Welcome Center staff will meet the needs of all visitors for campus information, directions to locations on and surrounding MSU's campus, and campus tours.

Plan Your Visit

Maroon VIP

Maroon VIPs ensure that all guests of Mississippi State University enjoy a warm and hospitable welcome to Starkville, Mississippi's beautiful campus. Composed solely of student volunteers, Maroon VIPs conduct tours to visitors of campus, and assist the Welcome Center, the Office of the President, and other university departments with information, tours and event assistance.

More About Maroon VIP

Tours Offered

Trying to plan a fun and interesting trip for yourself, your civic or church group, garden club, elementary or middle school class? Individuals and groups small and large can visit the beautiful Mississippi State University campus for a guided campus tour, historical tour, children’s tour or one of many other tours, all conducted by our Maroon VIP student guides.
