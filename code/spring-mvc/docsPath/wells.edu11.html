Jack and Nancy Daniels Virtual 5k


Click here to see the 5k results from 2020 >>

Run a 5k (cumulatively or all at once) during the week of May 3-8. This can be on roads, track, trail, treadmill, a 1983 StairMaster 6000™, laps around your living room coffee table—it doesn’t matter! You can run the miles all at once or split them up between multiple days. Walk, hike, skip, or bear crawl if you don’t want to run. What’s important is that we are honoring Jack and Nancy while giving back to the college where they ended their coaching careers.
