Alumni Membership

If you are a graduate of National University (associate’s, bachelor’s, master’s, and credential completers) you are a part of the Alumni Association. There are no dues or memberships to apply for. Alumni will receive ongoing communication from the Alumni Relations team regarding events, newsletters, and other university happenings. If you are not receiving communications from us, please make sure your information is up-to-date. You can opt-out at any time.

Alumni Verification

To get a verification letter stating that you are a member of the Alumni Association, submit a request via the link below and your letter will be emailed to you instantly. *You must be registered or signed in to access this form.
