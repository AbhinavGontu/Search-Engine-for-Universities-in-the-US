This is the 40th edition of UAB's Facts & Figures and the 19th edition available electronically. This factbook is compiled by the Office of Institutional Effectiveness and Analysis but reflects contributions from offices all over campus. Our objective is to produce a useful and reliable resource for a variety of information needs.
