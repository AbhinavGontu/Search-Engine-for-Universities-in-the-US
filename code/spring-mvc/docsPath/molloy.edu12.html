Stay Connected

Stay connected to Molloy upon graduation.  Attend events.  Engage in social media. Hire alumni.  Mentor a student.  Connect with Molloy for life.

 

By logging into your Molloy Alumni Online Profile on the connect.molloy.edu website you make it easier to complete any form on our site and you ensure that we have your most up to date contact information. 

To log into your connect.molloy.edu profile:

1.    Click "log-in", enter your last name, and hit submit.
2.    Select your name from the list shown.
3.    Enter your unique Molloy Online Constituent Code.  Don't have your Constituent Code?  Request one here.

If you have any trouble logging into your profile, or have any questions, please e-mail Mary Jane Reilly

Already accessed your account?  Log in using your username and password.
