Driver License/ID Card and REAL ID Checklist


Welcome to Driver License/ID Card and REAL ID Checklist

The Driver License/ID Card and REAL ID Checklist is an application that assists in preparing you for your Driver License Office visit. All original, renewal or replacement driver license and ID cards issued, will now have a gold star to indicate they are Real ID compliant. You may visit the Federal Real ID Act page for further information. Just follow the steps, and at the end, you will have a customized checklist of which documents to bring for your Driver License Office visit.

If you are under 18 years of age, we recommend assistance from a parent or legal guardian when using this application to ensure you have the correct information. Some of the documents will be required from the parent or legal guardian and not the minor.

Note: Using the Driver License/ID Card and REAL ID Checklist does not guarantee you will be able to obtain a Driver License or Identification Card; it simply helps you learn which documentation to bring in to prove your identity and residency.

Temporary Visitor

A temporary visitor is anyone who is not a U.S. citizen, U.S. national, lawful permanent resident, refugee or asylee.

A temporary visitor who wants to apply for a limited term Driver License or ID card must provide one of the following documents for verification through DHS. (Documents may be expired unless otherwise noted.)

  • A document (or combination of documents) issued by the U.S. Department of Justice, U.S. Department of State, U.S. Department of Homeland Security or U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services that shows lawful temporary admission to the U.S.

Limited term licenses and ID cards issued to temporary visitors expire when the personâs period of lawful presence expires.

No Proof of Social Security

Individuals who do not have a Social Security Card, have never been issued a Social Security Number, or are not eligible for a Social Security Number can visit any Driver License office and complete an affidavit attesting to such. Providing a false statement in connection with this affidavit will result in the cancellation of the Driver License or Identification Card and may result in criminal charges. An affidavit is not acceptable for the issuance of a CDL.

Need a Social Security Number replacement card? The Social Security Administration introduced the expansion of on-line services for residents of Texas available through its My Social Security portal at https://www.ssa.gov/myaccount/
