Graduate Programs

MBA Program

 

EXECUTIVE MBA

Take your leadership ability up a notch. Join fellow mid-to-top level managers and earn a Georgia MBA right in the heart of Buckhead in just 18 months.

FULL-TIME MBA

Transform your career with the #13 MBA among U.S. public institutions. This highly selective program offers you a market-driven curriculum to help you sharpen your leadership skillset.

PROFESSIONAL MBA

Earn a top-ranked MBA while maintaining focus on your career. Evening and weekend classes paired with online learning provide an MBA option to fit your busy schedule.

STEM MBA

The Georgia STEM MBA is a one-year Master of Business Administration (MBA) degree option open to any current undergraduate majoring in an eligible Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math (STEM) discipline and attending an accredited public or private institution in the United States.

Specialized Masters

MASTER OF ACCOUNTANCY

Start your career with confidence. Housed in the J.M. Tull School of Accounting, MAcc students continue to post some of the nation’s best CPA exam pass rates while developing the skillset and knowledge needed for fulfilling careers.

ONLINE MASTER OF BUSINESS AND TECHNOLOGY

Upgrade your IT and leadership skill-set with the #17 online graduate business degree. Designed to accommodate your busy schedule, you can complete all coursework entirely online.

MASTER OF MARKETING RESEARCH

Join the first program of its kind in the U.S. Across eleven months, MMR students go hands-on with the latest industry technology, tools, and strategies, earning a 100% career placement rate as a result.

MASTER OF SCIENCE IN BUSINESS ANALYTICS

Develop your expertise in collecting, analyzing, and interpreting big data in a business context. You’ll learn the latest industry tools and technologies while working on real-world projects with corporate partners.

Dual Degrees – MBA

LLM/MBA

Across two and a half years, graduates of foreign law schools can better their knowledge of U.S. law while earning their MBA with the Terry College of Business.

MBA/MPH

Available for MPH students pursing the Health Policy and Management concentration, this degree track teaches the skillset needed for those wishing to serve as the intersection of public health and business.

MD-MBA

MD students currently enrolled at any accredited U.S. medical school may choose to earn a Georgia MBA with one additional year of academic coursework during their medical studies.

PharmD/MBA

Doctor of Pharmacy students can get an edge on the competition with the PharmD/MBA dual degree track. Eligible students can complete both programs in five years. 

ENGINEERING/MBA

Undergraduate engineering students can earn both their Bachelor of Science in Engineering degree and an MBA in a total of five years through this Double Dawgs program, which is also the first and only BS/MBA degree offered by the University System of Georgia.

Dual Degrees – MAcc

 

5-YEAR BBA/MAcc

Current UGA undergraduate accounting majors can satisfy requirements for both their BBA and MAcc degrees with this path. Advisement necessary.

DOUBLE DAWG BBA/MAcc

Terry admits who are non-accounting majors can complete this program to pursue either public accounting, consulting or advisory positions.

JD/MAcc

Over three years, students who majored in accounting and who want to pursue a career in tax law and become licensed as both a CPA and an attorney can graduate with both a Juris Doctor and a Masters of Accountancy – Taxation.

MAcc/MS FINANCIAL PLANNING

Accounting undergraduates interested in both personal financial planning and taxation can complete this 3-semester program, earning two masters degrees and qualifying to sit for the CPA, CFP and PFS exams.

PHD PROGRAMS

 

PHD

PhD programs at Terry provide students with a rigorous education in research and with the skills necessary to become successful teachers.
