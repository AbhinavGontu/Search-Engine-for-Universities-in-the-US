Virtual Alumni Reunion Weekend 2021

As the fall semester comes to a close, our top priority is to continue protecting the health of our students, faculty, staff and alumni. Last spring, when the pandemic forced Oxy to postpone our planned Alumni Reunion Weekend, we were hoping to host an in-person reunion in June 2021 to celebrate all classes ending in 0s, 1s, 5s and 6s. Regrettably, we have had to make the difficult decision that we will not be able to host an in-person reunion this coming June.

Introducing Your 2020 Alumni Seal Award Honorees

Since 1965, the Alumni Association has paid tribute to inspiring alumni who represent the values and spirit at the core of the Oxy experience. Seal Awards are given annually in the categories of service to the community, professional achievement, service to the College, young alumni achievement, emeriti faculty and alumna/us of the year. The Alumni Seal Awards have traditionally been presented to honorees during Alumni Reunion Weekend. With the postponement of this year's Reunion, our 2020 honorees will be celebrated at an appropriate time in the coming year. In the meantime, stay tuned for details about our new Alumni Seal Conversations webinar series, which will feature these exemplary alumni.
