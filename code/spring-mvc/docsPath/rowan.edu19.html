The Bridge

Home/The Bridge

The Bridge delivers a span of opportunities for startup and growth stage companies looking to learn more about the business side of their enterprise, cultivate strategic business alliances and improve their ability to access capital.

As part of the South Jersey Technology Park (SJTP) at Rowan University, The Bridge provides:

  • Educational workshops for entrepreneurs
  • Pitch funding preparation
  • Coaches sessions

Our Partner in The Bridge

SJTP tenants and Rowan faculty – connect with The Bridge by scheduling 1-on-1 time with strategic consultant Robert Land during our bi-weekly virtual office hours!

Robert is a Venture Architect who thrives on leading teams and serving as a business strategist that can guide customers and strategic partners to mutually beneficial outcomes. The essence of his business and real-world experience is a result of having hands-on knowledge at every stage of information technology innovation. With his latest venture, DeepRealm, Robert helps faculty innovators, solo entrepreneurs, and small-to-middle sized enterprises be more effective and collaborative in navigating the future of business.
