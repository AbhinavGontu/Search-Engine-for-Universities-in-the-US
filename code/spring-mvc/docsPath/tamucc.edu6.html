Piano Celebration Week

 


 
Piano Celebration Week is an annual piano festival at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi. The main goal of this festival is to present high-quality piano performances, foster collaboration between piano faculty and students, and facilitate performance and educational opportunities for individuals both within and outside the university. We are very proud of what we accomplished so far, and look forward to contining to contribute to the cultural growth of our community. Join us in celebrating the mastery and artistry of piano performance!
Dr. Sangmi Lim & Dr. Dino Mulic, festival directors
