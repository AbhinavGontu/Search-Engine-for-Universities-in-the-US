Welcome

I would personally like to welcome you to the Virginia State University (VSU) website for reaffirmation of accreditation by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC).  

Virginia State University is accredited by the SACSCOC to award baccalaureate, masters and doctorate degrees.  Contact the Commission on Colleges at 1866 Southern Lane, Decatur, Georgia 30033-4097 or call 404-679-4500 for questions about the accreditation of Virginia State University.  SACSCOC is the recognized regional accrediting body for the southern region of the United States and for Latin America.  The Commission on Colleges is the representative body of the College Delegate Assembly and is charged with executing the accreditation process.

Respectfully,

Donald E. Palm, Provost
