Now More Affordable Than Ever!

Beginning in Summer 2019, our world-class graduate business programs have a reduced tuition structure of $789 per credit hour.  Contact our Graduate Business Programs Director, Dr. Omid Sabbaghi at sabbagom@udmercy.edu to apply today!

Our Accounting grads place #1 in the nation in passing the CPA exam!  See the ranking here!

Nationally Ranked and Internationally Accredited!

Our renowned business programs have remained fully accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business for more than 70 years. AACSB accreditation places us among the top 5% of business schools in the world.

In addition, Detroit Mercy's College of Business Administration has achieved numerous state-wide, national, and international rankings.
