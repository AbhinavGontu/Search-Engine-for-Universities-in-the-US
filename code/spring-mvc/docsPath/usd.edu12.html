KAOR-FM Student Radio Staff Meeting

When: Tuesday, March 16, 2021 5:00 PM
Where: Al Neuharth Media Center
Description: This is the weekly meeting of the staff for KAOR-FM, 91.1, USD's student radio station. All students are welcome, they do not have to be Media & Journalism majors to participate. They must be enrolled at USD and pursuing a degree to be part of the staff, however.
If you require a special accommodation to fully participate, please contact the following at least 48 hours before an event:

For Students, contact Disability Services at 605-658-3745. For Faculty and Staff, contact HR at 605-658-3660.
Contact: Department of Media and Journalism 605-677-5477 http://www.usd.edu/mj
