Community Impact serves individuals in need in the communities of Upper Manhattan while providing meaningful volunteering and leadership opportunities for students at Columbia University and Barnard College.

 

 

COVID-19 ANNOUNCEMENT

Earl Hall at Columbia University is closed and Community Impact has moved to remote operations. To contact our office, please leave a voicemail on our main line at (212) 854-1492 or contact individual program managers by email.
