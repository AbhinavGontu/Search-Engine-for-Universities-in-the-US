Need To Send Documents?

We strongly recommend sending application documents electronically through your high school's college counselor. In cases where you must mail something, address it to:

Tulane University
Undergraduate Admission
210 Gibson Hall
6823 St. Charles Ave
New Orleans, LA 70118

Due to the effects of COVID-19, processing of mailed documents may take longer than the normal two weeks. We strongly encourage you to submit any documents electronically for the most efficient turnaround.
