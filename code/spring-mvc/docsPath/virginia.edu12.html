Credentialing Services

The National Student Clearinghouse (NSC) is the official provider of enrollment and degree certifications, and loan deferments for the University of Virginia.

We strongly encourage all seeking certifications to take advantage of the speed and convenience of the National Student Clearinghouse online services. Requests received in our office will take five to seven business days to process!

Current Students

Enrollment Certifications from NSC are considered official documentation of enrollment. They may be used for insurance companies, employers, scholarships, or any other agencies/services that require proof of enrollment at the University of Virginia. Receive your current and/or past enrollment or anticipated date of graduation information from NSC at any time. There is no charge for this service!
  1. Log in to the National Student Clearinghouse (Required NetBadge)
  2. Select "current enrollment" or "all enrollment"
  3. Select "Obtain an enrollment certificate"

If you need credit hours or GPA on your certification, request the certification in the form of a transcript.

Enrollment Reporting

  • Initial enrollment data will be submitted after the add/drop period.
  • Enrollment information will be re-submitted each month during both fall and spring semesters.

In School Loan Deferments

Students who qualify for in-school deferment are automatically reported to participating lenders upon the completion of final registration. All deferral requests are completed by National Student Clearinghouse within 48 hours.

Participating Lenders

  • To check the status of your loan deferment, please visit the National Student Clearinghouse website.

Current Students/Club Sport Certification Process

Please follow these simple steps to help us make the process of certifying your members a smooth one.

  1. Obtain the necessary certification form from the national governing body that oversees the sport. Please keep the following requirements in mind:
    • The form must be typed.
    • Please do not substitute first/last names with nicknames. Complete names only.
    • The 9-digit University ID must be filled in.
    • Leave all academic information blank. This section will be completed by UREG.
    • If the form requests "The Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act" (FERPA) protected information, all listed students must sign the form to give UREG permission to share their information.

    If any of the above requirements are missing or incorrect, UREG will not be able to complete the certification process for your club.

  2. Once the certification process has been completed, UREG will contact you to pick up your form. This process usually takes 3-5 business days once received.

If you have any further questions, please contact UREG at (434) 924-4122 or ureg@virginia.edu.

Alumni

Alumni can request degree and enrollment verifications in the form of a transcript.

Employers and Educational Institutions

The National Student Clearinghouse is the official provider of degree and enrollment verifications. The National Student Clearinghouse may be accessed at www.degreeverify.org or by calling (703) 742-4200. If the degree or enrollment verification requires credit hours or GPA, you will need to request the certification in the form of a transcript.

For verification of training in Graduate Medical Education (Residents and Fellows), please contact the GME Office at:

Diploma Copies

If you need a copy of your diploma for certification purposes, take your original diploma to a copy center and have an 8.5" x 11" photocopy made. Mail the copy to UREG. We will certify and notarize it as being a true copy of the original. (If you do not have the original, diploma copies cost $60 and require 8-10 weeks to prepare.)

We recommend that you first verify that some other document won't serve the purpose. Usually, companies or government agencies are looking for official proof of graduation, and only in rare cases involving foreign governments is a copy of the actual diploma needed. A certification or transcript can be obtained at NO cost and in less time.
