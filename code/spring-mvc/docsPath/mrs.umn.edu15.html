Student Activities

Morris has your interests covered

Located in the lower level of the Morris Student Center, the Office of Student Activities is a one-stop resource for student organizations, programming, and reservations. We offer a wide variety of services to students and student organizations, supervise many campus-wide programs, and manage the operations of the Student Center.

MISSION STATEMENT

Student Activities, Conferences and Events coordinates facilities, programs and services that facilitate student co-curricular engagement and learning, foster a cohesive and supportive campus community, and connect the campus and area community through events, outreach and the shared use of campus facilities.

What can you do in Morris, MN?

Well, let us show you!

With over 120 student organizations and all of Morris’s offices and disciplines hosting more than 500 events per year, there is always something to do here. Join us for anything from an Art Sale to the annual Zombie Prom.

Student Activities and events at Morris provide an incredible opportunity for students to get engaged with their peers and life on campus beyond the classroom. This involvement provides near limitless opportunities for students to put the principals they are learning in the classroom into practice in our real-world laboratory of citizenship. Students learn and practice valuable leadership skills while developing and implementing programming for the campus. Whether bringing major bands such as Flogging Molly or Cloud Cult to campus or writing and producing unique theatrical pieces in less than 24 hours in the Meiningens’ “Play-in-a-day” program, Morris Students demonstrate amazing creativity and ingenuity
