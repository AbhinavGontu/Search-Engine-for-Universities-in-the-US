Support Your University. Become Arizona Loyal. 

Arizona Loyal is a giving program that celebrates the university’s most devoted champions.
Anyone who makes a gift of any size to the university for two or more years in a row joins Arizona Loyal.

Now, you can support the Alumni Association directly or make a gift to support any unit or program of your choice across the university.
This means you can impact the areas you’re most passionate about while still staying engaged with our alumni community.

Renew your giving every year and stay Arizona Loyal!

A Note to Sustaining Members

As a sustaining member, you will now become part of the university’s new Arizona Loyal program. When it is time to renew your sustaining membership, your gift makes you Arizona Loyal and you will join the most committed Wildcats in supporting the university.

To celebrate your commitment, you’ll receive special communications that highlight the impact of your Arizona Loyal gifts and the Arizona Alumni Magazine three times annually. And, you can continue to access benefits for all alumni.

Reasons to Support

Through consecutive years of giving, you become Arizona Loyal and support the people and programs that make our university extraordinary. Make an impact in the lives of students and wherever your area of passion lies.
