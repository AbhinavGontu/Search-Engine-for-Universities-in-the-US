Visit Campus

Do you believe in love at first sight? Evidently, many of our students do. Because it seems like once they've visited Nova Southeastern University's college campus, they're hooked. Situated just 10 minutes from Fort Lauderdale/Hollywood International Airport, and close to I-95, I-75, and Florida's Turnpike, it's not hard to get to NSU's 314-acre Fort Lauderdale/Davie Campus. But once you're here, it's awfully hard to leave. We offer a variety of opportunities throughout the year for you to visit the NSU college campus. For those up north, we recommend stopping by in the winter. You'll thank us! Just tell us when you plan to visit our campus, and we'll do the rest! See below for contact information to schedule a campus visit.

Undergraduate Student Tours

Your campus visit will consist of meeting with an admissions counselor to answer any questions you may have and a campus tour given by one of NSU's Student Ambassadors. Prepare to be on campus for about two hours to see everything!
