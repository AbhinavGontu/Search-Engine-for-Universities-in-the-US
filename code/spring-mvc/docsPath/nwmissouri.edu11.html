Our products do more than increase productivity.
They also keep you safE.

In a COVID-19 impacted world, NEOGOV's solutions empower government and higher ed HR teams to streamline processes and go touchless at the same time to promote a healthy workplace. NEOGOV's three integrated modules - Recruit, Develop, Manage - meet the unique needs of public sector HR teams by automating and supporting the entire employee lifecycle, while ensuring the highest standards of compliance.

 

THE RECRUIT MODULE

Attract | Screen | Hire

Accelerate time to hire by centralizing the process of attracting applicants, screening them, and landing a qualified hire that’s productive on day one, while ensuring compliance.

THE DEVELOP MODULE

Coach | Grow

Build a more capable workforce by empowering managers with professional development tools and customized training programs that support employees and maintain organizational compliance.
