UW Superior Sports Camps

Superior, Wisconsin

The University of Wisconsin-Superior and Yellowjacket Athletics provide a variety of outstanding opportunities through camps for athletes to develop their athletic skills. In addition to the fundamental instruction, campers will be exposed to sport specific strategies and will have the chance to improve in their individual areas. UW-Superior coaches and players will provide instruction and feedback that will help you improve your athletic performance and develop the skills necessary for success. We look forward to seeing you at camp and are we excited to have you on our campus!
