Contact Information Update Process

Did you recently receive an email that looks like it came from SEMO but redirects you to an "800" number or a postcard asking you to do the same? You're not alone!

Alumni Relations has partnered with Publishing Concepts Inc., a family-owned, Dallas-based publisher that serves educational, fraternal and military organizations, for what is expected to be a yearlong project to complete an update of the alumni directory.

All personal information provided to PCI will remain confidential, and alumni may elect to have certain information left out of the directory. Individuals will be included regardless of whether they purchase a directory or not. PCI may also be calling the phone number on file for you to update your information as well.

Alumni Update

As Southeast Missouri State University responds to the COVID-19 pandemic, we know the rapid pace of changes our campus community has faced. We can only imagine the same holds true for our alumni.

We created an online alumni hub with resources for you in this changing time, links to help students with an emergency fund, and the opportunity to share how you're doing with our alumni community.

While we can't hold our alumni gatherings at this time, we hope we can stay connected. Our Alumni Association social media accounts will also bring you the latest, and we encourage you to email us if you need anything. Stay safe and well!

As a Southeast alumnus, you are part of a worldwide community of approximately 70,000 members in all 50 states and nearly 80 foreign countries. The Southeast Alumni Association truly has a global presence. Regardless of where you live, we hope you will stay in touch with Southeast through our Alumni Association website as well as our Facebook, LinkedIn, and other social media networks.
