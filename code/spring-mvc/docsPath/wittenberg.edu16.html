Zumba: 

The Zumba program is a Latin-inspired, dance fitness class that incorporates Latin and international music and dance movements. A Zumba class combines both fast and slow rhythms that tone and sculpt the body using an aerobic/fitness approach to achieve a unique balance of cardio and muscle-toning benefits.  Both dancers and non-dancers are more than welcome to come, and no previous dancing experience is necessary!  This program is a great stress reliever, and a fantastic workout.  Why not burn tons of calories while having tons of fun?!
