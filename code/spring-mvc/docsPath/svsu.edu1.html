SVSU Foundation Operations Update

Dear Friends,

SVSU has been operating safely this academic year, and the SVSU Foundation has returned to our usual routine. 

The office is staffed from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. While we are not yet encouraging people to visit campus, you can still call the office at (989) 964-4052 or  (989) 964-4071, email us at foundation@svsu.edu or at one of personal emails, and make gifts online.

On behalf of the entire campus community, thank you for your continued support, especially during this time of uncertainty. We wish you well.

Andrew J. Bethune '87
Executive Director

Mission

The mission of the SVSU Foundation is to generate and cultivate a community of enthusiastic and loyal donors who contribute to the development of a premier university of outstanding students.
