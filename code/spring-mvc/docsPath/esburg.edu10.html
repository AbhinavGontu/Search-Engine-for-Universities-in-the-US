Homecoming @ Home 2020 Recordings

 

In case you were unable to tune into our Homecoming @ Home virtual events, we wanted to provide you with recordings of some of our programming. To view the recordings, follow the links included below. 

 

Thursday, September 24

Kickoff Event and Virtual Campus Tour: https://alumlc.org/waynesburg/3071

 

Friday, September 25

Alumni Faculty Panel: https://alumlc.org/waynesburg/3078

 

Saturday, September 26

WU Athletics Huddle with Coach Ron Headlee: https://alumlc.org/waynesburg/3079

 

We hope you enjoy these virtual events, and we hope to be able to celebrate Homecoming 2021 safely in person! Those tentative dates are set for Oct. 1-2, 2021.
