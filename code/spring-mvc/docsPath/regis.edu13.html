Getting Started

The Circulation and Reserves Department is located on the 2nd (main) floor of the Dayton Memorial Library. Services provided by this department include setting up library card accounts, checking out and returning books, renewals, access to materials placed on reserve by faculty, and assistance in locating items and using library machinery (copiers, microform printers). Circulation staff will also provide limited reference assistance in the absence of a reference librarian. Work-study positions in the circulation department, as well as other parts of the library, are always available. Inquire at the front desk for current work-study opportunities.

 

The circulation desk provides the following services:

 

  • Checking out/in books and materials
  • Print cards
  • Electronic and In-house reserves
  • Renewals
  • Provide change for copy machines
  • Assists in finding books
  • General information
