Benefits & Perks

Retirement Plans

Mississippi State offers both a defined benefit and a defined contribution plan, (optional retirement plan*).

We also offer a supplemental retirement plan through the Mississippi Deferred Compensation Plan and Trust 457 for all benefits eligible employees.

Learn more about our retirement, including employee contributions and employer matching.

* Only certain positions qualify for the optional retirement plan.

Visit Retirement

Insurance

You are covered from Day 1. Whether you are searching for health, life, dental, vision, disability, cancer, critical care, long term care, or pre-tax flexible spending account plans, we’ve got you covered.

Learn more about the various insurance plans and rates.

Visit Insurance

Educational Assistance

Mississippi State University offers tuition remission benefits to all benefits eligible employees and eligible retirees and their dependents.

Benefits eligible employees can take up to six hours per semester, upon approval from their supervisor.

Learn more detailed information regarding this benefit.

Visit Educational Assistance

Time Off

Vacation/Personal Leave

18 days/per year (144 hours) from the first month until 3 years of service.

Sick Leave

Nine Month faculty earn major medical leave in lieu of personal leave. (15 days/year.)

12-month Faculty and Staff earn 12 days/year from the first month until 3 years of service.

Holidays

University employees are afforded 15 paid holidays. Check out all our holidays.

Visit Time Off

Other Perks

Discounted Athletic Tickets

For many MSU employees, Bulldog sports are an important part of campus life. MSU employees can purchase discounted season tickets – 20 percent off baseball, men's and women's basketball, and football tickets.

Visit Ticket Office

MSU Golf Course

Regularly ranked as one of the nation's best golf courses, the 6,927 yard MSU Golf Course is an 18 hole, par 72, PGA sanctioned, championship course. MSU employees receive discounts on green fees and semester golf passes.

Visit Golf

Nissan Partnership

Mississippi State University is a business associate for Nissan's Vehicle Purchase Program (VPP). This program allows direct, full-time employees to purchase Nissan and Infiniti vehicles at pre-negotiated discounted prices.

Visit Nissan Perks

Work/Life Balance

The Mississippi State University Work Life Balance Committee aims to act as liaisons and advocates for faculty, staff and administration looking to balance work and life issues by offering support, information, resources and affecting new policies. Several particular topics fall under the scope of this committee, which include but are not limited to dependents, mental and physical health, and workplace policies and procedures.

Visit Work/Life Balance
