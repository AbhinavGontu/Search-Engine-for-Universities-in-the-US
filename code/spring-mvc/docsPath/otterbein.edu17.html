Printed Catalogs

The Otterbein Course Catalog is no longer offered in print format. This is done in the interest of having a more accurate catalog, and in keeping with Otterbein’s desire to reduce its impact on the environment.

Non-discrimination Policy

Otterbein University does not discriminate on the basis of race, religion, age, sex, color, disability, sexual orientation, national or ethnic origin, political affiliation, marital or veteran status in admission of students, educational policies, scholarships and loans, housing, athletics, employment, and other activities.

Disclaimer Clause

The provisions of this catalog are not to be regarded as an irrevocable contract between the student and the University. The University reserves the right to change any provision, requirement or policy without prior notice at any time within the student’s term of residence. The University reserves the right to cancel courses for lack of sufficient enrollment or to substitute instructors for courses. This document is intended to be used only as an informational guide.
