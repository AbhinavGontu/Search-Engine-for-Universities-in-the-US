OFFICIAL CAMP WEBSITE OF | SMC Softball Camps

 

SMC Softball Camps & Clinics are dedicated to all aspects of the game including skill development, game strategy, competition and mechanics. Each athlete will have the opportunity to develop their skills through individual instruction, group instruction and game experience.

Saint Mary's College is very fortunate to have some of the finest collegiate coaches in Northern California. Head Coach Sonja Garnett hand selects and trains her staff, composed of top-level coaches and collegiate players. All instructors have been chosen for their extensive knowledge of the game of softball and their enthusiasm to work with children and young athletes.
