Welcome to the UE Alumni and Friends Online Community

Welcome to UE Alumni Online, our newly updated online community for UE and Harlaxton alumni and friends. Our online alumni community is designed to help you stay connected. This is the place to read about alumni news and sign up to attend events. You can update your personal contact information, and sign up for a number of volunteer opportunities. We are always seeking volunteers, and have many ways that you can get involved.

Questions and suggestions about our UE Alumni Online community, or other alumni needs may be directed to alumni@evansville.edu. You may also reach us by phone at 812-488-2586.

Update Your Information

Sign Up

We are proud of all of our alumni. We love hearing your stories and sharing your accomplishments with others. Each Wednesday we highlight an alumnus on our social media pages and want to feature alumni stories on our news page. Click this link to sign up now and you could be chosen as our next Spotlight!

Nominate

Each year, the Alumni Association recognizes the outstanding service and achievements of its members by presenting awards in four categories – Distinguished Alumni, Young Alumni, Samuel Orr Honorary Alumni and Edie Bates Volunteer Service. Click here to learn more about each award and nominate a deserving candidate.

Volunteer

Alumni are our most valued volunteers. If you want to help, we have a role for you. You can sign up to talk to current students about your career path, mentor a student one-on-one, sit on a panel of presenters, act as a guest lecturer, join a board, assist with recruitment, and more. Click here to learn about all the volunteer opportunities available to you.

Refer

Did you know that when an alumnus refers a student to UE, the chance of converting them to an Ace is higher than any other identification method? Your knowledge of UE, its atmosphere, and the true value of a UE education are key pieces to successful recruiting. If you know a high school student who would make a great Ace, refer them today!

Alumni News

Nasa Names Crater After Alumnus Craig Tooley '83
According to Nasa's webpage, "This act pays homage to the numerous accomplishments and indelible contributions Tooley made to NASA’s exploration community during his 34 years of service. After Tooley’s passing in September, 2017, members of NASA’s Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO team) wanted to memorialize Tooley by having a lunar crater named after him."
Six Alumni to Be Inducted into EVSC Hall of Fame
Every two years, the EVSC names its EVSC Hall of Fame. The Hall of Fame, sponsored by the EVSC Foundation, honors distinguished individuals who exemplify outstanding achievement in their life’s work and who have made a positive impact on public education in the greater community. It also emphasizes the importance of service to community and inspires EVSC students to seek excellence in their own education and careers.
