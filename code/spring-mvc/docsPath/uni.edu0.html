    WHY UNIVERSITY OF NORTHERN IOWA?

    You’ll go further than you ever imagined.

    You have dreams, goals and expectations. So do we. Our purpose is to help you surpass what you thought was possible, to outstretch your expectations. The University of Northern Iowa is a bright and welcoming community of faculty, students and staff who are dedicated to your success.

    Best In The
    Midwest

    The University of Northern Iowa is ranked second in the “Top Public Schools Regional University (Midwest)” category, according to U.S. News & World Report.

    Affordable
    Education

    Knowledge is about making a better future. Your education should not revolve around piles of debt. At UNI, we’re committed to making college attainable for everyone.

    AACSB
    Accredited

    Earn your business degree from an AACSB accredited university, an achievement earned by less than five percent of the world’s business schools.

    Diversity, Equity and Inclusion

    We Are One

    You are welcome at the University of Northern Iowa. Your background, beliefs, hopes, dreams, fears and struggles make us stronger. Diversity delivers variety, allows us to challenge assumptions and grow as a community. And the community is here to support you, challenge you and accept you while giving you the boost to do more.
