Day in Denton

By YoloTX
September 6, 2019
4364
0
Share:

Take a trip with us down to the beautiful town of Denton – home of Texas Woman’s University. Their campus houses gorgeous architectural masterpieces created entirely by influential female figures. The nation’s largest university primarily for women is surrounded by an amazing nightlife, entertainment and festivals such as the Denton Arts and Jazz Festival. This festival is a local gathering for music, food and art lovers. Check out Denton for an amazing time!
