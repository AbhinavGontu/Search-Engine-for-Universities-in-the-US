Welcome Sonoma State University Alumni! 

Representing more than 72,000 graduates, the SSU Alumni Association awards student scholarships, hosts annual programs like the Lagunitas Mixer and scholarship fundraiser, supports campus and community events, and keeps graduates up to date on University news and events. 

 

The mission of the SSU Alumni Association is to foster, renew and enhance the relationship between alumni and the University. The Association is committed to providing service to Sonoma State University alumni as well as providing opportunities for alumni to volunteer, participate and support the advancement of SSU.

 

Get social with us! Keep up with current events and find out what your fellow alumni are up to.

       


 

Thank you to our 2020 corporate and community sponsors for their continued support!
