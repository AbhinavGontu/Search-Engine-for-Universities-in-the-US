One Aggie Network. Many Connections.

Cal Aggie Alumni Association connects UC Davis' 250,000+ alumni, parents and families, students and business partners to one of the best public universities in the country!

Become a member today:

Alumni and Friends- Alumni, honored alumni (ages 65 or older), spouses, non-degree alumni and the Davis community

International Alumni- Alumni living abroad for 2 years, or lived abroad 2 years prior and returning after graduation

Recent Graduates- Alumni who graduated within the previous five years 

Staff and Faculty- Current staff and faculty of UC Davis

Membership Benefits

  • Networking and social events throughout the world
  • Free search tools to find alumni-owned businesses and partners
  • Our monthly e-newsletter, Aggie Connections
  • Career networking opportunities
  • In-person access and borrowing privileges at all 10 University of California libraries
  • Discounts to restaurants, shops, home insurance and auto insurance in Davis and nationwide
  • Exclusive invitations to Alumni Wine & Beer Program events
  • VIP treatment at UC Davis events including Homecoming and Picnic Day
  • Discounts on UC Davis Continuing and Professional Education Courses
  • Exclusive yourname@alumni.ucdavis.edu email address
  • Travel opportunities throughout the world
  • Alumni locator service
  • And much, much more.
