Welcome to myEdison®!

The University portal is now the myEdison® student portal, providing students access to their courses in the new Moodle learning management system, Online Student Services, Google docs and other student resources.
 

Trouble Logging In?

Due to the new password policy implementation, your myEdison® password may have expired. To resolve this, log in to Online Student Services (OSS) with your current myEdison® password where you will be able to change/reset your password. Please note that you do not need the "students\" portion of the user ID when logging in to OSS. Once you reset your password in OSS, you should be able to log back in to the myEdison® Portal.

Learn how to resolve some common issues.
