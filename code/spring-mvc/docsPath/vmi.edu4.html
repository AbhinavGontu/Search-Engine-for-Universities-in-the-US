Compete to Win is your opportunity to get back in the game and support your team! During this weeklong challenge, $15,000 is up for grabs. There are three ways to help your team win it: 

  1. $5,000 for getting the most teammates involved 
  2. $5,000 for raising the most money for your sport 
  3. $5,000 for getting the highest percentage of your teammates to participate
