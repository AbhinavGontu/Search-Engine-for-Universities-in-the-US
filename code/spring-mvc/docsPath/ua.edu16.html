About Us

For more than 140 years, the University of Alabama School of Law has produced attorneys who become leaders locally, nationally and globally.

Alabama Law provides a top-rate education at a reasonable cost in a supportive and diverse environment. The school has nearly 400 JD students and a 6.4 to 1 student to faculty ratio, 7 clinical programs, and first-year JD students currently representing 24 states and 50 undergraduate institutions.
