It is a Legendary Institution

An opportunity to witness the Southern University Marching Band is an opportunity to witness music in motion. This band has raised the bar and set precedents for other collegiate marching bands and ensembles. Often imitated, but never duplicated, the band prides itself on the unique ability to execute precision drills better than any other marching ensemble in the country.
