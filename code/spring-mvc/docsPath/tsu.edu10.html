Facility Hours

The REC Center operates seven days a week throughout the year. The center is available to all students, alumni and community members with active memberships. For Hours of Operation, Click here!

REC Sports

Each student has the opportunity to stay active, meet new friends and have a ton of fun by participating in our REC Sports programs. Click here for more information on the recreational programs we offer.

Summer Youth Camp

Registration is now open for annual Tiger CAMPus youth camp! The camp will emphasize the core values of our department: Service, Integrity, Respect, and FUN!!! Children ages 5-14 are eligible to participate. For registration information, Click here!

Become a Member

Get activated so you can get ACTIVE! All currently enrolled students have access to the REC. To activate your membership, bring your ID to the REC, and we will get you ready to “REC”

Sign Up Now

What do I need to access the facility?

All students must bring their current valid Tiger 1 card to the facility in order to access it. All first time users must complete the membership application at the member services desk.

How do I reserve space in the facility?

All request for facility usage can be done by completing the facility request form. All requests are considering pending until final approval from administration.

What forms of payment are accepted at the REC?

We accept all forms of payment. If you want to make a credit/debit card purchase, please click here.

Is there a dress code to access the REC?

The following items are considered acceptable attire in program areas:

  • T-Shirts
  • Athletic Shorts
  • “Windbreakers” or Jogging pants
  • Tights (must be at least halfway down thigh)

Unacceptable attire

  • Sports bras
  • Blue jeans or any long non athletic type pants
  • Dress shoes
  • Tights that are too short or too revealing
