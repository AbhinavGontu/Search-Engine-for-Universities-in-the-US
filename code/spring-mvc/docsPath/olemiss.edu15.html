Mississippi's First School of Engineering

You'll benefit from our 120 years of inspiring students and driving innovation. And, you'll have access to an amazing network of alumni. Come find your fit in one of our ten degree programs.

 

Gain hands-on experience

You can spend a semester or two in a job relevant to your field of study through our co-op program - learning from the likes of NASA and Tesla, Nissan and DuPont. Or you can work with national and international specialists to tackle some of engineering's grand challenges.

 

Get prepared for a great career

Two-thirds of our graduates go into the private sector, taking positions in great companies across the US. One out of every five students pursues an advanced degree (engineering, law, medicine, business and more). And, others choosed to work in the government or military. We'll help find the right job for you.
