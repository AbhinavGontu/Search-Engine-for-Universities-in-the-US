    • 1947, March 16

      The festival of choruses from four women's colleges included performances by the Bryn Mawr Chorus, the Radcliffe Choral Society, the Smith College Glee Club and the Vassar College Choir.

    • 1971, March 18

      Raymond Sokolov, arts critic for Newsweek, lectured on Jean Renoir's The Golden Coach (1953), a period comic fantasy set to the music of Antonio Vivaldi starring Anna Magnani.
