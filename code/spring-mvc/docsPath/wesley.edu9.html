A COMMUNITY OF CARE

Your relationship with Wesley College is a lifelong experience. We take great pride in the successes of the more than 16,000 Wesley alumni all over the nation and across the globe. No matter how long ago or how recently you attended Wesley, your accomplishments enhance the reputation of the College, just as the growth and success of your alma mater continue to enhance the value of your degree. Stay in the know about Wesley College events, news and alumni exclusive opportunities by updating your email, home address and personal information.
