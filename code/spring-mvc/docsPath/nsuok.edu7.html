    Our Universities

    As the largest four-year university system in the state,

    the Regional University System of Oklahoma governs six of the state’s public universities.

    ​

    Click on a logo to visit each university's website. 

    40% of all Oklahoma college graduates are from RUSO . Learn more about our incredible alumni.

    Read About Our Graduates

    RUSO regents are appointed by the governor to provide oversight and control of administrative, academic and fiscal affairs, including

    hiring university presidents. 

    Learn About The Regents

    Six universities located around the state to meet the educational needs of Oklahomans through affordable, accredited, high-quality education.
