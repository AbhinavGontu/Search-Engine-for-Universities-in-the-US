Blue Jay Spirit Club

 

BLUE JAY NATION SPIRIT CLUB:  JOIN NOW!

Westminster College is pleased to announce the second year of its comprehensive, nationwide athletic club:  The Blue Jay Nation Spirit Club.  With over 50% of our students arriving to campus with plans to participate in varsity and club sports, athletics is a major part of our culture.

Ken Theroff ’88, Hall-of-Famer in baseball and basketball and CEO of Jefferson Bank in Jefferson City, MO, continues to serve as the first President of the Club and is getting involved because he believes in Westminster’s athletic programs.  “Westminster athletics has been a huge positive influence on generations of alumni, including me and my friends,” he says. “I am looking forward to supporting those who come to the college with the same goals as we all did.”

The Blue Jay Nation Spirit Club has two goals.

  1. To provide start-up funding for the Club and assist financially with the ongoing operations of the athletics department. 
  2. To foster support and camaraderie over the coming years among students and alumni across all sports and all decades and to give our alumni a chance to support the next generation of Blue Jay athletes.

Please renew your support or join in this effort this academic year (2019-2020) and become a lifetime Sustaining or Charter Member. 

Membership Levels

Sustaining Charter Member of the Blue Jay Nation:  $5,000+
 Benefits: Permanent membership as a Sustaining Charter Member recognized on website; Gold President’s Club membership and invitation to President’s Dinner; Blue Jay car decal; a Blue Jay spirit wear item; 30% coupon for Blue Jay spirit wear at bookstore; invitations to all events.

Charter Member of the Blue Jay Nation:  $1,250 - $4,999
 Benefits: Permanent membership as a Charter Member recognized on website; President’s Club membership and invitation to President’s Dinner; Blue Jay car decal; 30% coupon for Blue Jay spirit wear at bookstore; invitation to all events.

Member of the Blue Jay Nation:  $500 - $1,249
 Benefits: Recognition on website; Blue Jay car decal; 30% coupon for Blue Jay spirit wear at books bookstore; invitations to all events.

Friend of the Blue Jay Nation: $100 - $499
 Benefits: Recognition on website; Blue Jay car decal; 20% coupon for Blue Jay bookstore invitations to all events

Supporter of the Blue Jay Nation: all gifts up to $99
Benefits: Recognition on website; Blue Jay car decal; invitations to all events.
