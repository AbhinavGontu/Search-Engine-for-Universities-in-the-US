Campus Recreation

SPRING 2021 UPDATE:  The Spring Semester is here and we wanted to update you on our plans. The Mashouf Wellness Center is closed for in person programming but we are in operation as a Mass Vaccination site in conjunction with the San Francisco Department of Public Health. (For more information, visit COVID-19 Vaccination | SF State News (sfsu.edu) ).

For the time being, we will fully reopen when it is safe to do so. We are complying with San Francisco and State guidelines and will continue to update you on our plans. We will be offering VIRTUAL PROGRAMMING like Group X classes, workshops, and more all semester! There will be OPPORTUNITY FOR IN-PERSON Outdoor Day Trips  and Group X Fitness classes that are contingent on Covid-19 mandate approvals, so stay tuned for exciting updates on that. We’re sorry that we won’t be able to see you on campus, but we hope to see you virtually this Spring. (For information on fees, please visit https://fiscaff.sfsu.edu/campus-mandatory-fees.)

The safety and wellbeing of our members continue to be our highest priority. We encourage all of our members to maintain healthy habits, wash hands frequently, and appropriate social distancing. We appreciate your understand during this time.

Continue to check this website, social media @sfsu_campusrec and our |"FusionGo Mobile App SF State Mashouf Wellness Center" for updates. We will also be providing wellness content via social media during this time so that you can still practice wellness at home. 

Welcome to the Mashouf Wellness Center!

SF State Campus Recreation Department provides exciting and fun sport, fitness, and informal recreation opportunities for students, faculty and staff is our top priority. We encourage individuals to develop a lasting appreciation for recreational activity. Whether you are interested in playing an intramural sport, grabbing a quick workout, or training for a triathlon we have something for you!
 

We are excited to welcome our San Francisco State community to the Mashouf Wellness Center (MWC)!  The MWC, open as of August 2017, is already emerging as as a space for physical activity, healthy habits, holistic wellness, innovation and fellowship.  The intentional design of the MWC allows us to offer a little something for everyone.  We look forward to seeing you in the facility or participating in one of our programs very soon!
