Funding & Employment

Funding Your Graduate Education

Financial Support

Approximately 79% of graduate students enrolled at Illinois receive some form of financial support. This may include:

  • Assistantships (teaching, research, graduate, and pre-professional graduate, including those funded by Federal Work Study)
  • Fellowships (including traineeships)
  • Loans
