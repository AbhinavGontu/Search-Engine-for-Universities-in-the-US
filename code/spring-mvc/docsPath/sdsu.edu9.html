SDSU Alumni Lifetime Membership

Lifetime members play a critical role in shaping the future of San Diego State University. When you become a lifetime member, your membership supports the SDSU Alumni Lifetime Member Endowment and the SDSU Alumni Scholarship program. 

The Endowment provides the funding to offer alumni programs, activities and resources to further benefit our alumni community.

Lifetime members ensure that promising students have the resources they need to attain their dreams. Currently, three annual $7,500 scholarships are awarded:

  • SDSU Alumni Legacy Scholarship
  • SDSU Alumni Scholarship (for any SDSU student)
  • SDSU Alumni Scholarship for a first-year student

I am a lifetime member.

 

Tita Gray, '16

“I want to make sure I set the course for those who come after me. So that students have the ability to get scholarships. So that I show the athletes that I support them and that I am a role model. As an Aztec for Life, I feel like it’s my duty to give back.”


Mike Cully, '11

“I am extremely proud of my school because it has given me opportunities that I wouldn’t have had otherwise. I lived in San Diego and I have also lived in the Pacific Northwest so I see the alumni up there and their enthusiasm for the school. You don’t see this at every school you go to. And you especially don’t see all the generations that you see at SDSU today.”
