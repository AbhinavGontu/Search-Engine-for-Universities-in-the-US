      COVID-19 Information

      In response to the COVID-19 global pandemic, UAA has taken steps to protect the health and safety of our campus community. The spring 2021 semester will occur primarily via alternate course delivery, remote work and controlled access to campus facilities. A mandatory mask policy is in effect at all UAA campuses. Learn more on the College During COVID website and the UAA COVID-19 information site.
