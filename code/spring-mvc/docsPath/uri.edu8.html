Make Time for Play

 
 

We want you to feel happy, stay active, and find a healthy place where you belong. Choose from a wide range of programs in our state-of-the-art facilities, offering everything from competitive team sports to group fitness classes to individualized personal training, so you can be where you are and find your stride. Be Active. Be Well. Belong.

Campus Rec facilities are open for URI Students/Faculty/Staff.

You can make Day of Giving a success for the URI community. There are many ways to participate, whether you are a Rhody alum, student, parent, friend, or all-around fan. You can choose to support us and the students who participate in their health and well-being with the Annual Fund: Campus Recreation or the Annual Fund: Club Sports.
