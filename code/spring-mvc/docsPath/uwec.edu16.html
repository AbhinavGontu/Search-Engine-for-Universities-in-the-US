Housing + Residence Life

Welcome

Our 14 residence halls are consistently a popular choice for nearly 4,000 students at UW-Eau Claire. Our halls offer far more than just a place to sleep and relax between classes; there are many opportunities for Blugolds to get involved in their residence hall community by running for their hall government, getting involved in one of the many student organizations and attending any of the social, educational, and multicultural programs.

Secure your spot in the residence halls

Students are guaranteed on-campus living if the housing application and $75 housing deposit are submitted by May 1. Follow the steps below to apply.

STEP 1: Activate your account

To activate your account, you will need your username and campus ID, which are found in your admit letter or application acknowledgment email.

Activate your account

STEP 2: Submit your deposit

After being admitted and activating your account (STEP 1 above), sign in to MyBlugold CampS and follow these steps:

  1. Select Pay Housing Deposit from the menu on the left-hand side.
  2. Select Make a Payment or Deposit.
  3. Need help? More instructions on deposits.

From here you will check the Housing Deposit box. NOTE: You may pay the enrollment deposit (indicate Freshman or Transfer) at the same time you pay your housing deposit. Indicate the term you were admitted to, the Housing Year (for Fall 2020 you would indicate the 2020-2021 year) and how you wish to pay your deposit(s).

International students looking to do an international wire transfer to pay housing deposit should follow the instructions on how to use Flywire to complete payment. 

Submit your deposit

STEP 3: Apply for housing

Housing assignments are first-come, first-served — so don't procrastinate! Students are guaranteed on-campus living if the application and deposit are submitted by May 1. Both steps are needed to secure your spot in the residence halls.

Apply for housing

STEP 4: Find a roommate

If you already know who you want to request as your roommate, you may invite this individual to your roommate group and they must accept the invitation to make a mutual roommate request in housing contract and/or at My Housing Portal Roommate Group.

If you want to search for a roommate among other incoming students, the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire Housing + Residence Life is excited to offer a Roommate Finder functionality within your My Housing Portal. Only after a student has completed the housing contract do they have access to the Roommate Finder.

How to use Roommate Finder

REMINDER! You can update your contract information including changing your roommate preference until May 1 at 11:59 p.m.
