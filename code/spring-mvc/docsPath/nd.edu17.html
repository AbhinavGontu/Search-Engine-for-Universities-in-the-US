Streaming Basilica Masses: Frequently Asked Questions

Why are Daily Masses being streamed?
The live stream of Monday-Friday Daily Masses from the Basilica is being offered temporarily during the time of the COVID-19 pandemic. Once people are able to attend Mass in person, we will discontinue this stream.

Why are Saturday Masses no longer being streamed?
Due to the resumption of limited-attendance weddings, it is no longer possible to stream a Saturday Daily Mass except during Lent.

Can I view archived recordings of the Daily Masses?
Only the most recently recorded Mass is available for viewing.

Why is Sunday Mass streamed differently?
Sunday Masses at the Basilica have been streamed and broadcast for several years by CatholicTV and distributed through their channel. While streamed Daily Masses will not continue past the time of the COVID-19 pandemic, Sunday Mass will continue to be broadcast.

I would like to watch Sunday Mass live, but it seems I am being denied access:
The rights for the Sunday Mass broadcast are owned by CatholicTV. Some areas have restrictions for viewing the Mass live. Click here to find out more specifics for your region. Archived versions of the Sunday Masses are available here.

Where can I direct further questions?
These Masses are provided through a partnership with Notre Dame Campus Ministry. Please email other questions regarding the Masses to ministry@nd.edu.
