With the health and safety of our alumnae, alumni, alumnx, Wells staff, and the local community of paramount importance, Reunion 2021 will be a 100% virtual experience.


The Office of Alumnae and Alumni Engagement, in consultation with Christin Schaaf '99, WCA Vice President for Reunion, waited as long as possible to make the call. Planning and carrying out an annual Reunion is an incredible effort that demands a lot of careful planning, many hours, and “all hands on deck” to make sure that everything runs smoothly. Because of this, we had to make a decision now.


We have some really great ideas to make virtual Reunion 2021 a fabulous experience! We are looking on the bright side of the situation; a virtual program allows for greater access to the event.


We are still planning our events, but here is a tentative schedule of activities- times, dates, and details to be announced. We also will keep you up to date as we add to this schedule.


• Month of May Learning by the Lake (5/12): Fran Kissell ’78 & Amy McNaughton ’86 will present the history of the WCA

• WCA Award Recipient Ceremonies

• Reunion Trivia

• WCA Annual Meeting

• Reunion College

• Coffee with the Cabinet


Keep checking here and on the WCA Facebook Page for more updates
