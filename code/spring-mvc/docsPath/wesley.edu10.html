If you are a graduate of Wesley College or attended its predecessor, Wesley Junior College, then you are automatically a member of the Wesley Alumni Association! Proud graduates like yourself are the College's greatest ambassadors and your voice and involvement in the Alumni Association is very important.

Under the direction of its currently elected Board of Directors, the Association works closely and cooperatively with the Office of Alumni Relations and Annual Giving under the umbrella of the Office of Institutional Advancement at Wesley College. Together, they act as a liaison between the College and its graduates and aim to serve the interests of all alumni and keep them connected with each other and the institution through the programming and services they provide.

Alumni Board Involvement

The Alumni Association seeks dedicated alumni leaders who can serve a minimum three year term and proudly represent their fellow graduates. The Alumni Board has different committees that serve as advisors and outreach agents for programs and events to further engage alumni and develop partnerships that can benefit alumni, students and the College in general. The current Alumni Board committees are: 
  • Alumni Nominations and Awards Committee – this committee will establish the process and the application for creating Alumni Awards for outstanding alumni to be awarded at the All Class Dinner during Homecoming Weekend.  The award categories are: Alumni Service Award, Public Service Award and Professional Achievement Award.  Click here to download the Alumni Award descriptions and nomination form.
  • Outreach and Fundraising Committee – this committee will plan outreach programs and events for alumni and fundraising activities in support of the Alumni Scholarship Fund.
  • Scholarship Committee – this committee reviews scholarship applications and selects the student recipients for each award.  The Alumni Scholarships are: the Lewis Wells Scholarship, the Mildred B. Spencer Memorial Scholarship, the Scott D. Miller Leadership Award and the Wesley College Alumni Scholarship.

Nominations for the Alumni Board of Directors are accepted throughout the year and are voted on annually during Homecoming Weekend. You can nominate another alumnus or yourself at any time. Download the candidate application.
