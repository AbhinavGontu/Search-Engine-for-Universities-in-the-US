Mines Plans Return to Normal Operations this Fall

South Dakota Mines plans plans for a return to normal in the Fall 2021 semester with regular in-person classes, a full athletics schedule, student activities and the traditional homecoming celebration along with other events.

//

Faculty Member Receives Army Patriotic Civilian Service Award for Exceptional Teaching of Military History

Dr. John Dreyer, associate professor of social science at South Dakota Mines has been presented with the Department of the Army Patriotic Civilian Service Award for his commitment to Mines’ ROTC program and his exceptional teaching of military history. 

//

Mines Professor to Advise Top US Officials on Mining Safety

South Dakota Mines associate professor Dr. Andrea Brickey, has been appointed to the Mine Safety and Health Research Advisory Committee, a group of mining experts advising the Secretary of Health and Human Services, the director of the Centers for Disease Control & Prevention and the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health.

//

Mines Athlete Credits Coaches for Success at the Hula Bowl

When Jack Batho stepped onto the field at the 2021 Hula Bowl at Aloha Stadium, in Honolulu, HI, he became the first ever Hardrocker football player to join the all-star game.
