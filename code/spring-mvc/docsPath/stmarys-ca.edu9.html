Saint Mary’s Women’s Basketball

SMC Women's Basketball Camps & Clinics are dedicated to all aspects of the game, including skill development, game strategy, competition, and mechanics. Each athlete will have the opportunity to develop their skills through individual instruction, group instruction, and game experience.

Saint Mary's College is very fortunate to have some of the finest collegiate coaches in Northern California. Head Coach Paul Thomas hand selects and trains his staff, composed of top-level coaches and collegiate players. All instructors have been chosen for their extensive knowledge of the game of basketball and their enthusiasm to work with children and young athletes.
