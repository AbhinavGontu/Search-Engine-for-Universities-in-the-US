Graduate School

   

Office of Graduate and Professional Studies

Welcome to The Graduate School at Southern University and A&M College!

More students in Louisiana and throughout the U.S. are seeing the value of a graduate degree from Southern University.  The University has established programs that provide students with the knowledge and skills to meet work force demands.  The courses are taught by a dedicated graduate faculty which includes Fulbright Scholars, distinguished researchers, and mentors.

Your overall goal is achievable by earning your graduate degree, you should choose a field of study that suits your personality and your academic or professional goals.   The Graduate Program Brochure Search provides summary information about the degrees offered in each graduate program.

A more detailed information of all the graduate programs at Southern University-baton Rouge can be found at GRADUATE PROGRAMS.
