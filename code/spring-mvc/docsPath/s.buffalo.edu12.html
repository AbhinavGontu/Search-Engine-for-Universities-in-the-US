More Alumni, Students & Faculty

Julia Buscaglia Student Support Fund

The Julia Buscaglia Student Support Fund was created to help UB nursing students with unexpected hardships.

These funds offer temporary one-time financial assistance for immediate and current emergency situations. As health care workers on the front lines battle the COVID-19 pandemic, this fund is more important than ever.

Learn how to apply or contribute>

Justice, Equity, Diversity & Inclusion (JEDI) Fund

As nurses who pledge to provide care for all human beings, we must take a stand against the structural and systemic racism.

Contributions to the School of Nursing’s JEDI Fund will sponsor guest speakers, workshops, consultants, professional development and other programmatic initiatives that promote justice, equity, diversity and inclusion.
