Iconic Entrepreneurs

Kyle Gillis and James Carnes

Kyle and James grew up miles apart in the Northern Panhandle of West Virginia. But their paths would not cross until their junior year at WVU when a shared entrepreneurial thirst brought them together — and ultimately launched Iconic Air, an industrial IoT software platform that’s set to bring their company over $1 million in 2021.
