Transforming Northern State

Northern State University and the South Dakota School for the Blind and Visually Impaired are embarking on a transformational campaign to create state-of-the-art facilities that will forever change the educational footprint and experience for countless students from all over the region, state and the Aberdeen community. These facilities will serve as a point of pride for the Region and hub of activity for campus and community events.
