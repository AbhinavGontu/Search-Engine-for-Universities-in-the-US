Spending Transparency

 
Jackson State University is committed to transparency about the funds it uses and accountability to the students, taxpayers, sponsors, donors and alumni it serves. Each of these groups provides critical funding necessary to meet the University's mission-related responsibilities of teaching, research and public service.
