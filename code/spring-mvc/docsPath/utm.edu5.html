Capital Projects

The Office of Capital Projects supports the UT System campuses and institutions with the administration of the University’s capital improvement programs of Facilities Planning and Real Property & Space Administration.

 

Divisions

Capital Budget

Coordinates budgeting and financial management for all capital projects and reviews all contracts and transactions.

 

Capital Budget →

Facilities Planning

Administers design and construction of all facilities and capital improvement projects.

 

Facilities Planning →

Real Property & Space Administration

Coordinates all real property transactions, including gift, acquisitions, disposals, easements and agreements.
