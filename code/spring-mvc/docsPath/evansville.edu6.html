UE Athletics Hall of Fame

 
UE Athletics Hall of Fame

Mission: The University of Evansville Athletics Hall of Fame is an entity that was created to permanently honor those who have brought excellence to the Purple Aces.  It consists of student-athletes, coaches, administrators, teams and supporters who have been pivotal to the success of UE Athletics since its inception.  

Eligibility: Student-athletes and coaches must be at least five years removed from their time at UE.  Current employees of the University of Evansville Athletic Department are not eligible for induction into the Hall of Fame.  Student-athletes who are inducted must be graduates of the University of Evansville or have left the school in good standing to embark on a professional career.

Nomination Process: Nominations will be taken at any time by emailing rp113@evansville.edu.  In-person nominations will also be accepted by the department.  All nominations must be turned in by June 1 of the voting year in order to be taken into consideration.

Induction Process: Beginning in 2017, classes will be inducted into the Hall of Fame every two years.  After nominations are submitted, they will be put into a database of all candidates up for induction and presented to the Nominating and Athletic Committees.  The Nominating Committee will review all candidates and submit their recommendations to the Athletic Committee in July.  The Athletic Committee will make the final decision soon after.  Voting will take place a year and a half before induction.  For example, the Class of 2019 will be voted on in the summer of 2017 and inducted in the winter of 2019. 

Voting Structure: The voting structure for the UE Athletics Hall of Fame consists of a Nominating Committee and Athletic Committee.   The Nominating Committee includes an Alumni representative, a member of the UE Board of Trustees, former Athletic Directors, Emeritus Athletic Staff, Emeritus Coaches and media representatives.  This committee will review all nominations and submit recommendations to the Athletic Committee.  Included in this group are: the Athletic Director, SWA and Associate AD for Media Relations.  Final decision are made by this group.  There is no minimum or maximum number of inductees each year.  They will choose the best candidates based on the recommendations of the Nominating Committee.

Class Release: Once final decisions are made, the athletic department will contact the inductees and inform them.  This will take place in August.  Once these calls are made, the Media Relations office will release to the media, on the website and via social media.

Hall of Fame Banquet: All efforts will be made to plan the induction ceremony around a men’s basketball home conference game in the second to third weekend in January.  The time and location of the ceremony will be made as early as possible when home basketball dates are determined.  This will usually be announced in mid- September.  A full coordination with the UE Alumni Office will be made to set up an online registration for those who wish to attend the banquet.  The Assistant to the AD will be contact for all RSVP’s.  The SWA will be the contact for staff, student-athletes and coaches who will be attending the banquet.  Inductees receive free admission for themselves and one guest to the banquet.  All efforts will be made to put the inductees with the families, friends and attendees.  The department will also work with inductees on a video that involves them, teammates, coaches and administrators who will be able to add substance based on their time spent at UE.

Inductee Benefits: Inductees will receive a complimentary entry to the banquet for themselves as well as one guest.  Travel will be reviewed and given on a case-by-case basis.  The general expectation is that inductees coming from out of town will also receive a 1-night hotel stay through the athletic department.
