We are pleased to offer our alumni and friends a variety of means in which to stay abreast of the latest happenings and connect with the Wesley network.

Alumni E-Newsletter 

All alumni with a current email address on record automatically receive our e-newsletter. Launched in 2009, this resource is sent via email monthly, with the exception of January and July. Please ensure that your email address is up to date to continue to receive this in your inbox. And to avoid it accidentally ending up in your junk or spam folder, please add Wesley College and "alumni@wesley.edu" to your Safe Senders list and/or address book.

In the Loop

This printed newsletter is designed for all current members of the Wesley Society as a means of sharing insider news and information on upcoming events with our most committed donors. It is mailed approximately three times per year.

Wesley College Yearbook

The Wesley College yearbook, Eukairia, offers people a look back at the past year of memories at the College. We currently have yearbooks from 1960 - 1977 available digitally as well as 1955, 1970, 1975, 1980, and 1990.

You can click here to view previous yearbooks online.

Social Networking

Alumni have access to many exclusive features of social media within a more intimate network of Wesley alums through the online community on this alumni.wesley.edu site. These include an online directory, career networking, class notes, message boards and photo albums. Login today to take advantage of these resources and connect with your fellow alums!

In addition, Wesley has a presence on a variety of mainstream social media sites, including official pages on Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube and Flickr, where you can find photos of all recent College-sponsored events on and off campus.

There are so many ways to stay connected!
