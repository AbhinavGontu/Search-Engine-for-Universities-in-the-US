Forever Buffs are a powerful network of over 300,000 alumni who proudly call the University of Colorado Boulder their alma mater. 

The CU Boulder Alumni Association serves all Forever Buffs by providing meaningful experiences and valuable resources. 

Stay connected to fellow Forever Buffs and CU Boulder.

Learn More About Forever Buffs

Network launches to students

If you’re willing to open doors within your industry, offer job search advice or serve as a mentor to current CU students, there’s now an easier way. Join the free Forever Buffs Network, or if you’re already signed up, make sure your profile is up to date and indicates that you’re ready to help! Current students are signing up now and it's important to let them know their Forever Buffs family is here to support them.

Membership Benefits Without Membership Dues 

In 2008, the University of Colorado Boulder launched the Forever Buffs initiative, eliminating annual dues for all alumni. Today the Forever Buffs community includes more than 294,000 students, alumni and friends who engage with the university through the programs, events, publications and social media channels offered by the Alumni Association.
