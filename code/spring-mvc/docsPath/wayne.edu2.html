Virtual Match Day 2021

Date: March 19, 2021
Time: 10:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.
Location: Virtual
Category: Special Event

RSVP Now

Share

Please join us for Match Day 2021!

From 10 a.m. to noon on March 19, a live and interactive Zoom event hosted by your class leadership will feature as many of the traditional Match Day program elements as possible, including award announcements, Deans' remarks, mock awards, open mic session, an exciting Match statistics presentation, a map of your matched locations, and of course, the countdown clock. Family and friends are welcome to watch with you!

For medical students nationwide, Match Day 2021 is the culmination of four years of hard work and many consider it the most exciting day of their medical experience. On March 19th our nearly 300 Warrior MD's will receive the name of the institution they 'matched" with to begin their post-graduation residency in their chosen specialty. At exactly 12:00 p.m. EST, each student will open the email with the name disclosing that information. Join us virtually for the Wayne State University School of Medicine's 2021 Match Day celebration.
