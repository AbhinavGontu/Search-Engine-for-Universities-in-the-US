Hill Library Renovation

Extraordinary Changes are Here!

A major renovation of the Hill Library has been completed, including a full renovation of the second and third floors of the bookstacks tower with a new open stairwell connecting these floors with the Ask Us lobby. The Hill Library is now the home to a new Academic Success Center and new technology-rich Libraries spaces.
