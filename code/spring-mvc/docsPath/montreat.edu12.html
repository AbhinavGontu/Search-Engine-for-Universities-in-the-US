The Bob Watt Memorial Golf Tournament

Join us!

Monday, June 7, 2021

 

The Country Club of Asheville
Monday, June 7, 2021
The Country Club of Asheville

This year’s tournament will once again serve as the key fundraiser for the Montreat College athletics programs and fitness facilities. We appreciate your support of our Christ-centered athletics mission.

Tee it up with us to meet our goal of raising $40,000 by becoming a sponsor, entering a team, and enjoying all other tournament-day activities.

For more information, please contact events@montreat.edu or 828-419-2109.

Event Details

 
We look forward to seeing you at The Country Club of Asheville on June 7 for the annual Montreat College Bob Watt Memorial Golf Tournament.
