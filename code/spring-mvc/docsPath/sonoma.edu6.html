Announcements

Graduation: The final deadline to apply for Fall 2020 graduation is September 15, 2020. If you plan on graduating in Spring or Summer 2021, the final deadline to apply is February 1, 2021. 

What We Do

Welcome to the Office of the Registrar.  We strive to provide efficient, accurate user-friendly services that will contribute to the attraction, retention, and graduation of a diverse student body.  We provide an important link between academic policies of SSU and our academic departments and students.  It is the responsibility of the Office of the Registrar to safeguard the security and integrity of information, while working toward providing fast and friendly service.  We are committed to provide all constituents, e.g. students, faculty, university administrators, and interested parties, with resources that enable them to accomplish their objectives.

For the protection of student's right to privacy, the Office of the Registrar ensures the University's compliance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)

Our office can answer questions such as: “How do I get an official transcript?” or “When do I register for classes?” or "How do I apply for graduation?".  Not sure how a transfer course will come over, we can help with that, too. 

Take a moment to read through all of our links and familiarize yourself with our office - what we do, policies, FAQ's, and so much more!!!
