Welcome

Welcome to the Youngstown State University Foundation. We are the designated philanthropic entity for Youngstown State University.

Vision

The Youngstown State University Foundation will be a catalytic force for the advancement of YSU, the Youngstown community, and Northeast Ohio, and will be a critical source of support, opportunity, and excellence for YSU students and faculty.

Mission
Statement

The Youngstown State University Foundation is an independent, autonomous, private, non-profit corporation that pursues, manages and distributes resources to support scholarships and student initiatives, as well as the growth and development of Youngstown State University.
