Athletic Communications

The Norwich University Athletic Communications office is the branch of the institution's Office of Communications that publicizes and promotes news and statistics about the 20 varsity athletic teams.

The office manages @NorwichCadets social media channels on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube, maintains the NorwichAthletics.com website and provides live video streaming of a majority of the home varsity athletic events.

From home game statistics to game day programs, event recaps to student-athlete bios, we communicate the wins and the losses and facilitate local and regional media coverage. The Athletic Communications office is located at 207 Andrews Hall, directly across from the gymnasium.

STUDENT-ATHLETE PROFILES

The NU Athletic Communications staff requires that each student-athlete complete a student-athlete questionnaire form prior to his/her first game of the season. The information shared on the profile form will be used to publicize student-athletes and their accomplishments. We regularly update bios on the website, send releases on student-athlete successes to hometown newspapers, and nominate qualifying players for awards that require personal information.
