Still looking for the right fit? Let’s try these options.

Prospective Undergraduate Students

Prospective students should connect with your designated Admissions Counselor—they can answer questions and point you to additional resources to steer you in the right direction. And remember, you can start off as an undeclared major until you know what you want to study.

Current Undergraduate Students

Current undergraduates should contact your advisors or visit UAB’s Vulcan Materials Academic Success Center. Our counselors are pros at helping you find your a-ha moment.

Prospective Graduate Students

Students exploring graduate or professional programs should work with your advisors, explore the Accelerated Learning Opportunities offered by the Graduate School, or consider the Interdisciplinary Studies M.S. that allows you to build a program that’s right for you.
