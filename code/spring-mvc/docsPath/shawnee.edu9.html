Plagiarism 2.0

The following brief and informative videos provide an excellent introduction to plagiarism and how to avoid it.

    Viewers learn about different types of plagiarism.

  • Protected Content: Copyright and Fair Use (03:52)
  • Protected Content: Fair Use (01:52)

  • Fast Facts and Terms to Remember (01:31)
