UNT Mobile App

Download UNT's
mobile app!

Access everything you need
from UNT while you're on the go.

Check out UNT’s mobile app! Our mobile app was built from the ground up with busy students (and future students) in mind. Now, you can access everything you need from UNT while you are on the go. Get your grades, find your classes, learn about university events and contact faculty members − all with a few swipes and taps.

We’ll continue to add new features this year!

Features for current students:

Get final grade notifications more quickly
Read the latest UNT headlines with “What’s New”
Get your own “To dos” about specific actions you need to do
Check to see if there are any "Holds" on your account
Use “My Classes” to see a list of the classes you’re currently enrolled in and save the dates and times to your calendar
Use “Maps” to find your classes
See where your bus is using the "Bus Tracker"
Use the “Directory” to learn how to contact your professors
Find your “Final Exams” schedule each semester
See today’s, this week’s, this weekend’s or this month’s “Calendar” for upcoming deadlines and holidays
Check out “Social” for a look at what’s happening on the best social channels across campus
Learn about UNT’s “Traditions”

Features coming for current students:

  • Get notified when you can register for classes
  • Register for classes
  • View and get notifications about wait-listed classes
  • Get payment due reminders
  • Pay your tuition and other university bills
  • Update your emergency contact information

 

Features for our future students:

Read the latest UNT headlines with “What’s New”
Get information about UNT “Admissions”
See your list of “To Dos” to stay on track to apply or enroll
Check out our “Course Catalog” to see what courses UNT offers
Learn about all the ways we can help you pay for college
Search for specific classes with the app’s “Class Search”
See today’s, this week’s, this weekend’s or this month’s “Calendar” for events and important dates
Get familiar with campus before you get here with the app’s “Maps” feature
Sign up to “Take a Tour” of campus
Read the latest UNT “Sports” headlines
Request more information about UNT
Learn about UNT’s “Traditions”

Features coming for future students:

  • View your admissions status
  • Accept admittance
