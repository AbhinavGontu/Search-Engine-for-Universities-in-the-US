Administrative Information Management Systems (AIMS)

AIMS partners with University constituents to create, implement and support integrated information management solutions that contribute to the success of the University's mission.

It appears that you are visiting this page from an off-campus and/or public network. For security purposes, we require the use of a VPN to access WIUP and WIU Reports. Read more details on what this means for you.

STARS 

The Student/Alumni Records System (STARS) provides students, alumni, and employees with access to their university records.

WIUP

WIUP provides all employees with access to their own personnel, payroll and time reporting information as well as a campus directory and academic calendar. Some employees may also use WIUP to perform administrative processes related to their job duties. View more details about WIUP, including how access is granted.

As you are currently using an off-campus device, you must use one of WIU's VPN options in order to access WIUP.

WIU Reports (Pentaho)

WIU Reports provides access to online reporting, online analytical processing, analytics and data mining. View more details about WIU Reports, including how access is granted.

As you are currently using an off-campus device, you must use WIU's VPN client in order to access WIU Reports. The clientless VPN option does not support WIU Reports.

STARS and WIUP are offline for maintenance every night from Midnight to 3:00 a.m, and some Sundays between 7:00 p.m. and 9:00 p.m.
