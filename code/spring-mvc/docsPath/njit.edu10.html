Learn. Succeed. Lead.

Professional Development & Corporate Education developed by NJIT University

NJIT is your hub for innovative professional and personal growth. Whether you are starting your career, first time manager, or seasoned executive; we are here to grow with you.  There are many reasons why you might be considering a course or program from NJIT. Maybe you want to earn or prepare to earn a professional certification. Perhaps you are exploring a career change or need to develop specific skills or knowledge in order to earn a promotion or apply for a new position. 

We offer a variety of professional development and executive education courses. Please browse the list below to see our current programs and courses. If you are an employer who wants to develop several members of your workforce, create custom training programs or offer multiple employees the opportunity to further their education and training, you may  be interested in our group programs.

Why NJIT?

NJIT is recognized as a top 100 university and one of only 32 polytechnic universities in the United States. Over 75,000 employees, working in 665 New Jersey organizations, have been trained by NJIT’s Division of Continuing Professional Education since 1990.
