Get ready for Reunion!

We are looking forward to a combined Reunion celebration this year with Reunion 2020 and 2021 alumni. This year’s unprecedented event will bring together 20 classes to commemorate the alumni experience.

Later this year, Vanderbilt will announce the date and format for Reunion once we have more clarity regarding the pandemic. We will continue to plan Reunion with the health and safety of alumni and the entire Vanderbilt community as our top priorities. In the meantime, please explore this site for ways to connect with fellow alumni, and check back regularly for updates.
