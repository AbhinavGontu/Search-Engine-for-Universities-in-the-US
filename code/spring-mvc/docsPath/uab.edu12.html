Step foot on our campus—virtually, for now.

A click is all it will take to know you’ve found an amazing place. A place that gives you research opportunities right off the bat, allows you to pursue your dreams, and fosters your ideas to help you turn them into reality. Best of all, we do it on a diverse campus in an amazing city at an affordable price. Will you love UAB? We think so.

Visit Our Campus

Green Scene. Campus Scene. City Scene.

Schedule a Tour

Green Scene

UAB may be located in the heart of downtown Birmingham, but our campus feels like a world apart. For starters, we have a lush Campus Green that’s typically filled with students playing, relaxing, studying, picnicking—you name it. More than 4,000 trees all over campus offer shade, clean air, and beauty. We even have space for you to grow your own garden. How cool is that? We’d love to show you around — in person or virtually.

Visit UAB

Meet People, Make Friends

Campus Scene

Being a Blazer is about so much more than academics. We have 18 NCAA Division 1 sports teams, 20 club and intramural teams, 28 Greek organizations, 250 campus organizations, and one mighty sweet, state-of-the-art student center. Best of all, we’re proud to say we’re consistently recognized for our diversity. Want to know more?

Live the Blazer Life

The Magic City

City Scene

We can’t imagine a cooler city than Birmingham for a university like us to call home. Did you know there are 1.3 million people in and around this area? Birmingham is bustling with activity—entertainment, business, outdoor activities, you name it—and at UAB, we’re in the middle of it all. Our restaurants are among the best in America. We have more urban park space than you can imagine. And then there’s our museums, performing arts, sports. We’ve got it all. Let us show you around the Magic City. You’ll love what you see.

Explore Birmingham

Majors. For your Major Decision.

The first step to beginning your UAB education is finding your major, and lucky for you we’ve made that pretty easy. You can find all our programs in one website, along with potential careers and other useful information like research opportunities, pre-professional programs, study abroad, and much more. Find the major that’s best for you.
