Campaign Priorities

The Power of Stevens has three priorities: enabling student success, promoting faculty excellence and creating a vibrant campus.

Student Success

Since its founding, Stevens has prepared students for success. Scholarships open doors, and new investments in academics, athletics, arts and other programs enable students to grow professionally and personally.

Learn More
Faculty Excellence

Faculty come to Stevens because philanthropy creates an environment where they can excel. Named chairs and fellowships provide resources for professors to inspire students, drive innovation through research, and influence academia with their prestige.

Learn more
A Vibrant Campus

Generations of alumni and friends have cherished Castle Point, and their investments now are transforming the campus into a modern center of teaching, research, learning and living.

Every Gift Has a Story

Some gifts come from grateful alumni who make provisions for Stevens in their estate plans. Others come from eager new alumni who make their first gift soon after graduating, and begin to make philanthropy an annual habit. Still more gifts come from generous corporate and foundation partners who believe Stevens is a sound investment. Whatever the reason, and no matter the amount, every gift has a story and an impact.
