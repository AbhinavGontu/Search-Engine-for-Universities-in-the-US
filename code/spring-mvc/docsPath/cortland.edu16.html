  • Update your Record

You never call, you never write.

Alumni can now update their records in the Red Dragon Network. Please take a moment to log in to update your alumni record.

Updating your record will ensure that you receive:

• free editions of Columns (the alumni magazine), Alumni Adventures (the digital events listing) and Moments (the alumni e-newsletter),
• alumni event invitations from the College and Chapters,
• campus updates,
• and so much more.

Your information also helps us provide opportunities for alumni to enhance student education through professional networking and student career events. Please be sure to always update the Red Dragon Network if your contact information changes. You may also update your record by contacting Alumni Engagement at 607-753-2516 or alumni@cortland.edu. The Alumni Association values your privacy and protects your information as outlined in the Rights to Privacy Act of 1974.
