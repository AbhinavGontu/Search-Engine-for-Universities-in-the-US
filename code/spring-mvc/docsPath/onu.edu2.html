UPDATE: On-campus visits are now available. Schedule your on-campus or virtual visit today. Both options include an admissions presentation, campus tour and academic meeting. Scroll through this page to see all options available.

Daily campus visits

These visits are the most popular and personalized way to visit campus. ONU has taken extra precautions during this time to make the campus visit safe for you and the ONU community – including limiting the number of daily visits. Morning and afternoon visit times are available.

Schedule Your Virtual Visit

Virtual visits to ONU are offered three times each weekday: 9 a.m., noon and 6 p.m. Each virtual visit includes an admissions meeting, academic meeting and a student-led virtual campus tour.
  • Register Now

SATURDAY CAMPUS VISITS

Saturday visits begin at 9 a.m. and include the same meetings as our weekday visits. Academic and admissions meetings are conducted in a group setting on Saturdays. Visits conclude at noon.

We offer Saturday Campus Visits during the academic school year.
