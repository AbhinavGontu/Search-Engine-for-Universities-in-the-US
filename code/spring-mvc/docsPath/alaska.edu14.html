    College of Fisheries and Ocean Sciences Temporary Clerical Fairbanks 30 Jun 2021
    College of Fisheries and Ocean Sciences (CFOS) is looking for a part-time Administrative Assistant in Fairbanks, Alaska. The ideal candidate will be a friendly, proactive self-starter, who can read and understand manuals, likes to organize things and keep them organized, who is efficient in planning his or her work in accordance with position requirements. The candidate must be able to consistently meet processing deadlines. The position involves interacting with and providing various support to a diverse set of CFOS employees and students, both in-person and remotely, working with numbers, multitasking, and keeping the Front Desk, Mail Room, and Break Room neat and organized. CFOS is one of the largest units at the Fairbanks campus. It has a large population of research faculty, staff, and students in different locations across Alaska. The duties of this position are also diverse, from handling mail to fiscal work. While some work can be done from home, physical presence on campus is required at least 80% of the time.
    Clery Compliance Coordinator and Prevention Specialist Fairbanks 31 Dec 2021
    The University of Alaska Fairbanks is looking for qualified applicants to join our Center for Student Rights and Responsibilities (CSRR) team as a Clery Compliance Coordinator and Prevention Specialist. This position is responsible for the implementation and interpretation of applicable federal, state and campus policies/regulations required for compliance with the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act (Clery Act). This position works in coordination with the Associate and Executive Directors in CSRR to provide leadership, direction and integration of the Clery Act compliance for the Fairbanks campus, rural campuses, as well as separate campus and non-campus properties. This position will be responsible for managing reports, including the Annual Security and Fire Safety Report (ASFSR), as well as, records and documentation vital to ensuring campus compliance with the Clery Act.
