Frequently Asked Questions

  • Home
  • Alumni
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you have questions about getting involved with NU, want to find fellow alumni in your area or have questions about your membership benefits – here’s a list of our most asked questions.

How do I become a member of the Alumni Association?
If you are a graduate of National University (associate’s, bachelor’s, master’s, and credential completers) you are a part of the Alumni Association. There are no dues or memberships to apply for. Alumni will receive ongoing communication from the Alumni Relations team regarding events, newsletters, and other university happenings. If you are not receiving communications from us, please let us know. You can opt-out at any time.
How can I get verification of my alumni status?
You can request a letter from the Alumni Relations office by filling out this form. Your letter will be emailed to you instantly.
What benefits am I eligible for as an alumnus?

Insurance Discounts

AAA and other insurance companies offer up to a 10% discount for those who are a part of an alumni association. Since you are a graduate, you are a part of the alumni association and qualify for a discount. Contact your rep for more details.

Career Services Resources

Our Career Services team offers quality career and internship resources to help students and alumni identify and fulfill their career goals. They will help you define your unique potential for success and foster lifelong professional and career development skills. You will benefit from their innovative workshops, exceptional support, expanded employment opportunities, and community partnerships. Careers Services are available in person and online. Additionally, their job portal connects you with employers and enables you to easily apply for open positions and internship opportunities.

Alumni Online Library

A $50 annual fee entitles members to access online resources and borrow books from the National University Library.

Course Auditing Discounts

Alumni can audit many National University courses at 50% off of the regular tuition.

Extended Learning Discounts

Alumni enjoy a 10% discount on many extended learning courses.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways to get involved with the Alumni Association:

  • Become a mentor or be a mentee
  • Join one of our activity clubs in San Diego
  • Come to one of our many events that we host throughout the year
  • Serve on a Regional Chapter Board in Fresno, Sacramento, or San Diego
I need help finding a job. Can you help?
Yes! Our Career Services Department has many online and on-site resources to aid you with your job search. They provide counseling and career assessments; cover letter and resume writing assistance; and various job-related workshops – all at no cost. 
How do I obtain a copy of my transcript?
To order an official transcript, visit the Parchment website to place your order. National University does not accept transcript requests by phone, e-mail, or fax. Paper transcripts are $5.00 each with options for expedited shipping, for an additional fee. Electronic transcripts cost $7.00 each. An official transcript includes the Registrar’s signature, our college seal, the date it was issued, and delivered in a sealed envelope. It is a legal document and should not be opened by anyone except the addressee. Employers and potential employers typically require an official transcript.

Your unofficial transcript can be printed directly from your SOAR Student Portal. On the left side menu under Academics > My Academics > Request Unofficial Transcript. This will produce a pdf copy of your transcript to print or email to yourself. An unofficial transcript is the student’s academic record without signature, seal, date, or sealed envelope.

If you no longer have access to the SOAR Student Portal, forgot your student ID or password contact our Student Concierge Services at scs@nu.edu. For transcript assistance, contact transcripts@nu.edu. The Alumni Relations team does not have access to reset your account.
I'm an employer that would like to post a job for your students and alumni. How can I do this?

The Career Services department offers a job portal that employers can post their open jobs. Employers can register for a free account through the NU GradLeaders Online Career Center. This allows you to post as many jobs, internships, or volunteer opportunities as you would like online. As employers in our job portal you will have the ability to target candidates by GPA, major, program, etc.

Visit the job portal here: https://employer.gradleaders.com/NU/Employers/Login.aspx?jprid=5405 

For more information, visit our page Employer Information page.

Where can I get NU alumni gear?
Head over to https://shopnustores.com/ to get your gear and support your alma mater!
Can I get an alumni or NU email address?
Unfortunately, we are unable to offer email addresses to our alumni or students.
How can I get a duplicate diploma?
If your degree has already been awarded and you have received a diploma, you can request an additional diploma by submitting the Application for Duplicate Diploma.
How do I get in touch with an alumnus?
LinkedIn is a great resource to find alumni and the Alumni Association has many groups on LinkedIn organized by both region and areas of interest. We’ve created these pages to serve as a valuable resource and meet your local professional development needs. You will find announcements regarding fellow graduates, along with posts regarding services and benefits that are available – usually free of charge. There are more than 57,000 NU alumni to connect with and if you aren’t already a member, we encourage you to sign up.
I’m an alumnus looking to encourage higher education in my classroom. Where can I get items to promote NU?
Please contact us so we can assess your needs and provide you with resources to promote NU.
Now that I've graduated, can I still access the library?
Absolutely! There is a membership fee of $50 year to access the library. More information about the Alumni Library Subscription can be found here.
How do I change my name with the University?

University policy requires you to mail in proof of your legal name change. Please fill out the Name Change Request Form with a copy of your new social security card or court order showing your name change and mail or fax it to the Records Department:

Office of the Registrar
Attn: Records
11255 N. Torrey Pines Road
La Jolla, CA 92037
records@nu.edu
Fax: (858) 642-8718
*The Office of the Registrar cannot change your name without documentation.

How do I notify the University of a deceased alumnus?

In order for the University to mark an alumnus as deceased, you must mail in a copy of an obituary or death certificate. It does not have to be a certified copy, a photocopy will do. Please send to:

Office of the Registrar
records@nu.edu
Phone: (866) 682-2237 x8260
Fax: (858) 642-8721
