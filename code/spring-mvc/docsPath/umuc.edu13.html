Business Partners: Join Forces with UMGC

There has never been a better time to do business with UMGC, educate your workforce, or hire graduates.

Build your company's competitive advantage by partnering with University of Maryland Global Campus to offer continuing education and training for your employees. Hire UMGC students and alumni to fill your talent gap. Or expand your company's business by becoming a UMGC vendor. No matter your need, UMGC has you covered.

Elevate Your Workforce

Your organization's competitive standing is sure to advance when you strengthen your staff with proven and effective training and continuing education at UMGC.

UMGC offers degree and certificate programs, as well as corporate and leadership training. Our career-focused programs can help you identify emerging leaders, plan for succession, encourage innovation, improve employee productivity and effectiveness, and retain crucial employees.

We're proud of our extensive alliances with corporations and businesses around the D.C. region, the nation and the world, and we look forward to helping you transform your organization. Learn more about our Corporate Learning Solutions.

Recruit and Hire Students and Alumni

If you're looking to hire qualified, highly skilled individuals for openings within your organization, you've come to the right place. UMGC students and alumni come prepared with the skills and talent you need to help your company succeed.

UMGC prepares our graduates for success in the workplace. Here are just a few ways that our graduates stand out.

  • Ready to lead: We prepare our graduates with the soft skills necessary to be most effective in the workplace by teaching leadership, critical thinking, problem-solving, teamwork, and communication.
  • Prepared for challenges: UMGC's curriculum is designed with input from leading companies and focuses on applicable skills and knowledge for today's professional environments.
  • Motivated for success: While pursuing higher education, many UMGC students balance full-time jobs and family life. This dedication to self improvement and learning attests to their drive for career success.

Join CareerQuest to create a company profile, post job and internship openings, recruit at job fairs, and more. To learn more, call 240-684-2720 or e-mail Career Services.

Do Business with UMGC as a Vendor

Interested in doing business with UMGC? If you are a prospective vendor, UMGC provides all of the information you need to get started. And it's never been easier. Access our step-by-step UMGC Procurement Guide, Electronic Bid Board for solicitations greater than $100,000, and more by visiting procurement.

All students are required to pay tuition for all courses in which they are enrolled. Tuition rates are subject to the approval of the University System of Maryland Board of Regents. Notwithstanding any other provision of this or any other university publication, the university reserves the right to make changes in tuition, fees and other charges at any time such changes are deemed necessary by the university and the USM Board of Regents.

The Board of Regents has authorized the university to charge a student's delinquent account for all collection costs incurred by the university. The normal collection fee is 17 percent plus attorney and/or court costs. The service charge for a dishonored check is $30. Requests for services (for example, transcripts, diplomas, registration) will be denied until all debts are paid.

              Orientation to Graduate Studies at UMGC

              UCSP 615 | 0 Credits

              (Required within the first 6 credits of graduate study for all new graduate students, except those in programs requiring CBR, DCL, or PRO 600.) An overview of the skills needed for academic and professional success. Focus is on enhancing communication and critical thinking skills. Assignments provide familiarity with tools such as library and information resources. APA style and resources are also addressed.
