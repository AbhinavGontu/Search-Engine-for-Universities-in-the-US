Parents

The Valpo Family

With each student we admit, we are building on the hopes of previous generations. The college decision is one that affects the entire family with the potential to impact generations to come. We take this inherent trust to heart and do everything we can to support every Valpo family in our community.

Family Weekend

Experience Valpo as a family. It’s a tough decision for any one person to make. But together, you can help decide if this is the place where your student’s future is built. Later you can tell people that “Valpo just felt right!” Attend signature events throughout the weekend, meet other families, and get to know the Valpo community.

Valparaiso is a wonderful city and our campus is a close-knit community. We make sure that services are in place and available around the clock to ensure everyone can live and thrive safely at Valpo.
