SMC TENNIS CAMPS

OFFICIAL CAMP WEBSITE OF | SMC Tennis Camps

 

SMC Tennis Camps & Clinics are dedicated to all aspects of tennis including skill development, match strategy, competition and mechanics. Each athlete will have the opportunity to develop their skills through individual instruction, group instruction and match experience.

Saint Mary's College is very fortunate to have some of the finest collegiate coaches in Northern California. Head Men's Coach Greg Kennett and Head Women's Coach Lisa Alipaz hand select and trains their staff, composed of top-level coaches and collegiate players. All instructors have been chosen for their extensive knowledge of tennis and their enthusiasm to work with children and young athletes.
