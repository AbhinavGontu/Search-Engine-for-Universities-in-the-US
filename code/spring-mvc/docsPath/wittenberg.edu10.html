A New Standard Of Athletics Excellence

Thanks to an inspiring, leadership-level gift by Wes ’70 and Ann Bates ’70, and generations of Tigers, Wittenberg is now home to the fully renovated and expanded 265,000-square-foot Health, Wellness & Athletics (HWA) Complex, and its centerpiece, The Steemer indoor practice facility. Dedicated October 25, 2019, the complex is already setting the new standard of excellence in NCAA Division III, in community engagement, and in student success.

The new HWA continues to inspire students, the Wittenberg campus, extended community, and the region. See why in this special video.

Wholeness of Person

The HWA supports the entire student experience as it embraces the Tiger Up spirit that has defined athletes and non-athletes alike for generations. Whether used as a place to build community or train championship-level competitors, the complex positions Wittenberg as an innovative leader in holistic learning, health and wellness, and athletics excellence.

Learn More

Respecting Tradition

Wittenberg had a choice to move forward with a completely new facility or preserve its architectural heritage, which reflects its strong athletics history. The University chose the latter, as evidenced by the stunning restoration of the 1929 Fieldhouse and other updates and improvements across the Health, Wellness & Athletics Complex .

Learn More

A Regional Resource 

Fast becoming a sought-after facility for area high schools, community partners, conferences, and experiential learning, the HWA offers a range of options to support student success and regional engagement.
