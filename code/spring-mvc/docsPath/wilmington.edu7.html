Financial Aid & Scholarships

Student One Stop

Financial Aid, Scholarships & More

Student One Stop (located in Pyle Student Center) provides a “one-stop” location that assists students and their families throughout their educational endeavor. We are here to help you apply for financial aid, pay your bill, set up payment arrangements, register for classes, and more. Workstations are available whenever the office is open for students and their families to complete their FAFSA, complete entrance or exit loan counseling requirements, complete loan applications, or register for classes.

Don’t hesitate to contact us by phone at 1-800-341-9318 or 937.382.6661 (ext. 600) from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday or SEND US AN EMAIL 24/7. Representatives are ready to assist you.

OFFICE HOURS: 8 a.m. – 5 p.m., Monday – Friday

CHECK CASHING: 8 a.m. – 4 p.m., Monday – Friday
