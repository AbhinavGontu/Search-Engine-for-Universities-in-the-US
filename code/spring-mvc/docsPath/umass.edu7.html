Apr 21, 2021

New Course "Women in Business" Added
The McCormack Department of Sport Management has launched a new course for the spring semester titled “Women in Business.” Its goal is providing students with the opportunity to explore of the experiences of and opportunities for women in the sport and entertainment industry.
UMass Amherst Online Is Rising in the Ranks
U.S. News & World Report has released their rankings for 2021 and the online bachelor's programs at UMass Amherst have risen two spots to No. 21. The College of Nursing's online master's program has also gotten a boost to 12th in the nation.
UMass Amherst ranks 40th on the Princeton Review’s annual list of Best Graduate Entrepreneurship Programs
For the first time ever, UMass Amherst is ranked in the top 50 on the Princeton Review Best Graduate Entrepreneurship Programs annual list thanks in large part to the Berthiaume Center for Entrepreneurship.
Meeting the Growing Demand for Data Scientists
UMass now offers a master's program in Data Analyitics and Computational Social Science
Pending Campus Improvements Will Be Ready This Spring
UMass Facilities has been hard at work finalizing projects to make the campus safer, more accessible, and more enjoyable.
UMass Amherst Expands Free COVID-19 Testing
Partnering with the State of Massachusetts, UMass Amherst now offers free, asymptomatic COVID-19 testing for those living in the area.
Supporting the Safety of Local First Responders
Town of Amherst first responders and inspectors can now get tested for COVID-19 on the UMass Amherst campus.
