How Can I Report Fraud and Ethics Concerns?

Report Fraud, Compliance, or Ethics Concerns To:

  • Report to your department supervisor/director if you are an employee.

  • Report to the individual responsible for compliance of the concerned area - See Compliance Area Responsibility

  • Report to the SFA Department of Audit Services, Gina Oglesbee 936-468-6640 oglesbeegs@sfasu.edu.

  • Report to SFA Human Resources, John Wyatt 936-468-4075 wyattjohn@sfasu.edu.

  • Report to the SFA General Counsel, Damon Derrick 936-468-4305 derrickdc@sfasu.edu.

  • EthicsPoint  - 1-866-294-9539 or EthicsPoint website

  • Texas State Auditor’s Office – 1-800-TX-AUDIT or https://sao.fraud.state.tx.us/Hotline.aspx

  • Report to the Office of Research and Sponsored Programs, Jennifer Hanlon 936-468-1805 hanlonjd@sfasu.edu.


Additional information on Research and Sponsored Programs reporting is available as follows:

  • General Compliance
  • Whistleblower
  • Drug-Free

EthicsPoint is SFA’s third party reporting line.

Advantages of using Ethics Point to report fraud and ethics concerns:

  1. It is a 3rd Party Provider.
  2. You can report anonymously.
  3. Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
  4. Report in English or Spanish.
  5. You are able to follow-up on your report.
