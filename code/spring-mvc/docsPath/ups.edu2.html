Associated Students of University of Puget Sound (ASUPS)

ASUPS seeks:

  • To provide a student voice through representation
  • To foster a sense of community among students
  • To execute the official business of the Student Body

We do so with the authority granted to us by the Board of Trustees of the University of Puget Sound, and with a firm belief that the capacity of a unified collective is greater than the sum of all of its individual parts.

Your Ideas Wanted

If you could change one thing about the university, what would it be? Submit your ideas to the ASUPS Student Concerns Committee. We'll do our best to help.
