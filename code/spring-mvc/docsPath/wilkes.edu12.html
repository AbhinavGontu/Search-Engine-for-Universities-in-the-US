Marching Colonels

The region’s only collegiate marching band marches in drum corps style.

The Wilkes University Marching Band is one of the most visible groups on campus, performing at home football games and at events on and off campus.

Students of all majors are welcome to participate in the Marching Colonels. No audition is necessary. 

For more information please contact Dr. Mark Johnson: Mark.Johnson2@wilkes.edu or (570) 408-5533.

Wilkes University is a Conn-Selmer School.

Wilkes University Pep Band (MUS 198 A)

The Wilkes University Pep Band is one of the most visible groups on campus, performing at home basketball games for the men and women teams along with other associated athletic events. Students of all majors are welcome to participate. No audition is necessary. Please contact Dr. Johnson for more information.
