MAKE A VIRTUAL APPOINTMENT WITH SFS STAFF

 

Student Financial Services includes Financial Aid and Student Accounts at SUNY Cobleskill. Attending College is one of the biggest investments a student will make and Student Financial Services is committed to helping students understand their bill and maximize financial aid awards from all possible sources.

 

IMPORTANT OFFICE INFORMATION

The Student Financial Services Office is temporarily working from home. We are able to accept phone calls remotely between 9AM and 4:15PM (Monday-Friday), however our connections may occasionally be interrupted. We are making every effort to provide you with the answers and information you need and we appreciate your patience and understanding.

As a reliable alternative, please continue to send questions and comments to financialaid@cobleskill.edu or studentaccounts@cobleskill.edu. Our emails are being monitored closely and most emails will receive a response within 48 hours.
