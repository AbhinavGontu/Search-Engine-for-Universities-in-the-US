Spring 2021 Services and Resources

Find your seat at the Library 
Whether you are studying or just need to use a computer or printer, please reserve a seat using the seat reservation system.  

Research Help
Librarians are here for research help via the ASK US Chat from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. (ET) Monday through Friday. You can also schedule a research consultation with a librarian.

Library Hours and Events
Visit our Hours page for current and future hours, and Events for our schedule of events, workshops, and exhibits.

Research Guides
Specifically built for Stevens' subjects, courses, and topics, these guides can navigate you to the information sources you need.
