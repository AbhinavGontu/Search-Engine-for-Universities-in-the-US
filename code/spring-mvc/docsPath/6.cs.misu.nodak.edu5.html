Schedule a visit at Minot State University!

Monday-Friday Visits: Visit Minot State to see where you belong! Campus tours are given Monday-Friday at 10 a.m. and 1 p.m. with a student ambassador and include an appointment with an admissions counselor. Plan to be on campus for at least an hour and a half, but stay as long as you'd like!

Appointments must be made in advance and are limited to two students per tour. We ask that prospective students bring no more than two guests. Individual tours are available upon request.

In efforts to keep everyone safe, as of August 17, 2020, all visitors and staff on campus are required to wear a mask. 

CLICK HERE TO SCHEDULE YOUR CAMPUS TOUR

Transfer Student Visits: Transfer Student Visits will be available on weekdays from 9 AM to 3 PM Central Time. We know transfer students often have specific questions about transferring credits, paying for college, and academic programs. This visit will allow you to find the answers!

You will learn about admission requirements, can receive an unofficial transcript evaluation (of General Education requirements), and meet with a representative from the academic major of your choice. If you complete an application beforehand and bring all necessary, official academic documents, we will complete your file for an expedited admission decision.
