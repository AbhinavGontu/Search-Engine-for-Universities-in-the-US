Carson College of Business

Academic programs

The Carson College of Business offers two degree programs, four minors and one professional certificate:

  • Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration
    You may specialize in one of seven undergraduate majors:
    • Accounting
    • Business Administration
    • Entrepreneurship
    • Finance
    • Management
    • Management Information Systems
    • Marketing
  • Bachelor of Arts in Hospitality Business Management
    This degree prepares you to work in an expanding industry in the region or around the world.
  • Professional Sales Certificate
    The certificate program, open to all majors, prepares you for all types of persuasive communication as well as a career in selling.
