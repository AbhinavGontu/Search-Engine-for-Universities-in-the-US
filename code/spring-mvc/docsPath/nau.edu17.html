Welcome to LEAP at Northern Arizona University

Leadership, Engagement, Activities and Peer Mentoring

We’re focused on providing students with the resources and opportunities needed to enhance their educational experience. We help students cultivate the skills needed to become effective leaders in their lives and careers. We create opportunities for students to engage, have fun, and celebrate being a Lumberjack. And we connect students with peers who can offer support and encouragement throughout their academic journey.
