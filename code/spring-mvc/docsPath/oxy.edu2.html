The McKinnon Family Tennis Center is a 25,000 square-foot facility where Oxy's tennis teams train and compete. The sun sets over Anderson Field, home of Oxy's baseball team. The new De Mandel Aquatics Center will feature a competition pool and diving well for Oxy's water polo and swimming & diving teams to practice and compete. The Oxy men's and women's track and field teams compete in the Southern California Intercollegiate Athletic Conference (SCIAC) Track and Field Championships.
