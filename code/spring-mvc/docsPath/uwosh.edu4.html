Titans Are Leaders, Researchers and Champions

With a strong research focus and national ranking in sustainability, Titans demonstrate on a daily basis what students can do to change the world.

Student success is at the heart of UW Oshkosh. Students benefit from personalized support with small class sizes, a wealth of academic and personal support services, and personalized career and academic advising.

UW Oshkosh is part of the robust University of Wisconsin System—one of the largest systems of public higher education in the country. As a member of the System, UW Oshkosh provides students with state-wide resources and ensures top academic quality and accountability. 

There’s so much to discover and achieve at UW Oshkosh.

Titans build brighter futures every single day. 

Three campuses,

one university

UW Oshkosh is one institution with three world-shaping campuses. Students across the entire region have neighborhood access to UW Oshkosh with expanded program options and opportunities.

Downtown Oshkosh

Downtown Oshkosh is home to a great restaurant scene, coffee shops, stores, parks and even an amphitheatre. Take a stroll along the Riverwalk, visit the Oshkosh Farmers Market, or catch a live concert—all within walking distance of campus!

Parks & Recreation

Menominee Park, nestled along Lake Winnebago, is an ideal spot for walking, biking, boating, and more. With a lot of green space and even a zoo, it’s a great place to be.

Job Opportunity

Titans enjoy close proximity to world-class corporations and businesses who are eager to take them on as interns or new employees.

Event City

Oshkosh is known as “Wisconsin’s Event City,” due to the variety and scope of events we host. From Country USA to EAA AirVenture, there’s always something going on in Oshkosh!

Opportunity of Place

UWO has the distinct advantage of being in northeastern Wisconsin’s Fox Valley region, which is highly populated and home to many industry leaders with national and international reach. This close proximity provides students with access to high-impact internships and job opportunities.

Titans also enjoy an area with bountiful recreation options, fantastic restaurants, a thriving arts scene, endless shopping, and some of the biggest and most exciting events in the state.

Home Sweet Home

Titans enjoy a beautiful student-centered campus, sustainable high-tech facilities, small class sizes, more than 180 student organizations, and so much more. And the best part? We always rank very high in affordability and value. 

There’s so much to love about UW Oshkosh.
