Free Testing March 15-20

Free COVID-19 testing will be conducted at the Winthrop Coliseum from 9 a.m.-4 p.m. March 15-20. There is no testing on March 21. In cases of inclement weather, testing may be delayed or cancelled.

Eagles Face Villanova on March 19 in NCAA Tournament

Eagles Earn NCAA Bid By Capturing Big South Title

The Winthrop Eagles defeated Campbell 80-53 in the Big South Championship March 7, earning them a bid to the NCAA Tournament. The 12th-seeded Eagles face Villanova in the NCAA Tournament opening round on Friday, March 19.
