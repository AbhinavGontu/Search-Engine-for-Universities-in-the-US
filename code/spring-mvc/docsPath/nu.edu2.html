Welcome to the National University Alumni Association!

As a graduate of National University, you are a part of the Alumni Association connecting you to a community of over 180,000 well-connected, highly-educated professionals. What does it mean to be part of an alumni community? It's about lifelong learners making lifelong connections and tapping into lifelong resources and benefits.


Just like the classes you took here, the National University Alumni Association (NUAA) is largely accessible online. Browse through some of the advantages it offers you:

Membership

Upon graduation you are automatically a member of the Alumni Association. There are no dues, fees, or memberships to apply for.

Lifelong Connections

The National University Alumni Community allows you to share ideas, exchange data, stay current with an ever-changing world and keep in touch with colleagues in your field and your city. Take advantage of the following media:

NU Newsletter

Your source of NU-related news via email. The newsletter highlights alumni, faculty achievements, new curricula and relevant trends in industry, academia, and culture. If you are not receiving the Newsletter and would like to, let us know!

NU Alumni Connection

Read up on the latest news from your Alumni Association. The NU Connection features alumni, news, events, and more!

Alumni Events Calendar

An easy source for upcoming networking mixers, lectures, conferences, cultural engagements, career fairs, and other happenings.

Social Media

Lifelong Resources

Enjoy access to free and discounted resources that will help you with employment, research, and the promotion of school pride and affiliation. Check out the following:

MentorMe

 A free network of professionals where you can mentor to fellow alums or students or find a mentor who will assist you.  

Career Center

Offering free comprehensive, professional development and employment-related services, including an online job portal, resume and cover letter samples, occupational outlooks, salary information, job source links, and additional career information.

Online Library

For  $50 annually,  members can enjoy access to more than 440,000 resources, including books, journals, databases and videos, and access to our on-site library. Shipping of books is free.

Social Emotional Learning Professional Development Website for Pre-K-12 Teachers

The College of Education at National University has developed an interactive, engaging, and innovative professional development website that houses digital playbooks and other resources focusing on Social Emotional Learning (SEL) for teachers. Each playbook features in-action scenarios, steps for classroom implementation of SEL, guided reflections, and a place to share ideas and best practices.

National University Merchandise

Check out our collection of NU gear and support your alma mater!

Lifelong Benefits

Enjoy alumni discounts and easy access to your academic records with the following:

Insurance Discounts

Agencies such as AAA, USAA, State Farm, and others offer discounts to members of the Alumni Association (you!). If you need Alumni Association verification, you can fill out this form and will get a letter instantly (registered alumni only).

Course Auditing

Stay current by auditing a National University course at 50 percent off regular tuition. This benefit is offered on a space-available basis, with prior approval from the course instructor.

Extended Learning

Enjoy a 10 percent discount on certain Extended Learning courses. Explore a variety of innovative classes and training seminars designed to give you a competitive edge in the workplace.  

Transcripts

Access your transcripts quickly and easily via the Internet. Ordering and tracking a transcript has never been more convenient.

As an alumnus, National University encourages you to stay connected, remain informed and get involved. If you have any questions, comments or ideas to share, please let us know. We are here to serve you.
