Recruiting

Thank you for your interest in becoming a Cougar at Saint Xavier University and competing on one of our 19 varsity sports! Here at SXU, we are no strangers to success. Academically, Saint Xavier continues to rank in the top 25 percent of best colleges in the Midwest by U.S. News and World Report. And, according to the Chicago Tribune, Cougar Athletics has had "overall sports success impressive for any school at any level."
 
At SXU, we have found a direct correlation between success in the classroom and success in competitive athletics. To ensure this success, our faculty and staff work hard with our student-athletes to thrive in their chosen sport and outside the game.
 
If you are interested in becoming a part of Saint Xavier's tradition of success, please help us learn more about you by completing the following form.

 

Go Cougars!

 

(Please understand that many SXU coaches handle their recruiting personally and not through this online questionnaire, so please do not rely on this form as the only way of contacting our coaching staff.)
