Take Flight: Apply Now

Are you ready to join the Cardinal Family? The UofL application process makes it easy for you to take flight.

With more than 200 degree programs, the University of Louisville is committed to equipping students with the knowledge and resources to be successful and make their own unique contributions to the world. Our application process is easy, and you'll receive an admission decision just a few weeks after applying. We'll do everything we can to help make your dream of attending UofL a reality.

The application for Spring, Summer and Fall 2021 is now open.  Students may apply to UofL via UofL's Take Flight application or via the Common App.
