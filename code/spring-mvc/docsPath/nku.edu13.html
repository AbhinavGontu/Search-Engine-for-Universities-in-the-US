hello there!

Welcome to Northern Kentucky, or NKY as we like to call ourselves. In NKY the South quite literally begins at our front door — where small-town warmth & hospitality go hand-in-hand with big-city amenities and attractions.

Our unique style of South is what shines through… and you’ll find in NKY we do Southern with a bit of German fusion and a whole lot of sass! From how we make our Bourbon, to our vibrant culinary scene and a remarkable amount of things to do, NKY has something for YOU. 

As an Official Gateway to the Kentucky Bourbon Trail and the home of The B-Line as well as the Ark Encounter, we guarantee you a spirited visit.
