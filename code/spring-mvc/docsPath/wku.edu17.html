Membership Matters!

The WKU Alumni Association is dependent on the membership of its alumni to be able to provide the programs and events that you enjoy. When you join the Alumni Association, you are helping to make things happen for yourself and your fellow alumni.

The Alumni Association coordinates many programs and events throughout the year.  Your membership helps keep alumni connected to WKU in fun and exciting ways.

  • Homecoming Activities

  • Topper Tailgate Events

  • WKU Reunions

  • Hall of Distinguished Alumni

  • Young Alumni Events

  • Lifelong Learning Programs

  • Career Connections

  • Topper Travelers Programs

  • And More!   

 


Membership Benefits & Rewards

In appreciation, members of the WKU Alumni Association receive membership rewards and benefits.

Plus, we are continuously looking for new benefits for our WKU Alumni Association members.

                   



  • Permanent membership card for Lifetime members
  • Annual membership card for annual members
  • Alumni Association membership car window decal
  • Discount admission to select alumni events
  • Access to WKU Alumni Association Homecoming activities
  • Discounts at WKU Alumni Association Preferred Lodging Partners
  • Free members only bi-monthly e-newsletter, TOPScoop
  • Free subscription to WKU SPIRIT quarterly magazine
  • Free monthly e-newsletter, News From The Hill

 



A complimentary added benefit with your active WKU Alumni Association Membership

  • Up to 50% off at more than 400 locations! For the first time, WKU Alumni Association Members living anywhere in the U.S. can enjoy discounts at favorite restaurants, retailers, theme parks, travel vendors and more!

 



WKU Alumni Connection App

  • Members receive full access to all WKU Alumni Association mobile phone app functionality. This includes the alumni directory and Member Savings Program.
  • Members also receive a digital membership card in the app—a great way to check-in at Alumni Association events.
  • Download at alumni.wku.edu/app


  • 10% discount online and in-store at The WKU Store


 

  • 10% discount on select Continuing Education Program fees through Division of Extended Learning and Outreach (DELO)

  • Discount to WKU Raymond B. Preston Health and Activities Center memberships

  • WKU Library borrowing privileges

  • WKU email address for life




Through partnerships with Universities, Geico offers special savings on auto, home, renters and other property & casualty products.

 

USI Affinity offers a wide range of products including life, long-term care, health, dental and other personal insurance products.


  • 25% Discount to ESPN The Magazine subscriptions
