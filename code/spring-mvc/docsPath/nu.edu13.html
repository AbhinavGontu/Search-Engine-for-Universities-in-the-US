National University Career Services Online Job Portal

powered by GradLeaders

 

As a student/alumni, you can upload your resume for review, see upcoming events, search/apply for jobs, and make a career advising appointment.

Students

If you are currently a student, log in through your Single Sign On (SSO). Look for the icon below and complete the quick registration process.

 

Alumni 

Sign up for an account through the link below. Registration is easy and can be completed in just 5 minutes.

Alumni Login

 

Appointments

Not only will you be able to search for jobs, but you now have the ability to make appointments with the Career Services Team through our appointment scheduling feature.

  1. Log into the GradLeaders Job Portal and complete the registration process
  2. Click "Advising" on the left hand side and select "Make an Appointment"
  3. Click "Add Appointment" and select the campus closest to you
  4. Choose an available advisor and time slot
  5. Select your advising topic and appointment type
Students - access appointments by signing in through your SSO
Alumni - click the above alumni link
