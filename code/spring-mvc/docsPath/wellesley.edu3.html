    Student Life

    There’s a nearly irresistible energy at Wellesley that brings people and ideas together—from the classroom to the campus and beyond.

    The Office of the Dean of Students collaborates with students, faculty, and staff on all things student life. 

    Most students live on campus in Residence Halls—each Res Hall represents its own welcoming and organic community shaped and governed by the residents themselves. There are a number of dining options, including two student-run café and five dining halls. 

    There are plenty of clubs and student orgs to join—a group for just about any interest you may have.
