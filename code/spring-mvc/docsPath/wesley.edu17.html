The Wesley College Alumni Association, to which all graduates automatically belong, is led by a Board of Directors of up to 15 members. The Board is an elected body of individuals who personally demonstrate their involvement, support and commitment to Wesley College and seek to represent the interests of the greater alumni population.


The Board of Directors includes an Executive Committee consisting of a president, vice president, secretary and treasurer. Board members are elected into classes, which signify the duration of their term.
