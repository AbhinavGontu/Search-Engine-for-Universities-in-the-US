Departments

The Information Technology department is made up of three departments: Computing & Telecommunications Center (CTC), the Integrated Academic Center (IAT) and the Copy & Mail Center. All three groups work closely together to support the campus technology needs.

Computing & Telecommunication Center (CTC)

The CTC office is located in the Consolidated Information Center (CIC) and is staffed from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.

Phone: 372-7334

Email: tchelp@tricity.wsu.edu

Integrated Academic Technology (IAT)

The IAT office is located in Floyd222 and is staffed from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m., Monday through Thursday and Fridays from 7 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Phone: 509-372-7203

Email: iat@tricity.wsu.edu

Printing & Shipping Services

Printing & Shipping Services is located in Floyd 127.

Hours of operation:
Monday – Thursday: 9am – 4pm
Friday: 9am – 3:30pm

Phone: 509-372-7273

Email: tricities.pss@wsu.edu
