Library News

YSU Wellness Days 3/11 & 3/12

Thursday, March 11 and Friday, March 12 are YSU Wellness Days for spring semester. There are no classes and the library is open 8:00am-5:00pm.

Requesting Maag Materials

Due to safety and health protocols, Maag and CRC shelves are closed to browsing, but you can request items through the MaagNET Library Catalog for pickup at Maag or the CRC: Video: How to Request Maag Materials.

Check out materials using your Virtual Y Card in the YSU App (student life section), the YSU Portal (under e-Services), or directly at ycard.ysu.edu: Video: How to Use Your Virtual Y Card at Maag.

Study Spaces @ Maag

Looking for a place to study or engage in remote learning? Check out the various types of Study Spaces @ Maag. Remember to wear your mask at all times, practice physical distancing, keep furniture where it is (including chairs), and eat in the 3rd floor Maag Café area only.

Maag Library and the Curriculum Resource Center in Beeghly Hall are open with health and safety protocols in place to keep you and others safe: See the Maag Library Reopening Announcement for details. Thank you for your cooperation.
