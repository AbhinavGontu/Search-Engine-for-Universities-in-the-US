Campaign Priorities

The University of Oregon's fundraising campaign, already the most ambitious and successful ever undertaken by an organization in the state,
has a goal of $3 billion, built on three key themes.

Access Excellence Experience

You Make the Difference 

Your gifts have created the momentum. Your belief in excellence has inspired our faculty and students. Ducks like you are making a lasting impact through our campaign. Your next gifts will spark the dreams and aspirations of this campus for generations to come.

Finding a Better Way

The UO’s rigorous academic programs are designed to challenge students, share new knowledge, and offer real-world learning opportunities, in a beautiful campus setting that inspires learning. We couldn’t do it without you!

Curious, collaborative, and creative faculty members open students’ minds to the widest range of possibilities. What helps them flourish? Endowed chairs and professorships.
Alumna RoseMarie Beatty is lead donor for the Lyllye Reynolds-Parker Black Cultural Center Scholarship Fund.
Thank you UO donors, for making education innovation possible. The Tom and Carol Williams Fund for Undergraduate Education supported this life-transforming learning opportunity.
