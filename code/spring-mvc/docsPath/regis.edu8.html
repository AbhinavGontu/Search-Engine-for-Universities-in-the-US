About the Collections

The digital collections at Regis University contain a wide variety of open access, digital information in either born digital or digitized formats, including scholarly work by students and faculty of the university, as well as unique, digitized archival materials and institutional records from the Dayton Memorial Library's Archives and Special Collections.

The collections reside in two web-based repositories:

ePublications at Regis University

Scholarly works and journals authored by the Regis community

University Archives & Special Collections

Digitized archival materials and institutional records

Digital Collections

  • Aquilegia: Newsletter of the Colorado Native Plant Society
    This is a collection of the Society's newsletters from 1977 - present.
  • Archives and Special Collections
    Multiple digitized collections from the library's archives.
  • Armand William Forstall, S.J. Collection
    Digitized images from a series of glass negatives and lantern slides. The digitized glass negatives include scenes of Regis University in the 1890s; the digitized lantern slides were teaching aids and focus on people collecting specimens at geological sites.
  • Charles Roitz Photography Collection
    The Charles Roitz Photography Collection was donated by the Roitz family in 2013. The collection consists of approximately 350 original prints from two limited edition retrospective folios and nine specific project compilations.
  • Gerard Manley Hopkins, S.J. Conference
    Past programs from Regis University's Gerard Manley Hopkins, S.J. Conference, an annual gathering celebrating the life and work of this English poet and Jesuit priest.
  • Highlander
    Regis University's student-written newspaper.
  • Noam Chomsky and Family Collection
    The Noam Chomsky and Family Collection features interviews with Noam and Aviva Chomsky on the topics of social movements, labor history, propaganda, immigration, and environmentalism.
  • Regis University Presidential Archive
    This on-going collection documents the public communiqu√©s of Fr. Fitzgibbons as President of Regis University, Denver, Colorado from 2012 on.
  • Richard H. Truly U.S. Space Program Collection - Papers
    Scans of prints and documents from the Truly papers, including photos of the Art and Artifacts and scans of slides donated by Truly to the Library.
  • Richard H. Truly U.S. Space Program Collection - Photographs and Videos
    Scans of prints and documents from the Truly papers, including photos of the Art and Artifacts and scans of slides donated by Truly to the Library.
  • Santo Collection
    Santo, translated literally from Spanish, means saint, although this collection also features other Roman Catholic holy persons and devotional objects.
  • Regis University Theses, Capstones, and Final Projects
    Organized by college and degree program.
  • Regis University Yearbooks
    Regis University Yearbooks from 1920s on.

Open Access Journal

  • Counseling and Family Therapy Scholarship Review
    A peer-reviewed journal focused on promoting insights of diverse cultural-contextual perspectives to stretch the range of information available for the practice of counseling and systemic psychotherapy.
  • Jesuit Higher Education: A Journal (JHE)
    Jesuit Higher Education: A Journal (JHE) is a scholarly, peer reviewed, open access, online journal focused on the development, advancement, and critique of higher education in the Jesuit tradition.
