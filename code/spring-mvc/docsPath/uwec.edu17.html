First Year Experience

Get involved with the Blugold community

First Year Experience helps you make the transition into your college career with ease. There are many ways to get involved and make new friends with classroom experiences, online learning modules, and campus events. There is a lot to learn during your first year, so we're here to help you get all the resources you need to thrive and have a successful First Year Experience!
