Contact / Location

The Office of the University Registrar (UREG) is closed for in-person services in accordance with President Ryan's most current guidance, but our team is ready and eager to support you. The UREG team is available to answer questions and complete requests via email at ureg@virginia.edu or phone at (434) 924-4122 during our operational hours of Monday-Friday from 9:00am - 5:00pm (Tuesdays 10:00am - 5:00pm). Please do not hesitate to contact our office. Thank you.
