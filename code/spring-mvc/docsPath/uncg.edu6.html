Spring 2021 COVID-19 Updates

In accordance with UNCG's general COVID-19 guidelines, the Alumni House will be open from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Monday through Friday during the Spring 2021 semester. We will remain closed on weekends. We are requiring all our guests to enter through the front door on College Avenue (the back door will be locked for security). Members of the Alumni Engagement team will be available from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. should you require their assistance. To ensure your safety during your visit, please call our Administrative Assistant, Madalyn Thomas, to set up a designated time for your arrival: 336-334-5592. We will be glad to welcome you in the front foyer upon your arrival.

Face coverings are required at all times during your visit to the Alumni House. We appreciate your understanding.
