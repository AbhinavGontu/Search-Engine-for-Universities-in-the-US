Graduate Admissions

Education doesn't follow a single path, and neither do careers. Pursue your interests toward new opportunities at Rider with 50+ graduate degrees and certificate programs.

International Admissions

Broncs hail from all over, bringing diverse voices to our welcoming community. You’ll be close to major American cities and scenic destinations as well as new friends and mentors.

Pre-College Programs

High school students can take a step toward achieving their academic and artistic goals in Rider's pre-college summer programs, plus earn a $2,000 annually-renewable scholarship.

Learn More

Continuing Studies

Don’t just go back to school, propel yourself forward at Rider. We understand that an education takes flexibility, so our programs are made to meet the needs of busy adult learners.

Get to Know Us

Undergraduate Virtual Tours

See Rider's 280-acre Lawrenceville, NJ campus and get to know our vibrant community by registering for a virtual tour. Speak with a student tour guide and an admissions counselor, plus view our favorite spots on campus.

Register Now

Transfer Instant Decision Days

Schedule just 30 minutes with a Rider transfer counselor for an instant admission decision and an on-the-spot scholarship award – plus a review of the next steps needed to become a Bronc. See for yourself how we can help you to reach your goals.

Register Now

Graduate Admissions Appointments

Request a one-on-one virtual appointment with a Graduate Admissions counselor to discuss the application process, financial aid options and how to obtain a graduate assistantship or graduate scholarship.
