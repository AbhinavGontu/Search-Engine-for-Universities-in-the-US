Welcome to Conference and Event Services!

Located on the beautiful campus of Oral Roberts University, CES is positioned in South Tulsa within close proximity to hotels, the Tulsa International Airport, downtown Tulsa, and the Arkansas River. Our 100,000+ sq. ft arena and Conference Banquet Center is fashioned with exquisite catering, media resources, and gourmet services, making CES the perfect location for any conference, business gathering, banquet, or special event.

As a member of the International Association of Venue Managers and the Association of Collegiate Conference & Events Directors-International, it is our pleasure to provide you with exemplary service as we assist you in planning and hosting your special event. Our experienced team of event coordinators, audiovisual support team, and in-house caterers exert every effort in providing personal attention to every aspect of your event.

We look forward to providing you the special event that you desire while delivering the exceptional service that you deserve.
