The HawkID is your personal identification used along with your HawkID password to access most online services at the University of Iowa. It is usually made up of some combination of your first, middle and last name. For example, John F Doe might have the ID jfdoe.

A HawkID is also useful for guests, collaborators, contractors, or other external users who need access to University of Iowa systems.
