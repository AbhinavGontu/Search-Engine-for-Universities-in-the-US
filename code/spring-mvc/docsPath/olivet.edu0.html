Explore ONU

Nestled in the Village of Bourbonnais, just 45 miles from Chicago, is our 275-acre main campus. The setting includes park-like landscaping surrounding historic and state-of-the art facilities. With 3,500 students, it's large enough to offer a world-class education, and small enough that you know your fellow schoolmates.

Visit Olivet!

Every visit to Olivet is unique, and our Admissions team is here to help. We want you to plan the day that suits you: campus tour; class visits; professor meetings; financial aid consultation; lunch together- all these are available to you and your family. Purple & Gold Days

An Olivet tradition, Purple & Gold Days are unique, customized group visit days, designed specifically for high school seniors, and their parents. There’s so much to see and do! Customized faculty and student visits, campus tours and class visits, lunch together – there’s no better way to check out Olivet.
