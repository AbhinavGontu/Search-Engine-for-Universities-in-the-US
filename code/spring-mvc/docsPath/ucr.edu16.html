As a UCR Alum, you have the support of your campus community for life. Over 126,000 Highlanders strong, UCR Alumni Association offers career services to engage, support and unite a strong network of successful, influential alumni who can help you jumpstart or advance your career.

Career Center Services

New UCR graduates automatically receive a 1-year extension on all Career Center services (except on-campus interviews). Additionally, all alumni are welcome to attend career workshops and job fairs.
