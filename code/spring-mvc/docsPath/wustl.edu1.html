Give by Phone

Call toll-free: (866) 645-6448 between 8:30 - 5:00 CST Monday - Friday to make your gift over the phone. Washington University accepts Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express.

Give by Mail

To mail your gift, print and complete our Giving Form. Include your credit card payment or check payable to Washington University. Please mail to: MSC 1082, One Brookings Drive, St. Louis, MO 63130.

Matching Gifts

Find out if your employer or company offers a matching gift program and obtain matching gift forms online using the searchable Matching Gift Directory.

For questions or more information about obtaining a company’s matching gift form, please call (877) 215-2727 or email. If needed, provide Washington University Federal Tax Identification Number 43-065-3611
