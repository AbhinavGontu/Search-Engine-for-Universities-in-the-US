Bachelor of Arts with a Major in Biology (BA)

  • Degree Requirements
  • Four-Year Degree Plan

Hours Required

125 semester credit hours (SCH): 45 hours must be advanced, with fulfillment of degree requirements as specified in the "Requirements for Graduation" section of this catalog.
