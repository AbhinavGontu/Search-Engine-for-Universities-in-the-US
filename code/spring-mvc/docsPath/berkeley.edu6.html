Record Increase In Historically Underrepresented Graduate Applicants

While overall graduate applications have increased 19 percent when compared to last year, the number of underrepresented minority (URM) doctoral applicants increased by 42 percent and URM applicants to academic master’s programs increased by 82 percent. More »

UC Berkeley Graduate Division Awards $1.5 Million Grant to Advance Diversity

The Graduate Division recently awarded grants totaling $1.5 million to nine departments within the university through the new Graduate Diversity Pilot Program. The commitment is one of the largest single financial investments toward diversity efforts made by any graduate division in the nation. More »
