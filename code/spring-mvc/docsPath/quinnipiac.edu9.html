Career Development

 

Your Success Defines Our Success

Welcome to Alumni Career Development. Our goal is to provide opportunities for learning and professional development, over the course of your career trajectory.

Whether you're embarking on a job search, seeking a career change or hoping to maintain an edge in a challenging employment market, the alumni center has an extensive array of helpful — and free — resources for Quinnipiac alumni.

We are pleased to announce the development of a new suite of career services for alumni, with exciting programming launching over the next 12 months. There will also be several opportunities to give back to the alumni community through participation in programs and events.

 

Bobcat Connect

Unlock the Power of Your Community

Bobcat Connect is a new, easy-to-use platform for informal mentorship, networking and professional opportunities — designed exclusively for Quinnipiac alumni around the world. It’s a dedicated online network where you can choose to mentor and be mentored through a “flash meeting” or for a longer, sustained period of time.

In Bobcat Connect, alumni can match with others who share similar career fields, geographic locations or interests. Professionals of all levels are encouraged to seek guidance and share knowledge that will help facilitate career decision-making and create pathways to achieving professional goals.

Get connected and start leveraging your QU network today.

Schedule an Appointment

The career development staff offers resources and guidance to help navigate your personal career journey. Please contact Kristina Galligan, alumni career development officer, to schedule a phone, virtual or in-person appointment. Common discussion topics include resume and cover letter reviews, job search strategizing, mock interviews and career transitions.

Premier Webinar Series

Learn from Anywhere

Join your peers in an interactive virtual learning experience on a wide variety of trending career and professional development topics. Webinars are hosted monthly by members of the QU community, including alumni, faculty/staff and employer partners. Participate live by registering for an upcoming webinar or view recordings of previous webinar events.

The Power of Owning Your Career: Women in the Driver Seat

There has been much focus on the power of women in today’s society. We’ve witnessed them taking the helm of major corporations, joining boards, changing the political landscape and more. Are you wondering how you, too can make a bold career move in 2019? Join CEO, author and empowerment coach Simone Morris, ’90 for this special webinar in honor of International Women’s Day and learn how to get in the driver’s seat for your career!

Professional Development

Grow Your Skills & Advance Your Career

Alumni Career Development is partnering with QU Corporate Training to provide educational resources for furthering your professional skillset. Learn at your own pace with self-directed short courses. Courses are free for Quinnipiac alumni with code. Check back monthly for new course releases.

Browse Courses

Featured Course:
Skillful Collaboration in the Workplace

Successful collaboration involves far more than just working side-by-side. It’s a process that requires attitudes, skills, and practices that can be learned and strengthened—and mastering them will not only make your job easier, it will help you become more successful professionally. By working well with others to achieve a common goal, you can become a sought-after resource and team member, and maximize your value to your organization.
