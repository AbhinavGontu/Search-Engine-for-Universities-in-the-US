About Rockhurst Men's Basketball Tip Off Booster Club

About the Tip Off Club

Launched in 2018, the intent of the club is build community among former and current players, coaches, managers, fans, Rockhurst alums, neighbors, and all those connected, in any way, to Rockhurst University Basketball.  Energy and monies generated by the club will benefit the program in various ways, including:

  • Newly introduced Tip Off Social and Season Preview every October
  • Potential travel upgrades and team trips to exclusive tournaments and locations 
  • Updates to locker room to match those of like competitors
  • Creation of an Official Rockhurst Men’s Basketball Record Book
  • Team Shoes and team gear improvements
  • Game Day Experience upgrade and giveaways to assist in boosting home attendance
  • New game jerseys and warmups as needed

Rockhurst University Basketball first formed a team in 1921.  That team was led by Coach Pat Mason, one of the namesakes for our current facility.  Over the course of nearly 100 years, the Hawks have won well over 1,000 games.  The Tip Off Club strives to celebrate the great people that have impacted and enjoyed the program, to improve the conditions for the current team and to help us all understand what exactly it means to wear the Rockhurst jersey.
