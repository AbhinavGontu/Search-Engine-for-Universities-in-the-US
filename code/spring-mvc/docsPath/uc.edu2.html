University of Cincinnati Admissions

Explore how the University of Cincinnati makes your college experience unmatched.

Our beautiful urban campus is home to outstanding academic programs, world-class faculty and successful co-op partnerships around the globe.

At the University of Cincinnati, students come to us with the drive to succeed, we simply give them the tools to excel. Learn what it means to be a Bearcat.
