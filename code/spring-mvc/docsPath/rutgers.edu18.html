Rutgers University–Newark

Our Community Welcomes You

Rutgers University-Newark, the anchor educational institution in and of New Jersey's largest city, is a leader in research and scholarship, fostering excellence at the intersection of academic and civic engagement. With an enrollment of more than 12,800 students, its undergraduate, graduate, professional, and joint degree programs span more than 100 areas of study within seven schools and colleges.
 

Rutgers University–Newark is one of three universities within the Rutgers system. The others are:

Apply Now for Fall 2021 to Rutgers-Newark

Calling all high school seniors and transfer students - now is the time to apply to Rutgers-Newark. 2021 is your year to apply and our undergraduate applications are open. We welcome all students to embark upon this world-class educational journey with us at RU-N. Our community welcomes you. Complete your application and make RU-N your new home! You can apply through our university application portal and via the Coalition application.

Rutgers 2021 Application Is Open & Test Optional

The Rutgers spring and fall 2021 application is available for you now.  We are looking forward to helping you throughout your application process and have informative application workshops, Admissions info sessions and Financial Aid details to share with you. Our university has adopted a standardized tests optional policy for 2021 applicants. Due to the uncertainty of future test dates and availability of standardized tests, Rutgers will make submission of SAT and ACT test scores optional for students applying for spring or fall 2021 admission.
