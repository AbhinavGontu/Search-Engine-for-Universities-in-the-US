Important Dates and Deadlines

AUGUST 13

Fee Payment Deadline: Students who have not paid by 5:00 p.m. will be cancelled from their classes.

AUGUST 17-18

First Day of Classes: August 17. Drop/Add Period:Students may make final schedule adjustments until 5:00 p.m., Tuesday, August 18, 2020.

OCTOBER 1

2021-2022 FAFSA available on the Federal Student Aid website.. 

Southeastern's Federal School Code: 002024 

Please note: The 2020-2021 FAFSA was released for filing on October 1st, 2019 and uses 2018 income and tax data. To file, please visit the Federal Student Aid website. 

Requested Financial Aid Forms can be submitted to the Financial Aid Documents email fadocs@southeastern.edu or faxed to (985) 549-5077.
