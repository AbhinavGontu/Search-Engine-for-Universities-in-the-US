MuKappa, Ladder, and Axis Young Alumni Event

Nominate a Board Member

The Wheaton College Alumni Association is led by an 18-member Board of Directors that represents alumni concerns to Wheaton College. Directors are Wheaton College graduates who serve four-year volunteer terms and meet on campus four times a year. Find out more about Board responsibilities and all alumni are invited to submit nominations for potential Board members.
