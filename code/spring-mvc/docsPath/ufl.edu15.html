Join or Renew

Membership dues directly support the Alumni Association's student and alumni programming: Gator Clubs®, Florida Cicerones, Distinguished Alumni Professors, Outstanding Young Alumni Awards and so much more! The UFAA also provides over $400,000 in scholarships to more than 300 students annually. UFAA membership is truly the best way to support it all and strengthen the value of a UF degree!
