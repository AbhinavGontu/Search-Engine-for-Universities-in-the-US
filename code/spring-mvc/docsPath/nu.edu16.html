Identify Your Strengths

 

We can help you learn more about your personality, interests, and skills with the following assessment tools. Contact the career services office and schedule an appointment for:

Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI)

The purpose of the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator® (MBTI®) personality inventory help individuals understand more about their personality type and identify interests, reactions, values, motivations, and skills. The goal of knowing your personality type is to identify and understand more about your strengths and weaknesses.

The Strong Interest Inventory (SII)

The Strong is an interest inventory used in career assessment. The goal of this assessment is to give insight into a person's interests, so that they may have less difficulty in deciding on an appropriate career choice for themselves.  

TypeFocus

TypeFocus is a personal assessment tool that can help you identify your interests.
