Recent Announcements and How to Contact Us

Prospective students, please review our information for Future Students to find out more about applying for graduate studies at the University of Arizona. If you still have questions about the admissions process and requirements, contact the Graduate Admissions Office at gradadmissions@grad.arizona.edu. You may also wish to reach out to the admissions contact for your prospective program if you have specific questions about the program.

In response to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, the Graduate College is fully functioning from remote locations in compliance with recommendations from the Office of the President. Please communicate with us via email.

Please also see our Coronavirus Information for Graduate & Professional Students, Faculty, and Staff and COVID-19 Resources for Graduate Students.
