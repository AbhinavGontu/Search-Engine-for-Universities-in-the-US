About JerseyConnect

JerseyConnect is the state-wide technology services organization and network infrastructure delivered through the New Jersey State Library, an affiliate of Thomas Edison State University. JerseyConnect builds on the Hub Libraries program, which was established in 1998 to help to bridge the digital divide by providing low cost internet access and related services to public libraries in New Jersey.
