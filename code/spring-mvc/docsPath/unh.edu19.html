Visit Campus
See for yourself.

visit campus

Take a closer look.

UNH puts the health and well-being of our community and fellow and future Wildcats first. Following public health guidelines, most on-campus events have been moved to a virtual format. Limited in-person tours are available and require registration. Learn more about our policy for visiting campus.

Virtual Campus Tour Options

Guided Campus Tours

visit campus

Take a closer look.

There's really no better way to appreciate the opportunities and beautiful scenery at the University of New Hampshire than to experience them in person. We offer a variety of tours and informational sessions for prospective students, families and groups throughout the year. Choose a campus and schedule your visit today. We look forward to seeing you!

Take A Virtual Tour

Experience the University of New Hampshire right here, right now. Take a guided virtual tour through academic buildings, residence halls, facilities and more. Along the way, you’ll learn about our degree programs, student experience, community and what it means to be a Wildcat.

  • 105 Main Street, Durham, NH 03824

    Durham
    UNH Main Campus

    Home to more than 15,000 students, our main campus is located in one of the top 10 safest college towns in the U.S. We are just an hour’s drive from the White Mountains, Atlantic Ocean and Boston.

    Explore Campus

  • 88 Commercial St, Manchester, NH

    Manchester
    UNH City Campus

    Our Manchester campus serves 1,000+ students in New Hampshire’s largest city. UNH Manchester offers bachelor’s and master’s degree programs focused on career-driven learning in the sciences and liberal arts.

    Explore Manchester Campus

  • 5 White St, Concord, NH

    Concord
    UNH FRANKLIN PIERCE School of Law

    A top-100 law school nestled in the heart of New Hampshire’s historic capital, close to the State House, the NH Supreme Court, and many of the state’s major law firms and nonprofits.

    Explore Law School Campus
