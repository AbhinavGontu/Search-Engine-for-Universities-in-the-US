About Our Planned Giving Center

Thank you for your interest in learning more about the benefits of planned giving – for you, for your family, and for Mississippi University for Women.

Planned giving is as easy as (1) making a charitable gift, and (2) saving taxes. We’d like to make it even easier with this introduction to some of the best ways to give — and save.

We hope that this information will be helpful to you and your attorney, accountant, or other professional advisors.  If you have further questions, please give us a call or use the easy “Contact Us” link above.

Thank you for your interest and support.
