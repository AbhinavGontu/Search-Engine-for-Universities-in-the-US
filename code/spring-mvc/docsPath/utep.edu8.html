NOTICE of Operational Procedures - SPRING 2021 semester

Due to the current COVID-19 situation, the Dean's Office academic and administrative services are being conducted remotely. Community spread status will be assessed every two weeks.

Please e-mail your requests to: Engineer@utep.edu.

If you desire to leave a voicemail, please call (915) 747-6444. We will respond to your voicemail within 24 hours.

All appointments will be scheduled via email and will take place via electronic means. All paperwork required will be accepted by email only. We will respond to your email within 48 hours, Monday – Friday.

For more information about UTEP’s Resuming Campus Operations, please visit: https://www.utep.edu/resuming-campus-operations/?home
