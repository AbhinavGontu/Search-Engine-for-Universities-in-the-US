Pathway Programs for International Students
International students who don’t meet the direct entry requirements for a course can study a pathway program at the International Study Center. Students can choose the International Year for undergraduate progression or the Pre-Master’s Program for graduate progression. Upon successful completion, students can progress to degree level study at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi.
