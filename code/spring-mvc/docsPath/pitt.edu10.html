Study Abroad | Study Away | Virtual Experiences

PittGEO offers more than 350 programs in 75 countries, including the US!  Together, we're a team of more than 20 professionals who work with students, faculty, and staff at Pitt and across the globe to help Pitt students find programs that allow them to study subjects that matter, develop as academics, professionals, and citiziens, and engage with local communities in responsible and meaningful ways.

Get Started

The study abroad process can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be.  We've pulled together a ton of information to help get you ready to apply for programs and get your experience started. 

Find a Program

Pitt Study Abroad offers 350 programs in more than 75 countries that are designed to help you get closer to your academic, personal, and professional goals.  Take courses, work in an internship, give back through a service project, and more. 

Fund Your Experience

No matter the prorgam you choose, you'll have expenses to pay.  Scholarships, grants, loans, and personal funds can all contribute to getting you abroad.  

Meet Your Advisor

We know you've got questions about study abroad, so we've got a team of friendly faces that are ready to help you find the right experience.  

Upcoming Events

Check back soon!

There are no events coming up in the next few weeks. The Global Experiences Office offers a range of programming throughout the year, so check back in regularly to see what we have planned.

About

Founded in 1981, PittGEO develops programs and facilitates educational experiences in over 75 countries.

Students can study abroad during the summer term, fall semester, spring semester or during a full academic year.
