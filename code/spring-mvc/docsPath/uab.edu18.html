About the Academic Success Center

The Vulcan Materials Academic Success Center (VMASC) fosters undergraduate student success by creating a supportive environment and delivering academic services that encourage goal achievement and degree completion. Services provided include tutoring, supplemental instruction, academic advising, success advising, workshops and study hours. Stop by and see us in Sterne Library 222, on the second floor near the elevators and center staircase!
