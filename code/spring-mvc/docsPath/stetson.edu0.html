DeLand Campus

Best Regional

Stetson emphasizes active learning with a low undergraduate student/teacher ratio of 13:1. Sixty percent of classes have fewer than 20 students, and no classes have 50 or more students. Half of the first-year students finished in the top quarter of their high school graduating class.

Best 386 Colleges

Stetson University has been recognized as one of the leading undergraduate institutions in the nation by The Princeton Review for the fifth straight year. This honor, given to only about 15 percent of America's four-year colleges, appears in the 2021 edition of "The Best 386 Colleges."

Best Value

Which colleges and universities offer students the best value? The calculation used for this ranking uses a school's academic quality and the net cost of attendance for a student who received financial aid. The higher the quality of the program and the lower the cost, the better the deal.

LOCATIONS

Central Florida is the #1 tourist destination in the world for good reason, and Stetson is perfectly situated in the middle of it all - the attractions, shopping, dining, sunshine and some of the best beaches in the world.

Stetson's historic campus is located in DeLand, Florida. The College of Law is located in Gulfport, Florida and the Law Center is located Tampa, Florida – all along the Central Florida I-4 corridor.
