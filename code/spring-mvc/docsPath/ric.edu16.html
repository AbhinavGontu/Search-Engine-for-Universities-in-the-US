Blackboard Login Procedures

Your Blackboard courses are located at https://blackboard.ric.edu.

 

Faculty

Use your network username and password to log into Blackboard. If you have any issues logging into your Blackboard account, please contact the Help Desk at helpdesk@ric.edu or 456-8803.

Students

Use your network username and password to log into Blackboard. If you have any issues logging into your Blackboard account, please contact the Help Desk at helpdesk@ric.edu or 456-8803.
