TSU Online

Welcome to TSU Online, where we provide education within your reach!

We understand the demands that today’s students face, and our mission is to provide the flexibility along with quality and affordable learning opportunities that will allow our students to accomplish their educational goals.

For more information about TSU Online & our online programs please download our informational flyers below.

My Online Experience



"TSU Online allowed me to participate in the Texas Legislative Internship Program at the Texas State Capital in Austin, TX while remaining a full-time student. I served as a criminal justice policy analyst in the office of State Senator Borris Miles. I am thankful to TSU Online for affording me the opportunity to gain valuable work experience without interrupting my education.”

– Anthony Collier

The Vision

Texas Southern University will become one of the nation’s preeminent comprehensive metropolitan universities. We will be recognized by the excellence of our programs, the quality of our instruction, our innovative research and creative activities, and our commitment to be a contributing partner to our community, state, nation, and world.
