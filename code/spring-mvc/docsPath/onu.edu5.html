2019 Celebration of Polar Bear Athletics

Join us in celebrating Polar Bear Athletics at the 2019 Golf Outing on June 8, 2019.

Click the "Register" button above to sign up to golf, attend dinner, sponsor a hole, or purchase Zoombezi Bay Tickets. 

The ONU Athletic Department takes great pride in its past and has a passion for its promising future. Our main goal is to provide an outstanding experience and first-class environment for all Ohio Northern student-athletes. Our strong tradition of excellence is not possible without the generous financial support of our alumni and the many supporters of our student-athletes.

Schedule of Events:
1 p.m. Check in
2 p.m. Shotgun Start
Dinner immediately following golf
7:30 p.m. Awards and guest speaker
9 p.m. end of event

 Activities:
Round of golf and dinner - $125
Dinner only - $35
Hole sponsorship - $100
Zoombezi Bay tickets - $25
