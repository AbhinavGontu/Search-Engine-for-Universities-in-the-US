  • CORONAVIRUS (COVID-19) UPDATES

    Click here to learn more

    As the coronavirus (COVID-19) continues to have an impact on our communities, the University of North Texas is taking steps to help keep our Mean Green family safe and healthy. Based on our deep concern for the possible risk that travel and assembling in crowded spaces may create, we have made the decision to postpone upcoming events through summer 2020.

    Thank you for your understanding. Please visit healthalerts.unt.edu for details regarding UNT’s response and preparedness.

    If you wish to make an immediate gift to support students facing financial strain during these challenging times, please visit one.unt.edu/untcares.
