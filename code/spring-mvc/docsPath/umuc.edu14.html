Alumni Benefits

UMGC alumni could receive a special discount on GEICO auto insurance. GEICO offers great rates, exceptional 24-hour service, fast fair claims handling and high quality auto insurance to over 18 million policyholders across the United States. In addition to auto insurance, GEICO can also help insure your motorcycle and the GEICO Insurance Agency can help you with homeowners, renters, condo, boat insurance and more.  

Visit geico.com/alum/umgc, call 1-800-368-2734, or simply stop by a local GEICO office to find out how much you could save today! (Be sure to mention your affiliation with UMGC Alumni to be eligible for the special savings.) For those facing financial hardship, please visit geico.com/about/coronavirus to learn more on how GEICO can help. 

Alumni Benefits

See the lifetime complimentary benefits available to you as a UMGC alumni. This includes access to Career Services, exclusive discounts, alumni programming, networking opportunities, and much more. Watch now!

Discount Program

Join your UMGC Alumni Discount Program to save up to 50 percent off of top brands, retailers, restaurants, and more. Simply search by zip code or by type of discount either online or through the mobile app and start saving immediately.

Revature

The need for technology coding skills is not limited to Computer Science majors. Revature offers self-paced, online programs to help enhance software and coding skills employers want. This resource is available to UMGC alumni at no charge.

 


Education Offerings

UMGC is proud to work in partnership with The University System of Maryland and edX, the non-profit online learning destination founded by Harvard and MIT, to offer five MicroMaster programs. Click to learn more about these graduate level courses.
