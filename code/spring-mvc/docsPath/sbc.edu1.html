featured partnership

Virginia Center for the Creative Arts

The long-standing relationship between Sweet Briar and the Virginia Center for the Creative Arts includes events, seminars and classes. One such class is The Fellows Studio, which is a part of Sweet Briar’s leadership core curriculum and brings VCCA Fellows to campus to teach a class during each of the College’s three-week sessions.

Learn more about the VCCA
areas of focus

The Center for Creativity, Design and the Arts serves as the fulcrum for public discourse about art and creativity through forums, lectures, exhibitions, artists’ studio tours and colloquia open to the public. The Center leads curriculum innovation in the arts and creativity across the disciplines and supports the work of artists through fellowships and connections with students.
