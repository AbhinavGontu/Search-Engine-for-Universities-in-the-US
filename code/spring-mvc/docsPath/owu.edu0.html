The OWU Connection

Ohio Wesleyan gives you the support, resources, and guidance to help you become who you want to be. Your OWU Connection pathway will be as unique as you are.

THINK BIG. Span academic disciplines to explore important issues and conduct mentored research with great teachers.

GO GLOBAL. Engage in classes and unique projects from New York to Japan, Alaska to the Galapagos Islands.

GET REAL. Connect theory to practice through internships and real-world experiences.
