Faculty focus on research

VMI's award-winning faculty members are known for their expertise, mentorship, commitment to service, and encouragement of undergraduate research. The latest Institute Report highlights the strength of the faculty, not only as educators, but as researchers contributing to their fields. Topics range from social theater to philosophy, to grit, fit and gender, and even to the effects of beetroot juice on energy cost.

VMI Community Updates

Institute leadership continues to communicate important information for cadets, their families, faculty & staff, and the VMI community at-large:

  • The Spring 2021 page has been updated with athletics-related guidance on building access.
  • Post remains closed to the general public. Home sporting events will have their own regulations regarding spectators, and the most up-to-date information can always be found on vmikeydets.com.
  • Registration for virtual open house events for March 20 and March 27 is now open for all high school students interested in learning about the benefits of a VMI education.
  • Construction projects are ongoing and visitors to the area surrounding VMI are encouraged to note all posted warnings and notices.
  • The latest Institute Report is now available for viewing and download. Stories include faculty research, cadet awards, rising to COVID challenges, and more.
  • The Superintendent Search Committee will meet at 12:30 p.m. on Monday, March 22, 2021. A public notice, including information for registering for public comment, along with an agenda are available now. The meeting will also be livestreamed on the same Timeline and Meeting Schedule page.

Thank you for your support of Keydet Scholarships

In a year when challenges have been numerous, VMI’s cadet-athletes have put in the work to be successful on and off the field. With your gifts, they can Compete to Win. Thank you to all who donated during the recent campaign and to those who continue to support these cadet-athletes on their journey at VMI.

News from Post

PE Professor to Speak on With Good Reason

Maj. Dan Baur, assistant professor of physical education at Virginia Military Institute, will discuss the use of hydrogel technology by elite athletes on the public radio program With Good Reason March 20-26.

VMI Supports Community Vaccination Effort

VMI is assisting with setup and staffing of a mass vaccination effort against COVID-19, supporting vaccine administration at the former Peebles/Gordmans building located at 770 North Lee Highway in the College Square Shopping Center.

‘Something I Hold as a Privilege’ - Shahbaz Heads to National Stage in Cross Country

When looking at colleges, Cadet Jahanzib Shahbaz ’21 was drawn to the challenging atmosphere of VMI. His diligence and hard work at the Institute has led to his qualification for the NCAA Cross Country Championships meet.
