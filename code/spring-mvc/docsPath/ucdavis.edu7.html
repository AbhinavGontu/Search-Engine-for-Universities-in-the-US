Your contacts for the UC Davis TAG

Each California community college has one counselor and one UC Davis advisor who serve as primary contacts for the UC Davis Transfer Admission Guarantee (TAG). Students should work directly with their Community College Contact listed below for guidance on applying for a UC Davis TAG. Community college contacts should work with their school's assigned UC Davis Contact listed below regarding course availability and any other matters related to the TAG program.
