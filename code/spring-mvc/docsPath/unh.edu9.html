    Group Wellness Coaching (5-Week Group)

    Group Wellness Coaching (5-Week Group)

    Wellness coaching is an affirming and supportive process that recognizes you as the expert of your own life. Join a supportive and fun 5-week group to better understand your personal wellness, and how to use your strengths and values to make choices that enhance your personal and academic success. In the group you will learn about wellness, get clear about your values, understand your strengths, create your own wellness vision, and set goals that are realistic and attainable. Register here.

    Led by Dawn Zitney and Shannon Seiferth, certified wellness coaches.

    From Friday, March 12, 2021 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM EST at Online.

  • Mar
    12

    Life in Action: Ice Fishing

    Life in Action: Ice Fishing

    Life in Action is a free recreation therapy program that runs weekly for a 10 week series. Each weeks events will take place in a different location on campus. Life in Action is facilitated by Northeast Passage program specialists who use purposeful recreation engagement to support overall health wellness.   

    If you are searching for opportunities to engage in a community, socialize, create meaning, and utilize recreation participation safely come check out this program! Throughout 10 weeks we will be offering a variety of opportunities to explore, talk about, and create health wellness through structured recreation therapy events.   

    This week we will be Ice Fishing at the Durham Reservoir behind the Gables. If you can't attend for the start feel free to register and come when you can. Email nicky.davis@unh.edu if you plan to attend late.  

    **IMPORTANT: If you register and you can no longer can attend PLEASE email nicky.davis@unh.edu or de-register. Some sessions will have limited availability or are equipment heavy and we want to be accurately prepared. Thank you! 

    From Friday, March 12, 2021 2:00 PM to 3:30 PM EST at Durham Reservoir, bridge behind the Gables.

  • Mar
    12

    Sci-Fi Movie Night

    Sci-Fi Movie Night

    Join us for some crafting while we watch Back to the Future! Please remember your face covering and to maintain social distancing at all times during the event. A valid Wildcat Pass is required for entry.

    This event has a capacity of 14 people to maintain social distancing (on a first come, first serve basis).

    Please note the following attendance requirements regarding COVID-19. 

    You must participate in the UNH COVID-19 testing program. You may not attend if you have travelled by plane, train, or bus within the past 14 days. You may not attend if you have had any close contacts (10 minutes of contact or greater, closer than 6 feet regardless of mask wearing) with anyone with confirmed or suspected COVID-19. You may not attend if you have had a fever or chills in the past 72 hours without the aid of a fever-reducing medicine like Tylenol (acetaminophen) or Advil (ibuprofen) You may not attend if you have any of the following symptoms: new onset of cough, shortness of breath, muscle pain, sore throat, loss of taste/smell, and/or atypical headache

    From Friday, March 12, 2021 4:00 PM to 6:15 PM EST at MUB 332.
