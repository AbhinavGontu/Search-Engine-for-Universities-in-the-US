University of Rhode Island

Apply for first-yearApply for transfer

    Located on a stunning campus which has been named the "most beautiful coastal campus" in the eastern US, we are a medium-sized university offering more than 90 majors. As Rhode Island's flagship public research university, we offer an outstanding education at an affordable price. Our students say that URI feels "smaller" than it is; the faculty-student ratio is 16:1 and our community is welcoming and friendly. Our curriculum is infused with experiential learning, a global perspective, and opportunities to mix majors and minors. Visit us and discover why so many students place URI at the top of their college consideration list!

    First-year

    You are considered a first-year student applicant if you are: a first-time college student who has graduated from high school and never enrolled in college a first-time college student who has been home-schooled a first-time college student who has attended high school and college during the same time period (dual enrollment) or has taken some college courses during high school

    Transfer

    A transfer applicant is a student who has attended another regionally accredited college or university in any capacity after graduating from high school, regardless of whether any credits have actually been earned.

    Additional Information

    We offer merit scholarships ranging up to full-tuition and room and board. Submit your Common Application by our Early Action deadline of 12/1 for full consideration for these scholarships. No separate application required!
