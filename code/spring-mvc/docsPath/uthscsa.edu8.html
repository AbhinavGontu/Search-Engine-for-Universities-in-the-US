TechZone

The TechZone is a great resource for purchasing computer equipment and peripherals. We will help you get what you need! At the TechZone, you can "test drive" computers, check product specification sheets, and take advantage of HSC pricing on Apple and Dell products. We also carry computer and printer accessories. Apple and Dell warranty service is available. All Microsoft Products can be purchased from the Bookstore. Have any questions? Please check out our FAQ.

Contact Information:

  • Brittany Huff, 210-567-2833
  • Email orders to TechZone@uthscsa.edu

Requirements: Must be an HSC employee, student or retiree

Restrictions: We accept credit cards and checks only (no cash). Product availability is subject to change.

Time Constraints: Conduct business only during hours of operation:

  • Monday - Thursday 8:00 A.M. - 5:00 P.M.
  • Friday 8:00 A.M. - 4:00 P.M..
  • Inventory closures, usually last working day of the month
