Homecoming 2020


SDSU’s annual homecoming football game has been set for 1 p.m. Saturday, November 14 when the Aztecs take on the University of Hawaii at Dignity Health Sports Park in Carson, CA. Per California's COVID-19 industry guidance for institutions of higher education, and the specific interim guidance for Collegiate Athletics, spectators are not allowed.



For now, no fans are allowed at the stadium, but there are other ways to show your support! Donate $20 - the cost of a Homecoming game ticket - to help SDSU student-athletes compete at the highest level, both on the field and in the classroom.
