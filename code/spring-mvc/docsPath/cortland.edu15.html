Join us for an Alumni Adventure

Most alumni events listed on the calendar have an online registration form to RSVP. Alumni may also choose to call Alumni Engagement to register over the phone at 607-753-2516. Alumni Engagement also publishes a monthly e-newsletter with events sponsored by the Alumni Association. Contact the team with any questions about alumni events at alumni@cortland.edu. If you missed a virtual program visit our Red Dragons Go Virtual page.

Please note that as most events involve catering, we can not refund after the event RSVP deadline.
