Give The Gift of Membership!


 

If you’re having trouble finding that special gift for someone that loves WKU, we have the perfect solution for you.

 

Give the gift of a WKU Alumni Association membership. You already know the value of being a member in the WKU Alumni Association. Here’s an opportunity to surprise someone with a unique WKU gift. You’ll not only have pride in knowing you are helping the Alumni Association but you’ll also help keep someone connected to WKU.

 

Just click the link below and complete the online form. It’s that easy.

 

Give the gift of a WKU Alumni Association membership.
