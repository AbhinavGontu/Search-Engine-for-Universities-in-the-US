BBVA Ticket Office
The Ticket Office is located on the north side of the stadium across from Section 118 on Texas Street at Bastrop Street. The ticket office is open Monday-Friday as well as on event days.

Ticket Office Hours:
Monday - Friday 9 a.m. - 5 p.m.
Saturday Closed except event days
Sunday Closed except event days
Event Days Times vary. Please call the switchboard at 713-547-3000 for information.

* Texas Southern Students with valid TSU IDs are admitted without charge.

For more ticket information, call 713-313-4355.

Tickets for all home football and basketball games can also be purchased in the TSU Campus Bookstore, Monday-Friday, from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Please feel free to contact the Bookstore at 713-313-7030.

Individual Football Game Tickets
Individual ticket prices range from $15 to $53, depending upon seat location. TSU Athletics utilizes the automated (computerized) ticketing system AXS. With the online system TSU alumni and fans have the ability to login to purchase tickets as well as have the option to print an e-ticket. Online tickets can be purchased through www.tsusports.com (go to the football schedule) or visit the following link: Individual Game Tickets.

Tickets can also be purchased at the box office at BBVA Stadium.
