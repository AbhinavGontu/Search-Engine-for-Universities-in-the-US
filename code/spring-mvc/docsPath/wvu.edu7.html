Request Transcript

WVU Transcript

A transcript is a complete record of a student’s enrollment at WVU. This includes all undergraduate, graduate, and professional courses. Partial transcripts are not available. In addition, WVU will not provide customers with copies of another institution’s transcript that we may have in our possession.

All financial obligations to the University must be cleared before transcripts can be released. Transcripts may not be picked up by another party unless the student has given written authorization with the request. The designated person will be expected to show a picture I.D. upon collection.

Transcripts ordered for pickup that are not collected within one month of the specified pick up date will be destroyed, requiring a new order to be submitted.

How To View or Request Transcripts

Current Students:

For current students to view their unofficial transcript, please log into STAR. To order official transcripts, complete the WVU Transcript Request Survey then follow the instructions provided at the end of the survey.  

Former Students and Alumni:

For former students to view their unofficial transcript, please go directly to star.wvu.edu to log in. To order official transcripts, former students and alumni may order transcripts through Credentials Solutions.

Pricing

Official Transcript via mail or secure PDF*: $12
Priority Official Transcript (pick-up only) $18

*If dates of attendance were prior to the Fall of 1997, the PDF option is not available.

Personal checks or money orders from outside of the United States will not be accepted. Please use Western Union money orders or contact us for more information.

You may check on the status of your order on the TranscriptsPlus website.
