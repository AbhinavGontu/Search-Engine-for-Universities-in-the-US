The Mission Statement for the Registrar's Office is to support the success of colleges and students in their commitment to academic excellence through outstanding service.

First, the Registrar's Office has goals aimed at supporting the instructional mission for undergraduate, graduate, postgraduate, professional, and lifelong education.

Second, we support research efforts through Data Management. Indirectly, data generated through this division helps to support research initiatives across the University related to academic issues, enrollment, retention, and graduation concerns.

And third, we provide service to alumni and the public sector in a variety of areas.
