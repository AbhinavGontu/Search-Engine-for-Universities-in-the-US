SMC WOMEN'S SOCCER CAMPS

OFFICIAL CAMP WEBSITE OF | SMC Women's Soccer Camps

 

SMC Women's Soccer Camps & Clinics operate within eight core values and are the main building blocks of our youth, residential and college ID camps. The camp core values include: Academic Excellence, Leadership, Community Service, Competitiveness, Teamwork, Fitness, Sportsmanship and Continuous Improvement.

Saint Mary's College is very fortunate to have some of the finest collegiate coaches in Northern California. Head Coach Theresa Romagnolo hand selects and trains her staff, composed of top-level coaches and collegiate players. All instructors have been chosen for their extensive knowledge of the game of soccer and their enthusiasm to work with children and young athletes.
