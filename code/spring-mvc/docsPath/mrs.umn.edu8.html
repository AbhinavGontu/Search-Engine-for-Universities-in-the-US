Academics

At UMN Morris you’ll study a wide range of subjects and strengthen your ability to think logically, speak well, and connect ideas that lead to your passion.

You’ll receive a personalized liberal arts education, enhanced by faculty interaction and an exuberant exchange of ideas between you, your professors, and your classmates.

True to its liberal arts mission, the University of Minnesota Morris provides outstanding academic programs. UMN Morris students choose from 34 majors and 35 minors, 13 licensure areas, and nine pre-professional programs in education, the humanities, science and mathematics, and the social sciences.
