Virtual Reunion Schedule 2021

  • Home
  • Events & Programs

  • Virtual Reunion Schedule 2021

More details and events to come!  

 

Wednesday, May 12, 2021

7:30 p.m. EDT |  Learning by the Lake, History of the Wells College Alumnae and Alumni Association (WCA): Presented by WCA Chair Amy McNaughton '86 and Fran Trubilla Kissell '78 


 Tuesday, June 1, 2021

7:30 p.m. EDT |  Cabinet Update, Virtual capacity is 300- Registration required. Recording will be made available to all.

 

Wednesday, June 2, 2021

7:30 p.m. EDT |  Reunion College 2021, Preserving the Personal Connection as We Accelerate into New Ways of Learning. Virtual capacity is 300- Registration required. Recording will be made available to all.

 

Thursday, June 3, 2021

7:30 p.m. EDT |  Reunion College 2021, Preserving the Personal Connection as We Accelerate into New Ways of Learning. Virtual capacity is 300- Registration required. Recording will be made available to all.

 

Friday, June 4, 2021

7:30 p.m. EDT | Toast to WCA Award Recipients and Young Alumni Award Recipient, Invitation Only.

2020 Recipients: Gwen Wilkinson '77 and Stephanie Batcheller '79

2021 Recipients: Louise Mackie '61

2020 Young Alumni Award: Katie Fong '06

  

Saturday, June 5, 2021

10:00-11:00 a.m. EDT |  "Ask a Current Student!" Hour, Nash '23 is a current admissions tour guide & Oddline Songleader! They will have a Q&A about admissions tours and other information about student life. Virtual capacity is 300-Registration required.

11:00 a.m. EDT | Bell Ringing, Prerecorded. Sarah Viele McLean '92 returns to Wells to ring the bells. 

2:00 p.m. EDT | WCA Annual Meeting, Virtual capacity is 300- Registration required.

8:00 p.m.EDT | Reunion Trivia, Put on your thinking cap- or beanie or freshman sign- and join your classmates in some fierce & fun competition! Virtual capacity is 300-Registration required.
