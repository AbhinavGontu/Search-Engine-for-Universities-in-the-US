Petco Park Vaccine Site to Close After Saturday

​If you’re due for your second dose of Moderna vaccine at Petco Park, please make sure your appointment is scheduled this week by March 20. Please check your email or MyUCSDChart for your appointment date and time. If you don't have one, call 800-926-8273 or 211.

Expert Care for Chronic COVID-19 Complications

​Some people may experience prolonged complications from COVID-19 that last for weeks or even months. Our new clinic treats “long haul” symptoms of COVID-19.

COVID-19 Test Results in 48 Hours

You can now get your approved Hawaii travel test at five convenient drive-up locations. We also offer tests for school or work for people of all ages.
