Alumni Reunion

Dec. 9, 2020

Alumni Reunion 2021 Postponement

We are following our campus policy, that aligns with New York State's policy on large events and public gatherings, that "no outside speakers, alumni, or guests of on campus residents (including parents of the residents) will be allowed in campus buildings." This was a policy set for the fall 2020 semester, and will likely remain in place for the spring 2021 semester. It takes time to plan and coordinate a successful Reunion, so our team felt we needed to make the call now. Health and safety remains SUNY Cortland's highest priority. Visit the university's Fall 2020 Campus Guide for the latest information. Please contact the Alumni Engagement office with any questions or concerns at alumni@cortland.edu or 607-753-2516. 

Reunion 2021 may have been postponed, but you can still:

  • Save the date and mark your calendar for Alumni Reunion 2022, July 14 to 17
  • Keep an eye out for information regarding this summerâ€™s virtual reunion programming, or check out all of our virtual alumni programming, on our Alumni Adventures events listing
  • Participate in the Reunion Giving competition and support SUNY Cortland by making a reunion gift
  • Nominate a classmate for an alumni award
  • View future reunions to save the date for upcoming years
