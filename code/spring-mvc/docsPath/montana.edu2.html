Announcements

The All Staff Council is now accepting applicants for the All Staff Scholarship program. The application window is Feb. 15 through March 30.

Academic Technology and Outreach has extended its deadline for proposals for the creation of non-credit professional development courses for 2021. Proposals will now be accepted on a rolling basis.

Registration is now open for MSU’s Rural Journalism Camp, to be held July 11–16 on MSU’s campus. Applications are due by May 26.

MSU Outreach and Engagement Council is now accepting proposals for its Seed Grant Program. The deadline for applications is April 5.

Registration is open for the inaugural Soil Health Innovations Conference hosted by the National Center for Appropriate Technology. The conference is set for March 8-9.

University Communications has made a selection of COVID-19-related graphics and posters available for download, including social distancing and face mask fliers.
