Your annual gift to Mississippi College makes you an active member of the Alumni Association.

Membership benefits include:

  • opportunities to meet and interact with other alumni through nationwide chapters.
  • invitations to special events on campus and in your local area.
  • career planning and employment services through a partnership between the MC Alumni Association and MC Career Services. Alumni can participate in resume critiques, on-campus interviews, mock interviews, seminars, and training.
  • continuing education courses that offer lifelong learning opportunities and enrichment experiences to the community.
  • special travel programs.
  • opportunities to hold local or national offices in the Alumni Association.
  • subscription to Mississippi College's Beacon alumni magazine.

Share your voice.

As a friend of MC, you could share the stories of our campus, alumni, and students to your friends and family to let the world know about the great things happening here. Sign up for our shareMC email that will keep you updated and give you chance to lend MC your voice to the world.
