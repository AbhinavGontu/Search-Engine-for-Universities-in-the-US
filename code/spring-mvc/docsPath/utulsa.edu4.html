Whether you are a TU student, faculty, or staff, The Center for Global Engagement offers rich opportunities to engage with the global community.

Global Opportunities

In an increasingly digitally connected world, engaging with the international community has become a key piece of the American college experience. College graduates who possess the personal, academic and professional skills that are gained through international experiences are at the forefront of their fields, especially in competitive job markets.
