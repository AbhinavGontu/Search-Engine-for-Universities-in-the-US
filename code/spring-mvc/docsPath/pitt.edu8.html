  • Public Transportation

    Get shopping! Port Authority provides transportation around Oakland and Pittsburgh 7 days a week, including limited service on holidays. Interactive bus schedules are available online.

  • Safe Travel After Hours

    SafeRider provides safe transportation during the evening and early morning hours when special, nonemergency needs arise. 

  • Submit a Maintenance Request

    To report a maintenance issue, please submit a request online. For emergency repairs, call Panther Central at 412-648-1100. Submit a laundry machine maintenance request online with CSC ServiceWorks.

  • Deposit Panther Funds

    You or your parent can load funds to your card fast and easy. Panther Funds are a safe, convenient alternative to carrying cash. 

  • Panther Card Photo Upload

    Your Panther Card (Pitt ID) is the tool that every student, faculty, and staff member needs on their first day. Submit your photo to us in advance and allow us to preprint your Pitt ID.

  • Buses & Shuttles

    Pitt's Parking & Transportation Services helps you get around campus, the city, and home for the holidays!
