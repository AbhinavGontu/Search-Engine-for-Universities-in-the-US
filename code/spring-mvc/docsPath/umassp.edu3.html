UMass Lowell

Coburn Hall

Coburn Hall Addition and Renovation – This iconic building is the centerpiece of UMass Lowell’s South Campus. This project provided for the renovation and addition to its oldest and most architecturally significant buildings. The scope addressed much needed deferred maintenance and includes new building systems, code upgrades, accessibility improvements, classrooms, faculty offices and department suites.
