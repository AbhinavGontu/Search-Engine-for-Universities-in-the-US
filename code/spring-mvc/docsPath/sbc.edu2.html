Sweet Briar College
Personal, not virtual

An in-person visit is a great way to get to know a college better, but it’s not always easy to visit in person. We know you need to see more of the college where you might be spending the next four years of your life, and that you want to be able to picture yourself in your dorm room, walking across campus, or spending time with friends in classes, at the cafe, or at the gym. Let us show you around with a personalized virtual tour, designed to answer your questions and tailored just for you.

Let’s get to know each other.

We can’t wait to meet you in person. But until that’s possible, you still have options to get to know Sweet Briar College and the students, faculty and staff who contribute to our vibrant community. Whether you follow our social media updates, communicate with us directly by phone, text message or email, meet your future classmates with ZeeMee, or let one of our admissions counselors show you around in a personalized virtual tour, we’re here for you – even if we’re miles apart.

Take a Personalized Virtual Tour

We hope you’ll be able to visit in person soon, but until then a personalized virtual tour is a great way for you to learn more about Sweet Briar, answer some of your questions, and get to know us a little bit, while we get to know you too.

We have three virtual campus tours each week: Tuesdays at 9 a.m. EST, Tuesdays at 7 p.m. EST, and Thursdays at 5 p.m. EST. We hope one of these virtual sessions will fit in your schedule.

Equestrians: We are offering virtual tours of our riding center on Tuesdays at 10 a.m. EST and Thursdays at 6 p.m. EST. These special tours will be given by a Sweet Briar riding instructor. Just use the same link for the virtual campus tour and our instructors will meet you there!
