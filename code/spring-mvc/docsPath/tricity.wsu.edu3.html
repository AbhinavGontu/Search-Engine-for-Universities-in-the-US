Virtual Assistant

Get Virtual Assistance

Many student services are being offered through Zoom virtual desks. To connect with a staff member in a department choose from the options below. When you select a department you will be put into a waiting room and a staff member will join to help you as soon as possible.

Zoom can be utilized from a computer, phone, or tablet. For more information and help videos visit Zoom.us/resources.

If you know who you would like to contact, you may do so calling or emailing them directly via the directory.

If you would like to schedule an appointment with any department in Student Affairs you can do so by booking an appointment online.

Instructions for joining a Virtual Desk

Users will need to download and install the Zoom Client for Meetings.
You will need to have a registered Zoom account, either through WSU or a free account Zoom account.

Zoom Client for Meetings
