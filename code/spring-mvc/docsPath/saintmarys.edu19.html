Spirituality Retreat

  Saint Mary's College Alumnae Online Retreat

Spirituality During a Pandemic: Challenge or Awakening?

Date: June 27, 2020
Time: 1:00pm-4:00pm ET
Platform: Zoom

Register Here!

Come back to Saint Mary's College for the sixth annual alumnae retreat. This year's theme invites participants to reflect on their relationship with the Church, however that’s experienced, and to embrace hope during this time in our history. Together we will explore resources for discovering spiritual consolation and renewed commitment to our faith.

Description: As we continue to shelter in our homes and stay socially distant from other people, we may be experiencing some of the qualities of a monastic life. Aspects of monasticism such as solitude, honoring the rhythm of days, sacred reading, and dedicated prayer have the potential to provide us ways to understand and direct our longings during this time. Is this a time of challenge or awakening for you? Join in this virtual retreat led by staff from Campus Ministry and the Center for Spirituality to explore the spiritual gifts of this time of pandemic. We will read selections from In God’s Holy Light: Wisdom from the Desert Monastics by Joan Chittister and the article “We’re all monks now,” by Gregory Hillis in order to reflect on our current experiences of God through silence, prayer, solitude, community, and scripture. There will be prayer, large group discussion, a break for alone time, and small group discussion.


​Cosponsored by the Center for Spirituality, Campus Ministry, Office of Mission, and the Office of Alumnae Relations
