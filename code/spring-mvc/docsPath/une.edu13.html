Sports Information

We are UNE, the University of New England, and the Nor'easters. [exactly as shown here]
  
University of New England Sports Information is responsible for the internal and external promotion of the institution's 18 varsity sports.

To Opponents
All requests for rosters, logos, photographs, or any other information should be directed to Tristan Durgin, Director of Sports Information. Any SID planning to accompany a visiting team to UNE are asked to give at least 24 hours advanced notice, to confirm availability for proper credentials, seating, and resources.
  
To Media / Photographers
All requests for an interview of an student-athlete, coach, or administrator should be directed to Tristan Durgin, Director of Sports Information. Any media or photographer planning to attend an event at UNE are asked to give at least 24 hours advanced notice, to confirm availability for proper credentials, seating, and resources. Photographers are also asked to coordinate with an on-site administrator at least 15 minutes prior to an event, to establish the acceptable working locations.

Live Video
Through a partnership with BoxCast, UNE provides free live video coverage for all the home contests in the following sports: Men's Basketball, Women's Basketball, Field Hockey, Football, Men's Ice Hockey, Women's Ice Hockey, Men's Lacrosse, Women's Lacrosse, Women's Rugby, Men's Soccer, Women's Soccer, Softball, Women's Swimming, and Women's Volleyball.

Live Statistics
Through a partnership with SIDEARM Sports, UNE provides real-time competition statistics for all the home contests in the following sports: Men's Basketball, Women's Basketball, Field Hockey, Football, Men's Ice Hockey, Women's Ice Hockey, Men's Lacrosse, Women's Lacrosse, Men's Soccer, Women's Soccer, Softball, and Women's Volleyball.

Photo Galleries
UNE, and a partnership with David Bates Photo, provides photo galleries for select contests in all sports, as well as other events involving Athletics. View the selections HERE.

Social Media
UNE maintains social media accounts on Instagram (unesports), Facebook (UNE.Athletics), Twitter (unesports), and YouTube (UNEsports). Social media accounts for any individual sport within Athletics are managed by representatives of those programs, and are linked to the respective team pages.
