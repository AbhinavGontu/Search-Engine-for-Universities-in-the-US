Note to referring physicians and patients of SIU Medicine:
The SIU Call Center provides a direct connection to our primary care physicians, and services and is available from 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.(CT), Monday through Friday. When calling within the 217 area code in Illinois, dial 217-545-8000. When calling outside the 217 area code, dial 1-800 DIAL SIU (1-800-342-5748). Looking to find a Doctor? Try our Doctor Search »

Patients of SIU Medicine
Email communication between patients and our physicians and staff is not a standard practice, so should not be used. Specific clinical inquiries sent via email will not be answered, unless prior arrangements have been made; those communications should only be of a non-emergency nature.

Updates & Corrections
Directory information is based on official School of Medicine information received from Human Resources (name, phone number, department, location, mail code) and Information Technology (e-mail). Changes to the entries should be reported to Human Resources and Information Technology to ensure that all records are accurate. Errors should be submitted to jiffy_updates@siumed.edu.
