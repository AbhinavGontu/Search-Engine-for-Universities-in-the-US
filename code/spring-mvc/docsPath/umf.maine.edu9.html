Office Manager Position Available 

Gold LEAF Institute Senior College at the University of Maine Farmington (UMF)
is accepting applications ​for the Office Manager position. 
This is a part-time position involving up to 500 hours per year.  
​(The current number of hours are less in light of the current pandemic.)

The position requires strong communication, organizational, and computer skills. Candidates
need to be proficient in the use of MS Word, Excel, Publisher, and Google Drive.
 
This is an independent contractor/consultant position under contract with the University of Maine System.
The Office Manager has day-to-day responsibilities running all aspects of the Gold LEAF office
​as outlined in the Office Manual, supervising up to two part-time student assistants during the school year,
and working with the Executive Board and committees. 
 
​
To apply, send a letter of interest and resume to goldleaf@maine.edu or to
​Gold LEAF Institute, University of Maine Farmington, 186 High Street Room 318, Farmington, ME 04938.

Gold LEAF - which is an acronym for Lifelong Education At Farmington - is a member-run organization devoted to lifelong learning in the Franklin County, Maine area, for anyone age 50 or older (and spouse/partner, regardless of age) that offers:​​​
  • Courses that require no educational pre-qualifications;
  • Learning without stress, without tests or credits;
  • Intellectually stimulating classes and activities reflecting the interests of its members;
  • Socializing with people with a similar interest in enhancing their knowledge of the world around them;
  • Affiliation with Road Scholar (formerly called Elderhostel);
  • Affiliation with the University of Maine at Farmington, allowing access to UMF's faculty and facilities.
 ​
​Gold LEAF Institute is one of 17 senior colleges in the state of Maine. To learn more about the others, go to the Maine Senior College Network website.
