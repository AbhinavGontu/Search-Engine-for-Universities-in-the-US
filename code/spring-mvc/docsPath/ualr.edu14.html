Little Rock Athletics Hall of Fame

Join the Trojan Lettermen's Association
The Trojan Lettermen's Association provides a great opportunity to stay connected to Little Rock, former teammates and coaches and the current successes of Trojan Athletics. For just $65 annually you will receive the benefits of being a member of the University of Arkansas at Little Rock Alumni Association and the Trojan Lettermen's Association. Benefits of joining include the following:

  • Little Rock Athletics e-newsletter
  • Alumni Association e-newsletter
  • Invitations to University and Alumni Association events
  • Invitations to Lettermen's Association events
  • All-Sports Pass (more than $250 in value)
  • 15 percent discount at the Team Store
  • Special Team Store offer during Lettermen's Weekend
  • Hall of Fame voting rights


Donations to the Lettermen's Association are greatly appreciated. Your donations help enhance the student-athlete experience and assist with special projects.
