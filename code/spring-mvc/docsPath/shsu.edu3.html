About The Hotel

University Hotel is located in the heart of Huntsville and on the beautiful and historic campus of Sam Houston State University. Enjoy the convenience and comfort of onsite accommodations for visits with family members and athletic events as well as conferences, meetings, seminars or special events. Our hotel offers a wide range of popular amenities, a friendly staff, and a comfortable atmosphere where you can relax and enjoy your stay.

What's Nearby

Huntsville, TX

Huntsville is known for its quaint, friendly college-town atmosphere, where people take the time to say hello. Come to Huntsville and you will delight in a plentitude of historical sites and recreational activities ranging from museums and antiques, to forests and parks, to prominent state institutions and historical locations. Huntsville truly has something for everyone!

The University Hotel is located at the corner of Bobby K. Marks Drive and Bearkat Blvd on the Sam Houston State University campus.
