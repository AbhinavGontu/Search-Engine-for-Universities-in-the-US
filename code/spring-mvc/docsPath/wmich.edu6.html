Abstracts & Scientific Posters

Abstracts

The abstract of a research report summarizes the main points of an article: (1) the study objective or background, (2) the study design and methods, (3) primary results, and (4) principal conclusions.  It provides the reader with the main points without having to read the body of the paper.

Posters

A scientific poster advertises your research. It combines text and graphics to make a visually pleasing presentation.

Posters presented at scientific conferences are a great way to share current research with colleagues. Poster sessions  like that shown on the right allow scientists to browse each others work quickly while still receiving the necessary scientific information.

It is important in a sea of posters to stand out. Building an eye-catching poster will not only help you stand out of the crowd, it will make it easier for other scientists to remember you and your work.
