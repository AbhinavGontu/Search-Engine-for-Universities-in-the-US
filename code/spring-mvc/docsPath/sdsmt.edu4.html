Hardrock Club

The purpose of the Hardrock Club is to provide scholarship funds for our scholar-athletes as well as to support and promote SD Mines and Hardrocker Athletics. As the primary fundraising organization for Hardrocker Athletics, the Hardrock Club supports all 13 of our sports.. Visit Hardrock Club for more information.
