Coffee and Credits with the Transfer Student Success Center

Date: March 17, 2021
Time: 12:00 p.m. - 1:00 p.m.
Location: Zoom
Category: Recruiting

RSVP Now

Share

Wayne State University is hosting opportunities for you to find out more about us and why we should be your transfer destination. Come join us!

Coffee and Credits on Zoom with the Transfer Student Success Center  
Every Wednesday through August, 2021
Noon – 1 p.m.  

Note: a Zoom link will be sent once you RSVP.  

Grab your coffee and join us to talk about transfer credits and tips for success. Learn about transfer credit, transfer plans, articulation agreements, transfer scholarships, Michigan Transfer Agreement (MTA), how to apply for admission and get answers to your questions.

If you have any questions or would like more information, please contact the Transfer Student Success Center by emailing transfer@wayne.edu.

We look forward to seeing you!
