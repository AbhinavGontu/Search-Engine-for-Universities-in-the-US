NYU IT Service Desk: In-Person Appointments Now Available for Mondays and Fridays in NYC; Walk-in Service Remains Suspended

Due to COVID-19, walk-in IT Service Desk support will remain unavailable in the US this spring. However, in-person appointments for Mondays and Fridays only at 10 Astor Place in New York can now be booked in advance by completing this form. (A self-service scheduling tool will be available soon; please check back.) 24x7 support by phone and email remains available at all NYU locations. See below for details.

About the NYU IT Service Desk

Contact the IT Service Desk  

New York Campus

  • For help by telephone, NYU community members in New York can call 1-212-998-3333 to speak with IT Service Desk staff 24 hours a day, seven days a week. (On campus in New York, simply dial 8-3333.)
  • For help online, search the ServiceLink knowledge base, or submit your question (including as many details as possible) using the contact form or by sending email to AskIT@nyu.edu. A representative will respond to the email address you supply within one business day.
  • Walk-in hours are currently unavailable. See note at top of page for details.

Abu Dhabi Campus

  • NYU community members at NYU Abu Dhabi can contact the IT Service Desk 24 hours a day, seven days a week by sending email to nyuad.it@nyu.edu or by calling 971-2-628-8888 (or simply 8-8888 from on-campus phones).
  • NYU Abu Dhabi IT Support Center walk-in assistance is available on the ground floor of the Campus Center (C2) building. Check the NYU Abu Dhabi Intranet for current hours (login required).
  • The NYU Abu Dhabi Center for Academic Technology (CAT) is offering remote support Sunday-Thursday, 8am-8pm GST; Friday and Saturday, 12pm-8pm GST. For any assistance, please contact nyuad.academictech@nyu.edu

Shanghai Campus

  • NYU community members at NYU Shanghai can contact the IT Service Desk by submitting a question (including as many details as possible) using the ServiceLink contact form, by sending email to shanghai.it.help@nyu.edu, or by calling 9-5555 (from on-campus phones) or 86-21-20595555 (from off-campus phones).
  • For help online, search the ServiceLink knowledge base.
  • In-person assistance is also available at the Walk-in Support Center (see website for location and hours).
