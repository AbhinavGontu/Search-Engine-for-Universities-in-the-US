A Rich Heritage

Westminster has been a stronghold for Reformed theology since its founding. Our faculty has trained generations of pastors, scholars, and leaders to serve the global church.

Westminster has been a stronghold for Reformed theology since its founding in 1929.

a most wonderful place

“Westminster Theological Seminary is one of the most unprecedentedly wonderful places for theological education ever in the history of the Christian church. When I became President of Southern Seminary, I was invigorated and inspirited—and in many ways kept at it—by the example of the men such as J. Gresham Machen who founded this institution, who counted the cost and were willing to pay it.”

— Dr. Albert Mohler, President of The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary, in his 2011 Gaffin Lecture
a life shaping experience

“Studying biblical theology at Westminster Theological Seminary was a life-shaping experience for me. The professors there helped me see the deep structure of Scripture, in which every passage in each testament communicates the gospel of Jesus Christ.”

— Dr. Philip Graham Ryken, President of Wheaton College, in his recommendation of "Seeing Christ in All of Scripture"
absolutely amazing

“This institution is an absolutely amazing reality. Thank you, Westminster, for your commitment to the Bible. Thank you for your Reformed theology—your solidity and symbolic presence.”

— Dr. John Piper, Chancellor of Bethlehem College & Seminary, in his 2012 Gaffin Lecture
