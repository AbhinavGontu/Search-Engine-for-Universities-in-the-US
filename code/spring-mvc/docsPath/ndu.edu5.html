Institutional Data

National Defense University students are selected by their service, agency, department, or country to attend NDU programs.  NDU does not accept applications from individuals for admission.  Consequently, traditional measures of student outcomes are not comparable with civilian universities.  Almost 100% of our enrollees graduate in the time allotted, almost 100% of our graduates are employed during their enrollment and for several years after graduation, etc. Data concerning the organizational diversity of our student bodies follow, listed by component.  Additional detail is available in our most current annual report.
