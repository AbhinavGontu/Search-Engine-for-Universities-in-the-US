Leaders in Learning

Inquiry-based, hands-on training and discovery make UCSF one of the nation's foremost academic health centers. 

How It's Done

  1. Pharmacy students stepped up to the COVID-19 response by administering UCSF's first vaccines to frontline workers.

  2. Three UCSF researchers have received New Innovator Awards from the National Institutes of Health to help fund their "moon shots."

  3. Safe in-person classes are a challenge during the pandemic. See how UCSF is finding new ways to teach the next generation.
