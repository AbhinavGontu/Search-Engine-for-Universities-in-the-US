Become a Stephens Star

Thanks for your interest in Stephens College and Star Athletics!

How to Contact a Coach
Prospective student-athletes are encouraged to fill out the recruiting form for the sport you are interested in playing at Stephens. This information will be delivered directly to the respective head coach and lets us know that you are strongly considering Stephens. Don't hesitate to contact our coaches via email or phone as well.

Applying for Admission
All prospective student-athletes are required to complete a FREE Undergraduate Admissions application. Apply online today.  Browse academics to learn more about Stephens and see if we are a good fit for you!

Financial Aid
At Stephens, 98% of our students receive some sort of need- or merit-based scholarships, grants or other financial aid. Stephens alone awards more than $9 million in financial aid to students each year. We are dedicated to working with you and your family to find the best graduation plan that includes athletic and merit scholarships, need-based grants and a variety of state and federal aid. We recommend that you file a FAFSA as early as October 1 of your senior year. Learn more about financial aid.

Play NAIA
The National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics (NAIA) Eligibility Center is responsible for determining the eligibility of first-time NAIA student-athletes. We suggest that you register at PlayNAIA and submit the required materials as soon as possible. There are different requirements for high school seniors and transfer students.
