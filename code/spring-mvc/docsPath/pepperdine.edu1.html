Library Operations

As the University continues online teaching and learning, Pepperdine Libraries is firmly committed to serving the needs of our community, offering library support services for faculty and resources for students including virtual consultations, scan on demand, and e-reserves. As part of the University's allowances for limited on-campus activities, students, faculty, and staff may reserve study seats in designated library spaces. Our expert librarians are also available around the clock to assist with your information seeking and knowledge navigation needs.
