Information Technology

The Department of Information Technology (IT) provides email, word processing, spreadsheets, database management, and a variety of other software for its cadets, staff, and faculty.

IT also provides VMI faculty and staff with Help Desk support, hardware and software recommendations as well as installations, troubleshooting, and training classes. IT is responsible for the VMI local area network, and can provide access upon request.

VMI furnishes a significant number of computers for cadet use in academic buildings and laboratories, and provides personally owned computer support for cadet-owned laptops. 

Access to computer systems and networks owned or operated by Virginia Military Institute impose responsibilities and obligations upon users.  For more about user responsibilities, review General Order 50.
