Extended Campus

Why choose University of Northern Colorado Extended Campus? Because you share our passion for lifelong learning and changing the world around you. Identify opportunities. Test ideas. Seek Solutions.

Learn more about UNC Extended Campus

  • Conveniently Delivered

    UNC tailors coursework to fit your unique needs as an adult learner. Reach your professional goals with our accelerated programming and multiple delivery options.

  • Renowned Faculty

    Extended Campus faculty are experts who teach — and create — the latest advances in their fields. You’ll earn the same fully accredited degree as our on-campus students.

  • Realize Your Big Idea

    Discover an enduring community of learners who demand and support an immersive educational experience. Challenge yourself and change your life.
