Facilities User Policy (pdf))
The BFFC is a private recreation center serving the needs of the Whitman Campus Community. All patrons must have a current WID card for access. Guests and minors must be accompanied by a current WID card holder and sign-in at the front desk.

SHERWOOD ATHLETICS CENTER

Semester Hours
Monday - Thursday: 7 a.m.-10 p.m.
Friday: 7 a.m.-7 p.m.
Saturday: 10 a.m.-7 p.m.
Sunday: 10 a.m.-10 p.m.
Facilities User Policy (pdf))
Sherwood center is home to academic and group exercise classrooms, Varsity Gym, Multi-Purpose Gym, racquetball and squash courts, varsity weight room, team rooms, varsity team laundry service, general population locker rooms, and the men's and women's sauna. Please access the following calendars for facility availably
Multi-Purpose Gym Calendar | Varsity Gym Calendar
The Multi-Purpose gym serves the Whitman campus community. You must have a current WID card to use the MPG. To schedule activities in the Multi-Purpose Gym, contact Michelle Ferenz, Head Women's Basketball Coach. (ferenzmk@whitman.edu). 
The Varsity Gym is reserved for varsity competition games and practice. For schedule information contact Michelle Ferenz, Head Women's Basketball Coach. (ferenzmk@whitman.edu)
To reserve a group exercise room or academic classroom, contact Sherwood Center Administrative Assistant Sarah Bell at 509-527-5921 (bellsm@whitman.edu). Group exercise rooms are equipped with wood floors, mirrors and sound equipment.
Academic classroom calendar: Room 222
Group Exercise Room calendars: Room 106 | Room 114 | Room 223
Non-Whitman parties must fill in and submit a Whitman Athletics Facilities Waiver when seeking to reserve any of the Whitman facilities.
