Admissions & Aid

Now calling smart, creative, ambitious, quirky students.

Since 1903, MSOE has been dedicated to educating the brightest and most innovative students — the ones who aren’t satisfied with the status quo, who are determined to create new solutions to our world’s most challenging problems. Sound like you? Then we’d love to meet you.  

Join us for a campus tour to experience the MSOE difference. If you’re ready to apply, get started today. It’s free and takes just 10 minutes, no essay required.  

Wondering how to pay for college? We’ll walk you through it. With our generous financial aid and scholarship options, an MSOE degree is more affordable than you might think, and it’s an investment that will pay off for a lifetime.
