Welcome

This signature event facility can host a year-round calendar of events and activities that engage a variety of constituents including alumni, donors, business and community partners, and a global network of visiting dignitaries.

The Parma Payne Goodall Alumni Center is a gateway for the community to access San Diego State University’s rich academic, athletic and cultural assets. Located across from Viejas Arena on the northwest corner of 55th Street and Hardy Avenue, the Center completes and anchors Aztec Walk, an east-west pedestrian promenade adjacent to Parking Structure 7/AzTrack.
