Contact Us

The Office of Annual Giving and Alumni Engagement would love to hear from you! If you have a question or if you are not sure who to contact, visit the FAQs.

Please note our summer hours are M-Th, 8:00 a.m. – 5:30 p.m. The University is closed on Fridays over the summer. We have also been working remotely since March 16. We thank you for your patience as we manage your customer service inquiries!
