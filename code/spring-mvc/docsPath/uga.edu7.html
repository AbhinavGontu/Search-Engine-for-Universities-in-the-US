Admissions

The University of Georgia School of Law is regarded as one of the nation's top law schools. Our law school offers the Juris Doctor degree (J.D.) , the Master of Laws degree (LL.M.), and the Master in the Study of Law degree (M.S.L.) and features a welcoming community in a charming locale.  The challenging curriculum and experienced professors dedicated to teaching the theoretical foundations of law and providing hands-on experience make our School of Law an excellent place for your legal studies.
