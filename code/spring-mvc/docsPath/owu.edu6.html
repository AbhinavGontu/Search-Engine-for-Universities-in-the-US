Get Your Head In The Game!

Want to participate in good competitive fun? Check out what we offer in our Club Sports or Intramural Leagues 

If you're looking to learn about your sport from a coach, practice with your team and play against other schools, then you should check out Club Sports

If you like a more laid back atmosphere, making teams with your friends, competing against your peers, the Intramural League is for you
