  Title: Photos with A Thousand Voices: A Discussion with A Civil Rights Gem, Cecil J. Williams
  Date: Mar 18, 2021
  Time: 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  Calendar: *Campus Calendar
  Contact: Nikia Franklin
  Description: The Office of Diversity and Inclusion, African/African American Studies Program, and Film and Digital Media Program present a masterclass webinar with Civil Rights legend and Photographer Cecil J. Williams as he describes his role in the Civil Rights movement, introduction into photography as a 14 year-old national correspondent for JET magazine and how his images, like the viral photo of him drinking from a ?White only? water fountain in Walterboro, South Carolina, resonates within the current Black Lives Matter movement.
