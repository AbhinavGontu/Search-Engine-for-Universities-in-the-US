    Views

    If I had to pick one little thing about USF that I absolutely love, it would have to be the incredible views that we have all over campus! One thing that San Francisco is definitely known for is its hills. The amount of slopes people have to climb just to get around the city always throws tourists and newcomers to the city off. But with great hills come great views. USF is built on one of the tallest points in the whole city. It seems like no matter where you are on campus, whether you are in one of the residence halls or at the top of Lone Mountain stairs, there is always a great view to look at. USF is also located pretty much right in the center of San Francisco. This little factor combined with being on the hilltop means that you can see pretty much every part of the city from campus. Some of my favorite spots where I like to enjoy the view is the little picnic table area right in front of Rossi Wing and the top of the LoMo steps. The ability to see the entire city of San Francisco everyday is definitely what makes USF unique and it is one little thing that I appreciate every time I am on campus; just how lucky we are to be located where we are located.
