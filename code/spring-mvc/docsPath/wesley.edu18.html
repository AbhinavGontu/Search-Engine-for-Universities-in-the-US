Using this Website

Q: What is the Wesley Alumni Online Community?

A: The Wesley Alumni Online Community is a networking website designed specifically for Wesley College alumni. This resource allows alumni to stay connected with one another and their alma mater by accessing campus and alumni news and announcements of upcoming events, searching for classmates and networking with other Wesley graduates through the online directory, class notes and message boards. Other functions of the site provide alumni users the ability to conveniently and securely make commerce transactions to register for alumni events or make a gift to Wesley online.

Q: How do I get started using the Alumni Online Community?

A: The first step is to register in order to access your personal profile and the other password-protected areas of the Online Community. Users have individual profiles where they can confirm and update their contact information for Wesley College records and determine what information is viewable to other alumni through the alumni directory. Please visit the Privacy Policy page for more information. How-to instruction sheets are available to guide you in using various functions of the site. Please click here to view.


Q. How do I register on the Alumni Online Community?

A: To register, follow the instructions below for your initial login. Please note: this only applies if this is your time first logging in and you have not yet set up a username and password. For your convenience, you can choose to log in using another account, such as Facebook, Twitter, Google or Yahoo.

1. Click on the "First Time Login" link that appears in the gray box on the homepage or top right of any other page.
2. Follow the prompts by typing your last name, selecting your record, and typing your ID #. This number unique to each alumnus can be found printed above the address label on recent Wesley mailings you may have received, or at the top of our e-newsletters.  Or, you may request it by contacting the Office of Institutional Advancement.
3. Follow the instructions to verify and complete your personal profile. Here you can create a username and password of your choosing for future visits to the site.


Q. How do I access the Alumni Online Community if I have already registered?

A: If you have previously completed the First Time Login, please follow the instructions below. For your convenience, can also choose to log in using another account, such as Facebook, Twitter, Google or Yahoo.

1. Click on the "Login" link that appears in the gray box on the homepage or top right of any other page.
2. Type in the username and password that you set up during your initial login.
3. If you have forgotten your username, please click on the "Forgot your username?" link to have your username sent to your email address on file.
4. If you have forgotten your password, please click on the "Reset Password" link to send a link to your email address on file where you can create a new password.
5. If the email address you provided when you initially logged in is no longer valid, please contact the Office of Institutional Advancement for assistance in accessing your account.

Q: How do I change my password?

A: Users are able to change their password at any time by clicking on the "Reset Password" link on the login screen. Please remember that for security purposes, the system requires that your password uses a combination of upper and lower case letters, numbers and symbols and from time to time, you may be prompted to change your password as an added security measure.

Q: What do I do if I cannot find my name during the First Time Login process?

A: If you are unable to find your name in the online listing, please contact the Office of Institutional Advancement so that we can check our records and set up your account, if needed.

If you are a married female and have not yet informed the College of a name change, it is possible that you are still listed under your maiden name. Once you log in, please update your profile with your current name, marital status and spouse information.

Q: After I have created my profile, how do I use the other available features?

A: Once you are logged in to the site and set up your personal profile, you can access any pages on the site through the navigational menus. You can expand your profile by setting up your personal friends list, adding photos, class notes or even your own blog! You can also access the online directory or send messages to Wesley friends/

Next to your name on your profile screen, there is a link to view a tutorial with specific instructions on how to use the following features:

Adding and Editing a Blog
Adding and Editing Feeds in the Profile
Adding and Editing Widgets in the Profile
Adding and Editing Member Fields in the Profile
Using the Friends List Tab
Using the Groups Tab
Using the Photos Tab
Using the Class Notes Tab
Using the My Activity Tab
Create and Edit Custom Tabs

Q: How do I get to Wesley College’s main website and how does that site differ from the Alumni Online Community?

A: The Alumni Online Community also serves as the official Wesley website section for the College’s Office of Institutional Advancement, including Alumni Relations and Annual Giving, Development, Advancement Services and Marketing. The Online Community is an extension of this, including access to webpages geared specifically for Wesley alumni that require a login. The top navigational menu on the Online Community webpages includes links back to the main Wesley College website at www.wesley.edu, where you can find information on all other campus departments, programs and services, including Admissions, Academics & Registrar and Athletics, among others.


Services for Alumni and Friends

Q: How do I become a member of the Wesley College Alumni Association?

A: Alumni who graduate from Wesley are automatically members of the Alumni Association. There are no fees or memberships cards. All members are entitled to various alumni services that the College provides. Please visit the Benefits and Services page for more information.

If you attended Wesley and did not graduate but feel a close connection to the College and wish to be a member of the Alumni Association and receive Wesley mailings, please contact the Office of Institutional Advancement.

Q: How can I find and contact classmates or Wesley friends with whom I have lost touch?

A: Because Wesley College’s privacy policy limits the release of alumni contact information, even to other alumni, the best way to find classmates or friends is through the alumni directory within the Online Community. This searchable listing of all Wesley College alumni on record provides the most recently updated alumni information and is the most direct way to get in touch with other Wesley alumni.

Just click on the Search for Alumni (Online Directory) link under "Member Tools" in the navigational menu. You must first be logged in to access this feature. Once you are directed to the Search page, you can type in the field(s) by which you wish to search (Name- First, Maiden, Last; Class Year; Registered Online status) and then click the Search button at the bottom of the page. The system will pull up an alphabetical list of all records that meet that specific criterion from which you can then select the correct individual. Further instructions are available on the Search help bar by clicking on the blue icon on the Search page.

Once the search results appear on the screen, you can hover with your mouse over any of the icons to the right of an individual’s name to select among the various options (i.e. send an email, view photos, add to friends list) or click on the individual’s name to view his/her profile. Deceased alumni are marked with a yellow ribbon icon to the right of their name.

If you are unable to find an individual’s information through the online directory online, please contact the Office of Institutional Advancement for assistance in contacting the alumnus on your behalf (i.e. forwarding a letter or email to the address on record).

Q: How do I submit my news or announcements for Class Notes?

A: Wesley has a listing of Class Notes on the Online Community and also has a Class Notes section in Wesley magazine. Alumni are invited to submit to either and if you submit your Class Notes online, you can check off a box to have it included in print as well. Class Notes online is an ongoing posting system where you can submit news, photos and announcements on your own at any time. Please click here to be directed to the Class Notes page (or you can select "Submit" or "Search" in the Class Notes section on the News page). From the Class Notes page you can type in your news in the box provided. To upload a photo with the class notes, please click on "Browse" to find the digital photo file from your computer. Then select "Preview" to view the note before submitting. 

You can also send a Class Note to be included in Wesley magazine. Please include your name, maiden name (if applicable), class year and daytime phone number along with your news as you would like it to be printed and email it to us or mail to the address below. Digital photos are encouraged. We prefer that they be in a in .tiff or .jpg file format with 300 dpi or above resolution. Non-digital photos are also welcome and can be returned to the address provided, if requested. We prefer 4 x 6 photos or larger. Please mail submissions to:

Office of Alumni Relations and Annual Giving
Wesley College
120 N. State Street
Dover, DE 19901

Q: I am not receiving Wesley alumni magazine or other mail from the College. How do I get put on the mailing list?

A: If you are not receiving mail from us, chances are that we do not have your current address in our records. The best way to notify us of information changes and to ensure that you will receive future mailings is to update your personal profile on the Online Community. Once you are logged in, the bottom of the homepage will show the current information we have on record, as well as a link to update. Please provide your current address, phone number, email address and other information in the appropriate profile fields. These changes or additions are processed by Advancement staff and updated in your permanent Wesley College alumni record. Please allow up to 10 working days for information updates to take effect.

If your profile information is already up-to-date and you still have not been receiving Wesley mail, please email the Office of Institutional Advancement or call 302-736-2467.

Q: What does the Wesley Fund support and how does my gift make a difference?

A: Every gift to Wesley makes a difference and collectively, the gifts of hundreds of alumni and donors make a huge impact and make it possible for Wesley to provide a quality education to the next generation of leaders. Additionally, overall alumni participation is an important criterion when Wesley is considered for funding from foundations and corporations so your involvement, at any level, is critical. Visit the Support for Annual Giving page for more information on what the Wesley Fund supports and the various giving opportunities.

Q: How can I make a gift to Wesley College?

A: All information about giving to Wesley can be found under "Support Wesley" in the navigational menu, including the various options to make a gift. Giving online via credit card is easy and safe through the secure Give Now form. This online donation form utilizes industry-standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) servers to process your transaction. Please visit the Privacy Policy page for more information. The "Support Wesley" section also includes information on giving priorities, donor recognition, the Wesley Society and planned giving as well as contact information for the Office of Institutional Advancement staff.

Q: How do I obtain a copy of my official Wesley transcript?

A: To obtain your Wesley transcript, you must go through the Wesley College Office of Registrar. Specific information on transcripts requests can be found on the Request a Transcript link on the main College website or by contacting 302-736-2379.

Q: How can I purchase Wesley College apparel or merchandise for myself and/or my
friends and family?

A: The Wesley College Bookstore offers an assortment of Wesley apparel and other items which can be purchased online as well as on campus. Their selections include some Wesley Alumni branded items. Be sure to check back frequently for special offers and new items. You can also visit the Official Lids Sideline Store of the Wesley Wolverines, a partner with Wesley Athletics department, to find a variety of general Wesley Wolverines and customizable sports related apparel.

Q: How do I get information on upcoming alumni events or Wesley athletic events?

A: Information on upcoming alumni events are posted on the Events page of this Online Community. Please visit http://athletics.wesley.edu for detailed information on athletics events, schedules, statistics and tickets.

Q: Does Wesley offer Career Services to alumni?

A: Yes, the Office of Career Services at Wesley does offer career information and job-seeking resources for alumni. Click here for details. In addition, this Online Community offers a Career Center area where alumni can search for and network with fellow alumni who have made their business information available through the site. Alumni are also invited to add postings for employment or internship opportunities in their organizations. When alumni create or update their profile, they can choose to complete a Business WebCard, Mentor Profile or Resume Profile to be included in the Career Center search.
