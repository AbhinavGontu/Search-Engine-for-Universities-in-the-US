Consumer Information

The Higher Education Opportunity Act (Public Law 110-315) (HEOA) was enacted on Aug. 14, 2008, and reauthorize the Higher Education Act of 1965, as amended (HEA). The HEOA of 2008 contains numerous federal reporting and disclosure requirements for information from various administrative areas of higher education institutions. In order to make this information readily available, the Office of Institutional Research & Assessment (OIRA) has created this content, which is designed to provide quick access to these data.

The following are general consumer information subject areas, which provide links to references, reports and additional details. Please visit HEOA for more information.
