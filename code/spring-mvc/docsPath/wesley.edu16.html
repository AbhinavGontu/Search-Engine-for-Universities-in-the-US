Wesley College is a private, liberal arts and sciences institution affiliated with the United Methodist Church. The College prides itself on its student-centered philosophies and a personalized and holistic approach to learning. With a 17:1 student to faculty ratio, individualized academic preparation accompanied by strong athletics and extracurricular programs define a Wesley education. The College offers more than 30 areas of study in arts and sciences, business, health sciences, education, and fine arts, including four graduate programs.

Office of Institutional Advancement

Building mutually beneficial relationships is at the core of what we do in the Office of Institutional Advancement. Whatever your affiliation with Wesley, we strongly value your interest and involvement and consider you a member of the Wesley family and a partner in our goal of continued success.


The Office of institutional Advancement exists to promote awareness of the incredible impact that contributions have on the future of our institution and the experiences and long term successes of our students and alumni. We strive to forge the connections and cultivate the necessary support and volunteer leadership to better position Wesley as a private liberal arts college, as we compete for talented students, faculty and staff, and philanthropic funding to propel the institution forward.
The Office of Institutional Advancement is comprised of four functional areas:
-Development
-Advancement Services
-Alumni Relations & Annual Giving
-Communications & Marketing

We invite you to utilize this site to learn more about the great things happening at Wesley, explore the plentiful opportunities to engage with fellow members of the College community and the Alumni Association, and discover how much your contributions make a difference at Wesley and in the lives of our current students.

Alumni Online Community

If you are an alumnus, welcome to our online community! It is a place on the web that you can call your own and engage with your alma mater and Wesley friends. We take great pride in the successes of the more than 16,000 Wesley alumni across the globe, and maintaining strong relationships with alumni is very important to us. No matter how long ago or how recently you attended Wesley, your accomplishments enhance the reputation of the College, just as the growth and success of Wesley continues to enhance the value of your degree.

With this online community at www.alumni.wesley.edu, we hope to facilitate the lifelong connection between you and other members of the Wesley family by providing useful information and tools to find and communicate with classmates, learn about upcoming events, expand your career network, share news with your Wesley friends and much more! Since it is an exclusive website, some pages require an alumni login and password so you’ll want to join the community, if you haven’t already, to enjoy all the benefits. Bookmark the site, take advantage of the many resources, and visit often!
