Home to the Offices of Alumni Relations, Advancement Services, Development, and Marketing and Communications, the Otterbein Division of Institutional Advancement is committed in its work to steward and grow valued relationships with alumni, donors, students, parents, partners and friends; to effectively communicate Otterbein’s story and the news of its community with local, national and international audiences; and to ensure that the mission of this university is honored and advanced with the investment and philanthropic vision of its loyal donors and partners.

Welcome to Institutional Advancement

We are excited that you are visiting our division’s website to learn more about our collective work to advance the University’s mission. As higher education continues to experience changes and challenges, it is now more important than ever to ensure that we stand strong for generations of students to have an opportunity to pursue an Otterbein education. Thus, we strive daily to create opportunities to support Otterbein’s goal of providing a first-class education to its students by securing resources for the institution, promoting its reputation, and building access to people and networks.

On behalf of our University, thank you for your support. You make all the difference in the lives of our students as they benefit from an affordable education with our talented and caring faculty and staff and a model community that believes in them, so they can go to make an impact in their professions and communities.

– Michael R. McGreevey, Vice President for Institutional Advancement
