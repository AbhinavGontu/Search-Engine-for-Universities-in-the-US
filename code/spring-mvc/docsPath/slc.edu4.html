Due to the ongoing Covid-19 situation afflicting our communities, all community programs through Sarah Lawrence Recreation have been suspended until further notice.  

 

Children's Swim Lessons:

Please make sure that you register each child according to the class that best fits his or her ability.

Classes are not based on your child's age.  Lessons are for children ages 4 and older.

If you have any questions about your child's ability please do not hesitate to contact us at recreation@sarahlawrence.edu

 

Registration for Children's Swim Lessons open in January 2020! 

*You will need to create an account to register for programs, and to view dates & times*

5 weeks, NO MAKEUP CLASSES for $160

Level 1 & 2 run 30 minutes long

Level 3, 4, & 5 run 45 minutes long 

 

FAQ:

The pool temperature is kept at 81-82 degrees

Class sizes are kept at no more than a 5 -1 ratio, many classes are kept lower

 

Class time and size are limited and should fill up quickly.  We will not have additional class times to offer once they are filled. We do not offer make up classes, so please review the dates before registering.  Thank you for your understanding.

For questions regarding swim lessons, please contact Remi Urreta, our swim lessons coordinator, at remi.urreta@gmail.com.

Spring Lap Swim Semester Pass

Registration Opens in January! 

This is a drop in Lap Swim Pass for the current semester. Open to those looking to swim some laps, all lap swim abilities welcome. Lane availability will be on a first come basis. Participants will only be permitted to come during this schedule pool time. Program is reserved for participants 18 years and older, make-up classes not available.

Lap Swim available between 8:30am - 10:30am Monday through Friday. The cost for the semester-long pass is $650.

Lap Swim begins Monday, January 27 and ends Friday, May 1

No Class held between Monday, March 16 through Friday, March 20

Classes resume Monday, March 23

DIRECTIONS: Campbell Sports Center 

(Kimball Avenue and Wilgarth Road - Use 12 Wilgarth Road for GPS)

 

We are located off of the Cross County Parkway, which is accessible from the Hutchinson River Parkway, the Bronx River Parkway, the New York Thruway or the Saw Mill River Parkway.

 

If you plan to use GPS navigation, our building is located at 12 Wilgarth Road, Yonkers, NY. This is not a mailing address.

 

If you are coming EAST on the Cross County (from the Saw Mill Parkway or the NY Thruway): Take EXIT 5, Kimball/Midland, go to the bottom of the exit ramp and turn LEFT onto Kimball Avenue.  You will go under the parkway, through the first traffic light and the building will be on your LEFT.  Go just past the building and turn left and then left again into the driveway.  Park in the back.

 

If you are coming WEST on the Cross County (from the Hutchinson Parkway): Take EXIT 5, Kimball/Midland, go to the end of the exit ramp and turn RIGHT at the stop light.  At the next traffic light turn LEFT onto Kimball Avenue.  The building will be immediately on your LEFT.  Go just past the building and turn left and then left again into the driveway.  Park in the back.
