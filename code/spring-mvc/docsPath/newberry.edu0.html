About

About

Newberry College has a rich tradition of providing personal attention, challenging academic programs, and a close-knit community that welcomes everyone. We combine our strong liberal arts foundation with specialized professional degree programs to prepare students such as you for successful futures.

Academics

Imagine Yourself at Newberry College

Prime Location

The beautifully landscaped 90-acre campus is situated in the heart of historic Newberry, South Carolina: one of Sweitzer & Fields’ 50 Best Small Southern Towns.

Learn more

Loyal Alumni Family

Newberry Born, Newberry Bred! Our alumni are one big, connected family!

Learn more

Competitive Athletics

The Wolves are a pack to be reckoned with in the South Atlantic Conference, offering 21 NCAA Division II sports, cheerleading and dance.

Learn more

Close-knit Community

With a welcoming atmosphere on campus and a 13:1 student-to-faculty ratio in the classroom, Newberry is your home away from home.

Learn more

Challenging Academic Programs

We offer 32 majors, online degree-completion programs and five dual-degree programs, grounded in the liberal arts to prepare you for lifetime success.

Learn more

Highly Ranked

Newberry College is consistently recognized for outstanding value, diversity, and overall quality.

Learn more

Rich Heritage & Traditions

Founded in 1856 and affiliated with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America, Newberry remains rooted in the Lutheran liberal arts tradition.
