ORDER YOUR RING

The official Southeastern ring is a symbolic link to your university experiences, classmates, and fellow alumni. Each ring symbolizes your academic achievements and your memories of your alma mater. It’s never too late to be a part of the tradition!

ORDER RING

LEAVE YOUR MARK

Project Friendship Bricks are a great way to honor a loved one, celebrate a "future lion", share a special Southeastern memory, or commemorate your school years. Whatever the reason, your Friendship Brick will symbolize the pride you have in Southeastern.

GET CONNECTED

Build your network on Southeastern Connect, the new online platform built exclusively for Southeastern graduates. Connect with former classmates, find alumni in your current city, search job listings in your industry, and find or be a mentor.
