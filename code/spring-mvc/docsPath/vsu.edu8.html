WVST Radio


91.3 WVST Is The Source, the region’s superstation for jazz and information. With this in mind, WVST strives to be your source for the best:
  • Music
  • News
  • Weather
  • Sports
  • Information on what’s going on at Virginia State University as well as in surrounding communities
  • Information you can use in your everyday life
  • Informative talk shows

Off the air, WVST is a resource, or shall we say re-source, for those interested in learning more about broadcasting. We provide opportunities for Virginia State University student, as well as members of the community, to:
  • Learn more about radio
  • Develop skills for use in the broadcasting industry
  • Gain valuable experience in radio broadcasting through volunteering or an internship.  


We do it all. That’s why we are The Source, and the region’s superstation. Your superstation, not only for jazz, but also information and much more!  

More to come! More to come!

 

Listen To WVST (91.3 FM)

Smooth Jazz with a little R&B, Hip Hop, Gospel, Reggae, Oldies, and the Blues. It is all here at WVST! It is music for everybody!
