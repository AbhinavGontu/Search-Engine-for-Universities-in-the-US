Welcome

Whether your an external guest or a URI community member planning an event, we’ll help you take advantage of our award-winning dining services, apartment style accommodations, state-of-the-art meeting facilities and our vast recreation fields. Our amenities plus our proximity to the City of Providence, Newport, and New England’s finest beaches are the perfect ingredients for a memorable event.

Accommodations

From single occupancy, apartment style suites to traditional housing, we can accommodate up to 6000 overnight guests.
