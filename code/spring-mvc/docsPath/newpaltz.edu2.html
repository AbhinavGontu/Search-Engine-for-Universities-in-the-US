General Freshmen applicants must complete

The Common Application
-or-

  • Please use only one application.
  • The application fee is $50 for either application.

Fall semester: General Admission May 1

Accelerated 3+4 Optometry Program January 1

 

EDUCATIONAL OPPORTUNITY PROGRAM (EOP) FRESHMEN APPLICANTS

All Freshmen EOP applicants must complete

The Common Application
-or-
The SUNY Application

  • Please use only one application.
  • The application fee is waived for all EOP applicants. 
  • The Educational Opportunity Program (EOP) is a New York State sponsored program designed to assist students who show promise and potential for academic success at the college level yet demonstrate both an academic and historical financial need for assistance in order to attend college.
  • The EOP program is open to New York State residents.
  • The application fee is waived for EOP applicants.
  • For more information on our EOP program, visit our website.

SUNY New Paltz EOP does not admit a spring semester freshman class.

INTERNATIONAL (FRESHMEN & TRANSFER) APPLICANTS 

International students are visa-seeking students who want to study in the United States.  For specific information, please visit the Center for International Programs website. 

All international applicants must complete

The Common Application

-or-
The SUNY Application

  • Please use only one application.
  • The application fee is $50 for either application.

The Center for International Programs (CIP) at SUNY New Paltz offers students a wide-range of services. To get more information, or to speak with a counselor or current international student, please visit our website. We are here to help, and we welcome you with open arms! 

Fall Enrollment May 1
Spring Enrollment December 1

READMISSION APPLICANTS

If you were matriculated at SUNY New Paltz and your attendance at the college was interrupted for any reason for two or more consecutive semesters, or if you thought you graduated but did not, you are considered a readmission student. 

For specific information regarding readmission, visit our website.  

To apply: Fill out the Readmission Application. 

Spring Art/Visual Art Education Enrollment October 15
Spring Enrollment November 15
Fall Art/Visual Art Education Enrollment March 15
Fall Enrollment April 15

IMPORTANT INFORMATION FOR ALL APPLICANTS

  • SUNY New Paltz reserves the right to change applicaiton due dates without prior notice to applicants.
  • SUNY New Paltz Admission uses email as its official method of communication.  In order to ensure you receive all communications related to your application, please make sure the email address you provided on your application is correct. Please add admissions@newpaltz.edu to your address book to avoid important emails from New Paltz being sent to spam/junk mail folders.
