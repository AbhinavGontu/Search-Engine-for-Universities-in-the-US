Giving to UD has the power to change the world. 

Our students and faculty work every day to solve society’s most complex problems with groundbreaking solutions. Solutions fueled by passion, drive and philanthropy. 

From our loyal alumni to dedicated parents and visionary corporations and foundations, our donors are not just giving to UD, but through UD, to help advance our campus, our community and our world.

Honoring The Past While Looking To The Future

Jim O’Neill led UD’s Center for Economic Education and Entrepreneurship (CEEE) for more than 40 years before he retired in 2013. Now, as the CEEE celebrates its 50th Anniversary, his legacy of innovation and leadership is being honored with a fund in his name, the James B. O’Neill CEEE Faculty and Staff Support Fund.

Read More

FLOCKING TOGETHER TO HELP BLUE HENS

This past spring, UD launched the Blue Hen Strong Fund to provide direct support for students with a focus on providing tuition assistance, advanced technology and enhanced online learning, career readiness resources and more. Support has only grown as our incredible UD community has flocked together to show their blue-and-gold spirit.
