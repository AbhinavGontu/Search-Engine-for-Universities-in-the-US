    Welcome

    Welcome to the webpage of the Office of Global Engagement (OGE) at The University of Mississippi! Our office is responsible for the development, promotion, and support for all global engagement activities on campus. Our goal is the comprehensive internationalization of the university, and the development of our students, faculty, and staff as global citizens.
