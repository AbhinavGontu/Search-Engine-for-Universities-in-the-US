Unrivaled Clinical Training

Our curriculum is known for its comprehensive breadth that places an unrivaled emphasis upon hands-on clinical practice. In fact, pharmacy students at TTUHSC complete more clinical training than do students from any other pharmacy program in the country.

Emerging Excellence

Barely 20 years old, we are already ranked in the top quarter of pharmacy programs nationally and as high as #16 in one recent poll.

Diverse, Interprofessional Curriculum

Every pharmacy student benefits from the dynamic interplay between our four departments – biomedical sciences, immunotherapeutics and biotechnology, pharmaceutical sciences and pharmacy practice – our Pharm.D./MBA dual degree program and a host of interprofessional opportunities alongside medical, nursing, biomedical and health professions students.

Jerry H. Hodge School of Pharmacy

Welcome to the Jerry H. Hodge School of Pharmacy! If you are looking to pursue your pharmacy degree in a school that helps you learn how to use your clinical skills in any practice environment, you have found the right place.

Uniquely spread across West Texas and DFW, TTUHSC is known nationally as one of the best programs for training practice-ready pharmacists. Find your passion for pharmacy and find your future as a pharmacist who transforms healthcare.

20+years transforming pharmacy

Programs at a Glance

In addition to our distinguished Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D.) program, we also offer the Pharm.D./MBA dual-degree program, M.S. and Ph.D. programs in pharmaceutical sciences and biotechnology, a graduate pharmacy residency program and a host of continuing education options.

Why Pharmacy?

Texas Tech University Health Science Center's School of Pharmacy has an abundance of exciting, cutting-edge programs that allow students many different paths for those who are seeking a career within the field of pharmacy.

Choosing pharmacy as a career path opens the door to a myriad of opportunities such as retail and independent pharmacy, pharmaceutical science and research, hospital and clinical pharmacy, business and others.
