The School of Architecture is a community of about 225 students in total, served by around 32 accomplished full-time and adjunct faculty. The school emphasizes a progressive hands-on approach to education that is also driven by close personal relationships with our dedicated faculty. We offer degrees in architecture, architectural engineering, and community development.
