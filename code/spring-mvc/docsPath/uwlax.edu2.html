University of Wisconsin-La Crosse Catalogs

(To return to this page from elsewhere in the catalog, click on the "Home" link in the breadcrumb trail on the top of subsequent pages.)

Welcome to UW-La Crosse's undergraduate and graduate catalog site. These catalogs contain important information about undergraduate and graduate programs, courses, policies, faculty/staff, and facilities.

UWL catalogs do not establish a contractual relationship. Instead, they summarize the total requirements a student must meet before qualifying for a degree and exist, not only to help prospective students and others learn about UWL, but also to provide a statement of policies, requirements, regulations, and procedures in a form helpful to all.

These catalogs are published yearly and are a snapshot of UWL at the time of publication in late July. Changes that occur after publication will be included in the next catalog.
