Student Success

Succeed at UH

Student success is my top priority. That's why our office works hard to bring you programs that encourage timely graduation and strategic academic planning. We're here to help you succeed, whether you're an incoming freshman, transfer student, or graduating senior.

Welcome and GO COOGS!

- Paula Myrick Short, Provost

Need Guidance?

The Undergraduate Advisors at UH can help you plan your steps towards completing a degree in a timely manner with one-on-one advising and academic maps. Schedule an appointment with an advisor today!
