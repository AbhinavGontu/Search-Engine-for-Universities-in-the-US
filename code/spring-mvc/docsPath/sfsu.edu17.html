Congratulations, Graduates! #SFSU2021

Dear Graduating Students,

First and foremost, congratulations on your upcoming graduation! To complete your studies and graduate is a tremendous accomplishment and your perseverance through all the challenges of 2020 to make it to the finish line is remarkable and we are proud of you.

Virtual Commencement: Friday, May 21, 2021

The San Francisco State University community is proud of all of the hard work, dedication and fortitude it took to get you to your graduation.  Your accomplishment should not go unnoticed which is why making the decision to postpone an in-person Commencement ceremony was so difficult. 

The health and safety of our graduates and guests must take precedence and so we will be moving forward with a virtual Commencement Ceremony for the Class of 2021.

So, please save the date for a virtual Commencement ceremony to be held Friday, May 21, 2021 at 5:30 p.m. A Commencement app and registration site will launch on March 25.

We are strongly committed to holding an in-person ceremony just as soon as we can safely gather. You will be celebrated and recognized in the grand style our students deserve. 

We have been able to keep the fee to apply to graduate at $100 for many years now. This fee covers the expenses associated with reviewing applications, issuing diplomas, graduation communications and printed materials, the Commencement Ceremony and a wide variety of graduation initiatives and activities. With this year’s ceremonies being virtual the fee will be $40 for all students who graduated in Fall 2020, students applying to graduate in Spring 2021 and students who apply to graduate in Summer 2021 by April 1, 2021. The University issued a credit of $60 to the Student Center account for students who applied to graduate before this announcement.

Stay engaged after graduation

One benefit we are happy to announce for our graduating students is a free membership to the Alumni Association. Once a Gator always a Gator! You can stay connected and keep your SF State Family close no matter where you go.  Sign up by June 30, 2021 and enter NOPAY in the promotion code box and you’re in! Check out all the Alumni Association has to offer.

Play it Forward

Play It Forward is the first ever official SF State livestreaming event on Twitch. This event raises money in honor of the 2021 Class Gift campaign, all gifts made during the livestream benefit the HOPE Crisis Fund for current SF State students in need. Guest streamers include staff, students, and President Lynn Mahoney playing games like Animal Crossing: New Horizons and Among Us. Save the date and stay tuned for more information!

April 29, 2021; 6 – 9 PM
