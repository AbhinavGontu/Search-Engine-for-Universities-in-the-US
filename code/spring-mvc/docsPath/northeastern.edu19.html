Rigorous Academics + Experiential Learning

Acquire the skills and knowledge needed to work across national cultures and differing business practices by immersing yourself in transformative global experiences in D’Amore-McKim’s BS in International Business program. By graduation, you will have studied in one or more foreign countries and gained work experience at home and abroad through two or more cooperative education assignments. Ultimately, students who choose the BSIB program will learn how a globalized economy impacts business, especially within the business concentration areas they’ve chosen to study.

Complete the degree in four or five years, depending on the program of study you design. There are also several business concentrations and business minors to help you customize your unique Northeastern educational experience.

Upon graduation, you’ll be ready to work for a home country company that operates internationally or to work overseas on an international assignment.

Choose a Concentration

Complete one of the following concentrations. A second concentration is optional. Certain concentrations require additional prerequisites. Please consult your academic advisor to ensure you have met these requirements.

Undergraduate Concentration in Accounting

Accounting is a dynamic profession that requires people to possess sound technical knowledge, critical thinking skills, superior interpersonal skills, and the ability to communicate effectively.

Undergraduate Concentration in Corporate Innovation

In order to excel in the workplace or in a new venture, you will need to lead with innovation.

Undergraduate Concentration in Entrepreneurial Startups

Learn the ins-and-outs of startups to prepare to fully immerse yourself in a new business venture

Undergraduate Concentration in Family Business

Develop insight and understand the distinct challenges, opportunities, and practices of family businesses

Undergraduate Concentration in Finance

A fast-changing business world is putting finance front and center, with financial knowledge seen as essential to effective management.

Undergraduate Concentration in Fintech

A seamless blend of skills in technology with finance help prepare you for the future

Undergraduate Concentration in Healthcare Management and Consulting

Working in healthcare requires knowledge and skills in several cutting-edge areas such as service delivery innovation, process and quality improvement, talent management, analytics, and supply chain management.

Undergraduate Concentration in Management

Effective managers must know the business, be interpersonally competent, behave ethically, and diagnose human and organizational dynamics.

Undergraduate Concentration in Management Information Systems

Develop the cutting-edge technical and problem-solving skills that are in high demand by employers.

Undergraduate Concentration in Marketing

Product design, research, pricing, packaging, transportation, advertising, selling, and servicing: marketing impacts every one of these essential aspects of business.

Undergraduate Concentration in Marketing Analytics

Learn the cutting-edge skills necessary to harness, process, and analyze data to address modern marketing challenges.

Undergraduate Concentration in Social Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Create and manage businesses with a focus on the betterment of society

Undergraduate Concentration in Supply Chain Management

All companies that produce, sell, or distribute products have a supply chain management function that must be effectively managed to be competitive.
