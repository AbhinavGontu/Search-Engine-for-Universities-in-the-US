Experience UNO Virtually! 

We invite you to explore our campus and learn more about life as a Maverick at UNO through a variety of virtual visit options and experiences.

We’ve created some awesome virtual events that allow you to visit UNO from the safety of your living room. Join us via Zoom as we share our knowledge of campus spaces, the admissions process, and student life.

Maverick Visit Experience

While we are not currently hosting in-person visit experiences, we hope to resume a version of our in-person visits later this spring. In the meantime, we encourage you to check out our Virtual Visit offerings.
