Book returns & buy back

All book returns must be completed through the virtual site, in the first two weeks of the quarter. Course materials must be returned within two weeks after class start date or within 21 days of the date they shipped, whichever is later. After the first two weeks, books may be sold during book buyback the last day of the quarter: It is the responsibility of the student to obtain and keep receipts. If you need assistance, please stop by the Bookstore to see a staff member.

The SKC Bookstore is not your only option to purchase your books. You can use the online ordering tool to see what books are necessary and order from other sources such as Amazon Text Books
