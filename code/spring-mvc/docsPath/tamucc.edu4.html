Counseling and Training Clinic

The Counseling and Training Clinic offers counseling to individuals, couples, families, and children.

All sessions are facilitated by counselor trainees. Sessions are private and confidential and are supervised by fully-trained, licensed professionals in an environment of safety and trust.

Our current offerings:

Counseling (for individuals, couples, families, and/or groups)

Schedule: Monday - Thursday 12 pm - 8 pm

Who is eligible: Anyone age 6+

Register: 361-825-3988

We offer the following services:

  • Individual Counseling
  • Family Counseling
  • Couples Counseling
  • Group Counseling
  • Multifamily Group Counseling for Children, Adolescents, Adults, and Families

We can assist with topics including:

  • Parenting Skills
  • Self-Esteem
  • Career Planning
  • Interpersonal Skills
  • Divorce-Separation-Blended Families
  • Discipline/Behavior Problems
  • Couples Communication
  • Study Skills
  • Time Management
  • Grief and Loss
  • Relationships
  • Communication
  • Assertiveness Training
  • Anger Management
  • Stress Management
  • Relaxation
  • Coping Skills
  • Fears, Anxiety, Phobias & PTSD
  • Sadness/Depression
  • Social Skills
  • Life Skills

Free Health Screenings

Schedule: Mondays

  • 2-6 pm starting February 2, 2021 (online or phone meetings)

Who is eligible: Anyone age 18+

Register: 361-825-3988

Details:

TAMU-CC nursing students will address your health-related questions, review health histories, and provide appropriate referrals as needed.

DBT Wellness Group

Schedule: Wednesdays

  • 5:30-7:00 pm starting March 17 (online meeting)

Who is eligible: Anyone age 18+

Register: 361-825-3988

Details:

This free group is designed to help you develop healthy skills and habits that you can use in
your everyday life, as well as help you reduce stress.

Hours & Directions

Sessions are offered by the Counseling and Training Clinic on the Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi campus, and are currently taking place via telehealth.

The clinic is open Monday through Thursday from 12pm - 8pm. Offices are closed during university holidays. Please call first to check our schedule.

*We are not an emergency clinic. If you are in need of immediate help please call the University Police Department at 361-825-4444, or call 911.
