Online MBA

The Online MBA For Emerging Leaders

Earn your AACSB-accredited Online MBA in 12 months with skills employers want. Our award-winning faculty offers you the experience and insight you need to master the fundamentals of business. 

Pending WSCUC Approval

Thank you for your interest in Saint Mary's Online MBA Program

We look forward to learning more about your career goals. We'll be accepting applications soon. Add your name to the priority list and we'll contact you shortly. 

Complete your Online MBA in one year

Gain marketable skills that accelerate your career in just 12 months. The AACSB-accredited Online MBA is designed for students who need a flexible option to accommodate their schedule. Our program empowers you to master the fundamentals of business through the experience and insight of our award-winning faculty. Our applied approach gives you the skills to develop business strategies in real-world business settings and apply them to your work immediately.

  • Achieve your degree without interrupting your career
  • Access the same quality curriculum and faculty as on-campus
  • Take advantage of personalized career and leadership consulting services
  • Live, study, and work from anywhere
  • Network with some of the best minds in industry, including over 7k alumni
  • Tailor your classes and course load to your schedule

Personal and Engaging 

Although you'll access your classes when it fits your schedule, it doesn't mean you'll be alone. By joining the Online MBA program, you will engage in classroom activities, online discussions with professors and business partners, experiential learning, business writing, and interact with students and professors from around the world, all of which have been carefully selected for this innovative program.

Reputation for Excellence

For more than 80 years, the School of Economics and Business Administration has trained future leaders to make a greater impact on business around the globe. The college holds some of the most prestigious rankings worldwide, including US News & World Report, Princeton Review, and Wall Street Journal.

Tuition and Aid

Tuition for the 2021-2022 Online MBA program is $45,480.

We offer the following to help support your tuition and fees:

Scholarships:
Scholarships are awarded on various criteria usually reflecting the values and purposes of the donor or founder of the award.

Payment Plans:
Plans for paying any outstanding debts. Within a payment plan, the borrower agrees to pay back a certain amount of money each month to repay the debt.

Financial Aid:
Financial assistance intended to aid students in reaching their educational goals. This assistance may come in a variety of forms such as grants, scholarships, work-study, and loan programs.

Veterans' Benefits:
Saint Mary's College is approved for the continued education of Veterans and their eligible family members under the Department of Veteran Affairs. All Graduate Business Programs (MBA and MS) are recognized as full-time, and we are also proud to participate in the Yellow Ribbon Program.
