Undergraduate and Graduate Catalog Information
To identify which UMF Catalog you will use for your time at Farmington, first select your student type: Undergraduate Catalogs or Graduate School Catalogs and choose from the dropdown menu the year you enrolled.

For instance, if you are enrolled as an undergraduate degree-seeking student for Fall 2020, you would use the 2020-2021 Catalog. Each Catalog also contains other important information you will need during your UMF education.

Note: Undergraduate students who have been separated from the University for a continuous period of 2 or more years will be held to the Catalog requirements of their year of reentry.
