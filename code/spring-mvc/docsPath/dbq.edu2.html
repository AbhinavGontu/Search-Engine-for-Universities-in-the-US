A Community After God's Heart

Apply Visit Campus Explore Give

The University of Dubuque Theological Seminary is a vibrant Christian community that seeks to follow Jesus, walk in the Spirit, and join God’s mission. Shaped by faith in the One God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit—we engage in teaching, learning, scholarship, and worship to form servant leaders for Christ’s church.

Quick Links

  • Master of Divinity (MDiv) - 75 credits - Residential or Distance
  • Master of Arts in Mission and Discipleship (MAMD) - 40 credits - Residential or Distance
  • Master of Arts in Christian Leadership (MACL) - 30 credits - 100% Online through the University of Dubuque
  • Young Adult Ministry Scholars (YAMS) - Earn a MDiv or MAMD while working on campus. Tuition-free and stipend provided
  • Unclassified Courses - Discerning your call or just want to explore a topic? Apply to become an unclassified student and take graduate-level courses. 
  • Commissioned Ruling Elder/Christian Leadership Program Online Courses - Courses specially crafted for lay leaders who long to go deeper into God's word and explore the practices of the church

2000 University Avenue, Dubuque, IA 52001 | webeditor@dbq.edu | 800.369.8387

The University of Dubuque Theological Seminary, an ecumenical seminary of the Presbyterian Church (USA), offers the Master of Arts in Mission and Discipleship (MAMD), the Master of Divinity (MDiv), and the Doctor of Ministry (DMin) degrees. A leader in distance theological education, we offer the MAMD and MDiv in both distance and residential formats.
