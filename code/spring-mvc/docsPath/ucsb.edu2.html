Program Highlights

  • UCSB Students

    Summer Sessions offers over 600 courses, including online courses, and works with faculty and departments to develop unique learning experiences in special courses and programs well suited to the summer term.

  • Freshman Summer Start Program

    The Freshman Summer Start Program (FSSP) is an exciting, innovative opportunity designed for admitted UCSB freshmen to get a head start on coursework and make a successful academic and social transition to UCSB.

  • Transfer Edge

    This program immerses new transfer students in all that UC Santa Barbara has to offer. Get a head start on crucial courses, learn strategies for academic success, and build a community of friends to rely on throughout your undergraduate experience. 

  • International Students

    Summer Sessions at UC Santa Barbara offers open enrollment to all qualified domestic and international university students. Choose from hundreds of in-person and online courses. 

  • Pre-College Programs

    High school students can experience UC Santa Barbara's world-class educational environment through a suite of programs designed to advance their academic and professional goals.
