WHAT CAN I EXPECT DURING A QUEST DAY?

Join us for an exciting, safe on-campus experience at Oral Roberts University! During this event, see what makes our education and leadership culture worth the investment:

  • Explore all of ORU's opportunities and take a campus tour with Student Ambassadors
  • Hear from ORU President, Dr. William M. Wilson
  • Worship with us at Chapel 
  • Learn why our education and faculty standout at Collaborate: Academics
  • Learn more about our 150+ majors, minors, concentrations, and pre-professional programs
  • Enjoy a safely distanced lunch and connect with current students
  • Experience what makes our community so special during Collaborate: Campus Life 
  • Connect with your Admissions Representative

Parents: We have special moment prepared for you throughout the day to hear about financial aid and scholarships. We're excited to share how we've adapted our campus to comply with state and CDC guidelines. You don't want to miss these sessions. 

WHO CAN ATTEND? AND WHAT IS THE COST?

Sophomores, juniors, seniors, high school graduates, and transfer students – parents and sponsors also welcome to attend! This is a FREE, on-campus event. Registration closes one week prior to the event and space is limited so don't wait to register!

WHAT ABOUT LODGING?

As an added precaution to ensure your safety and the safety of our current students, we are not accommodating guests to stay overnight on campus. Click here for a list of hotel options in the area. 

BEFORE YOU ARRIVE

Once you arrive on campus, please park in Lot B. At check-in, we will check your temperature and ask you to complete a Wellness Form. It’s best to fill this out prior to your arrival, and don’t forget to bring a mask! University policy requires all faculty, staff, students, and guests to wear this while on campus to maintain the safety of our community. To access our campus map, click here.

QUESTIONS?

For questions, please call 918.495.6165 or email campusvisits@oru.edu.
