OFFICIAL CAMP WEBSITE OF | SMC Volleyball Camps

 

SMC Volleyball Camps & Clinics are dedicated to all aspects of the game including skill development, game strategy, competition, and mechanics. Each athlete will have the opportunity to develop their skills through individual instruction, small group sessions, and game experience.

Saint Mary's College is fortunate to have some of the finest collegiate coaches in Northern California. Head Coach Rob Browning hand selects and trains his staff, composed of top-level coaches and collegiate players. All instructors are chosen based on their extensive knowledge of the game and their enthusiasm to work with young athletes.
