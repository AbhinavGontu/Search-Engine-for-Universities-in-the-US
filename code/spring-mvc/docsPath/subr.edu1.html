Admissions Login

  

You have reached the online admission application for Southern University System.
You can apply for Southern University Baton Rouge, Southern University New Orleans and Southern University Shreveport using this application.

You must create a login account that will be used to submit an online application.
The login account will also be used to complete an unfinished application or to check the status of a submitted application.
It is highly recommended you write down the log information you create. Institutions cannot retrieve your account information and
unsuccessful attempts will lock your account.

First-time users: Click the "First time user account creation" link to create your login information.

Returning users: Enter your login information below.
Please note that your Login ID is case sensitive. Do not create multiple applications, this will delay the processing of your application.
If there are changes to your application after you submit the application, contact the institution you applied to including your full name and date of birth.
Southern University Baton Rouge, subr.admissions@sus.edu; Southern University New Orleans, suno.admissions@sus.edu; Southern University Shreveport, susla.admissions@sus.edu.
