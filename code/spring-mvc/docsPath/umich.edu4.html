La Tertulia Virtual

Spanish Conversation Hour

Please join us for Tertulia Virtual! Tertulia Virtual is a fun, friendly, informal and stress-free environment where students can practice their speaking, listening, and conversational skills with other students or instructors. All students, faculty, and staff are invited and all levels of Spanish are welcome. It's also a great way to prepare for oral exams or for upcoming study abroad programs or a chance for returning students to keep up their conversational skills.

Please contact Julie Harrell at harrelju@umich.edu with any questions.
