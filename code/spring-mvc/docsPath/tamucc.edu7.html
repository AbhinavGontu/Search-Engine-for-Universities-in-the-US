Island Day, Campus Preview Event 

Join us for an Island Day event! Island Day is Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi's official campus preview event designed especially for prospective undergraduate students and their families.

At Island Day, you will discover the Island University and the many opportunities we can offer you! During our virtual events, you will have an opportunity to login to our unique platform and watch live presentations, student panels, chat with representatives and watch on-demand videos.

What to Expect

We invite you to join other Future Islanders as we showcase the many opportunities available to you at Texas A&M-Corpus Christi.

  • Hear Student Perspectives on our Academic Programs

  • Learn about Student Services

  • Ask Questions and Chat with University Staff and Students 

  • Learn about admissions and next steps after acceptance

  • Learn about financing your education (financial aid and scholarships) 

  • Learn about Islander Housing 

COVID-19 Guidelines for 3/27 Event

To keep our Islander community safe, all event attendees must agree to the following terms prior to registering and engaging in the Island Day event. By registering to the event, you agree to the following: 

  • I will follow CDC, the State of Texas, The Texas A&M University System, Nueces County, and the City of Corpus Christi guidelines/regulations as posted or verbally stated at this event.
  • I will wear a face covering during my entire participation in this event.
  • I will socially distance at least 6 feet from other event participants and staff.
  • I will not attend this event if I have been diagnosed with or suspect I have COVID-19, exhibit any COVID-19 symptoms, or I am awaiting results from a COVID-19 test.
  • I have not been in contract with someone who has tested positive for COVID-19 in the last 14 days.
  • I have not been ordered to self-quarantine by a government official or medical professional.

If you are unwilling or unable to comply with these terms for any reason, you may not participate in Island Day.
