Giving to Vanderbilt

Vanderbilt University FY20 Donor Impact Report

Thank you for making a difference when it matters most.

In a year of monumental change for our campus and our world, we are especially grateful for your support of Vanderbilt University. Despite the many challenges we have faced, our caring community of alumni and friends has provided resources to help us advance our mission of education, discovery and leadership.

Your partnership fuels our students and faculty as they learn, lead, innovate and adapt—and as they contribute to their neighborhoods and our world. Thank you for supporting the promise and potential of our university and its people during a time when it matters more than ever.

The power of philanthropy

On Giving Tuesday Now, our community quickly responded to the immediate needs of our students facing hardship and to the call for advancing COVID-19 research. View a message of thanks from recent graduate Sophia Anderson, who benefited directly from the Student Hardship Fund as well as other philanthropic gifts during her time at Vanderbilt.

Scholarship support, as well as gifts in support of the Vanderbilt experience, allowed members of the Class of 2020 to excel on campus and in their communities. We invite you to read more about their accomplishments, which you helped make possible.

A generous $20 million gift from the William K. Warren Foundation will establish the Warren Center for Neuroscience Discovery, furthering pharmaceutical breakthroughs and treatment for brain disorders such as Alzheimer’s, schizophrenia and Parkinson’s disease.
