Our Schools & Colleges

A degree from one of St. John’s schools and colleges transforms your life. We offer more than 100 degree programs at the associate, bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral levels that prepare you for a lifetime of success. 

Schools and Colleges Research at St. John's Find a Program

See What Awaits

When you attend St. John's, you join a Catholic and Vincentian University with a 150-year legacy of preparing students to succeed. We offer more than 100 majors at our seven campuses and locations, including three in New York City. You will also benefit from our unique study abroad programs, state-of-the-art technology and labs, and a network of more than 190,000 proud St. John's alumni.
