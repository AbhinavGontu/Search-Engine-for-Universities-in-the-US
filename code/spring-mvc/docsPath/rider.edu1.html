2020-2021 Academic Catalog

President Greg Dell'Omo and Program Director Leonard Goduto welcome the Inaugural Cohort of the Ed.D. in Educational Leadership to Rider University.

Welcome to Rider University’s online academic catalog for the 2020-2021 year.

This annual publication is the official catalog of courses, degrees, policies, and degree requirements. You will also find complete information about every undergraduate degree, graduate degree, and certificate program offered. In addition, we provide full course descriptions, sample academic plans, general policies, the academic calendar, honors program information, and numerous other learning opportunities. Each major, minor or certificate also features a general overview that summarizes its specific focus, along with contact information for further inquiry.

Current and prospective students should visit Rider’s website for admissions and financial aid related information. All other policies and student service information can be found in Student Code of Social Conduct, Rider’s student handbook.
