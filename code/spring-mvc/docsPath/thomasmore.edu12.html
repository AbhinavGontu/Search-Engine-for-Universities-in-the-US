St. Elizabeth Healthcare: Sports Medicine 

Top Sports Medicine Care. Right Here.

Whether you’re training for a half marathon, playing a round of golf or coaching your child’s baseball team, we know you’re always on the move. Occasionally, your body can get out of balance from all the jumping, swinging, running and moving that everyday life requires. 

The sports medicine experts at St. Elizabeth Healthcare are here to help restore that balance. If you have a strain in your back or joint pain in your ankle, our team of specialists provides comprehensive, personalized sports medicine care to help you get back to the top of your game, quickly and safely. Call (859) 212-5600 to schedule an appointment today for full-spectrum sports medicine care. 

Sports Medicine Care: What to Expect

During your initial sports medicine appointment, our experts will evaluate your entire musculoskeletal system. This exam ensures that our team can address the full spectrum of your injury, down to each twinge or tweak that might be bothering you. 

After your evaluation, our sports medicine specialist will create a personalized treatment plan for you, pulling in additional St. Elizabeth resources if needed: physical therapists, rehabilitation specialists, athletic trainers and more. Our integrated team works closely together to help minimize the number of appointments you need while increasing your rate of recovery, including 1:1 physical therapy sessions to ensure individualized care and healing. 

We also have onsite X-ray facilities, a full line of orthopaedic bracing and a partnership with orthopaedic surgeons, if surgery becomes necessary down the road. We truly care about the well-being of each patient, and we’re proud to be a one-stop shop for all of your family’s sports medicine needs. 

Sports Medicine Care: Our Team 

When you make an appointment with the sports medicine team at St. Elizabeth, you’re getting personalized care with some of the best experts in the area. Dr. Michael Miller, Dr. Tyler Browning and Dr. James Hahn are all board-certified to provide the most comprehensive sports medicine care available. They are also certified as primary care physicians and/or pediatricians, ensuring that you or your family member receives whole-body care. Call (859) 212-5600 to schedule an appointment – we’ll get you in quickly and help get you back in the game.

We offer a comprehensive concussion program that includes ImPACT™ testing, as well as student-athlete sports physicals and continued treatment and rehabilitation of sports injuries.
