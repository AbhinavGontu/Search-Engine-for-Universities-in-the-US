The Saint Mary's All-Sport Camp is perfect for kids who want to experience a variety of sports and recreational activities during the week. The All-Sport Camp is a Monday through Friday day camp with morning, afternoon, and full day options:

  • Morning - 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM
  • Afternoon - 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM
  • All Day - 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM

We will have two age groups 6 to 9 and 9 to 12. These groups will participate in activities separately and sometimes together. Campers can expect to participate in friendly competitions and fun activities each day throughout the week.

Saint Mary's All-Sport Camp sessions will be using various Saint Mary's College Athletic and Recreation facilities, including possible afternoon sessions at the Aquatic Center. Campers can expect to be inside the Recreation Center, Outside on various fields/courts, and again in the pool on some afternoons.

Information related to drop off, pick up, what to bring etc. will be emailed to you after we receive your registration.
