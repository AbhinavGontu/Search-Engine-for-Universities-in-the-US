Alumnae Travel

 The Great Journey Through Europe
July 8 to 18, 2020


Retrace the "Grand Tour", and elite tradition undertaken by enlightened ladies and gentlemen of the 17th, 18th, and 19th centuries on the Great Journey through Europe. Traverse four countries and the western heart of the Continent on this carefully planned, exclusive itinerary. Cruise the storied UNESCO World Heritage-designated Middle Rhine Valley, passing hillside vineyards of the renowned Rheingau wine region, and meandering through the Netherlands's expansive waterways flanked by polder land reclaimed from the north Sea. Experience three of the world's great railways journeys and delight in the opportunity to experience six astounding UNESCO World Heritage sites.

For more details on the trip, click here to see the full brochure.
