Muskie Athletic Club

Get Hooked on the Muskies

Your membership in the Muskie Athletic Club helps support the Muskingum Athletic Department. The mission of the Muskie Athletic Club is to provide student-athletes with an outstanding athletic and academic experience and to strengthen the overall competitiveness of the Muskingum athletic programs.

Your support helps

At Muskingum, we continually strive to improve programs and facilities so that our teams may compete at the highest level. Your membership dollars support programming for student-athletes, uniforms, and equipment, facility improvements, recruiting efforts, travel for all Muskie teams, and professional development for the coaching staff. 

Join the Muskie Team

Join the Muskie Athletic Club online by clicking HERE. After joining, you will receive a letter in the mail detailing the benefits of your membership. If you have additional questions, please email Muskingum Vice President and Athletic Director Steve Brockelbank at stevenb@muskingum.edu.
