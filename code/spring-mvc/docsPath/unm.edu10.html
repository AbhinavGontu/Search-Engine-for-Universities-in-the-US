WHAT IS AN R1 UNIVERSITY?

R1 is a designation by the Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education, and is reserved for doctoral universities with very high levels of research activity.

The University of New Mexico is the only university in New Mexico with this prestigious designation.
