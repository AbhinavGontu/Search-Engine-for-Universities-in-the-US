Athletics Alumni

 

You spent countless hours in the gym, on the field, or running the trails. Now, return home for special events for Pioneer alumni during PUC’s annual Homecoming celebration.

 

We’ll be hosting alumni games against the current teams in Basketball, Soccer, and Volleyball. You’ll also have the chance to learn about what’s been happening with Pioneers athletics, from Cal Pac honors to service activities off the court.
