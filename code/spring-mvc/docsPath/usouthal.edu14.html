Membership

Be Engaged

By joining the USA Alumni Association, you are not only adding value to your degree, but you are supporting the many programs offered by the association, including student scholarships. Your membership gives back to USA and sets a foundation for generations to come.

Join
SOUTH Magazine

Stay Informed

The USA Alumni Association produces two publications for alumni. South Spots is our monthly e-newsletter, published the first of each month. South Magazine is our alumni magazine published each spring and fall, available in print and online.

1974 Society

Make a Gift

The 1974 Society is major source of funding to build the new MacQueen Alumni Center on campus. Your gift of $1,974, which can be paid over three years, will allow you to be an integral part of this beautiful facility.

Alumni Businesses

Find a Business

The Belltower Business Directory connects you with alumni-owned businesses across the country. The new Directory gives you the opportunity to support Jaguar-owned businesses and to submit your own business for inclusion.
