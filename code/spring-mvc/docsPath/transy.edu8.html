Transy Campus Access

Please login to visit our sites that are only available on campus. If you followed a link and arrived here, you will continue on to the internal site after login. If you are using a public computer or other shared device, please be sure to close your web browser or logout when you are finished so that your access cannot be used by someone else.





Publicly accessible Transy sites

  • www.transy.edu - Transy www site
  • google.transy.edu - Transy e-mail and Google Apps
  • moodle.transy.edu - Moodle course management
  • tnet.transy.edu - TNet site for grades and student information
  • password.transy.edu - Student password change site for your Transy ID
