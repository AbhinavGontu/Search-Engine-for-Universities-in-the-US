Southern University Online

 

Continuing the Southern University Tradition

Southern University Online is an extension of Southern University and A&M College, a comprehensive institution offering four-year, graduate, professional, and doctorate degree programs.  The University is part of the only historically Black Land Grant university system in the United States.  We are student-centered and our primary mission is your success. We share the same rich history, commitment to academic excellence, and student success. 

Through Southern University Online, we are able to provide students virtual access to undergraduate and graduate degree programs, as well as certificate programs and continuing education for working professionals. Our online students enjoy rewarding academic experiences facilitated by world-class faculty. In addition to classroom rigor, we foster and cultivate student success with a host of support services such as remote registration, academic advisement, and access to career services.

Join the Jaguar Nation

At Southern University Online, we believe "your time, is the right time." Our online programs allow you to maintain your family, career, and community obligations while completing your education.  Begin your journey here by requesting more information or scheduling a virtual appointment to speak to an academic advisor or admissions counselor.
