Alumni Data Verification

Have you received an email or postcard asking you to verify your data?

UC Santa Barbara Alumni is working on updating the latest version of the Alumni Directory. We work in partnership on this project with Publishing Concepts, a Dallas-based national company that publishes directories for most of the major public universities in the U.S. The new UC Santa Barbara Alumni directory will be available early next year for purchase.

Please contribute your current contact information so we can not only update the directory but also keep you informed of Gaucho alumni events in your area.

If you have questions about this project, please contact samantha.putnam@ucsb.edu.
Thank you for staying in touch with our great family of Gauchos.
