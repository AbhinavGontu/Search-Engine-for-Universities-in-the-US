Welcome to the Quinnipiac Alumni Community

 

A Bobcat for Life

As alumni, we share the privilege of continuing our relationship with Quinnipiac, and broadening that relationship. Whether it’s by participating in an online career webinar, getting together with friends at an alumni event, making a donation, or mentoring a student, we offer our support. That's because we understand that we are strongest as a connected and committed alumni community.

Who we are in relation to Quinnipiac travels with us. The relationship we’ve built with our school stays relevant and important. Quinnipiac University and its strong heritage pull us back time and time again.

The strength of the Quinnipiac community

A Lasting Foundation for Today... and for Tomorrow

Camaraderie, friendship and a sense of community hold us together, as alumni who have shared the same great university experience. The relationships, grounded in our experiences and campus memories as Quinnipiac alumni stay with us for a lifetime. Time and time again, we go back to those memories and those people. We take the proud Quinnipiac heritage with us wherever we go.

Nothing can take the place of the bonds we forged here — bonds that began at Quinnipiac but grew over time. Pride in our school, our friendships and a sense of community hold us together for today, and for tomorrow.
