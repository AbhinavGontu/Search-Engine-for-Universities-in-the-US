Admissions

Empowering you to explore and discover

As one of the top ten public universities in the Midwest, UW-Eau Claire provides a transformational learning experience and prepares you for a successful career. Our campus strives to create a supportive community where students are our top priority. Here, you'll be immersed in academic, cultural and social experiences that shape who you are and provide the skills and tools you need to compete in today's job market. At UW-Eau Claire, you will be empowered to explore worlds unknown, take the path less traveled and reach your full potential.

Why UW-Eau Claire?

UW-Eau Claire is redefining what higher education should be through our leading-edge curriculum, practical learning and student-focused campus community. Here, students feel motivated and empowered to take risks so they can follow their dreams and pursue their passions. Through distinguished academic programs, life-changing opportunities and unwavering support, you will be prepared for whatever career path you choose.
