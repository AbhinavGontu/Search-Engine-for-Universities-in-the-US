  • Employers

We offer the following services to allow you to meet our students and build a partnership with the university:

  • Online recruiting (through our GradLeaders Job Portal)
  • Multiple career fair events throughout the year
  • Corporate Presentations/Information Sessions
  • On Campus Recruitment Tabling

 

Job Portal

Employers can register for a free account through the NU GradLeaders Online Career Center.  This allows you to post as many jobs, internships, or volunteer opportunities as you would like online. As employers in our job portal you will have the ability to target candidates by GPA, major, program, etc.

Career Fairs  

Career Services offers multiple career fair events through the year. In the spring our annual education fair is held with school districts attending from all over the state of California. 

Internship Policies

  • For-profit organizations must provide appropriate compensation as required by the US Department of Labor.
  • Internships must meet FLSA requirements and be structured as a learning experience*. Refer to the Seven Factor Test.
  • Company email must be used in order to verify your affiliation with the institution.
