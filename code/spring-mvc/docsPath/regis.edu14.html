Services for Faculty

The Regis University Library supports faculty with their teaching, research, and other information needs. 

Subject Librarians
Meet the subject specialist assigned to your department and get an orientation to the resources available through the library. Librarians also offer research consultation appointments for faculty members. If you would like to recommend an item for purchase, please contact the appropriate subject liaison.

Research and Instruction
Learn about research assistance and instruction services available to faculty.

Digital Initiatives & Preservation
The department is a full service media support facility.

Course Reserves
Whether physically at the library or hosted online through the EReserve system, reserve materials can be used to supplement course materials.

Copyright
Links to policies, guidelines, and informational resources.

Room Reservations
When not in use for library services, select classrooms and conference rooms may be reserved for use by faculty.

Faculty Carrels

Jesuit Library Passport

What is the Jesuit Library Passport Program?

The faculty of Jesuit colleges and universities have reciprocal borrowing privileges at all 28 Jesuit member institutions in the United States. The Dayton Memorial Library is a program participant. The program is sponsored by the Association of Jesuit Colleges and Universities (AJCU) library directors.

How do I participate?

If you anticipate traveling to and using other Jesuit libraries, please fill out the linked form. You will need one form per library. Each institution you visit will issue an AJCU Passport Program library card with a barcode specific to that institution's library. Present the form and your Regis faculty photo ID at the Circulation Desk the first time you visit the other institution's library.

What borrowing policies are in effect?

Each lending library sets its own lending policies. Consequently, you may wish to verify which materials may be checked out, lending periods, fine structures, etc. at each library you visit. Most policies are also available via the lending library's web site. Your signature on the reciprocal library borrower form indicates your intent to abide by the policies of the lending library.

How do I return borrowed materials?

Materials checked out from another Jesuit institution may be returned via Interlibrary Loan at the Dayton Memorial Library. Please indicate clearly that they are Passport Program materials. Note that faculty are responsible for materials checked out until they are received by the lending library. Items may be subject to overdue fines if they are received by the lending library after the due date.
