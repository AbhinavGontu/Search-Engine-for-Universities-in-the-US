    A Place Like No Other

    Rigorous and rewarding study. Welcoming and supportive community. Beautiful, vibrant campus and setting. Whitman College — where you can grow, think, explore, thrive and change the world for the better.

    Spring 2021: Staying Safe, Moving Forward

    Whitman College students, faculty and staff have returned to campus for in-person and hybrid learning. Our COVID-19 Dashboard provides information on the health of the Whitman community and the measures we're taking to live, learn and work together safely this spring. Campus buildings are closed to visitors. Visitors wearing face coverings and following social-distancing protocols are welcome to walk through outdoor areas of campus.
