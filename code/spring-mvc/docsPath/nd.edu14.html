Everyday Holiness Podcast

We invite you to listen to our FaithND Podcast, Everyday Holiness. During each episode, we hear from members of the Notre Dame family as they discuss living out their individual vocations and the universal call to holiness.

    
 

Season Four, Episode Seven
Our final episode of the season features Joe Gelchion, a Notre Dame parent and former employee, as he describes strategies for professional and personal success, his gradual understanding of his vocation as a husband and father, and his radical trust in God's plan for his life.
Season Four, Episode Six
Recent graduate Anja Renkes '20 shares her experiences of God's steadfast grace during periods of suffering, the lessons of being part of the Notre Dame Rowing Team, and how her passion for evangelization through sacred art shapes her future.
Season Four, Episode Five
Join Elizabeth and Joe Nava as they offer insights from their first several years of marriage, explain loving each other and their children in a self-sacrificial way, and discuss how they have managed the unexpected suffering brought about by the pandemic and a struggle with mental health.
Season Four, Episode Four
Our fourth episode this season features Sister Mary Lynch, S.S.J., the longtime rector of McGlinn Hall, and she shares her calling to religious life from a young age, some lessons from her experience recovering from cancer, and important principles of building community.
Season Four, Episode Three
Meet Joe Delaney, a longtime leader of the Notre Dame Club of Staten Island and retired partner at Deloitte, as he describes his family, career and service contributions, personal experience of September 11, and his outlook on living a holy life.
Season Four, Episode Two
Fr. David Guffey, CSC, recounts his path as a priest whose ministry has ranged from serving those affected by homelessness to producing inspiring films in Hollywood, including the newly released movie Pray, a biography of fellow Holy Cross priest Venerable Patrick Peyton.
Season Four, Episode One
Join Haley Scott Demaria ' 95 as she shares her perspective on overcoming tragedy and suffering, especially her own experience of a swimming team bus accident that claimed the lives of two of her friends, nearly left her paralyzed, and continues to impact her life in many ways.
Season Three, Episode Seven
For our final episode of the season, listen as Vice President and Associate Provost Fr. Dan Groody, CSC tells about the many migrations in his own life, his special love for people in transition, and the consistency of his vocation throughout various roles as one who serves and tends to matters of the heart.
Season Three, Episode Six
Deacon Mel and Annie Tardy provide insight into the beauty and universality of the Catholic faith, give an inspiring perspective on the hope of racial harmony, and describe the principles of a strong and vibrant marriage.
Season Three, Episode Five
Meet Sister Edelquine Shivachi of the Little Sisters of St. Francis of Assisi as she describes her upbringing in Kenya, her discovery of and entry into religious life, and how her vows have led her to the deeper study of theology at Notre Dame.
Season Three, Episode Four
Hear the story and scholarship of Professor Gabriel Reynolds as he describes his journey to a deeper Catholic faith, his sense of true vocation, and his hopes for interreligious dialogue between Muslims and Christians.
Season Three, Episode Three
For our third episode this season, listen to sacred musician Danielle Rose Hesley '02 as she describes her winding path of discernment to marriage and motherhood as well as her prayerful method of creating music.
Season Three, Episode Two
In episode two, hear senior Cecily Castillo describe her faith journey, its development while at Notre Dame, and the contribution she leaves on campus as she graduates.
Season Three, Episode One
In our first episode of season three, catch up with former Notre Dame Head Football Coach Lou Holtz as he tells us about important moments in his life, his principles to success, and how to find hope during difficult times.
Season Two, Episode Seven
In our final episode of the season, meet senior Maddie Link as she shares with us her story of being a student, using her voice to sing God's praise, and living with a significant visual impairment.
Season Two, Episode Six
Listen to the story of Kevin Phelan '80 MBA, a former business executive who gave up his corporate career to serve in two dioceses grappling with the Catholic Church's abuse scandal.
Season Two, Episode Five
Knott Hall Rector Patrick Kincaid '12 M.Ed. joins the podcast and shares the challenge of losing his father at a young age, the impact of teaching and forming young people, and giving the gift of his music to others.
Season Two, Episode Four
Hear Ron and Beth Grisoli '87, '90 M.A. describe the joys and crosses of their marriage and family life, including loss and distance from family, raising a child with special needs, the upheaval of Hurricane Katrina, and the grace and love that has sustained them along the way.
Season Two, Episode Three
Meet Sr. Benedicta Duna, a 2008 Notre Dame graduate and Sister of St. Francis of Perpetual Adoration. She considers the important moments of her calling, the sacrifices and joys of religious life, and how each person can find God's will in life.
Season Two, Episode Two
Fr. Pete McCormick, CSC, Director of Campus Ministry and chaplain to the Notre Dame Men's Basketball team, discusses the surprises of life and God's plans, the love and lessons of sports, and the people who inspire him as a priest.
Season Two, Episode One
Sofia Carozza '19, the valedictorian of her class, gives a glimpse into her life's pivotal moments, discusses her love of and future in neuroscience, and provides insight into what is truly worth pursuing in life.
Season One, Episode Seven
Fr. Peter Rocca, CSC, outgoing Rector of the Basilica of the Sacred Heart, enlightens us with reflections on his ministry as a priest, the liturgical changes since Vatican II, and holiness lived in the context of religious life.
Season One, Episode Six
Hear Cindy Sescleifer '85 talk about being a mother, walking the Camino de Santiago, and the lessons of being on a continual path of discernment.
Season One, Episode Five
Hear graduating senior Joe Crowley describe his discernment to attend Notre Dame and his calling to education as he moves to the next phase of life.
Season One, Episode Four
Listen to Eric and Jill Sims as they discuss life, marriage, and the challenges of losing a child and caring for another with special needs.
Season One, Episode Three
Meet Marian Appleton, the woman who has responded to thousands of prayer requests each year through FaithND!
Season One, Episode Two
Listen as Audrey Barbe, a third-year exchange student from France, discusses her life and discernment.
Season One, Episode One
Listen to Fr. Pat Reidy, C.S.C. discuss his vocational discernment.
