2020-2021 Wesley College STEM Program Scholarship Recipients
Wesley College’s STEM UR-CATS (Undergraduate Research – Center for Analytics, Talent, and Success) allows for engaged STEM learning in different domains and at varying levels of intensity. In fall 2020, through external federal and state funding, Wesley’s STEM UR-CATS program awarded $86,000 in scholarships to sixteen Cannon Scholars for the 2020-2021 academic year.
Virtual Commencement 2020
Due to the Coronavirus (COVID-19), Wesley is planning both a Virtual Commencement Celebration Ceremony on May 23, 2020 and an On-Campus Ceremony for the Fall to celebrate the Class of 2020.
Founders Day 2020
As we look forward to celebrating the 147th anniversary of Wesley College’s founding, please plan to join us for a celebration of scholarly achievement that has always been a part of our history.

Dina, Ikwuagwu selected as All-ECAC
Wesley College senior forward Chimaobi Ikwuagwu and junior defender Matthew Dina were named to the 2018 Men’s Soccer All-Eastern College Athletic Conference (ECAC) team.
Crystal Trust Foundation Awards Wesley College $250,000
Wesley College is the recipient of a $250,000 grant from the Crystal Trust Foundation to support the College with various needed capital improvements across our campus that will help enhance the overall living and educational experience for our students. 
INBRE Grant Helps Wesley Build New Infrastructure
Wesley College STEM programs have been selected to receive an IDeA Network of Biomedical Research Excellence (INBRE) grant.
