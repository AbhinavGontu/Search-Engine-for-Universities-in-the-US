Faculty

At the University of Massachusetts Amherst, our faculty represents the highest level of academic excellence. In a university that offers over 90 bachelors degrees, 76 master’s degrees, and 47 doctoral programs, UMass professors are incorporating students into their groundbreaking research. The high quality of learning in the classroom is supplemented with a hands-on education in labs, libraries, and field studies throughout the campus.
