Advent Reflections

Reflections for the Season of Advent

November 29, 2020 - December 20, 2020

   

Celebrating the Season of Advent

These reflections for the Advent season are an initiative of the Alumnae Association Board of Directors. We invite you to enter into the season with us through this series of readings and reflections. Now, in its fifth year, we believe that this series is an excellent reminder to all of us that we must do our best to remember the real reason for the season of Advent. We all need reminders to slow down and savor the darkening days and quiet nights of winter. These can be some of the best weeks of the year if we focus on what is really important in our lives. We pray that this Advent season will bring you and your family together in a spirit of love and appreciation of the birth of our Lord.
