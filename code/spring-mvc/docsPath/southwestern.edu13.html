Scholarships and Financial Aid

For a world-class liberal-arts education at one of the top schools in Texas, Southwestern is a great value. We strive to make the Southwestern Experience possible through a comprehensive offering of merit scholarships and need-based financial aid.

When you apply to Southwestern, we will automatically consider you for merit-based scholarships. For need-based financial assistance, Southwestern only requires the submission of the FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid). Our institutional code is 003620.
