Hours and Location

Regis University Archives and Special Collections do not circulate and may be accessed only in the library, by appointment. Patrons that have not set an appointment are not guaranteed consultation with the archivist. Please contact Archives and Special Collections at 303.458.4128 or email archives@regis.edu with any questions.
