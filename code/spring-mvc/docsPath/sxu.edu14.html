  • All-Sports Cup

The CCAC All-Sports Cup is awarded annually to the institution that earns the most points from a total of 12 sports – the top six men's and women's teams – out of the 16 sponsored by the conference (men's & women's cross country, men's & women's soccer, men's & women's volleyball, men's & women's basketball, men's & women's golf, men's and women's tennis, men's and women's track & field, baseball and softball).   Points are awarded based on conference finish in each sport. An additional point is awarded to any school that claims a conference tournament title.
