UMF Fitness & Recreation Center

The FRC is committed to keeping you safe and informed. Please click here to view our COVID-related entry requirements, safety protocols, and current program/area status.

Open to the public as well as University of Maine at Farmington students and staff, the UMF Fitness & Recreation Center is the campus and community hub of activity, health and outdoor recreation.

Hours of Operation

   FRC Pool
Monday-Friday 6am – 8pm Closed
Saturday-Sunday 10am – 5pm Closed

The UMF Fitness & Recreation Center features:

  • A fully-equipped weight room with free weights and machines
  • A large cardio fitness area with equipment
  • Three multi-purpose courts
  • 1/8 mile walking/jogging track (walking only due to COVID)
  • 25-yard swimming pool (currently closed due to COVID)

The Fitness & Rec Center offers a wide range of group fitness classes for all levels and abilities: step-aerobics, Zumba, spinning, yoga, pilates, water workout, kick boxing and more. Plus professional fitness trainers, swim lessons, children’s programs, American Red Cross programs and more.

Mission

The mission of the UMF Fitness & Recreation Center is to offer a wide variety of high quality programs, events, and opportunities in a safe environment to improve the health, fitness and well-being of members of the University and local community while providing a hands-on, positive learning environment for student employees.
