On-Campus Visit Opportunities

 

On-Campus
Campus Tours

Tour campus, see the city in the Dragon Wagon, and hear from an Admissions Counselor about how to become a Blazer.

On-Campus
Academic
Information Sessions

Meet with an academic representative to learn about majors, scholarships, and also get an inside look at where you’ll be spending time learning.

On-Campus
Self-Guided Tour

Tour campus at your convenience with our self-guided tour and listen as our students tell you about each stop on the tour.

Virtual Visit Opportunities

Virtual
Campus Tour

iVisit is a digital hub for everything future Blazers need to know about our home here.

Virtual
Information Sessions

Join admissions counselors and other UAB representatives for information sessions about admissions, academics, and more.

Virtual
Preview Days

Our annual UAB Day and Transfer Day at UAB are going virtual. We’ll host a variety of online sessions throughout the day for you to join.

Virtual
UAB Nights

Meet other students from your area during a virtual event with your admissions counselor.
