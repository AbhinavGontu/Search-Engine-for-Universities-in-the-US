Hunter Henry Center

The Hunter Henry Center's ultra-modern allure adds just the right touch of sophistication for meetings and special events. Numerous spaces of various dimensions are available throughout the complex.

A popular spot for stand up cocktail receptions and other after-hours events is the lobby area. The lobby can be reserved daily beginning at 5 p.m. An upper lounge area, Legacy Hall, is available for more intimate receptions.

The grounds of the Henry Center feature several attractive water features including a large reflecting pool, smaller water gardens and an amphitheater. The entire complex lends itself beautifully to indoor and outdoor events depending on the season.

The Henry Center offers many options for special events in a very accessible location.
