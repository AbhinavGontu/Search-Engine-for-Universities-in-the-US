Customer Service Statement

The university community depends on Directory Assistance for fast, accurate, and courteous communication. First impressions are important and we realize that Directory Assistance often provides callers with a first "glimpse" of the customer service offered by our institution as a whole. We are committed to making every caller feel like a welcome guest and work to ensure that the information we provide is accurate and up to date. Call Directory Assistance and see for yourself that customer service is our top priority. Your source for department, student, faculty and staff phone listings.
