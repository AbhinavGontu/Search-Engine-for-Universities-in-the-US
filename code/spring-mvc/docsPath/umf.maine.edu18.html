Apply to UMF – FREE

Whether you're applying as a First-Year Student (a freshman), a Transfer Student or an International Student, now's the time to apply to the University of Maine at Farmington. Apply FREE today!


First-Year Students (Freshmen)

Each year the University of Maine at Farmington receives applications from prospective First-Year Students (freshmen) from all 16 counties in Maine and from across New England, New York, New Jersey; from coast-to-coast and around the world.

Apply Now – FREE for Fall 2021. 

The University of Maine at Farmington CEEB Code is 3506

Transfer Students

You’ve experienced another school and now you’re considering becoming a part of the special place we have here. We thank you for your interest in us and look forward to working with you on transferring to UMF.

Apply Now – FREE for Fall 2021. 

The University of Maine at Farmington CEEB Code is 3506

International Students

In the past few years, the University of Maine at Farmington has accepted and enrolled First-Year and Transfer students from Canada, Mexico, Japan, Morocco, Myanmar (Burma), Russia and New Zealand, to name just a few.

Apply Now – FREE for Fall 2021. 

The University of Maine at Farmington CEEB Code is 3506

Apply Now – FREE

  • The Common App
  • ApplyMaine
  • Paper applications available by request
