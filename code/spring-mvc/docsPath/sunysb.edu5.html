COLLEGES AND SCHOOLS


College of Arts and Sciences

The College of Arts and Sciences is the intellectual core of the University, spanning the sciences, mathematics, humanities, social sciences and fine arts. Our elite faculty are curious scientists, scholars, artists and thinkers, driven for excellence in their disciplines and poised to mentor the next generation of leaders, thinkers and creators. Explore this college (College of Arts and Sciences)

College of Business

The college prepares students for leadership roles, fosters entrepreneurial growth and conducts the latest research that informs business practice. Our prominent faculty achieve rigorous standards for research productivity and teaching effectiveness. We attract top-quality students and engage them in the highest level of hands-on education. Explore this college (College of Business)

College of Engineering and Applied Sciences

Stony Brook is inventing the future of engineering and applied sciences education, research, and professional practice. CEAS is committed to advancing an innovative, cross-cutting research agenda at the nexus of engineering with medicine and natural and social sciences, well-positioned to drive regional and national economic development. Explore this college (College of Engineering and Applied Sciences)

Graduate School

Stony Brook offers graduate programs across the arts and sciences, engineering, business, journalism, health and medicine. As a member of the Association of American Universities and with our partnerships with Brookhaven National Lab and Cold Spring Harbor Lab, we offer a unique opportunity to become involved in pioneering research. Explore this school (Graduate School)

Renaissance School of Medicine

The Renaissance School of Medicine educates physicians and investigators in the biomedical and clinical sciences so that they are empowered to advance the frontiers of research, education, clinical practice and advocacy. As part of a large research university, we bridge the gap between clinical laboratory sciences and healthcare delivery. Explore this school (Renaissance School of Medicine)

School of Communication and Journalism

Our award-winning faculty educate journalists, public communicators and news consumers to value and transmit information that adheres to the highest standards of clarity, accuracy and reliability. Our school was created specifically to prepare you for a dynamic and evolving multi-media future. Explore this school (School of Communication and Journalism)

School of Dental Medicine

Integrated within Stony Brook Medicine, the School of Dental Medicine has a distinguished history and a vibrant future. The school continuously pursues excellence in education, patient care, discovery and leadership, and advances oral and general health throughout the local and global community. Explore this school (School of Dental Medicine)

School of Health Technology and Management

From baccalaureate to doctoral degrees, the school offers a variety of high-caliber programs that promote professional growth in a supportive environment. We focus on the latest technological advances, exceptional teaching and meeting the growing healthcare needs of our community. Explore this school (School of Health Technology and Management)

School of Marine and Atmospheric Sciences

Our location on Long Island allows students and faculty to explore a variety of habitats and to tap into nearby resources like the National Weather Service. SoMAS faculty are internationally known for their leadership in research in the atmospheric sciences, all the major disciplines of oceanography and sustainability studies. Explore this school (School of Marine and Atmospheric Sciences)

School of Nursing

The School of Nursing prepares nurse leaders at all entries of practice to advance the health of the local and global communities through high-quality educational programs, clinical practice, and public service. The advantage of being part of an academic medical center gives our graduates a competitive edge. Explore this school (School of Nursing)

School of Professional Development

SPD is home to graduate and career development programs that help professionals across fields continue to learn and to increase their earning power. We offer a host of high-quality, professionally relevant programs, including excellent graduate programs in face-to-face and convenient online settings. Explore this school (School of Professional Development)

School of Social Welfare

The School of Social Welfare is committed to building a more equitable society based on the values of human dignity, inclusiveness, diversity, equality, and economic, environmental and social justice. Opportunities exist for collaborative research and training with the health sciences and other disciplines. Explore this school (School of Social Welfare)
