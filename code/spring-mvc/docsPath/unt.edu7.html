UNT is committed to supporting you academically and personally! While the campus is closed in response to Coronavirus (COVID-19), we’re here to help you by phone, email, live chat, video and virtual events. You can contact our international recruitment team at study@unt.edu who can provide more information about UNT's COVID-19 resources for international students.

Begin your university experience in the heart of Texas. Located in Denton, UNT is one of the largest public research universities in the country. Ranked a Tier One research university by the Carnegie Classification, UNT is a catalyst for creativity, fueling progress, innovation and entrepreneurship. One of the most diverse universities in the nation and home to 2,500 international students from 141 countries, UNT is committed to your success. International student support programs and activities include pre-arrival orientation, workshops offering tax and acculturation guidance, Football 101, and an American Thanksgiving Dinner.
