NMHU Alumni Events Postponed

Highlands Family, Alumni and Friends,

Due to the fluid coronavirus situation, out of an abundance of caution and to remain compliant with Governor Lujan Grisham’s request to avoid large gatherings, the NMHU Alumni Office will postpone our future events through June 30 2020.

This includes the Alumni Family Pool Party, previously scheduled for June 12.

 

NMHU will be making regular updates at www.nmhu.edu/c19.

An ongoing list of canceled events can be found here: https://www.nmhu.edu/coronavirus-covid-19-information/nmhu-event-cancellations/

We will be sure to communicate at least two weeks before any event to inform you if that event will continue as scheduled, or be postponed.

Thank you so much for your understanding, and we hope to see you soon!

We have a full year of Alumni Events planned!
