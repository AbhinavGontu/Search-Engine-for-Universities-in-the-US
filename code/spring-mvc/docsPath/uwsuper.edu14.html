Residence Life

Why live on campus?

  • No deposit required
  • Room key access
  • Fully-furnished rooms
  • Location, location, location
  • Worry free utilities/maintenance
  • Higher graduation rates than students who live off campus
  • Juniors and seniors receive a 20% discount!
  • Gender inclusive housing options
  • Our residence halls are safe and secure

At UW-Superior, over 96% of first-year students live on campus. By living on campus, you have the opportunity to live at an affordable cost and have ease of access to academic-related activities. 

Want to live on campus? 

Complete your contract, pick a roommate, and pick a room with Live@UWS. For more information, visit our Forms and Policies section.
