Facility Rental & Reservations

Wittenberg faculty, staff and students may schedule events or activities using the university's athletics facilities through 25Live, Wittenberg's online scheduling assistant.

For other campus inquiries, contact the Assistant Director - Events & Facilities, Deb Howard, via email at howardd2@wittenberg.edu

For inquiries regarding facility rental by outside organizations, click here to submit an HWA Complex Reservation Request Form. If you have questions about our facilities, contact Assistant Director - Events & Facilities, Deb Howard, via email at howardd2@wittenberg.edu
