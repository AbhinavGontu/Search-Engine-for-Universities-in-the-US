Featuring nearly 3,500 students seeking undergraduate degrees in 30 distinct majors, UNC Asheville is a dedicated liberal arts institution. The curriculum, taught by an award-winning faculty, covers everything from biochemistry and psychology to business management and music. Sports are a big part of the school as well, as UNCA fields a variety of both men’s and women’s teams.

Sports

There are many athletic teams at UNC Asheville. Men compete in baseball, basketball, cross country, soccer, tennis, and track and field, while women compete in basketball, cross country, soccer, swimming, tennis, track and field, volleyball, and cheering/dancing.

Cultural Events

Each school year, the Office of Cultural Events presents up to nine concerts featuring major touring performing artists. Past performers have included ukulele aficionado Jake Shimabukuro and the New Orleans-style Lagniappe Brass Band.

FAQs

Are there tours available of the UNCA campus?

You can most certainly check out the campus via a student-led tour. On the tour, you’ll see the library, health and fitness center, student union, dining hall, and residence halls.

Are there events that are open to the public?

Yes, many events are open to the public, including concerts, lecture series, and athletic contests. Visit http://www.unca.edu/ for a list of upcoming events.
