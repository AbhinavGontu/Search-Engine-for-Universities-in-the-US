How to Stay Connected

  • Home
  • Alumni
  • Be Connected
  • How to Stay Connected

In a time of physical distance, a number of fun and productive opportunities are available in order to stay socially close with the Brockport family:


The Port

The Port e-newsletter is here for the academic year. Keep up with the latest news, stories, videos, photos, events, Class Notes, and much more the first Thursday of every month (with a break in January and the summertime). Update your info to ensure you receive The Port and other emails from us all year long.

 

Golden Eagle Opportunity Fund

Be the lifeline for a student in emergency need. Your gift will provide direct, immediate support for students to continue their education in times of hardship. Help cover the costs of students' child care, food, medical or dental expenses, rent or other housing, transportation to school, and more by supporting this emergency fund.

 

Brockport Family Holiday Cookbook

From the Fourth of July to Hanukkah, Easter to Kwanzaa, and everything in between, culinary traditions run deep. The entire college community is invited to help us celebrate our diversity by sharing recipes for the Brockport Family Holiday Cookbook, featuring signature dishes for each holiday throughout the year.

 

"Bport Grad" Facebook Frames

Update your profile pic with new "Bport Grad" Facebook frames. On your profile page, hover over your profile picture and click "Update Your Profile Picture," select "Add Frame," and search "Bport Grad." Your choices: green and gold, of course. While these frames are perfect for alumni of all class years, we're also featuring special frames for 2020 grads. Search "Bport 2020" to access them.

 

Brockport Alumni Connect

In addition to serving as an exclusive networking platform, Brockport Alumni Connect now allows you to find the perfect mentor/mentee connection through an automated matchup system. Here's a how-to. Or, feature your business/service for free in the new online business directory. Watch the business directory training video. Download the Brockport Alumni Connect app in the App Store or Google Play.

 

Zoom Backgrounds

Add a touch of Brockport to your virtual meetings, hangouts, and happy hours this summer with custom Zoom backgrounds. The Office of College Communications has created six to choose from, featuring breathtaking campus scenes: an aerial shot, the underpass mural, the "Joy and Happiness to All the Children of the World" (Special Olympics) fountain, the statue of Prometheus, Eagle Hall, and Hartwell Hall.

 

Career Connections

Leverage the power of the Brockport network, make connections, and find career opportunities on Brockport Alumni Connect or Eagle Links, the College's official LinkedIn group; share job or internship opportunities with students on Eagle Connect; or utilize Career Services for a lifetime of professional guidance.

 

Ellsworth Coloring Pages

Download. Print. Color. Repeat. Keep the kiddos entertained with a fun assortment of Ellsworth coloring pages. Or, dive in yourself to take a break from work, unwind, and get your creativity flowing. (Adult coloring is proven to foster many health benefits.) Share your completed masterpieces on social media using #BportTogether or by emailing us. Our favorite artists will win Bport prizes.

 

Bport Connectors

Interested in virtually sharing your unique talents and hobbies, your knowledge and expertise, your artwork and creations, your professional advice, a day in your life, or your adventures in Brockport and beyond? Now's the perfect time for us to inspire and learn from one another on our YouTube channel, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

 

Have an idea for keeping the Brockport alumni family socially close in a time of physical distance? Check in with the Office of Alumni Engagement by emailing alumni@brockport.edu.
