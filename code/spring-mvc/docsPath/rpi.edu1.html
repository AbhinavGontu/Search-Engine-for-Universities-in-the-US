The Division of Student Life

Preparing Students for Leadership and for Life

Designed to enrich and augment classroom and laboratory studies, the student life experience is a vital part of Rensselaer's undergraduate education.

Institute COVID-19 Website

Get the latest news and official communications from the university regarding COVID-19 (coronavirus).

Athletics

Home to 23 NCAA athletic teams (12 men’s and 11 women’s) including Division I men’s and women’s ice hockey, athletic compliance, and the Student-Athlete Advisory Committee (SAAC).

Campus Experience

Campus Experience, a large and vital residential community, is comprised of the Dean of Students, Student Rights, Responsibilities, and Judicial Affairs, Student Living and Learning, including Residential Commons, Greek Life Commons, Off-Campus Commons, and the Student Union.

Student Success

Student Success provides developmentally specific advocacy, support, and programming for students through our Class Dean model, First-Year Experience, Parent and Family Programs, Graduate Student Experience, Student Success Programs, and the Archer Center for Student Leadership Development.

Student Support and Resource Centers

Through Disability, Health, and Counseling Services, Health Promotion, and Athletic Training, Student Health Services strives to keep students healthy so they can achieve their academic, personal, and athletic goals.

Student Transitions

The Center for Career and Professional Development, International Services for Students and Scholars, Registrar, The Arch, Transfer Services, Veteran and Military Student Services (Yellow Ribbon and ROTC), and Multicultural Programs helps transition students throughout their academic journey.
