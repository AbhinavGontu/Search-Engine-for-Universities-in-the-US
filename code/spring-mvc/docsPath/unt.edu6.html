Future Students

UNT's top priority is the health and safety of our current and future students. As we all continue to adjust our lives in response to COVID-19, we are committed to supporting you academically and personally as you take the next steps toward an advanced education at UNT. UNT Graduate Admissions is here to help you by phone, email, live chat, video and virtual events. Visit our Ways to Connect Resource page for more!

Tier One. Diverse. Professionally Driven.

With 94 master’s and 36 doctoral degree programs, more than 80 online program options and industry-focused degrees offered at UNT at Frisco, a UNT graduate degree will give you the tools you need to rise to any challenge and make a difference in the world around you.

As one of only 16 Carnegie-ranked Tier One research universities designated a Hispanic-Serving Institution, UNT is proud to also have been designated a Minority-Serving Institution and to support an incredibly diverse student population. Additionally, we're a three-time Higher Education Excellence in Diversity award winner.

Explore our free Graduate Webinar Series and learn more about graduate program options. Hear from faculty and staff about degree plans, admission requirements and program outcomes.
