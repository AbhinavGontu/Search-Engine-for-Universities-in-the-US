Minor in Esports

With a minor in esports, students will be prepared for competitive jobs in the fast-growing industry and will learn to lead as they explore the field from both business and communication perspectives.
The 18-credit minor is open to any student regardless of major.

AS in Nursing

Establish a solid foundation for continued learning through a curriculum that integrates nursing theory and hands-on clinical experience with the humanities, science, mathematics and social sciences.

BS in Biology

Program Spotlight

CAS in Special Education

Designed for licensed educators with their master's degree, the completion of this program will increase the types of teaching opportunities, whether in general education, special education or both.
This 30-credit advanced program can be completed in one-and-a-half years and is designed for working professionals.

MS in Human Clinical Nutrition

This unique clinical education offers dynamic coursework in conjunction with clinical placements and service learning opportunities, focusing on a holistic approach to nutrition in preventing and treating disease.
Gain the skills needed to make an impact in the advancement of health, healing and wellness as a practicing nutritionist.

MS in Digital Marketing

Learn the latest digital marketing techniques, and apply these skills through high profile, hands-on projects for real companies, becoming a sought-after 21st century storyteller.
Gain the technical, analytical and creative expertise to thrive as a leader in digital marketing strategy.

MS in Exercise and Sport Science

Be trained as a practitioner capable of eliciting the highest level of athletic performance from your athletes or clients, as well as understand instrumentation and data analytical skills needed to become a professional sport scientist.
Enter a career with endless opportunity and growing demand.

PhD in Social Work

Advance your career in social work and social justice, develop a pedagogical approach and make a meaningful contribution to the current body of social work research.
Social Work practitioners can earn their PhD with SHU’s part-time, online program.
