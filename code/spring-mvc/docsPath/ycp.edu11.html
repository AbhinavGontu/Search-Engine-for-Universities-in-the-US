Come Visit Us!


The Grumbacher Sport & Fitness Center is located in York, PA.  The entrance to the facility is located at the intersection of S. Richland Avenue and Indian Rock Dam Road in Spring Garden Township.

GPS address: 899 South Richland Avenue, York, PA 17403

GENERAL YORK COLLEGE DIRECTIONS


From Harrisburg (Follow I-83 SOUTH) & From Baltimore (Follow I-83 NORTH)

Follow I-83 to Exit 15 (Business 83 / S George St).  Continue on the exit ramp for approximately 2 miles.  Continue straight through 2 traffic lights in the left lane.  You will pass York Hospital on the right.  Turn left at the 3rd traffic light onto Country Club Road.  Continue on Country Club Road for approximately 1 mile.  Turn right onto S. Richland Avenue, staying in the right lane.  Make a right at the first traffic light onto Indian Rock Dam Road / York College Drive.  Continue straight at the stop sign, into the Grumbacher Center parking lot.

From Gettysburg:
Approximately 50 minutes

Head north on PA-116/US-15-BR toward Lincoln Square.  At Lincoln Square take the first exit onto Lincoln Hwy/PA-116/US-30.  Continue to follow Lincoln Hwy/US-30 for approximately 10 miles.  At the traffic circle, take the 2nd exit onto US-30/York Rd.  Continue for 4 miles.  At the next traffic circle, take the 2nd exit onto Lincoln Hwy/US-30.  Continue for 10 miles.  Continue onto W Market St/PA-462 E for approximately 3.2 miles.  Turn right at S. Richland Avenue and continue for approximately 1 mile.  Turn left at the traffic light onto York College Drive / Indian Rock Dam Road.  Continue straight at the stop sign, into the Grumbacher Center parking lot.

From Lancaster:
Approximately 40 minutes

From the Harrisburg Pike, take US-30 W towards York.  Continue for approximately 22 miles.  Turn left onto N George St.  Continue south on N George St for approximately 2.7 miles.  Turn right at the traffic light onto Country Club Road.  Continue on Country Club Road for approximately 1 mile.  Turn right onto S. Richland Avenue, staying in the right lane.  Make a right at the first traffic light onto Indian Rock Dam Road / York College Drive.  Continue straight at the stop sign, into the Grumbacher Center parking lot.
