COVID-19 Information

  • The library is open to RIC Community ONLY. Rave Guardian app and RIC ID must be presented to enter.
  • Masks are required at all times and please maintain social distancing.
  • Levels 1A and 1B are closed until further notice.
  • Free black and white printing is now available for students from the public desktop computers on Level 3. No reservations required.
  • Borrow technology: use our online request form. Other library materials, request via the library catalog.
