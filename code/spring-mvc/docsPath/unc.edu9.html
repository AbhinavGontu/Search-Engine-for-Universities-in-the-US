Events

Please refer to the Transportation and Parking Spring 2021 page for the current status of transit/parking availability and operations.

UNC hosts a wide variety of events on campus including sports, concerts, plays, and VIP guests. Departments hosting events on campus will often need to make special arrangements through Transportation and Parking to accommodate the event. Please plan large events in cooperation with Transportation and Parking.
