Ways to Give

When you give, you invest in the legacy of Quinnipiac University, an institution that is committed to preparing its students to be lifelong learners and enlightened global citizens. Below are some of the ways you can make an impact.

Visit qu.edu/give to make a one-time or recurring gift using MasterCard, Visa, American Express or Discover.

Designations other than the Quinnipiac Fund may be selected in the drop-down menu of the Advanced Search online giving page. Make a Gift

Checks (payable to Quinnipiac University) may be made with a notation indicating the designation of the gift. Please mail to:

Quinnipiac University
AH-DVP
275 Mount Carmel Avenue
Hamden, CT 06518-1908


Gifts may be made over the phone by credit card (MasterCard, Visa, American Express and Discover) by calling Andrew Bernstein '97, director of annual giving, at 203-582-7882.


Outright gifts of long-term appreciated securities (stocks, bonds, mutual fund shares) can provide you with significant tax advantages, including a charitable deduction for its full fair-market value and the avoidance of any potential capital gain tax.

If you will be transferring stocks or bonds to Quinnipiac University, please follow the steps below:

1. Contact Eve Forbes at 203-582-3995 or eve.forbes@qu.edu to provide the name of the security, the number of shares and the gift purpose.

2. Provide the following instructions to your broker:
DTC #: 0226
Receiving Institution: National Financial Services
Account Name: Quinnipiac University
Account Number: 676-965082


Prior to making a gift by cash wire transfer and to ensure proper credit and handling, please contact Eve Forbes, director of gift planning, at eve.forbes@qu.edu or 203-582-3995.


Your employer—or your spouse's employer—may match all or a portion of your gift to Quinnipiac University. Be sure to complete the appropriate matching gift form and send it along with your gift to Quinnipiac University. Visit matchinggifts.com/quinnipiac for a list of matching gift companies. Please contact the Office of Development and Alumni Affairs at 203-582-8660 for more information.

Note: All new scholarship gifts of $100,000 or more are matched 50 percent by Quinnipiac University.

Quinnipiac University Federal Tax ID: 06-0646701

Why Give?

It’s not just what we give, it is how we give that makes the difference. We don’t just give for a moment—we give for a lifetime. The future of Quinnipiac University, and its important legacy, are only achievable in partnership with the entire Quinnipiac community—alumni, parents, faculty and friends. Each of us plays an important role. 

Your generosity is critical to sustaining the Quinnipiac tradition and legacy for decades to come. 

 

We Depend on You

Because …

  • The costs to run and maintain a major university are significant. So, while it may seem like student tuition should be sufficient, the fact is that we need private support in order to bolster and secure the Quinnipiac legacy for the future.

  • While applications for admission are at an all-time high, many prospective students will not have the resources to fund their education at a private and prominent school like Quinnipiac. This is where student scholarships become critical, ensuring that the university can accommodate a diverse, engaged, high-potential student population.

  • Your gifts allow Quinnipiac University to respond quickly to need, and each year thousands of donors invest in the university in numerous, varied ways that help the school grow, thrive and plan for the future.

The Impact of Scholarships

Anthony Boguslawski, MHS ’18

Anthony Boguslawski, MHS ’18, a physician assistant and Iraq War veteran, shares the impact that the Physician Assistant Faculty Student Endowed Scholarship had on his academic career. He is especially grateful to the alumni donors who made his Quinnipiac journey an extraordinary one.

 

Julian Papasian '18

When Julian Papasian '18 was 6 years old, his father passed away, leaving his mother to raise him and his two brothers by herself. For his mom, the prospect of sending Julian to college as a single parent seemed impossible. Receiving the Frederick J. Mancheski Presidential Endowed Scholarship Fund for Engineering made the "impossible" possible.

“Higher education was like a pipe dream, especially at a high-level institution like Quinnipiac University. Because of scholarships, it isn't a pipe dream; it's a reality.”
Julian Papasian '18

Julian, who is majoring in computer science, is one of the many students at Quinnipiac who is benefitting from the generosity of donors. A Quinnipiac education has the power to transform lives, and it goes far beyond a traditional classroom.

Students like Julian discover their passions, develop into leaders and become engaged global citizens through our study-abroad programs, student-faculty research, internships, athletics and performing arts.
