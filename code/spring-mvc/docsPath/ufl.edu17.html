Campus Visit Opportunities

Out of an abundance of caution, all on-campus tours and events have been suspended indefinitely. Please check back in the future for updated information about visit opportunities; we will also post information on our social media.

For Prospective Students

The University of Florida Office of Admissions virtual information sessions will give students and their family members a chance to learn more about our community and the opportunities that make UF a great college experience at an affordable price. The sessions will feature a presentation by one of our admissions officers with information about the University of Florida and our application process, followed by a live Q&A session. Sessions are available Monday, Wednesday and Friday.

Register for Information Sessions

The UF Student Experience Q&A will provide the opportunity to learn more about life as a UF student, academics, and the unforgettable experience of being a Gator. The Q&A session will feature current students who will talk about their different paths and experiences, followed by a live Q&A session. The UF Student Experience Q&A sessions will take place every Thursday at 3:30 pm Eastern Time throughout the spring semester.

For Newly Admitted Students

These sessions are specifically for newly admitted freshman students and their family members, and will give you a chance to learn more about our community and the opportunities that make UF a great college experience at an affordable price. Our admissions representatives will share information about our academic and research opportunities, student activities and support services, as well as the next steps for enrollment. Then, you will have a chance to get your questions answered during our Q&A session.
