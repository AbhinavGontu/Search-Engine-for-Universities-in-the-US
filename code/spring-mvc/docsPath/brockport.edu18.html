Career Connections

  • Home
  • Alumni
  • Be Connected
  • Career Connections

Tap into the power of the Brockport alumni network: Hire an alum, connect with another alum, or provide an internship to a student. We make it as easy as 1-2-3. Use any of the resources listed below to strengthen the Golden Eagle network.

1️⃣ The Brockport Network

Brockport Alumni Connect

This online tool allows you to reconnect with old classmates as well as utilize the exclusive SUNY Brockport environment to expand your professional network. Mentor a student from your computer or phone (or find a mentor) through an automated matchup system, post your business/service in a free online directory, and build connections in a trusted space.

Eagle Links – The College at Brockport Alumni LinkedIn Group

This is the official group of SUNY Brockport, dedicated to connecting alumni and students. Use it for networking opportunities, professional career-related discussions, learning about upcoming alumni events, or simply connecting to friends and classmates.

2️⃣ Jobs

Eagle Connect 

Looking for a job or a job candidate? Is your employer hiring? Does it offer internship opportunities? Alumni and their employers are invited to promote jobs and internship opportunities on Eagle Connect, a content management system to which only Brockport students, alumni, and employers have access. The program's goal is simple: provide Brockport students and alumni with employment opportunities and professional experiences. We hope you consider giving back to your alma mater through this community system.

Promote opportunities to fellow Golden Eagles with the hashtag #BportHired on social media posts.

Instructions for navigating Eagle Connect and posting jobs/internships

If you would prefer to get in touch with an office representative to post a job or internship opportunity on Eagle Connect, please contact (585) 395-2159 or jobs@brockport.edu.

Job openings at SUNY Brockport

3️⃣ Career Services

Career Services provides free career-related support and resources for alumni any time after graduation. Stop by or get in touch online for help with:

  • Career advisement
  • Conducting a job search (including résumés, cover letters, and interviewing)
  • Networking and connecting with alumni
