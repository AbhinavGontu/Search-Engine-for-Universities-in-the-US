    OUR MISSION

    The Thunderbird Athletic Foundation (TAF) is the official fundraising arm of Southern Utah University Athletics. The primary goal of TAF is to enhance the lives of young men and women, providing them resources to be ELITE in competition, the classroom and the community.

      WHY JOIN?

      Charitable contributions to the Thunderbird Athletic Foundation have a direct impact on our ability to provide our hardworking young men and women with a championship level student-athlete experience. The growth of the Thunderbird Athletic Foundation, both in membership and annual dollars raised, is the key driving force behind our ability to holistically develop our student-athletes and compete for championships in the Big Sky Conference and beyond.
