Why NIU Law?

  • Personalized Attention
  • Value
  • Bar Passage and Employment
  • Proximity to Chicago

Personalized Attention

While everyone is experiencing uncertainty because of COVID-19, rest assured that our outstanding faculty will give you a quality, individualized education whether our classes are held in person, online or in a hybrid format.

Learn More

Value

Recognized as a "Best Value Law School" by National Jurist for four of the past five years, NIU Law offers an unbeatable combination of high quality education, excellent outcomes and affordable costs.

Learn More

Bar Passage and Employment

NIU Law has consistently strong employment statistics for each graduating class. Our current ultimate (two year) bar pass rate is 86.57%

Learn More

Proximity to Chicago

Located in a vibrant university community just over an hour from Chicago, NIU Law combines the benefits of a large university campus with access to the Chicago metropolitan area, home to one of the nation’s most dynamic legal communities.
