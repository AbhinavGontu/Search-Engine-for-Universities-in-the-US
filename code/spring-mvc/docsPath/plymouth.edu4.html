Schedule a Visit

Plymouth State is expanding our on-campus and virtual tour offerings and has updated visitor and guest policies to ensure the safety of our campus community. Due to the complex nature of the COVID-19 situation, all buildings on the campus of Plymouth State University will be temporarily closed to the public until conditions improve. You may review our temporary guidelines here.

Virtual Visits and Information Sessions

Guests may register for any information session by clicking the “Schedule Your Visit Now” tab at the top of the page. After signing up for a featured event, attendees will receive an email confirmation containing the session link. All sessions will take place through Zoom.

Livestream Campus Tours

Join a current PSU Panther on a real-time livestreamed campus tour! Guests are invited to virtually follow along as our current student’s tour through various buildings to showcase classrooms, study spaces, residence halls, and other campus features. Questions will be answered live throughout the duration of the tour.

Livestream tours will happen on select Wednesdays and Saturdays throughout the spring at varying times. To register, please click the “Schedule Your Visit Now” tab at the top of the page and select the “Livestream Campus Tour” option under featured events. After registering, attendees will receive an email confirmation containing the Zoom link to attend.

Undergraduate Information Sessions and Virtual Campus Tours

Engage directly with an Admissions Representative to learn more about PSU, our admissions process, and academic programs. Attendees will follow along on a virtual campus tour, have an opportunity to ask questions, and meet some of our current students! Sessions are offered weekly throughout the winter and spring semesters.

Transfer Student 1:1 Information Sessions

Transfer students are invited to connect individually with their Transfer Admissions Counselor to learn more about transfer admissions requirements, credit evaluation, course selection, housing, and more! Please book time to meet with your individual counselor by clicking on their name below.

International Student Information Sessions

International students who are interested in studying at Plymouth State are encouraged to join in on a virtual discussion with Sandy McGarr, the International Admissions Counselor at PSU! Attending a session will allow you to ask questions and learn more about our campus and location, student VISA’s, athletics, housing, and more. Sessions are held at 9:00 a.m. (EST) on Wednesdays and 2:00 p.m. (EST) on Thursdays throughout the spring.

You can also click here to schedule a time to meet virtually with Sandy McGarr!

Parent & Family Office Hours

Parents and guardians may book a time to speak directly with the Director of Admissions to have your questions answered! Sessions are usually 20-30 minutes in length, please click here to book your call or video chat! 

On-Campus Visits

Plymouth State is closely monitoring the COVID-19 situation and is operating under the guidance of local, state, and campus officials. The NH Dept. of Health and Human Services amended restrictions for essential travel to include visits to college campuses and universities. Families traveling from outside of New England for a college visit are not required to travel-quarantine.

On-Campus Small Group Tours

Plymouth State is expanding our on-campus weekday tour offerings to include a limited amount of small group tours! Visitors may enjoy the natural beauty of campus during a 45-minute outside walking tour while following safe social distancing and personal protective equipment requirements. Tour groups will be limited to 2 students with each student being allowed 2 guests for a small group of 6 total people.

To complement the walking tour, we recommend that visitors attend one of our daily virtual information sessions to learn more about academics, our admissions process, and connect with our admissions representatives!

To register for an on-campus visit or virtual information session, please click the “Schedule Your Visit Now” tab at the top of the page. After registering, attendees will receive an email confirmation containing important visitation guidelines and next steps.

Self-Guided Campus Tours

Plymouth State now offers self-guided campus tours through your mobile device! Visitors can access the self-guided tour and learn more about our PSU by downloading our interactive Guidebook app. Visit https://guidebook.com/g/psu/ on your mobile device and tap “download” to get the free PSU guides app!

In addition to this mobile option, a printed self-guided campus tour booklet has been developed for guests to explore campus! The booklet includes a walking map with information on each of the 15 buildings you will see along the tour route. Registration for the self-guided tour is not required, and guests may access these booklets at any time while on campus. The self-guided tour booklets will be available for visitors to pick up in the front vestibule of our admissions office (Russell House). Russell House is located at 27 Highland Street, Plymouth NH 03264. You will be able to enter the front screen door of the vestibule and find the booklets to your left.

Guests may also access a digital copy of the booklet here: PSU Admissions Self-Guided Tour.
