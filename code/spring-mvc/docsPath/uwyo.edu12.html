Learn more about Distance Education

You don’t have to live in Laramie and take courses on campus to earn a top-notch degree at the University of Wyoming. No matter where our students choose to live, UW's distance education programs offer flexibility to achieve your goals. You can even take UW distance courses as a non-degree seeking student! Click here to search UW Distance Education courses.

Cost of Attendance

 

Tuition and Fees

Cost of attendance for the 2020–21 academic year is as follows:

 

Undergraduate Cost Estimate


Wyoming Resident: $16,978

Non-Resident: $30,718

Children of Alumni: $19,153

Graduate Cost Estimate


Wyoming Resident: $6,518

Non-Resident: $16,616

Children of Alumni: $9,056

*Undergraduate cost estimates do NOT include books, travel or personal expenses.  
*Graduate cost estimates are tuition and fees only.
