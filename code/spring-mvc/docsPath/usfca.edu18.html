    Hello all! Currently I am sitting on a rocking chair on the first floor of a 200 year old house enjoying the views of La Have, Nova Scotia. So only a tad different from my normal routine. There is this very large tree that I am looking out on that reminds of one of my favorite spot in Gleeson library.

    Spot the difference, which one is San Francisco and which one is in Canada?

    2)

    I think the fog might have given that one away.

    There are plenty of spots on campus, it might just take you some time to find which one is best for you. This view is actually underneath my spot on the third floor of the library. There are half circle windows that have wide window sills which overlook the city to Sutro Tower and Twin Peaks. It is perfect for long readings.

    So, find your spot. Oh and plot twist: Gleeson Library actually has five floors. There is a lower level that I just found out about last month! I didn’t know one of the stairways actually lead to more study areas! To prove it here is my face when I found the lower level.
