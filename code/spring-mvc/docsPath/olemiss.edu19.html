COVID-19 Update for Prospective M.S. and Ph.D. Applicants

The Department of Civil Engineering recognizes the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on our prospective applicants. We understand that many applicants are not able to take a GRE test to complete applications on time. Hence we temporarily waive the GRE requirement for admission for the 2021 Spring, Summer, and Fall terms.
