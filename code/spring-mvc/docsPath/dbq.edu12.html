Greetings From Your Alma Mater!

On behalf of the Office of Advancement and Alumni Engagement, we hope you and your family are safe and healthy.

We can’t wait to see you again, whether it be at our campus home, an alumni event, or a personal visit. Until then, here are several ways you can stay involved and connected with University of Dubuque!

. . . . .

THE OFFICE OF ALUMNI ENGAGEMENT PROVIDES OPPORTUNITIES YEAR-ROUND TO HELP YOU STAY CONNECTED!

Follow the University of Dubuque Alumni Association social media accounts to connect with fellow alumni and friends:
Facebook: facebook.com/UDSpartanNation  |  Twitter: twitter.com/UDSpartanNation  |  Instagram: instagram.com/udspartannation

Stay up to date with news and events at www.dbq.edu/Alumni/Events.

View photos from Alumni Events, Homecoming, and Commencement at udalumni.smugmug.com. You can even download photos for free or order prints.

Tell us what’s new with you! Have you moved, gotten married, welcomed a new baby? Share your information and milestones at www.dbq.edu/AlumniUpdate. 

THE UNIVERSITY CONTINUES TO SHARE UPDATES, VIRTUAL SERVICES, AND MORE.

Find updates on our response to COVID-19 at www.dbq.edu/COVID-19.

Follow University of Dubuque’s official social media accounts: 
Facebook: facebook.com/UDubuque  |  Twitter: twitter.com/UDubuque  |  Instagram: instagram.com/universityofdubuque

View UD Talks with President Bullock at www.youtube.com/UDBQMedia.

Participate in Campus Ministry Worship Services at facebook.com/CampusMinistryatUD and instagram.com/spartanministry.

Watch daily prayers with the Seminary at www.facebook.com/DubuqueSeminary.

. . . . .

SHARE A GRAD GREETING at www.dbq.edu/Alumni/GradGreeting. Students love hearing advice and words of encouragement from fellow Spartans!

REFER PROSPECTIVE STUDENTS to UD! Know someone who is searching for a college to call home? UD is moving forward in anticipation of another stellar entering class for fall 2021, and our admission team is here to help at www.dbq.edu/Admission. 

SUPPORT CURRENT AND FUTURE UD AND UDTS STUDENTS - THE NEED IS GREATER NOW MORE THAN EVER. We are deeply moved by the response from Spartan Nation’s alumni and friends. Let’s continue to work together to find ways to support and invest in students, so their journey to graduation is not interrupted during these uncertain times. Join us at www.dbq.edu/Invest.
