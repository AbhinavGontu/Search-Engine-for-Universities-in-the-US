Welcome to The Inn at Ohio Northern University

Experience the comfort and charm of The Inn at Ohio Northern University. This full-service choice among hotels in Ada, Ohio is located in a rural village setting, on a beautiful 300-acre University campus. The Inn at ONU is just minutes away from walking and biking paths, the Freed Center for the Performing Arts, Presser Recital Hall, Elzay Gallery of Art and the ONU Dial-Roberson Stadium. Our convenient location is perfect for campus visits, social events, romantic getaways, business travel and weekend leisure.

The Inn at ONU welcomes meetings, groups, and special events. Thanks to over 3,000 square feet of flexible meeting space, groups as large as 100 people can convene a seminar, meeting, wedding or other event. Offering state-of-the-art technology, certified meeting professionals and hospitality experts, our goal at The Inn is to ensure that your next business conference, reception or personal event is truly a success. Call the Inn at ONU today to request your complimentary event consultation and learn why we are the choice for incredible meeting and accommodation needs among hotels in Ada, Ohio.

The Inn at ONU Includes:

  • Deluxe guestrooms featuring one king or two queen beds.
  • One- and two-room suites; specialty rooms featuring a fireplace.
  • Over 3,000 square feet of function space for events for up to 100 people.
  • Located on the Ohio Northern University campus.
  • Business travel amenities such as wireless high speed Internet.
  • A 24-hour fitness center with state-of-the-art equipment.
  • Pub open evenings for cocktails, light menu, with a wide screen television for viewing.
