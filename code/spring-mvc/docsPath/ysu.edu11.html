Powering the Penguins

A Commitment to Craftsmanship and Community Service

From the construction of Stambaugh Stadium in 1982 to countless projects across campus, NECA-IBEW Electricians member contractors and union electricians have been intertwined with Youngstown State University.

We bleed the red and black of the Penguins. And, giving back to the university and its student body is a priority for us.

You’ll see us mentoring tomorrow’s engineers involved with the YSU Student NECA Chapter. We also fund a scholarship for those pursuing degrees that benefit the electrical industry.

YSU is a bedrock of our community, and we believe in supporting it.

Our Story

NECA-IBEW Electricians is a professional organization of the Mahoning Valley’s world-class, union electrical contractors serving Northeastern Ohio, Western Pennsylvania and beyond.

Our member contractors and union electricians strive to deliver high-quality commercial, industrial and residential electrical service, along with staying at the forefront of technology and educational opportunities as the industry evolves.

We are affiliated with the Mahoning Valley Chapter of the National Electrical Contractors Association and International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers Local 64 in Youngstown and Local 573 in Warren.
