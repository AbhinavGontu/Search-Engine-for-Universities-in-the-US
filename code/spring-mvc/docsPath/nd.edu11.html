Songs of Notre Dame: A Lenten Offering

During the season of Lent, FaithND will provide meditations on this season of preparation through musical offerings from the many Notre Dame choirs and sacred music ensembles. Songs will be featured on Ash Wednesday, each Tuesday and Saturday of Lent, and every day of Holy Week through the Octave of Easter. We invite you to lift your hearts in prayer to God with the beautiful sounds of sacred music from Our Lady's University.

Ash Wednesday


"God so Loved the World"
Notre Dame Liturgical Choir

Saturday After Ash Wednesday


"He'll Be with You"
Voices of Faith Gospel Choir

Tuesday of the First Week of Lent


"The Summons"
Notre Dame Folk Choir

Saturday of the First Week of Lent


"If There Be Tears"
The Vocare Ensemble, Notre Dame-Newman Centre Dublin

Tuesday of the Second Week of Lent


"I Will Lift up Mine Eyes"
Notre Dame Children's Choir

Saturday of the Second Week of Lent


"Wondrous Love/Cuán Admirable Amor"
Notre Dame Magnificat Choir

Tuesday of the Third Week of Lent


"Coventry Litany of Reconciliation"
The Vocare Ensemble, Notre Dame-Newman Centre Dublin

Saturday of the Third Week of Lent


"Love is His Word"
Notre Dame Folk Choir

Tuesday of the Fourth Week of Lent


"Jesu, the Very Thought of Thee"
Notre Dame Liturgical Choir
