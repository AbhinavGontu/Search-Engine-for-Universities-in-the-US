All events are free, virtual and will be held from 7:30-8:30 p.m.

Please register ahead of time. Attendees will be sent a presentation link on the morning of the event. For any questions, please email communications@wells.edu.

Jan. 20, 2021

"How to apply for a job with the Federal Government" hosted by Heather Kowalski '90. Heather breaks down the application process and shares best practices on working for the Federal Government.

Click here to watch

Feb. 10, 2021

Discover the "Hidden Stories from Aurora's History." Some of Aurora's settler families arrived here enslaved. Others found this village on their search for a free life. We are pleased to have Dr. Linda Schwab -- a 1973 Wells graduate and Aurora Village Historian -- who will tell some of the rediscovered stories of Aurora's long African-American history.
