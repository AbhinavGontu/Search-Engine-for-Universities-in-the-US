Plymouth State University has all of the program options and opportunities of a university plus a friendly, small-town vibe in a spectacular setting. Far from the sameness of many colleges and universities, Plymouth State is an innovator where you can get the skills to become a leader in the field. It’s different up here, because at PSU you have the freedom to see how much further you can go.

The Plymouth State Learning Model

PSU’s Integrated Clusters feature interdisciplinary, project-based learning with an emphasis on open education principles. The Wall Street Journal reported that Plymouth State ranked first among public colleges and universities across the country for our ability to improve students’ critical thinking abilities.
