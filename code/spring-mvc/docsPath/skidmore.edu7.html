Talk to Us

  • Our Office of Gift Planning »
  • Call 518-580-5658

When you create a future gift for Skidmore today, you join a growing tradition of philanthropy that began when our founder, Lucy Skidmore Scribner, left nearly her entire estate to the College. Skidmore’s Legacy Society now consists of nearly 900 alumni, faculty, parents and friends who have supported the College with a planned gift commitment.

Gift planning (sometimes called deferred giving) can support everything from scholarships and internships, to faculty support and athletic programs. Your planned gift – no matter what size – can leave a legacy that ensures a transformative education for future generations and the growing strength of this remarkable institution.

Creative thought matters.  So too does creative planning. Contact Andy Campbell, Director of Gift Planning, and discover the many tailored, often simple ways to combine your philanthropic, retirement and estate planning goals.
