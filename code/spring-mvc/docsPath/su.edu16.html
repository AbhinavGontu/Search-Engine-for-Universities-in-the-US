Welcome!

Welcome to the Shenandoah Music Theatre Database (SMTD)!

This site primarily serves to document the history and productions of the Shenandoah Summer Music Theatre, a summer stock theatre in Winchester, Virginia.

Click on “Productions” in the left menu to view details about each show, or use the search tool to find a production, actor, or crew member.

We need your help!
The SMTD represents hundreds of hours of hard work, but it’s far from being complete. If you are – or have been – involved with SSMT productions, please contribute to the Database by submitting your biography and headshot.
