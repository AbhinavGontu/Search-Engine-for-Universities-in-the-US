Parking + Transportation

Announcements

All parking regulations apply and are currently being enforced (including AMP timed parking).

Mission Statement

The mission of Parking and Transportation Services is to manage and enforce a limited number of parking spaces which are available to students, faculty, staff and visitors, plus work cooperatively with the city of Eau Claire to provide timely transit transportation to all members of the university community.
