University Advancement


Under President Ali. A Houshmand’s leadership, Rowan University has remained bold and unwavering on its course to change the face of public higher education. Now, Rowan is rising to new heights of excellence with the launch of its first-ever comprehensive campaign, RISING: The Campaign for Rowan University.

Affordable, accessible and quality education remains at the forefront of the University’s mission to change lives regionally, nationally and globally. This campaign has set a goal of $120 million to fuel Rowan's continued rise, focusing on three key areas: changing a student's life, supporting new discoveries and providing learning and living environments that inspire.

Giving during the course of the campaign can make a direct impact in all three areas and propel the institution farther than ever before. Join us as we rise together in efforts to advance Rowan University's legacy and deliver long-lasting results.
