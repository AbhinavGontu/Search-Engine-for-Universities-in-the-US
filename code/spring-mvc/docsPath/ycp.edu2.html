Why use Summon before Google Scholar?

Summon lets you limit your searches to specific kinds of sources (books, journals, magazines, etc) and provides options to filter results by both subject and discipline.

The catalog connects you to the physical books, dvds, and magazines our library has to offer.

Find materials easier and limit contact by placing a hold on items in our catalog. We'll email you when they are ready to pick up at the main desk.

Unable to pick up your materials? Send us a message to discuss alternative delivery options.
