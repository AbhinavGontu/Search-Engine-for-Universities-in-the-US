More than a campus. A home to experiential learning.

Our campus in the heart of Raleigh, North Carolina is home to a vibrant community of students and faculty, all working together in an environment that’s hands-on, adaptive, and nimble – with career preparation built in from start to finish. Our immersive curriculum and innovative teaching methods ensure our students graduate career-ready and prepared to thrive in the organizations of today and tomorrow.

Your dream internship = your first job.

Internships provide more than a competitive edge – they forge connections, build confidence, and ensure career preparation. That’s why you’ll secure one during your time at WPU. Find an internship that enhances your professional skills and bolsters your resume, and join the 67% of WPU students who land jobs directly through their internships.

Internship Opportunities

At WPU, work in your dream career, today.

Career planning shouldn’t be an after thought or left until late in your college experience. At WPU, it starts your first day on campus. The Career Design Center can help you plan for tomorrow, build a stellar resume, and take the first steps to landing your dream job, today.
