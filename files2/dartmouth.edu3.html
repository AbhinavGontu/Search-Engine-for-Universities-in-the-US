Whistleblower Policy

Dartmouth is committed to maintaining a community where faculty, staff, and students are free to raise good faith concerns regarding the College's business practices; Dartmouth encourages such reporting. Dartmouth will not retaliate against any person who, in good faith, has reported a suspected violation of law or made a complaint against Dartmouth or another individual or entity with which Dartmouth has a business relationship.
