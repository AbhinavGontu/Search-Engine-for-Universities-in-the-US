Championships

2020-21 Conference Championships

Calendar and Schedule Subject to Change

 

FALL

 

2020 Women's Volleyball - TOURNAMENT HOME

Moved to March 2, 5, 8, 2021(All games played at campus sites, highest seed hosts)

Bracket: http://www.heartofamericaconference.com/article/4275.php

Format

  1. The four top teams from both the North and South divisions will advance to postseason.

  2. The regular-season champion will be the 1st seed in the draw with the 2nd seed going to the top qualifier from the other division.

  3. 2 Automatic Berths to NAIA National Championship: Heart Regular-Season Champion; Heart Tournament Champion. If the team is the same, then 2nd berth goes to tournament runner-up

 

2020 Men's Soccer - TOURNAMENT HOME

Moved to April 10, 14, 17, 2021 (All games played at campus sites, highest seed hosts)

Format

  • 8 Teams - Higher Seed Hosts - Single-elimination
  • 2 Automatic Berths to NAIA National Championship: Heart Regular-Season Champion; Heart Tournament Champion. If this team is the same, then 2nd berth goes to the tournament runner-up

 

2020 Women's Soccer - TOURNAMENT HOME

Moved to April 3, 7, 10 (All games played at campus sites, highest seed hosts)

Format

  • 8 Teams - Higher Seed Hosts - Single-elimination
  • 2 Automatic Berths to NAIA National Championship: Heart Regular-Season Champion; Heart Tournament Champion. If this team is the same, then 2nd berth goes to the tournament runner-up

 

2020 Men's and Women's Cross Country

November 7 - Hosted by Baker University
 

Format

  • Team champions earn team Automatic Berths to NAIA National Championships
