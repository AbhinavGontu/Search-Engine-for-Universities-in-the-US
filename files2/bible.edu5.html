Complete Your Degree With Us

Our Focus is on Your Journey to Fulfill God's Purpose for Your Life

LBC | Capital is here to help continue your educational journey.

We understand that having your educational goals disrupted can be challenging and somewhat daunting. Here at LBC | Capital, we are committed to helping you find the best pathway to complete your degree program—bachelor through doctoral.

As a Faith Theological Seminary (FTS) approved college, LBC | Capital offers degrees taught from a biblical worldview by professors who care about you as an individual and want to assist you to fulfill God’s purpose for your life. You can choose from classes that are either 100% online or in person at locations in Lancaster, PA, Washington, D.C., and Philadelphia, PA. Our degree programs are certified by the Maryland Higher Education Commission (MHEC) and accredited in both Pennsylvania and Maryland.

Since 1933, LBC | Capital has held to its mission to educate Christian students to think and live a biblical worldview and to proclaim Christ by serving Him in the Church and society.

If you desire a sense of belonging in your education, we hope you will take the next step to join the LBC | Capital family today.

Financing Your Education With Us

If you are a FTS Qualifying Student, you will not pay, nor will LBC | Capital charge you, any tuition or fees in excess of the lesser of these:

  1. the remaining amount that you would have paid to FTS to complete the program enrollment contract you had with the seminary.
    OR
  2. LBC | Capital’s otherwise applicable tuition and fees.

For example, if the student paid tuition in full to FTS, the student may be charged only the cost of books and materials to complete the LBC | Capital degree program. If partial tuition was paid to FTS, the student may be charged no more than the lesser of the balance due to FTS, or LBC | Capital’s otherwise applicable tuition and fees.

LBC | Capital has a Degree Program that Meets You Where You Are in Life

At Lancaster Bible College | Capital Seminary & Graduate School, our degree programs and flexible learning opportunities make it easy for you to prepare for the role God is calling you in the church or society.

Whether you are searching for the right on-campus or online education, aiming to finish your undergraduate degree, or looking to pursue or complete a master’s or doctorate degree, LBC | Capital has the programs and faculty to help you get there.

How Classes Are Offered

Bachelor Program
Our adult education programs are designed for people with busy schedules so you can balance working full-time, raising a family, ministry and more. Our B.A. in Biblical Studies is offered online or in-class at our Washington, D.C., Lancaster, PA or Philadelphia, PA locations. What makes our program unique? Courses are just six weeks long and meet just once a week, usually evenings or weekends. But best of all, your LBC education will be taught from a biblical worldview. Learn more.

Master and Doctoral Programs
We combine the best of online learning with on-site instruction from faculty with real-world experience – no long-term residency required. This way, you can stay connected to your ministry or job while earning your degree without sacrificing that crucial face-to-face, life-on-life component of learning. Learn more.

Have an Impact on the Church & Society

Our graduates are making a real difference in the world for Christ. From leading a company, ministering in a church, fighting social injustice or educating the next generation, our students are equipped to change the world.
