We don't want to be good. We want to be great.


I am excited about the combination of our new and returning student-athletes' talent in women's basketball this year. 

The Quakers look to build upon last season’s success which saw the team advance to the Old Dominion Athletic Conference (ODAC) Tournament championship game for the sixth time in the last 10 years. Guilford has captured three ODAC Championships and made four NCAA appearances in Coach Stephanie Flamini’s tenure. The Quakers are working hard and look forward to returning to competition in the spring semester.

Our success also depends on you. As a valued supporter of our team, you are integral to how well we do. You can help us address funding needs for uniforms, equipment and travel expenses.
