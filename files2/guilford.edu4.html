Guilford Forward Fundraising Challenge 

 

The Board of Trustees on Jan. 5, 2021, set out to resolve Guilford College’s financial challenges through a highly collaborative effort involving faculty, staff, students, alumnae/i, parents and friends. As part of this effort, members of the College community are fervently and graciously rallying to raise $6 million in budget-relieving funds for the FY22 budget.


All gifts to the $6 million goal must be received by the College according to the following timeline:

  • At least $2 million or before March 31, 2021
  • A total of at least $4 million on or before May 31, 2021
  • A total of at least $6 million on or before Jan. 31, 2022

How Can You Help?

You may give online to the Guilford Forward Fund, to be applied to the College's FY22 operating budget.

 

Individuals who wish to make a sizable gift with the option of designating that gift should contact:

You may also reach out to any of the members of the Fundraising Challenge Team (in the drop-down below) or any member of the Advancement Team with any questions you might have.

 

How We Got Here


Following heartfelt and constructive criticism from College constituents to a proposed plan to terminate a significant number of tenured and non-tenured faculty and to eliminate a host of long-standing academic programs, the Board of Trustees developed a process in late 2020 to identify an alternative plan for balancing the FY22 operating budget. The Guilford Forward budget-relieving fundraising goal is a fundamental component of this plan.

Where We Are Going

In building a sustainable future for Guilford, working teams of College constituents are focused on four key areas:

  • Enrollment Stabilization and Recruitment
  • Donor Challenge/Fundraising
  • Faculty and Staff Collaborative
  • Constituent Engagement

The teams include representative members of the board, faculty, staff, students and alumni. Other working team members are being added as well.
