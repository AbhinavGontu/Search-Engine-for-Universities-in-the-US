Competitive Clubs

Maloneâ€™s competitive club program has recently been expanded and offers students with a variety of interests opportunities to participate in contests against students from other institutions. All programs will be supervised by qualified staff members and will utilize campus facilities.

Interested in learning about Malone's Competitive Clubs? 

Visit our athletics page and select Sports, then Club Sports. 

We offer

  • Men's Rugby 
  • ESports
  • Disc Golf
  • Men's Volleyball
  • Ultimate Frisbee
  • Cheerleading
  • JV Baseball
  • JV Women's Soccer
  • JV Men's Basketball
  • JV Women's Basketball

Additionally, our cross-country program welcomes many levels of runners and often competes in multiple levels of meets annually, depending on the competitive level of the runner. 

Check back as we are developing more programs each year!
