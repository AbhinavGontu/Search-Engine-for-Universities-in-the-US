Official Georgia Southern University License Plate

The official Georgia Southern University license plate is available from any tag office in the State of Georgia. Learn more about pricing and distribution from the Georgia Department of Revenue’s Motor Vehicle Division.

A Portion of proceeds will support the REACH Scholarship

In 2014, Georgia Southern University announced that it would be the first University in the University System of to double-match funds for the REACH Scholarship (Realizing Educational Achievements Can Happen), Georgia’s needs-based scholarship program. A portion of the proceeds from the purchase of your Georgia Southern license plate will be used to support the REACH Scholarship.

During the program, REACH Scholars receive a renewable annual tuition scholarship of $2,500 for use at a HOPE-eligible institution. Georgia Southern will contribute $5,000 to the program.

The plate was designed by Georgia Southern graphic design alumna Victoria Sprankel.
