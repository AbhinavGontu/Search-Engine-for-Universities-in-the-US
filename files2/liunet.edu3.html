The Land of
Great Gatsby

At the turn of the 20th century, opulence spread on the North Shore of Long Island redefining the area known as the "Gold Coast". Wealthy industrialists, bankers and entrepreneurs built their lavish estates here, including the daughter of C.W. Post - Post cereal magnate. Long Island University's Post campus uniquely resides on the 329-acre estate formerly known as Hillwood.
