  • Winter 2021 Snowsports School

    Dartmouth Recreation is offering a four-lesson Snowsports School for students approved to be on campus during winter term 2021.  Classes are offered for beginning and intermediate skiers and snowboarders.

    Registration for wintersport classes opens on Wednesday, January 13. 

    Classes begin the weekend of February 6 and 7.
