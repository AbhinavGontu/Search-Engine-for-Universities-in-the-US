What is the Sun Devil Club?

The Sun Devil Club is the primary fundraising arm for Sun Devil Athletics. A 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. The Sun Devil Club, through the support of its members, is dedicated to providing resources which afford Sun Devil student-athletes the opportunity to thrive and grow.

When you become a member of the Sun Devil Club, you are enhancing the student-athlete experience and investing in their futures by:

  • Funding scholarships for in-state and out-of-state tuition
  • Supporting academic programs
  • Implementing an innovative approach to championship life, social impact and community embeddedness opportunities
  • Ensuring the health and well-being of all student-athletes to perform at an elite level
  • Building state-of-the-art facilities

For the 650-plus Sun Devil student-athletes competing in 26 varsity sports, Sun Devil Club support is the foundation for their collegiate experience, which is unlike any other in the nation.

Only together can we create this experience, and change the lives and shape the futures of countless young men and women.

Together our potential is limitless!

Go Devils!

 

How can you help?

Anyone can join the Sun Devil Club and any amount helps our student-athletes! Sun Devil Club benefits begin for as low as $9/month or $100 annually. You can even designate gifts to go toward particular sports, programs or other more specific purposes!

Invest in our student-athletes today!
