The Cooper Union Office of Alumni Affairs & Development plans a wide array of events aimed at informing and engaging alumni, parents, donors and friends. From career development and networking to tours of student exhibitions, the annual Reunion Weekend and events around the country, we hope you will join us to reconnect, reunite and support Cooper. We also invite you back for our robust schedule of public programs and exhibitions each semester.

If you are looking for events open to Cooper Parents, please click here.

For questions regarding alumni events, please contact us at (212) 353-4139 or alumni@cooper.edu.
