King's College is hosting a variety of ways to learn about Admission & Financial Aid while making safety a priority.

We are following the updated guidelines issued on Monday, March 1, 2021 by our state governor. To read the details, use this link.

 

Accepted Students Day

We are excited to host and celebrate with an in-person Accepted Students Day. This unique opportunity is for our accepted students and will be held on the remaining available days of your choice (click to register): 

  • Sunday, April 25

Arrive early and take advantage of our early bird tour at 8:30am or attend a Financial Aid Presentation.

Formal program begins at 10:00am. Students will:

  • Watch a virtual welcome message from our new president Rev. Thomas P. Looney
  • Receive Admission & Financial Aid information
  • Go on a Campus Tour with a King’s Admission Ambassador
  • Meet with faculty in engaging academic sessions
  • Learn about student services and athletics
  • Join a parent or student centered panel presentation 
  • Tour the School of Nursing on the Sunday, April 25th event

Due to the growing concern of the pandemic, we will also be taking extra precautions to ensure the safety of all accepted students and guests are met. At this event, students & their guests will receive a pre-packaged lunch, and we will follow CDC guidelines to ensure social distancing standards are met. Thank you in advance for understanding and working with us to ensure the safety of everyone involved.

 

Sneak Peek Class Week

A Virtual Introduction to King's College Classes

Excited to start your college journey or want to know what class at King's College is like? Join us the week of March 22nd to the 25th for our Sneak Peek Class Week! This event is for high school seniors who are currently accepted into King's College. You will have the opportunity to experience virtual college class activities and have a Q&A with our King’s College professors. Classes across many academic disciplines are available. Students are welcome to register for multiple classes and sit in on any Sneak Peek Class that interests them.

  • Business Sneak Peek Class Week Monday March 22, 2021 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Political Science Sneak Peek Class Week Monday March 22, 2021 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Engineering Sneak Peek Class Week Monday March 22, 2021 4:30 PM - 6:00 PM
  • Education Sneak Peek Class Week Tuesday March 23, 2021 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Career Planning Sneak Peek Class Week Tuesday March 23, 2021 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Biology Sneak Peek Class Week Wednesday March 24, 2021 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Spanish Sneak Peek Class Week Wednesday March 24, 2021 3:30 PM- 5:00 PM
  • Mass Communications Sneak Peek Class Week Thursday March 25, 2021 03:30 PM - 5:00 PM

Register for Sneak Peek Class Week

 

Personal Visit

Experience a glimpse into life at King's with a 90 minute visit Monday-Friday. Visits on Saturdays are available as well. The Saturdays in May are dedicated to our Accepted Students. The visit will include a private campus tour given by an Admission Ambassador, as well as an Admission & Financial Aid information session. The tour will allow you to view a classroom, a Residence Hall Room, the Sheehy-Farmer Campus Center, as well as the McGrane Gymnasium. 

Read the precautions and register here

 

Online Sophomore/Junior Discovery Day 

Discover the next chapter in regard to King's College by signing up for the online Open House on Sunday, May 16 from 10:00 a.m.-12:00 p.m. 

This event is designed for students who are sophomores or juniors in high school.  This online open house event will provide students an overview of the college search process, how to apply for admission and financial aid, video campus tours, a time to learn about majors, and most importantly, you'll have the opportunity to attend sessions with the people who make King's College a special place.  You may register for this event online by clicking here. 

Read the precautions and register here

 

Virtual Visit 

In signing up for a virtual visit you will receive a link via your preferred email to speak with an Admission Counselor, face to face.  During your virtual visit call, you can speak to the Counselor about such topics as: Financial Aid, Academic Major, Campus Life, Athletics & Next Steps. We hope this option meets your expectations under this unique circumstance. Thank you in advance for adjusting. We look forward to meeting you and being helpful in your journey to higher education especially in regard to King’s College.

ROTC Information Session 

Meet with the King’s College Army ROTC department's Enrollment Officer, Bill Ramsey GS, and discuss the leadership and scholarship opportunities offered by Army ROTC as well as future service and employment as a United States Army Officer. Army ROTC offers 2-5 year full tuition and fees scholarships to qualified high school students to attend college and train to serve as future Army Officers.  The scholarships are currently open to any major offered at King’s. Rising seniors in high school may apply here: Scholarship Application 

A session will cover:

  • The national and local academic scholarship process.
  • Time commitments both during and after college.
  • The academic and military training available to King’s College students.

ROTC sessions are available Monday, Wednesday or Friday from 10:00am-2:00pm. Select "ROTC" on the APPOINTMENT FORM.
