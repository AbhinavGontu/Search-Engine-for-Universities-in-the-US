Welcome to the HealthCareID Solution Center. This system allows you to change your password and unlock your account, even if you have forgotten your password.

 

To learn more about HealthCare IDs, click HealthCareID Guide.


To change your HawkID password, click HawkID Tools.


If you are experiencing a problem with your HealthCareID and would like to speak with someone, please call the HCIS Help Desk (319) 356-0001.

 

  • Reset (I forgot my password) You will need to reset your password if you have forgotten it. Select this option to reset your password.
  • Unlock (My account has been locked-out) Your account will be locked if you enter the wrong password too many times. Select this option to unlock your account.
  • Change (My password will expire/has expired) Select this option if you know your password and would like to change it.
