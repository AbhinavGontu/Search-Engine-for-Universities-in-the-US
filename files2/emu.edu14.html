Athlete of the Week

CVC Player of the Week - Kevin Duffy Kevin Duffy, Juniata College Kevin Duffy had a memorable week for Juniata, not only helping the Eagles to a win over Immaculata but doing so in a near-record fashion. Duffy tallied 18 kills with just errors on only 24 attacks in helping his team to a sweep of Immaculata, avenging a five-set loss earlier in the day. Hit hitting percentage of .708 was the third-best in a match in school history. On the week Duffy averaged 4.25 kills per set, totaling 34 kills in the two matches while hitting .640 with five total blocks.
