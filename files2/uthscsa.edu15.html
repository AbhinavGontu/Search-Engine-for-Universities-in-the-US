Academic, Faculty and Student Affairs

Academic, Faculty and Student Affairs is a vice presidential division at UT Health San Antonio. We are committed to academic success, faculty success and student success. By working collaboratively with the Schools and our institutional administrative partners, we build and sustain environments so that our learners and our faculty can thrive.
