Our New Global Headquarters

Thunderbirds new state-of-the-art global headquarters on ASUs Downtown Phoenix campus is under construction, scheduled for completion in the fall of 2021 in celebration of the 75th anniversary of the Schools founding. The cutting-edge facility will allow Thunderbird to deliver exceptional leadership and management education for generations to come.
