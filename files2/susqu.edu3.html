Ways to Give

What are you passionate about supporting?

Alumni and friends have an enduring tradition of paying their good fortune forward by supporting Susquehanna and its students. Indeed, the generous spirit of the Susquehanna community has sustained and transformed the institution since its founding, and in so doing, helped us launch generations of difference makers into the world.

Ongoing Needs

Developing Global Citizens

Show a Susquehanna student the world by supporting our award-winning cross-cultural program, Global Opportunities (GO).

Preparing Citizen Leaders

Internships and summer research open doors to great career paths and graduate school placements.

The Total College Experience

Engaged students are more successful students, more likely to stay in school and graduate in four years. Learn how you can provide campus experiences that keep them here.

Scholarships

Providing Access

Scholarships give promising students access to a Susquehanna education regardless of their financial circumstances. Learn how you can help.

The Center for Diversity and Inclusion

Support the center's annual programming, including Let's Talk lunches, student-employee projects and Black Student Union events such as Martin Luther King Jr. observances and Black History Month activities.

The Student Opportunity Fund

Help students of limited means make the most of their collegiate experience by supporting them in co-curricular activities that would be otherwise out of their reach.

Research

Plant Seeds for Sustainability

Support the Center for Environmental Education and Research's immediate needs for new tools and gear.

The Human Experience

Give biomedical science majors and students pursuing the health care studies minor the opportunity to learn human anatomy from a real cadaver for the first time.

Champions for Growth

Champions for Growth

Join this select group of Susquehannans by fulfilling the traditions of a champion. Learn more and get started here!

Senior Champions

Soon-to-be grads, don't go without your ginkgo! Challenge yourself and your classmates to collect all five traditions of a Susquehanna Champion!

A Tradition of Service

Demonstrate how you're living out the university's mission of service by dedicating the hours you volunteer each year to SU SERVE.

A Tradition of Advocacy

Help Us Recruit the Next Generation of Susquehannans and Advocate for SU's Future!

A Tradition of Spirit

Show your school spirit and stay connected to your alma mater.

A Tradition of Success

Help us show current students what's possible after graduation.

Planned Giving

Appreciated Securities

Smart gift planning combines charitable intent with cost-efficient planning techniques.

Cash, Checks, and Credit Cards

A gift of cash is easy to make.

Retirement Plans

Consider retirement-plan benefits for a significant gift to Susquehanna.

Life Insurance

Life insurance may fund a gift or replace the value of a gifted asset.

Tangible Personal Property

Tangible property contributions provide charitable deductions based on a standard of related use.

Real Estate

Most kinds of real estate may be donated to Susquehanna.

Closely Held Business Stock

Business owners contributing closely held stock are allowed a charitable deduction.
