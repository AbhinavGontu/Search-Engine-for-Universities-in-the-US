Membership Information

Rockhurst Tip-Off Club Member Benefits

$100 Annual Membership

  • Members will receive bi-weekly email updates from Coach Diener throughout the basketball season
  • Members will have access to Game Day Shuttle service for Rockhurst Men’s Basketball Home Contests (must email a request 6 hours prior to tip)
  • Membership includes one ticket to Season Tip Off Social and Preview Event (food and drinks are provided)
  • Members will receive an official Rockhust Basketball/Tip Off Club TShirt
