Alumni April

A virtual celebration

We've transformed the traditional Alumni Weekend programming into a monthlong celebration of Mudd through virtual events. Alumni April will include alumni panels, Mudd Talks by Outstanding Alumni award recipients, a special Homework Hotline event and more!

OID Event

"Deconstructing APIDA Identity & Solidarity," March 24

"Challenging Complicity in AntiBlackness: Deconstructing APIDA Identity & Solidarity" is part of the Office of Institutional Diversity's spring Anti-Racist Series. Marissiko Wheaton, director of SCORE at Scripps College, will explore Asian Pacific Islander Desi American (APIDA) critical race resistance and discuss how the legacy of anti-Blackness plays a central role in the work of racial justice.

Magazine

Fall/Winter 2020 Issue

In celebration of the WHO's International Year of the Nurse and the Midwife, we feature alumni health professionals who describe their career journeys and what it's like for them during the age of COVID-19. This issue also contains the 2019–2020 annual report and articles about key College events—Harvey Mudd students built an airplane!—and community members who are making an impact on campus and beyond.
