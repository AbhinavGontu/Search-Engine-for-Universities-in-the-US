    • Login Instructions

      To log into this site, use an Albion College network username and password. These are the same credentials used to log into an Albion College computer and to connect to Albion College Internet. 

      Still having Trouble? Double check that:

      • Your username does not include @albion.edu (correct : acb51 /  incorrect : acb51@albion.edu)

      • If you cannot remember your network password, you can reset it here or contact Helpdesk at helpdesk@albion.edu.
