Butler Blue

Hello, I am Butler Blue IV but you can call me, Blue! I am the Official Mascot of Butler University. 

I am a Red Fawn and White, Male, AKC-Registered English Bulldog and can be easily spotted by the marking above my right eyebrow. At the end of the day I am happy-go-lucky Dawg who loves his job.

I started this gig on January 24, 2020, debuting to a sold out crowd at a Butler Bulldogs Men’s Basketball game at Hinkle Fieldhouse. I may be pretty new to the game but I already love to make appearances, attend Butler sporting events, and I am getting pretty good at Social Media (@TheButlerBlue). I can’t wait to meet you!

Good Boy, Blue!

Penned by Michael “Pops” Kaltenmark, illustrated by Jingo de la Rosa, and published by Mascot Books, Good Boy, Blue! is an adorable book about Butler Blue, the University’s live bulldog mascot, and his quest to become a good boy during his first day on the job.
