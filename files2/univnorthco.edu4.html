Bears support Bears

UNC is all about people. A community of uncommon individuals - alumni, donors, friends, faculty, staff, students and parents - coming together to invest in something far bigger than one person. Thank you for your support.

Make a gift!

Click below to make a gift and join our connected community of Bear donors. Your donation will go to work immediately to support UNC's students, faculty, and programs.

If you have questions about supporting UNC, please email donor.relations@unco.edu.

GIVE NOW

During this time of uncertainty, the global and local landscapes are changing rapidly, and so are the financial, social and emotional needs of UNC students. Through it all, UNC remains committed to putting students first, and you can help us do that by making a gift to one of our priority funds.

Disaster Relief Fund

Gifts to the Disaster Relief Fund will go to UNC students facing emergent and unforeseen circumstances.

GIVE TO THE DISASTER RELIEF FUND

 

UNC Scholarship

The UNC Scholarship provides general financial aid support to UNC students - over 75% of whom rely on financial aid to make their UNC experience possible.

GIVE TO THE UNC SCHOLARSHIP
