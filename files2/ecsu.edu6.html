Experience ECSU


As the only four-year institution of higher education in northeastern North Carolina, ECSU hosts a diverse array of events that contribute to the intellectual, cultural, athletic, economic and social fabric of the region â€” with the intent toward the holistic development of students, while providing an opportunity for the community to experience presentations by academicians, experts, thought leaders, innovators, artists and celebrities in various fields.

No matter the time of year, there is always something going on around campus, so be sure to check our  campus calendar often and make plans to attend any number of our reaccuring annual events!


Tickets

Tickets for Elizabeth City State University events are typically available from tickets outlets and/or online. The university utilizes Etix as the service provider for online ticket sales in order to provide a convenient and secure means for purchasing tickets to ECSU events. Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover are accepted methods for payments.

For more information about purchasing tickets online or from a ticket outlet, please visit our ticket information page.
