Important Announcement

Chesapeake College is currently operating in Phase YELLOW.
Wye Mills and Cambridge Center buildings are currently closed to the public. College staff are available via email and phone, during regular business hours. For information regarding our current operating protocols, please visit www.chesapeake.edu/peakestrong.

Whether you were a dual enrollment student in high school, took a few credit classes, graduated with a degree or certificate, took non-credit continuing education, participated in the Institute for Adult Learning, or were part of the adult basic education program, you are a Chesapeake College alum.

As an alum, you are an important representation of the value of a Chesapeake College education, and we look forward to getting to know you.

We are currently working to build our alumni program to ensure that we are giving you meaningful opportunities to connect with the College as well as other alums. And, we want to be a resource in helping you achieve your personal and professional goals.

We encourage you complete our Alumni Request for Information, so we can stay in touch with you and seek your input as we work to develop an alumni program that encourages our alumni to stay connected with Chesapeake College. If you have any questions or would like to speak with someone about being involved in our alumni efforts, please contact Elizabeth Devlin at edevlin@chesapeake.edu or 410-827-5866.
