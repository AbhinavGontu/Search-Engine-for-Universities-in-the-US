A Message from Dean Barry

Your Campus

The South Texas campus is located in bustling downtown Houston, within walking distance of local and federal courthouses, law firms, and some of the nation’s top corporate offices. Within our walls, you’ll find a robust law library, state-of-the art courtrooms, an entire floor dedicated to clinical studies, and dozens of inviting spaces for studying and socializing. Take a look around!

Your Community

South Texas is widely known for a lot of things—from our renowned legal writing program to our world-champion advocacy teams. But, our people are really what set us apart. The sense of community you’ll find here and the lifelong connections you’ll make will have a lasting impact. Don’t take it from us; hear it from our students and alumni.

Your Classmates

The South Texas student body is diverse, dedicated, and committed to making an impact — on the legal profession, in the community, and throughout the world. Get to know a few of our student leaders. You are also invited to reach out to any of our Student Ambassadors.

Your Education

At STCL Houston, our mission is to prepare you to be practice-ready from the day you graduate. We follow through on that commitment by providing you exceptional course offerings, co-curricular experiences, and skills institutes for a balanced, comprehensive legal education.
