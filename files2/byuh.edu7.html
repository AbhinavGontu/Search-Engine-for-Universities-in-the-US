Campus Tours

Interested in seeing more of our campus?

We offer both in-person and virtual tours. Our virtual tours will be provided over Zoom for prospective students and families to see and ask questions about BYU-Idaho from any location around the world. To schedule either an in-person or virtual tour, click on the "Schedule Tour" button under the "Prospective Student Tour" section.

BYU-Idaho offers four kinds of tours, tailored to specific audiences. Please choose the tour best suited for you.

Prospective Student Tour

Prospective Student Tours are for: Students considering BYU-Idaho, students who have applied but have not yet been admitted to BYU-Idaho, 7th-12th grade school groups, and youth groups.

Admitted Student Tour

Admitted Student Tours are for: Students who have already been admitted to BYU-Idaho.

Public Tour

Public Tours are for: Elementary school groups, alumni and family, community members, families of current students, and anyone who is not a prospective or an admitted BYU-Idaho student.

Virtual Campus Tour

Virtual tours are for: Any one not able to travel to Rexburg for an in-person tour. The virtual tour is a great way to get a feel for campus and the many opportunities available to students.
