Student Financial Assistance Services Update

The Office of Student Financial Assistance (SFA) is committed to the well-being
of students, employees, and the NKU community-at-large as we transition to being on-campus amid the Coronavirus pandemic. In accordance with the Norse Nine principles for “NKU Moving Forward”, we are providing recommendations for accessing SFA services safely and securely from home.  All SFA services can be conveniently accessed online/remotely.  Students and visitors are encouraged to utilize the following options to obtain assistance from SFA:

  • Book a Virtual Appointment  
  • Contact us by phone at 859-572-5143
  • Chat with a member of our team online
  • Email us at ofa@nku.edu

Need to submit documents to SFA?  Submit your documents via email to ofa@nku.edu from your NKU student email account.  Please include your student ID number.  Note: Please allow up to 10 business days for document processing.  This is the standard processing time for any documents submitted.

Visit our Coronavirus Updates and FAQs page for answers to frequently asked questions from the Office of Student Financial Assistance
