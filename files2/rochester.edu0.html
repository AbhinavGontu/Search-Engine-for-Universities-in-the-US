Eastman School of Music alumni win Grammy awards
Three alumni of Rochester's Eastman School of Music and a professor who taught at Eastman for more than 20 years were winners at the 63rd Grammy Awards.

Rochester professor named to Time magazine's list of 100 innovators
Ranga Dias was named to the 2021 Time100 Next list of innovators for his creation of material that is superconducting at room temperature.

New orthopaedics campus construction set to begin
The largest capital project in University history will create one of the most comprehensive ambulatory orthopaedics facilities in the United States by 2023.

University of Rochester again named a top Fulbright producer
The University has been named a top producer of US Fulbright Student Grant recipients for the fifth time since 2014. The prestigious grant draws thousands of applicants each year.

Work by Rochester historians to preserve testimonies is recognized as an "outstanding model of applied environmental history scholarship".

New coronavirus vaccine seeks to 'boost' immune response
