Be Yourself!

KSSU is dedicated to providing Sac State students with a radio broadcasting learning experience. KSSU is run entirely by Sac State students. Our main goals are to improve student involvement by hosting innovative and engaging events across campus, providing air time to involved DJs to express their ideas and creativity, and to provide an outlet for alternative and culturally diverse music formats not readily available in Sacramento.
