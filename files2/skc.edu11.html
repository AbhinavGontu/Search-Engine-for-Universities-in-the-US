Apply to Live at SKC!

Salish Kootenai College offers safe, clean, and affordable on-campus dormitories and family housing. SKC has a total of 55 Family Housing units and a 42-bed Dormitory.

These units are designated transitional for full-time students while they attend SKC.  The atmosphere is in harmony with that of acquiring an education and personal growth in a tribal college setting.

SKC Student Housing units are located on the east side of campus, within walking distance of all classes on campus. The SKC Student Housing Department is located in the Robert DePoe III Building.  Stop on in and say hello!
