Cost & Aid

Make the world better—without worrying about cost.

Did you know that at the UA, more than 85% of our undergraduate students receive some sort of aid?

Choose the below description that best fits you, then learn which types of financial assistance are available.

First-Year

You are an incoming first-year if you will soon graduate from high school, you have graduated, or have a GED and have not enrolled in college since graduating.

Cost & Aid

Transfer

You are a transfer student if you have attended other colleges/universities and have completed 12 or more units since graduating from high school.

Cost & Aid

International

You are an international student if you either are not a US citizen or you do not yet hold US Permanent Resident status.

Cost & Aid

Arizona Online

You are considered an Arizona Online student if you would like to complete an Arizona undergraduate degree, or a graduate degree or certificate, entirely online from anywhere in the world.

Cost & Aid

Graduate College

Graduate students are those who have already completed an undergraduate degree and wish to pursue a higher degree at the certificate, master, or doctoral level.

Cost & Aid

Returning Students

You are a returning or re-admitted student if you have attended the UA as a degree-seeking student in the past and have missed at least one semester without receiving a formal leave of absence. 

Cost & Aid

Non-Degree Seeking

You are a non-degree seeking student if you are not seeking a degree from the University of Arizona, or if you are working to fulfill pre-requisites in order to qualify for your graduate program.

Cost & Aid

MILITARY
As a veteran, you may be eligible for a number of VA benefits as well as other types of financial aid.
Learn more about military and veterans benefit programs

WESTERN UNDERGRADUATE EXCHANGE (WUE)
Under WUE, out-of-state students who are residents of 14 Western states (other than AZ) pay a tuition rate of 150% of Arizona base resident tuition (plus fees).
Learn more about WUE programs at Arizona

Not sure which description matches you best?
