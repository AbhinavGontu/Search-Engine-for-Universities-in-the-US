Below, you'll find a list of our most frequently asked questions here in the Division of Advancement. If your inquiry isn't on the list, please feel free to contact us.

How do I request a transcript?

How do I log in to the website and find an old classmate? 

I forgot my password or have an email issue.

How do I update my contact information on record?

Hover over the "Be Connected" tab in the green navigation bar click "Update My Info." Fill out all the fields that are relevant to you and need updating. You can also access this page by visiting: alumni.brockport.edu/update.

How do I request a transcript?

Order an official transcript online through the Office of Registration and Records.

Where can I view or submit a Class Note (a life update)?

Find the page to submit your own and view others' class notes by hovering over the "Be Connected" tab in the green navigation bar click "Class Notes." From there, you can submit a note and view the current Class Notes listing. You can also access this page by visiting: alumni.brockport.edu/classnotes.

How do I log in to the website and find an old classmate?

Hover over the "Be Connected" tab in the green navigation bar click "Alumni Directory" follow the instructions on the page. You can also access this page by visiting: alumni.brockport.edu/login.

I forgot my password or have an email issue.

On the login page (see above to access it), you will notice a link next to the password box which provides instructions on how to change your password. You will have to enter the email address you used when registering your account, and an email will be sent to you with a link to reset your password.

A message indicating your email address can't be found indicates that the system has a different email address associated with your account. It's possible you are using a different email address, and your account information is not up to date. Contact the Office of Alumni Engagement at (585) 395-2068 or alumni@brockport.edu to solve the issue.
