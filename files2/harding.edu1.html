Our main campus is in Searcy, Ark., a city of 20,000 people in White County. Located in east central Arkansas, Searcy is about 50 miles northeast of Little Rock and 105 miles west of Memphis, Tenn., providing access to cultural and entertainment opportunities of metropolitan areas without sacrificing the special charms of small-town living. Our residential campus also provides a sense of home.

Welcome to Harding University

At Harding you can find more than exceptional academics — you find people. It’s a community of mission where higher education and a higher purpose go hand in hand.
