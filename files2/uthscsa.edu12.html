The Office of Human Resources

 

How to access Human Resources Services during COVID 19 conditions

The UT Health San Antonio Office of Human Resources will transition primarily to a remote workforce and deliver essential HR services virtually through video, email and phone.


I9 Verifications
During the COVID-19 national emergency, verification for I-9 documentation for new hires will be accepted via email. Once the emergency declaration is lifted, HR representatives will coordinate with new hires to verify the original I-9 documents in person.
