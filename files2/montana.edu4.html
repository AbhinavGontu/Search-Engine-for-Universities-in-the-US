Alumni and friends,
Because your health and well-being are of utmost concern to us, we have been following the guidelines of the Centers for Disease Control and the State of Montana in decisions related to reopening our facilities and resuming activities curtailed due to the coronavirus pandemic. Our MSUAF office is open to the public and staff is available to welcome and assist visitors. We continue however, to postpone large group gatherings until further notice and we can be sure it will be comfortable and safe for our guests to gather. We appreciate your patience as we do our part to slow the spread of the virus to protect you and our staff from undue exposure.

MSU Alumni Foundation and the COVID-19 virus
With the news of the COVID-19 virus spreading, our first priority is the well-being of alumni, friends and guests who are engaging with the MSU Alumni Foundation through programs and events. We are closely monitoring the situation and following guidelines outlined by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), state and local health agencies. We are also staying in step with responses and restrictions from the university as they develop. The MSU Alumni Foundation will communicate directly via email with those affected by any changes to our programs and events.
