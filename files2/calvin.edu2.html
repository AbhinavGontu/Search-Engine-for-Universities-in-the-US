Welcome to Get Connected!

This guide is an orientation to Hekman Library's services and resources for Calvin University students, both online and on-campus. Keep reading to get answers to all your questions about accessing materials and getting more help from your online librarians!

Remember, always go through the library website to get electronic resources like ebooks or ejournal articles - just Googling any of our databases won't provide free access, like we do! To view the full text for our library resources, you may be prompted by the library system to log with your Calvin ID and passphrase if you are a Calvin University student (Calvin Seminary students should follow the alternative instructions). If you ever have any issues or questions about getting access to online resources, always ask a librarian for help.

Help at Hekman

The library offers personal research help in several different ways (online, in person, etc.), answering all kinds of questions. Both on and off-campus, there are librarians available and eager to help you! Check out the links below to find multiple ways of contacting the library for help.

Fall 2020 Update: Library help hours may vary as we respond to changes this semester, but you can look for the colors on in the Live Chat when quick help is available - with Live Chat, you can talk with a live person (no bots!) for quick help and also to arrange follow-up for more in-depth questions.

  • Library FAQ Portal
    Have a particular question or scenario you're not sure about? Send a message to a librarian, and we'll add it to our FAQ - click the link here to explore our most commonly asked questions about the library.
  • Ask a Librarian
    Prefer to email? Use the Ask a Librarian email form for your questions to the librarians at Hekman, and you'll usually get a response that day or within 24 hours (except for weekends and holidays!).
  • Live Chat and Text
    Need even quicker help? Check out the Live Chat box on the Hekman homepage; if it's lit up, a librarian is available and you can instant message us, or text at 616-214-3355.
  • Make an Appointment
    If you'd like to talk to a librarian in person, make an appointment to go through your questions in-depth over Zoom, MS Teams, or your favorite video-chat platform (librarians may also be available on-campus during the week for face-to-face conversation)!
  • Librarians' Research Specialties
    Not sure who to talk to? You can find a list of all the librarians, which departments they are assigned to, and their contact info on the library website
  • Subject Guides
    Want to start your research on your own, but unsure where to begin? Check out our subject guides, which give an introduction to doing research in the major areas of study at Calvin! These guides include recommended research databases, reference resources, and other advice; they also highlight the librarian who works with that department.
  • Class Guides
    Or a librarian may have even created a research guide for your specific class! These are even more tailored for certain assignments or research needs.
