Meet Jenny (Hoover) Bonnano '97

Jenny graduated from Marist in 1997, earning a BA in communication. After a career in multiple production roles, including two of her own production businesses, Jenny, now a certified professional cook, decided to start a third business, Jenny B Cooks, showcasing her passion for cooking. Lowell Thomas: Newsman, Author, Entrepreneur, and Explorer
Have you ever wondered about the Lowell Thomas Communication Center’s namesake?  John Ansley, Director of the Archives and Special Collections, will share photos, film, and audio from the College’s Lowell Thomas Papers while discussing many aspects of Thomas’s life, including the creation of the Lawrence of Arabia legend, Thomas’s 1949 expedition to Tibet, his five decades on the radio, and much, much more!
