Commencement 2021

Cal State LA is committed to celebrating the achievements of the Class of 2021* while addressing the health and safety of our students, faculty and staff. We are currently reviewing our options for Commencement and planning is underway. Plans regarding Commencement will be made in consultation with public health experts and will adhere to their guidance. We will notify the Class of 2021 and update the Commencement website when our plans are finalized and approved. Thank you for your patience and understanding.

* The Class of 2021 includes the graduates of fall 2020, winter 2021, spring 2021, and summer 2021.

Commencement 2021 Q&A

Is Commencement week being rescheduled? 
The University is currently reviewing its options for Commencement and planning is underway.

When will Cal State LA decide on its plans for Commencement?
Cal State LA is focused on addressing the rapidly changing developments connected to the COVID-19 pandemic. As soon as we are able to provide additional information, the Class of 2021 and the University community will be notified.

Will I still graduate and earn my degree? 
Yes, students will still graduate and earn their degrees. Due to COVID-19 public health orders, graduation applications and fees must be submitted online. Please visit the Graduation Office website for more information.

Will I still receive my diploma? 
Yes, you will still receive your diploma. You will receive your diploma by mail after official certification that you have successfully completed all degree requirements. Please allow up to four weeks for the Graduation Office to review all applicants individually.

After your degree is awarded, you can track the progress of your diploma online.

Will undergraduate students still receive their honor cords?
Honor cords will be mailed out starting the week of May 3, 2021, along with the diploma covers. As posted on the Graduation Office website regarding eligibility for honors, an undergraduate student must have completed a minimum of 45 units in residence at Cal State LA and have attained the appropriate standing (in terms of cumulative grade point average earned at Cal State LA) as described below:

Summa Cum Laude: GPA equal to or greater than 3.9
Magna Cum Laude: GPA equal to or greater than 3.75 but less than 3.9
Cum Laude: GPA equal to or greater than 3.5 but less than 3.75

All students attaining these minimum grade point averages will be awarded the corresponding honors upon conferral of degree. Please note that Commencement honor status recognition will be based on information provided as of April 26, 2021. All final honor designations on your transcript will be based on final grades and verification by the Graduation Office.

Will undergraduate and graduate students still receive their diploma covers?
Diploma covers will be mailed out starting the week of May 3, 2021.

Will undergraduate and graduate students still be able to purchase their regalia and Commencement-related merchandise?
Graduating students can order their caps and gowns through the University Bookstore’s regalia vendor, Oak Hall. For master’s and doctoral candidates, please refer to the degree hood color chart.

Sashes and other Commencement-related merchandise are available to order at the University Bookstore’s website.
