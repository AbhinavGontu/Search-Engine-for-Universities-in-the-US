Admission

Apply for Admission

Our application for admission is always free. Apply today using our online application or the Common Application.

Didn’t take the ACT or SAT this year, or feel like your scores don’t accurately tell your story? Not a problem. Augustana University is proud to offer test-optional admission.

Scholarships

100% of Augustana students receive a scholarship, grant, or other form of financial assistance. We’re proud to partner with you and your family to make an Augustana education possible. Submit your application for admission to determine your scholarship eligibility. The graphic below represents incoming Fall 2021 students.

Value

Augustana is nationally-recognized as one of the best colleges in the country for jobs and career outcomes. An Augustana education is an investment in your future, not just the next four years.

Visit Campus

What’s the best thing about Augustana? It’s a tough call, but we love the community here. Visit our campus (virtually or in-person) to experience it for yourself. Ready to learn more? Connect with your personal admission counselor or request more information today.
