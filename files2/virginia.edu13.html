Spring 2021 Degree Conferral

The last day to apply for Spring 2021 Graduation in SIS Self Service was Wednesday, February 17th. If you need to apply for Spring 2021 Graduation, please contact your school registrar. Diplomas will be ordered for all Spring 2021 degree recipients after all degrees have been verified and posted in SIS.

The deadline to update the Primary Name that will appear on the diploma is May 1st, 2021.

Diplomas will be mailed to the student's Diploma Address in SIS. The Diploma Address can be updated in the SIS Student Center under Personal Information. In order to guarantee shipment of the diploma to the correct address, any changes in SIS must be completed by May 14th, 2021. Graduates will receive an email once diplomas ship and should allow six to eight weeks from the shipment date for delivery to domestic addresses. For international shipments, additional delivery time may be required. CeDiplomas will be available the same day as diplomas ship.

Fall 2020 Degree Conferral and Diplomas

Degrees for all Fall 2020 graduates have been conferred. Diplomas have been ordered and are being shipped to the student's identified Diploma Address in SIS.

Graduates should allow six to eight weeks from the shipment date for the delivery of diplomas to domestic addresses. For international shipments, additional delivery time may be required.

CeDiplomas will be available for downloading the same day as diploma shipment. At that time, graduates will be sent an email to their Virginia.edu account on how to retrieve and download their CeDiploma through their SIS Student Center. If you have questions or need additional assistance, please contact UREG.

Diploma Information

  • If you plan to have your diploma framed, please note that framing processes that require heat can damage the diploma and should not be used.
  • While there is no "official" placement of the orange and blue ribbon, it is most commonly displayed diagonally across the top left corner, blue above orange. Replacement ribbon is available upon request at no charge.
  • Double majors are reminded that they receive only one degree—and thus only one diploma—but have both majors noted on their transcript.

The size of the UVA Diploma is 17" x 22".

Replacement/Duplicate Diplomas

Replacement Diplomas: Unless the original diploma is returned with the request form, the replacement diploma will have the word 'replacement' inscribed at the bottom.

Duplicate Diplomas: A duplicate diploma will have the word 'copy' inscribed at the bottom.

  • Complete the Diploma Replacement Request Form and send it with payment to:
    UREG (Office of the University Registrar)
    P.O. Box 400203
    Charlottesville, VA 22904-4203
    or
    UREG (Office of the University Registrar)
    1001 N Emmet St
    Charlottesville, VA 22903-4833
  • Printing may take up to 3 weeks after we place the order; diplomas are ordered approximately the 15th of each month.
  • Replacement and duplicate diplomas are printed in the current format, including use of the signatures of the current president, dean, and registrar.

Thank you for your interest, however as of March 1st, 2019 the miniature diploma is no longer offered by the University of Virginia.

Certifications

Beginning with May 2016 graduates, the Certified Electronic Diploma (CeDiploma) can be utilized for providing proof of graduation in instances for which the diploma is explicitly required. This electronic credential can be easily shared with third parties and validated by the University.

If you need a copy of your diploma for certification purposes, take your original diploma to a copy center and have an 8.5" x 11" photocopy made. Mail the copy to UREG (Office of the University Registrar) and we will certify and notarize it as being a true copy of the original.

We recommend that you first verify that some other document won't serve the purpose. Usually, companies or government agencies are looking for official proof of graduation, and only in rare cases involving foreign governments is a copy of the actual diploma needed. A certification or transcript can be obtained for less cost and in less time. (See Credentialing Services).

International Authentication (aka. The Hague Apostille): Contact Virginia's Secretary of the Commonwealth. For individuals utilizing the CeDiploma, an Apostille is neither required nor necessary. Documents can be validated through this University website to provide absolute confidence in the credential's authenticity. Questions should be redirected to ureg@virginia.edu.
