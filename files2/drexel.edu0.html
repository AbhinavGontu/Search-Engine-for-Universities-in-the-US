    • Ambition Can't Wait

      Drexel combines academic rigor with a transformative cooperative education program, preparing students to address the challenges of a changing world. Charles D. Close School of Entrepreneurship alumnus Evan Ehlers ‘19 founded Sharing Excess, a nonprofit startup that redistributes unused food to at-risk community members across the Philadelphia region.

      Learn More Learn more about Drexel's cooperative education program

    • Life-Changing Science

      The School of Biomedical Engineering, Science and Health Systems integrates engineering and life sciences in a global context. The school teaches students to develop solutions and translate science into improving health and saving lives, while encouraging expansive collaboration to take on the world’s emerging challenges.

      Learn More About Drexel's Colleges and Schools

    • Pushing the World Forward

      Through groundbreaking innovations, Drexel engineers are changing the way we live and interact with one another. Working alongside renowned research-focused faculty, students in the College of Engineering learn in a dynamic academic environment and contribute exciting advances that address society’s greatest challenges.

      Learn More Learn more about our colleges and schools

    • The Future of Business is here.

      Students in Drexel’s LeBow College of Business learn to tackle industry challenges with guidance from faculty who are leaders in their fields. With access to state-of-the-art facilities, including our Finance Trading Lab, LeBow students gain the hands-on experiences needed to succeed in highly competitive business-related fields.
