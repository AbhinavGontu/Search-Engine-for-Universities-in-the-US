shaping the future

For more than 128 years, University of Alabama women have played a key role in shaping history.

They are trailblazers, having integrated UA in more ways than one. They are leaders, running global enterprises and changing the world. They are artists, starring on stage, the small screen and the silver screen.

The University of Alabama honors the women who have made lasting contributions to the University, state, nation and beyond and who are actively shaping the future.

research

Research is at the core of everything we do at UA. That is one of the many reasons why we are recognized as a Very High Research Activity doctoral institution according to the Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education.

  • Strategic Plan

    ORED’s strategic plan facilitates the growth and development of faculty and significantly increases the size and impact of research and scholarly activity.

  • Undergraduate Research

    Undergraduate students involved in our Emerging Scholars Program are taking part in cutting-edge research impacting their fields and changing lives.

  • UA Engineers Developing Advanced Battery System for Electric Vehicles

    Dr. Jaber A. Abu Qahouq, a UA electrical engineer, devised a system that draws power wirelessly from several portable battery modules that can interchange quickly and safely.
