2020-2021 Student Handbook

This handbook contains policies and general information for academically enrolled students of Bastyr University. This handbook is not a complete list of all policies, as Academic Policies are available in the University Catalogue. In addition, significant information about offices and University events and services is available on the intranet, MyBU, and students are strongly encouraged to utilize MyBU as a resource. This handbook is compiled through Student Affairs, and any questions, concerns, clarifications or requests for additions should be sent to Susan Weider  sweider@bastyr.edu.
