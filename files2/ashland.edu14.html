Calculate Your Costs

How much should I expect to pay?

The Ashland University Net Price Calculator is here to help you estimate your out of pocket costs for your incoming year. The calculator is designed for incoming freshman, transfers, and Adult Online Criminal Justice students. Incoming freshman will need some basic financial information, your high school GPA, and your ACT score. Transfer students will need basic financial information, your cumulative college GPA, and college credit hours accrued.

Please note that this is an estimate and its accuracy is contingent upon the information that you provide. You will receive an official offer letter once your FAFSA and all required documentation are received.
