Visit

The moment you set foot on Rocky Top, you’ll find a place that feels like home. Campus is filled with energy and tradition, but it’s also easy to find green spaces and quiet corners for study and relaxation. Just off campus, you’ll find a friendly Southern city and plenty of opportunity for outdoor adventure.
