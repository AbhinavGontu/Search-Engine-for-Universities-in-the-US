FY20 University of Alaska Wellness Program

The FY20 University of Alaska Wellness Program is now available. As of September 2019, the new program uses the Prevention Cloud app that you can download from the Apple App Store or Google Play. Using the app, you can connect your Apple Health Kit or Google Fit to track your activity.Fitbitt connects directly from the web site. You can find more information on the Wellness website. 

FY20 Wellness Rebate

Employees who qualified for the FY20 rebate under last year's program, completed April 30, 2019, were paid the rebate on the Run 24 payday - November 22, 2019.
