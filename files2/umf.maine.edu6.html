Please Note: Until COVID restrictions ease, access to the Mantor Library building will be restricted to card access only (UMF students, faculty, and staff). Members of the general community may continue to access Mantor Library resources online or through curbside checkout. Reference services will be provided online to all via Zoom, Monday to Friday, 10-4pm. At the discretion of the library director and on a case-by-case basis, exceptions may be made to these restrictions for specific community member research needs (e.g., accessing on-campus databases or microfilm).
