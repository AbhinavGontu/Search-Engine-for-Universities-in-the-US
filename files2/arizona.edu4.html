Important Update

Thank you for your interest in the University of Arizona and Undergraduate Admissions. We are committed to providing prospective Wildcats with the information needed to make the best college decisions, while also protecting the safety of our community in light of the situation with COVID-19. 

While we are disappointed that we are unable to show off our beautiful campus with in-person tours or on-campus events, we have built a Virtual Visit, which offers an online tour and videos that feature our students’ favorite parts of campus. 

All admissions team members are working remotely and while the office is closed to visitors, we are available during regular business hours of 8 am – 5 pm MST to respond to your messages. Please check our FAQ pages for Freshman, Transfer and International students for answers to some of the most frequently asked questions during this unprecedented time. If you need to contact us directly, please call us at (520) 621-3237 or email us at admissions@arizona.edu. You can also find your recruiter and other detailed contact information here.

Note for International students: The Department of State has temporarily suspended routine visa services at all U.S. Embassies and Consulates. They are unable to provide a specific date when they will resume services at this time. Please continue to check the Department of State website for updates. If you are outside the U.S. and unable to enter for your starting term, please contact your admissions recruiter to discuss options for beginning your program from your home country.

We appreciate your patience and understanding, and we look forward to welcoming you to our offices in the near future.

Admission Requirements at a Glance

Are you under 22 years of age when you apply? The Arizona Board of Regents (ABOR) high school course requirements for admissions are:

  • English (4 units/years)
  • Math (4 units/years)
  • Laboratory sciences (3 units/years)
  • Social sciences (2 units/years)
  • Second language (2 units/years)
  • Either fine arts or career and technical education (1 unit/year)

Admission Requirements at a Glance

Are you 21 years of age or younger when you apply?
Requirements include:

  • you have 12 or more transferable credits post-high school,
  • you have at least a 2.0 recalculated college/university GPA and 2.5 cumulative un-weighted high school GPA,
  • and you have fulfilled the course competencies required by the Arizona Board of Regents (ABOR)
