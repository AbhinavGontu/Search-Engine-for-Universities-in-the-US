Visit Dickinson

WITH OVER 144 ACRES OF CAMPUS, THERE ARE PLENTY OF PLACES FOR YOU TO FIND YOURSELF.

Virtual Visits

We encourage you to explore our campus virtually through a rich array of sessions, videos, galleries and resources.

Explore Virtually

Personalized Campus Tours

Walk the campus with a member of the admissions team! Tours are being offered for individual families and are largely outdoors. Registration in advance is required.

SELF-GUIDED QR CODE TOUR

Visitors to campus are invited to explore on their own with the help of our self-guided QR code campus tour.


Virtual Tour


Discover Carlisle

Access an array of resources and information, including maps and directions to campus, places to stay, area shops and restaurants and more about Dickinson’s location in the Harrisburg-Carlisle region.


FAQS ABOUT VISITING

Send any questions you may have about visiting campus to admissions@dickinson.edu, contact your regional counselor or consult our guide to frequently asked questions about the campus visit.


PHOTO GALLERY

Explore our campus, our community, our residential experience, our global sites and more through our Flickr albums.
