Welcome to Dixon University Center

Welcome to Dixon University Center, Where Education Meets Flexibility

Dixon University Center is foremost an academic and governance center, providing area residents the advantages of both public and private institutions. An expansive menu of undergraduate, graduate and certificate programs, professional development, and customized training that are offered on-site by a consortium of State System universities and private colleges and universities for adult students, in the evenings and on weekends. The Center is also a cultural resource, a testing ground for new technology, and a community gathering place. For a complete listing of degree programs and offerings, click here.

Regional Higher Education Center

Welcome to
Dixon University Center

Whether your objective is educational enhancement or career advancement, learning opportunities presented through Dixon University Center (DUC) can help you achieve your goals. A convenient location, just minutes off I-81 along Harrisburg’s riverfront, provides easy access to this academic hub, which now has more than 2,000 enrollments and hosts 600-plus meetings and events a year.

Dixon University Center serves as home to both the Pennsylvania's State System of Higher Education and to a consortium of educational institutions that currently offer over 30 degree programs; continuing education, professional development, and customized training solutions for adult learners.
