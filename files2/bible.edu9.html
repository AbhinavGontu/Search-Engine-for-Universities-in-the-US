Lancaster Bible Ticket Information
Print
1. For events held in Horst Athletic Center (basketball and volleyball), ticket prices are as follows:
    -$5 for adults
    -$3 for students (elementary, high school, college) and LBC Alumni
    -FREE for children up to age 5
    -Lancaster Bible College students are admitted for FREE when they show their ID

2. For men's and women's soccer games, ticket prices are as follows:
    -$5 for adults
    -$3 for students (elementary, high school, college) and LBC Alumni
    -FREE for children up to age 5
    -Lancaster Bible College students are admitted for FREE when they show their ID

3. There is currently no cost to attend baseball, softball and lacrosse games, as well as no cost for cross country races.

4. For those interest in purchasing a full-season sports pass (covers cost of all athletic event admission for one academic year):
    (2) Complimentary parent passes are provided to all incoming freshman / transfer parents
    $70 for adult ($50 per LBC parent)
    $50 for seniors - 60+ ($40 per LBC grandparent)
    $50 for students (elementary, high school, college) -- $25 if LBC Athlete sibling
    $50 for Lancaster Bible alumni

5. Questions regarding tickets may be directed to Rod Baughman -- rbaughman@lbc.edu
