Travel With Waldo

While our travels may be a bit closer to home these days, we hope Waldo is still tagging along! The Weber State University Alumni Association's “Traveling Waldo” mascot is a fun way to show your Weber State pride whether you live in or out of state. Take lovable Waldo on your backyard adventures, snap his picture and share it with us so we can post it on our WSU Alumni Association Facebook page. 

NEED A FREE WALDO CUTOUT?
We'll send it! Complete the request form here and we will send it to you via mail.


Share your fun, quirky, and original moments of Travelin’ Waldo while you are working, playing, celebrating, hiking, fishing or however you are spending your days.

Email your photos to alumni@weber.edu
