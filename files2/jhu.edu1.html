Student perspectives
A turning point and opportunity
Published March 12, 2021
The Black Student Athlete Association, founded in June, is a combination volunteer organization, social network, and on-campus program. Open to all Black student athletes at Hopkins, the group was founded in an effort to amplify their voices in the Johns Hopkins community and provide a safe and supportive space for Black students to share their experiences and connect with fellow athletes.
