With several events a year held throughout the country, you have a wealth of opportunities to connect with your fellow Quinnipiac alumni. You're invited to Bobcat sporting events, regional gatherings, concerts, campus lectures, volunteer days, as well as career programming.

Quinnipiacs Office of Alumni Affairs is committed to offering a wide range of engaging events and experiences for our alumni community. We welcome your thoughts and inputs! If you have ideas to contribute or want to get involved in planning an event, please contact alumni@qu.edu.
