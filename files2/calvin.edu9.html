Research and Reference Assistance - Spring 2021

During spring term, 2021, the Research Assistance Desk (2nd/main floor of the library) will not be staffed in-person, but Hekman librarians are still available to help with your research and reference questions! 

Here's how you can reach us during our regular hours (9am-10pm Mon-Thurs; 1pm-5pm Fri-Sat):

  • Chat with a librarian or student research assistant, from the library homepage
  • Text us: 616-214-3355
  • Call us: 616-526-6307

And anytime, you can:

  • E-mail us 
  • Make an appointment with a Research librarian for more challenging questions
  • Check our Library FAQ

Note if you have questions about the current state of library services, you can also review our reopening page.
