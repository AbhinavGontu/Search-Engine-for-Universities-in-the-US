            Office of Admission

            Now is the time. Millikin is the place. 

            Millikin is home to Performance Learning, an approach to education that begins in the classroom, moves to the field with real-life work experiences and results in a prestigious degree and lifelong connection with our community of support and professional resources. It’s why Millikin has been named the top school in Illinois for moving students into jobs post-graduation and why more students than ever before have applied for the fall semester. 

            Millikin is ready to welcome you. Are you ready to Make it Millikin?
