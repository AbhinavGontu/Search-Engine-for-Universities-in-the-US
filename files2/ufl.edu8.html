External Funding Opportunity Resources

UF Funding Opportunities

The Funding Opportunities page is updated throughout the weekdays with both internal and external funding opportunities, and UF announces required internal coordination procedures for limited submission programs.

Want weekly alerts of funding opportunities that are available to only UF faculty, staff, and students? Visit the Funding Opportunities page. If off-campus or on VPN LogIn with Gatorlink (login link at the top right of the page), login to manage your subscription under the Subscription tab. Login is not required for on-campus access.

Graduate Student Funding Opportunities

The Graduate Student Funding Opportunities (pdf) represents a snapshot of some of the recurring funding opportunities available for graduate students. These opportunities are jointly maintained by the Division of Research Program Development and the Graduate School.

Foundation Center Foundation Finder

The Foundation Center Foundation Finder provides contact information, financial data and access to 990-PF tax forms, which often include information on how to submit a proposal to the foundation (see Part XV).
