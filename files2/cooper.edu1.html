Cooper Parents



Parents and families are an important part of the Cooper community. Their most important role is to support their student as they undertake the rigors of a Cooper education and we invite parents to directly impact and enhance the student experience. Through encouraging parent philanthropy, engaging leadership donor parents and hosting parents events, we view parents as partners.

If you have any questions or concerns, the Office of Alumni Affairs and Development would love to hear from you. Contact us by sending an email to parents@cooper.edu or calling (212) 353-4164.

To make an Annual Fund gift, visit cooper.edu/give. Thank you in advance for your support.
