ATHLETE OF THE WEEK
Harry Cohen
Men's Tennis

Junior Harry Cohen began his Chapman career with wins in singles and doubles as the Chapman University mens tennis team returned to the court for the first time since March 2020. Cohen and freshman Alex Granados took the No. 2 doubles match 6-2 for the first Chapman win of the season. Cohen then went to the No. 3 singles match and took a straight set victory. After battling to a 7-6 victory in the first set, he won the second set convincingly for the 7-6, 6-1 win. Cohen and the Panthers return to the court at Azusa Pacific on Wednesday.
