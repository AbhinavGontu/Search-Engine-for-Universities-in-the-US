OFFICIAL CAMP WEBSITE OF | SMC Men's Soccer Camps

 

SMC Men's Soccer Camps & Clinics are dedicated to all aspects of the game including technical skill development, tactical team play, and mental strength. Each athlete will have the opportunity to develop their skills through individual instruction, group instruction and game experience.

Saint Mary's College is very fortunate to have some of the finest collegiate coaches in Northern California. Head Coach Adam Cooper hand selects and trains his staff, composed of top-level coaches and collegiate players. All instructors have been chosen for their extensive knowledge of the game of soccer and their enthusiasm to work with children and young athletes.
