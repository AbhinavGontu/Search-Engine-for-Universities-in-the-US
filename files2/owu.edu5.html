Sports Camps & Clinics

Camps & Clinics Waiver

Ohio Wesleyan’s sports camps and clinics let young athletes learn from accomplished college coaches and players. From fundamentals to advanced instruction, campers will learn to be better, stronger, and smarter individual and team players.
