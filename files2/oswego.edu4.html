Program and Services

 

Lifelong Learning

Lifelong Learning is a multiple 4-day program for adults ages 50+ to pursue exciting educational and social experiences on campus.

Learn more

Small Business Development Center

Small Business Development Center (SBDC) is a team of professional counselors who can assist you with all aspects of small business ownership.

Learn more

Workforce Development Board

Workforce Development Board (WDB) is a non-profit workforce grant-making organization that oversees employment-related programs.

Learn more

Leadership Oswego County

Leadership Oswego County prepares a select group of county residents to become the community stewards of the future

Learn more

Retired and Senior Volunteer Program

Retired and Senior Volunteer Program (RSVP) encourages and fosters the development and maintenance of volunteer opportunities for Americans aged 55 & over.

Learn more

Request for Qualifications

Request for Qualifications (RFQ) is an online system that posts bidding opportunities to pre-qualified consultants on behalf of the NYS Office of Temporary and Disability Assistance (OTDA).

Learn more

Professional Development

OBCR provides professional development training to employees of businesses & other organizations of all sizes.

Learn more
