Give Now!

Giving Instructions

To make a gift, click on the department that your designation falls under. If you are unaware of the department or do not see your designation, please click on any department and write your designation within the “Comments or Special Processing Notes” box.

If this is a one-time gift, please check the “One Time Gift” box. If your gift is to reoccur overtime, please check the “Recurring Gift” box.

Once you choose your designation and amount you would like to donate, you’ll be able to fill out the appropriate forms with your credit card information.

The collective power of our alumni and friends cannot be overstated. On behalf of the YSU faculty, staff, students, and the YSU Foundation, please accept our thanks.
