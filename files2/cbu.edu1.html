COVID19 Response Plan

Christian Brothers University is committed to providing a safe and supportive environment during the COVID-19 public health crisis. For information about our COVID-19 response plan, please see www.cbu.edu/coronavirus or contact us at COVID19-info@cbu.edu.

Inclement Weather Alert

The CBU campus will be closed on Thursday, February 11 due to inclement weather. We will be operating in an “Online First” mode. Classes will be conducted online and wherever possible, operations will continue remotely. Please check your CBU email for additional information.
