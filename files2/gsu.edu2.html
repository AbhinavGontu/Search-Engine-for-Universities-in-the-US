Student Engagement and Programs

You can access important aspects of student life at Georgia State University in an online format. All instruction at Georgia State University is online through the end of the spring semester and the campus remains closed to students and the public. However, even in a virtual world, the Office of Student Engagement & Programs is here to support you.  We are building the Panther Family and all parts of the Georgia State student community and have designed experiences that promote success, leadership, service, and Panther pride.

A Message from the Vice President

Welcome to the Division of Student Affairs

On behalf of the entire Student Affairs staff, I invite you to take a few minutes to explore these pages and discover the many outstanding co-curricular activities and experiences that are offered at Georgia State University. At the University, we are proud of our students, of our distinguished faculty, and of our dedicated staff who serve the campus and create its vibrancy. The Student Affairs program is responsive to student expectations and is committed to ensuring opportunities for our students to develop skills in leadership, teamwork, and social responsibility to prepare them for their future roles in society. Our mission informs our daily interactions with students to ensure that their experience is one that maximizes student success.

The Student Affairs staff works in partnership with the faculty and other administrators to create a learning-centered culture that encourages student development and growth. We are just one of the many reasons why Georgia State University is the university of choice for its students.

Vice President for Student Affairs
