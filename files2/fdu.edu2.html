Join us for Virtual Admitted Students Week

The fun starts on March 15th-and lasts all week!

We’re bringing FDU right to you with our Virtual Admitted Students Week. Starting on March 15th, we will be hosting a variety of virtual activities for you and your family each night leading up to our main event-Virtual Admitted Students Day.

Saturday, March 20 is the Main Event: Virtual Admitted Students Day!

After a week of interactive sessions, join us on Saturday, March 20 for Virtual Admitted Students Day at FDU! With more than 40 Academic, Student Life & Services Sessions, this event will allow you to learn everything you need to know in order to join the FDU Community this fall.

Ready to Join the Fun?

Sign-up by clicking the “Register Now” link below. You’ll be able to register for any of our virtual events during the week AND our main event, Virtual Admitted Student Day on Saturday, March 20.

Need some more information? Continue below to learn more about our full week of events, and to preview our agenda for Virtual Admitted Students Day!

RSVP Now!
Register Now

A Full Week of Events

Every evening, from Monday, March 15 to Friday, March 19, you can join our current students and staff for fun and interactive events to help you learn more about the FDU Community. You can register for all the events or the ones you are most interested in; it is up to you!

This week leads up to our grand finale– Virtual Admitted Students Day. You won’t want to miss it! 

Admitted Student Festivities
Starting March 15, we’re hosting fun activities for you and your family every night. Select any or all the virtual festivities that interest you. There’s something for everyone!
Explore Your Options Below

  • Around Town Tours

    At 6:00 pm (EST) Head into town with us! Soak in the surroundings with a guided, virtual tour of the nearby towns, Madison and Teaneck.

     

  • What’s Cool at FDU Chat

    Ready to join our community? At 6:00pm (EST), check out our interactive panels with students, alumni and faculty discussing all the cool things FDU offers!

  • Mindfulness Meditation

    Stressed out? Let’s deal with it! Join us for a meditation/mindfulness workshop with great tips to relax at 4:00pm (EST).

     

  • Mocktails & Foodie Demo

    Whip up a snack and mocktails with a foodie demo with FDU’s Hospitality Department at 6:00pm (EST).

     

    Wroxton College Tour

    At 7:00 pm (EST) travel across the pond with us! Experience our international campus in England with a guided, virtual tour with current students and the Office of Study Abroad.

     

  • FDU/New Jersey Trivia Night

    At 6:00pm (EST), It’s game on! Show off your knowledge of FDU/NJ at our trivia night. So play the game, make virtual friends and win some FDU swag!

     

March 20th- Virtual Admitted Students Day
Join us Saturday morning, March 20 at 10:15am (EST) for the main event! Our virtual experience brings together thousands of admitted students, parents, faculty and staff together. Learn about your academic experience, campus life, and important next steps.
