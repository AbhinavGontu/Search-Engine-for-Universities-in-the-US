  • A paralegal certificate from Hamline prepares you to perform substantive legal work under the supervision of an attorney or to work in a law-related setting such as corporate compliance, contracts administration, or the court system.

  • Undergraduate Pathway

    You can earn your graduate paralegal certificate as an undergraduate student with the option to apply the credits to the Master in the Study of Law and get both in just five years.

    Learn more
    Graduate Certificate

    Already have your bachelor’s degree? Get your paralegal certificate in as few as nine months. Plus, you’ll have the option to go on to the master’s program.
