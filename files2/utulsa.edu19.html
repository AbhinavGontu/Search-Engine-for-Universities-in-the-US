Podcasts

Classical Tulsa 

  • The Classical Tulsa Podcast spotlights upcoming local concert performances through extended interviews with the music-makers themselves.

Found@TU

  • Found@TU is a monthly interview podcast in which University of Tulsa faculty discuss their research in a clear, accessible, and engaging manner.

Museum Confidential 

  • Museum Confidential, which began as an art exhibition, is a podcast co-created by Philbrook Museum of Art and Public Radio Tulsa exploring "what goes on behind the scenes" in a museum. Unseen. Unheard. Until now. 

Masterworks in 10 Minutes or Less

  • Each MW10 episode introduces a classical masterwork through musical examples and engaging commentary from Classical Tulsa host Jason Heilman. It's musicological insight you can use, and a great opportunity to get to know classical music on your own schedule!

StudioTulsa 

  • StudioTulsa features down-to-earth interviews that make sense of complex issues and offer new perspectives on topics we might take for granted such as the arts, sciences, books, politics, culture, economics, history, and much more.
