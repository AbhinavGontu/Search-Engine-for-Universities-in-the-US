Office of International Education (OIE) 

OIE is committed to supporting, promoting and celebrating individuals in an intercultural environment. Our staff advocate for and facilitate international and cross-cultural experiences, perspectives and initiatives.

Study Abroad programming is also promoted and coordinated by OIE, which acts as a liaison to the university for international students, researchers and professors.
