#IamGeorgiaSouthern

"Through my participation on campus, I have met some of the most wonderful people from whom I have learned a lot and have supported me throughout. Georgia Southern to me is like a puzzle. Students and faculty come here from all over the country and world and they fit perfectly, like pieces of puzzles would."

Shivani Patel
Business Management

Being Middle Eastern and Muslim has never been easy especially in the south. I like Georgia Southern because it allows me to show my perspective to my peers who have many misconceptions and generalizations about people of my demographic. There’s nothing better than having the opportunity to give your minority perspective and having the ability to change negative thoughts into friendships.

Mohammad Abdallah
Supply Chain Management, Operations Management

"I love Georgia Southern, because I don’t feel like an outcast. Everyone treats me with open arms, and all my friends can make me smile on my worst day."

Kayla White
Multimedia Film and Production

"Back in 2010 I was stationed about an hour away in Hinesville. I came here to mingle, meet new people and to network. I was always comfortable with this area and the diversity. Not too big of a town, not too small of a town and plenty of people… I am Jazmine Murphy, I am a soldier and I am Georgia Southern."

Jazmine Murphy
Cadet with the Eagle Battalion
