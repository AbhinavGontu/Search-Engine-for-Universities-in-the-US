Visiting Dickinson

​​​​​​​Dickinson has made the decision to move classes online for the rest of the semester. The campus is not open to visitors until further notice.

  • Kline Fitness Center: Alumni have free use of this spectacular facility with a Dickinson photo ID.
  • Waidner-Spahr Library: Alumni have free use of the library with a Dickinson photo ID.
  • The Handlebar: Bring your bike and learn the skills of bike repair and maintenance.
