Send a Video Message to WU Seniors

Video encouragement for the Waynesburg University Class of 2020 

Your message should be friendly and reassuring, letting the Class of 2020 know that despite the way their final semester ended, they will always be remembered. These seniors had a lot of positive impact on the University, and they should be celebrated for all of their accomplishments. The final draft will be edited as a compilation between 1:00-1:45 for better consumption on social media. 

Technical / submission details:

  • Record in a quiet room or outside while practicing social distancing
  • Wear your favorite Waynesburg shirt if you have one!
  • Horizontal video, not portrait orientation, for ease of video editing
  • Keep your message to no more than 30-45 seconds
  • Share via Google Drive link, Dropbox link or an email to paul.hicks@waynesburg.edu

Prompts and sample phrases to use:

  • Hey / what's up / how's it going class of 2020! I hope you are doing well.
  • Even though your senior year was way crazier than mine, I just wanted to reassure you / tell you / let you know that…
  • College only happens once, so don’t forget those incredible memories!
  • Thanks for your blessings / impact / spirit…
  • These past four years would’ve looked very different without all of you!
  • Go out into the world, and do great things, just like so many Waynesburg alumni have before you.
  • Go Jackets!
  • Jacket up!
  • Once a Jacket, always a Jacket!

Thank you for your interest in encouraging our seniors! If you have any questions, please contact us at alumni@waynesburg.edu.
