The GLBT Center is currently practicing social distancing per the CDC and university guidelines, and thus is operating on a remote basis until further notice. As a result, all walk-in hours will be suspended; however, we will be available to schedule phone and virtual appointments, as needed. Please feel free to email glbtcenter@ncsu.edu for any questions or concerns.

We care about our Pack community. As such, we encourage you to follow CDC and university social distancing guidelines, stay safe and know that we are one community who prides itself on supporting one another and being united during challenging times.

Day of Giving

In giving to the Gay, Lesbian, Bisexual and Transgender Center Fund on Day of Giving on March 24, you will help us bring in renowned speakers, provide students with opportunities to participate in local and national conferences and fund alumni gatherings.
