  • Our Team
SUNY Cortland Alumni Engagement is responsible for collaborating with the Cortland College Alumni Association, Inc., an independent non-profit organization, to serve the campus community and manage membership of over 76,000 living alumni through a variety of special programs, events and digital media. Alumni Engagement works with several campus departments to coordinate events for alumni and students such as chapter programming, philanthropy activities, career speakers series, alumni awards and helping support scholarships for students. 

The Alumni Engagement team can help you connect with the College and the Alumni Association programs and activities. General alumni inquires and questions can be sent to alumni@cortland.edu or calling 607-753-2516. Reservations or questions related to the Parks Alumni House can be sent to parksalumnihouse@cortland.edu or calling 607-753-1561.


Alumni Engagement is located on campus in Brockway Hall, Room 207 and the Parks Alumni House is located on 29 Tompkins Street.
