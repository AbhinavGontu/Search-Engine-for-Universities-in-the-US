June 02, 2017

Spartan Advance Announces Golf Fundraiser Date

The York College athletic department has announced the date for the 2017 Spartan Advance Golf Fundraiser event. This year’s tournament will be held on Friday, October 6, 2017 at the Country Club of York.

August 19, 2017 Men's Soccer

Spartan Men's Soccer Announces Week of Giving Event

York College head men’s soccer coach Evan Scheffey has announced that the Spartan men’s soccer program will sponsor a “Week of Giving” from August 21-25. The week of giving corresponds with the start of the Spartans’ pre-season practice schedule.

July 27, 2016 Women's Golf

York College Adds Women's Golf to Spartan Varsity Lineup

The York College athletic department is happy to announce that women’s golf will become the school’s 23rd varsity sport starting in the fall of 2017.

July 14, 2017

Spartans Lead CAC All-Academic Squad

The Capital Athletic Conference announced another record-setting academic performance by the conference’s student-athletes. The York College Spartans again headline the group as, for the second year in a row, the Spartan athletic department placed the highest percentage of student-athletes on the CAC All-Academic list.

May 17, 2017

Spartans Announce 27th Hall of Fame Class

The York College athletic department is proud to announce that Duane Bastress, Brandon Bushey, and Mike Welch have been selected for induction into the York College Athletic Hall of Fame. The threesome will comprise the 27th class in the Hall of Fame’s history.
