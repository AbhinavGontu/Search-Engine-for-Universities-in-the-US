A Little Day Music

Weekdays 11am - 2pm on WVTF Music
  • Hosted by Kevin Sanders

Classical music and unique tracks with WVTF Music host Kevin Sanders.

Michael Slon Brahms' Requiem

By Kevin Sanders • May 13, 2019

A Little Day Music host Kevin Sanders speaks with Michael Slon, Music Director Oratorio Society of Virginia about Brahms' Requiem, the Notre Dame Cathedral fire and even UVa campus parking.

Peter Leonard's New Projects

By Kevin Sanders • Mar 7, 2019

Kevin Sanders talks with Peter Leonard about the latest news with the Opera on the James and his new projects with the Academy Center for the Arts in Lynchburg and the Metropolitan Opera.

A Conversation with Peter Leonard, Opera on the James

By Kevin Sanders • Nov 2, 2018

Kevin Sanders talks with conductor Peter Leonard about the upcoming season of Opera on the James.

A Conversation with Steven White, Opera Roanoke

By Kevin Sanders • Oct 9, 2018

Kevin Sanders interviews Steven White, Conductor of Opera Roanoke about the season opener "Opera to Die For".

An Interview with Pianist Richard Masters

By Kevin Sanders • Apr 13, 2018

Kevin Sanders interviews pianist Richard Masters of the Virginia Tech School of Performing Arts.  Masters previews his April 16th recital featuring music from Debussy and Shubert, part of the Music on Mondays series at Virginia Tech.
