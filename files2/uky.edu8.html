Spring Services Updates

March 10, 01:56 pm
A-Z Databases: Explore the Latest Updates
More...

UK Libraries is working to improve discovery of library databases and will be implementing some big changes to our Database Search Page soon. Patrons can expect several improvements in the new format, including an updated search interface, new and popular database highlights, easy drop-menu browsing by topic and more.

Access a preview of UK Libraries' new A-Z Database List. 

Visit Safe & Smart RESTART for additional spring semester updates.

March 9, 05:50 pm
Access Your Electronic PDFs with Just One Click
More...

Accessing PDFs through UK Libraries' website just became easier with the help of LibKey Nomad from Third Iron. Now available on more browsers and content platforms, this browser extension enables users to access content on publisher websites, Wikipedia, PubMed, Web of Science, Scopus and more with just one click. 

Visit UK Libraries' Electronic Resources blog to download LibKey Nomad or learn more.
