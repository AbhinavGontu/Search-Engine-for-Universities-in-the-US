Report an ATI 508 Web Accessibility Issue

If you are having an issue with using assistive technology on a website please let us know. We will work with the department to resolve this issue. We make it our policy to make web resources accessible to all regardless of disability.

Stop! You may be in the wrong place!

This form addresses section 508 standards for dealing with accessible technology and NOT access to websites or their materials.
If you have an issue with logging into a campus site, email, MyCSUDH, or any other technical support issue, please submit an IT HelpDesk ticket.
