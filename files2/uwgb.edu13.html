Faculty & Staff

The University of Wisconsin-Green Bay faculty and staff provide an interdisciplinary, problem-focused educational experience that prepares students to think critically and address complex issues in a multicultural and evolving world. The University enriches the quality of life for students and the community by embracing the educational value of inclusivity, promoting environmental sustainability, encouraging engaged citizenship and serving as an intellectual, cultural and economic resource. Learn from the best and enroll today.

World-class Faculty

The classroom is a place of growth and collaboration at UW-Green Bay. Our highly acclaimed faculty provides students with a broad range of experiences and expertise through hands-on learning, exciting travel opportunities, and courses that are always diverse and engaging.

UW-Green Bay faculty members are known as sensational teachers, researchers, authors, and mentors. In recent years our faculty from across disciplinesHistory, Political Science, Human Development and Education, to name a fewhave received state and national honors for exceptional teaching. This best-practice teaching involves helping students define and solve problems from multiple perspectives, and engaging students in hands-on learning alongside the very best as they study real-world issues.
