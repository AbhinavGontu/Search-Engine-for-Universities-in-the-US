Need Help?

If there’s something you need, we have an office or resource for it. Check out the FAQs below to see all the ways we’re here to help you succeed. 

Can I receive tutoring or get help with course assignments?

Success starts with having the right support. Rider's Academic Success Center offers free tutoring and other forms of academic support remotely.

Please visit our TutorTrac page for a list of services offered remotely and the processes to request an appointment.

 

TutorTrac Web Page

What are my options for ordering books and learning resources?

Go to the Rider Bookstore web page to view current hours of operation. Bookstore staff are available by phone until 5 p.m. or you can email [email protected].

Orders are also available through Rider's online store, however please understand shipments may take a few days longer than normal.

 

Bookstore Web Page

How do I access and use Canvas?

Canvas is the Learning Management System that Rider University uses for learning and teaching.

You can log in using your RIDER KEY User ID. You’ll find a list of the courses that you are enrolled in under the “Courses” or "Courses & Groups" tab, along with any groups of which you are a member.

View the Canvas for Students page for more FAQs.

 

Canvas for Students

Can someone help prepare my resume or practice interview skills?

Whether you’re just starting to think about your major or career, or you’re gearing up to join the job market, the office of Career Development and Success is here to help. 

Visit the Careers website to search for jobs on Handshake, practice your interview skills, get resume help, watch educational videos and more.

 

Career Development & Success

What type of counseling services are available to students?

Counseling services are free, confidential, and available to students.

We currently offer phone and video-based online consultations. If you are interested in scheduling a teletherapy consultation please contact Zoerner House at 609-896-5157.

If you are currently away from the local campus area, we suggest that you consider local resources for therapy. In the event of an emergency, please dial 911 immediately.

Visit the Counseling Services web page to view additional resources. 

 

Counseling Services Web Page

Where can I find academic calendars or request a transcript?

The Registrar's office offers a variety of web-based services.  Please go to the Registrar's web page to view student schedules and registration info, view grades, request transcripts, apply to graduate, and much more.

If you need to have your transcript sent immediately, please request to have it sent electronically. Printed transcripts are now available for pickup. Please allow up to 2 weeks for processing and mailing.

 

Registrar's Office

Are the University Libraries open remotely?

Many library services and resources are available to students and faculty from the Libraries homepage, library.rider.edu, including access to materials, research assistance, interlibrary loans, and assistance with archives and special collections.

Go to the University Libraries web page to view hours of operation.

 

University Libraries Web Page

Wander with Intention

When you are a Rider Bronc, you are free to engage, free to change and free to explore. That might mean studying subjects you've loved for years. Or following one interest to the next until you arrive exactly where you are meant to be. There are so many possibilities ahead, and all you have to do is take the first step with us.
