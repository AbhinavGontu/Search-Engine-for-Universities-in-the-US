Locations

Main Campus

St. Scholastica's main campus is in Duluth, Minnesota, on 186 wooded acres. Founded in 1912, it features the iconic Tower Hall, the Science Center, Burns Wellness Center, and a variety of on-campus dorms, suites and apartments. For more than 2,600 students each year, it's home.

Our main campus offers a full slate of traditional undergraduate and graduate programs, as well as a variety of Extended Campus programs.

Other Locations

With our additional locations, there are more opportunities than ever to get a degree from St. Scholastica. Our undergraduate and graduate degrees are offered through online programs, accelerated course schedules, and onsite evening and weekend classes. These programs offer the same quality, values-based education and sense of community St. Scholastica is known for - with added flexibility for busy lives.
