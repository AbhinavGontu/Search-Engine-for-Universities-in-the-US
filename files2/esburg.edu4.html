    Alumni Q&A with Veteran John "Buzz" Walters

He graduated four years later and went on to a career in education while continuing his service to our country. This past summer, Buzz was awarded the John C. “Ace” Mann Blue Cap of the Year Award by the American Legion Department of Pennsylvania.
