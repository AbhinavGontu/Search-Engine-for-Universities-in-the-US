Shuttle Service – Spring 2021

Effective Tuesday, January 19, 2021

 

During inclement weather shuttles will operate on the Weekend Schedule (Every 40 minutes). Shuttles will service Main Campus, Ballston Academic Building and Rixey Apartments as well as the Ballston Metro. The Ballston Express Service will not be operating.
