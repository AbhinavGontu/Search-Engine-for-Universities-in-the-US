    OLLU Athletic Fund
     

       

    Our Lady of the Lake University's OLLU ATHLETIC FUND
    provides resources to the University's Athletic Department, student athletes, coaches
    and teams, as well as helps promote academics and sports on the West Side of San Antonio.
    The funds raised will help OLLU continue the success of the sports programs and inspire
    students to excel on the playing field and in the classroom while following the mission and core values of OLLU.


    Here you are able to gift directly to:
    Men's Baseball, Basketball, Cross-Country, Golf, Soccer, Tennis and Track and Field;
    Women's Basketball, Cross-Country, Soccer, Softball, Track and Field and Volleyball.

        All monies given to the OLLU ATHLETIC FUND are tax deductible.
