The Mother Pauline Society
The Planned Giving Society at Saint Mary’s College

A planned gift is a wonderful way to leave your legacy at Saint Mary’s. Through a planned gift you can meet your own financial goals and make a significant impact on the future of the College.

A planned gift can help you:

  • Increase your income.
  • Pay fewer taxes.
  • Provide the best inherited assets to your heirs.
  • Make a substantial gift to Saint Mary’s.

The gift planning professionals at Saint Mary’s are here to help you as you navigate this process. We are here to work with you and with your financial and legal advisors to develop an individualized gift plan that meets all of your needs.

If you would like more information on planned gifts at Saint Mary’s or if you have already made a planned gift to Saint Mary’s, please contact us. We look forward to working with you and welcoming you into the Mother Pauline Society, the gift planning society of Saint Mary’s.
