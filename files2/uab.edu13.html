Students

Our students are the main focus of the UAB Graduate School. Our vision is to improve lives globally through the accomplishments of our scholars, and in order to see our vision fulfilled, we know that requires investment in our students. Whether you are beginning the application process, are just getting started in your program, or are completing your degree, we are here to support you and help you fulfill your vision at UAB.
