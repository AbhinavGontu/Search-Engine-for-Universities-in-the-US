What If...the Campaign for Austin Peay

Austin Peay State University is embarking on a new philanthropic campaign, representing the most ambitious fundraising effort in the University’s 93-year history. "What If" is the theme for this comprehensive campaign. Read more here.

 

Message from Interim President Whiteside

The time has come for the University to move forward with this important initiative. To continue providing students with a high-quality education, while also maintaining the University’s distinct culture, Austin Peay needs to expand and grow our reach. Read more here.

 

Campaign Priorities
As a result of the emergence of the COVID-19 pandemic, initiatives such as scholarships and area funds of excellence will have an elevated role in promoting the success of the University and its students. Your support will allow us to continue producing thoughtful leaders and service-minded citizens for generations to come. Read our list of priorities.
