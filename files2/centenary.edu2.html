Undergraduate Tuition & Aid

A respected Centenary degree is a wise investment. The College offers a wide variety of financial aid options for students and their families, and 96% of Centenary students receive merit aid.When you apply to Centenary, you are automatically considered for academic scholarships. But there are many other options to help make your education affordable, and Centenary's Financial Aid staff will help you identify and apply for student loans, work-study jobs, and other forms of aid.

An important first step is filling out your FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) which serves as the application for all federal programs and Louisiana's TOPS awards. The FAFSA is also used to award need-based institutional scholarships provided by Centenary.

Centenary's FAFSA code is 002003.
