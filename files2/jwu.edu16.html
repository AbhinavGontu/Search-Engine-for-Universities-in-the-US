Welcome to the JWU Employment Site!

At JWU, each one of us — students and employees — has come to JWU to excel, to contribute and to follow our passions and meet our goals. We cultivate and take pride in our diverse and inclusive community, which inspires excellence, innovation and an enhanced student experience.

With a history that spans over 100 years, JWU provides students with an exceptional blend of traditional and experiential education. Our university is composed of highly dedicated employees who share a wide spectrum of skills, abilities and talents.

We welcome and invite you to explore both JWU and those opportunities that mirror your own passions, experience, skills and aspirations for success.

EEO Statement

Johnson & Wales University is an equal opportunity employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, age, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, genetic information, national origin, disability status, protected veteran status or any other basis prohibited by law.

Reasonable Accommodations

If you are a qualified individual with a disability, you have the right to request a reasonable accommodation for purposes of participating in the application/hiring process with Johnson & Wales University. If you are unable or limited in your ability to use or access the JWU Employment Site as a result of your disability, you can request reasonable accommodations by calling 401-598-1034. Thanks for your interest in JWU!

Contact Us

Contact Human Resources via email or phone at 401-598-1034 if you have questions regarding employment at JWU.
