University Marketing

University Marketing produces and disseminates information about the programs, research, and activities of the University to both internal and external audiences using traditional and electronic media. Our professionals also partner in University recruitment efforts through the development of branded, strategic marketing tools that enhance the University's worldwide visibility and reputation.
