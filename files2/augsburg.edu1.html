Application Information

You are called to do great things. An Augsburg University degree gets you to your career. More importantly, we help you think differently. Our blended schedule combines classroom and online learning to optimize student engagement and convenience. Our faculty are passionate about teaching and learning.

Where are we? We are conveniently located in Bethel Lutheran Church on 3rd Ave. SE, with free parking, just blocks from downtown Rochester.

We’ve had the pleasure of offering education in the Rochester area for more than two decades. Our graduates are everywhere in Rochester. Become one of them.

Why Augsburg?

Our programs are geared toward the working professional. Our faculty and staff understand the challenges faced by students who work full time, have family responsibilities, and still want to go to school to earn their next degree. Students determine their own course load term-by-term, providing additional flexibility for working professionals.

Financing your degree. Many students finance their degree program through a combination of financing options, including state and federal loans and grants, scholarships, and employer tuition reimbursement programs. Augsburg also offers a variety of payment plans and tuition discounts, for those who qualify.

We are fully accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. Our education means something and we’re proud to have the backing of HLC.
