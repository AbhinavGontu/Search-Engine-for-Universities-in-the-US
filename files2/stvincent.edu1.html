The Latimer Family Library is pleased to offer this Web-based interface to its catalog of resources.

Search the Library Catalog - The Library's online public access catalog containing our collection of books, videos, compact discs, etc.

Serials A-to-Z - A comprehensive list of electronic journals that can be accessed from the Library, including links and coverage information.

EBSCOhost - This will take you to several databases including Academic Search, ATLA Religion Database, ERIC, MEDLINE, MLA, PsychINFO, and others.

Gale Virtual Reference Library - Provides access to an electronic version of Environmental Encyclopedia, 3rd ed., Dictionary of American History, 3rd ed., and New Catholic Encyclopedia, 2nd ed., including the 2010 supplement.

Interlibrary Loan Request - Interlibrary Loan allows us to borrow materials and obtain articles from other libraries. This service is available to all current students, faculty and staff.

IPA Source - A database providing phonetic pronunciation guides for vocal performance.

JSTOR Arts & Sciences I, II, III, VII and Biological Sciences - An archival database of journals in a variety of disciplines, including the humanities, social sciences, and natural sciences.

Lexis/Nexis Academic Universe - A database containing full text newspapers and a variety of other business and legal resources.

Past Masters - The world's largest collection of full-text electronic editions in philosophy, including significant collections in political thought, religious studies, sociology, the history of science, economics, and classics.

Featured Lists - Access library-defined lists highlighting special collections.

THE BONIFACE WIMMER MUSIC COLLECTION (BWMC) - The Boniface Wimmer Music Collection includes details on over 4,200 compositions covering 1,600 composers. This music has been carefully inventoried and stored by Father Jerome Purta, O.S.B.; completed in early 2015, it can be accessed by appointment at the Latimer Family Library. Inquiries may be made by email to library@stvincent.edu.

View Your Own Record - Find out about items you have checked out, holds status, and fines.
