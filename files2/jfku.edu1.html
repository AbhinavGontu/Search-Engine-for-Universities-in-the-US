Welcome Alumni!

Every year, more than 6,000 graduates join the 180,000+ National University alumni living across the country and internationally. As a member of the Alumni Association, we welcome and encourage you to get involved with us.

The National University Alumni Association (NUAA) offers many ways to stay in touch.

  • Attend our alumni and student events to connect with old friends and new.
  • Take advantage of our career services that offer comprehensive career and employment-related services to you at no cost.
  • Become a mentor and share your knowledge and experience with current NU students or fellow alumni.
  • Get involved with one of our regional chapters or join an activity club.
  • Keep in touch via Instagram, Facebook or LinkedIn and let us know how you’re doing today!
