Coronavirus Response

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, Woodward Library is open to APSU employees and currently enrolled students only. Everyone entering the library must swipe in with their APSU ID card. Per university guidelines, wearing a mask is required while in the building, and social distancing protocols must be followed.
