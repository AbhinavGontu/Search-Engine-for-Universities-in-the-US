Stations of the Cross

Throughout the season of Lent, FaithND will offer virtual experiences of the Stations of the Cross every Friday. Each prayer experience will meditate on the Way of the Cross from the perspective of a different theme and will feature the Stations of the Cross from the art of a new location on campus.

Invite family and friends to join us on the Lenten journey by subscribing to the Daily Gospel Reflection.

 

Friday after Ash Wednesday


 Stations of the Cross in the Time of Covid-19

Featuring art from St. Francis of Assisi Chapel in Breen-Phillips Hall

 


Friday of the First Week of Lent


Stations of the Cross with Mary, Our Lady of Sorrows

Featuring art from St. Teresa of Calcutta Chapel in Johnson Family Hall

 


Friday of the Second Week of Lent


 Stations of the Cross: Family Life

Featuring art from St. Charles Borromeo Chapel in Alumni Hall

 


Friday of the Third Week of Lent


Stations of the Cross: The Paradox of the Cross

Featuring art from St. Martin de Porres Chapel in Baumer Hall
