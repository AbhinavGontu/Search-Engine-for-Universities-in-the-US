Reunion Giving

  • Home
  • Events & Programs
  • Reunion
  • Reunion Giving

Reunion is a wonderful time to honor your fellow classmates and support current students with a gift to the Wells Fund!

Here's how it works

Your class Reunion gift includes everything given or pledged by members of your class between July 1, 2020 and June 20, 2021. This includes gifts to the Wells Fund, gifts designated to another specific fund or purpose at Wells, expected employer matching gifts, planned gifts contracted during the Reunion year and gifts made by friends and family members in honor or memory of your class.

If you would like to make a sizeable gift over a period of time, consider making a multi-year pledge and donate over three years. All pledges made between July 1, 2020 and June 30, 2021 that are due by June 30, 2023 will count toward your Reunion gift. You can also do a monthly pledge.

Your gift made by 6/30/21 + Your pledge payable by 6/30/22 + Your pledge payable by 6/30/23 = YOUR REUNION GIFT

For more about Reunion giving, please contact Jessica Corter, director of annual giving at jcorter@wells.edu

For more information about making a planned gift, please visit wells.edu/plannedgiving or email advancement@wells.edu
