Faculty & Staff

You teach. You collaborate. You set goals and you achieve them. You also help UNCG students find their way, and when it comes to making a difference in their lives, you make it happen. While you’re working hard contributing to the UNC Greensboro experience, remember that UNCG is also here to make a meaningful difference in your pursuits. Stay informed with the Campus Weekly email. Take in a variety of special events in athletics, academics, and the arts. Get connected to support centers in technology, health and wellness, professional enrichment, leadership development, and more. A University environment like ours offers many benefits, resources, and communities that help you make a difference and fulfill your goals.
