Hall of Fame - Malone University

NEWEST CLASS INDUCTED!
The 2020 class was officially inducted during a ceremony on Malone's campus on Friday, January 24th, 2020.  A banquet was held in Malone's Johnson Center at 6:30 pm that evening.  The inductees were also recognized on Saturday, January 25th during the Malone basketball doubleheader against Hillsdale College.  Click here to read about the nine newest inductees. 

The nomination deadline for the next induction class will be on July 1, 2020.
