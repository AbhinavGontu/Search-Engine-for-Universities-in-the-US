C&IS Hall of Fame Inductee Tributes

The College celebrated its 21st class of C&IS Hall of Fame inductees on February 19, 2021. This historic class includes award-winning journalist, children's book author and community hero, Nancy Parker Boyd; former C&IS Dean, distinguished author, historian and educator, E. Culpepper Clark; CBS Chief Legal Correspondent, NYT best-selling author and SCOTUS expert, Jan Crawford; and lawyer, founder of the Equal Justice Initiative and social justice activist, Bryan Stevenson.
