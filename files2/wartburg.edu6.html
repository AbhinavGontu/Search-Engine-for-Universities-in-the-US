Through a unique college-community partnership, the 200,000 square foot Wartburg-Waverly Sports & Wellness Center
administers year-round athletics and recreation for Wartburg College and the City of Waverly.

Welcome Desk: 319-352-8311 | thew@wartburg.edu

Membership Options - Click to View

Membership Types and Prices

Individual – $50/month ($50 activation fee)
A month-to-month membership for any individual 12 years of age or older. 

Individual + 1 – $70/month ($70 activation fee)
A month-to-month membership for two people living in the same household and they must be considered immediate family.  Immediate Family:  A married couple and/or single parent and their children.  The children must be 19 years of age or younger and living at home.  If a child is between the ages of 20-24 they are eligible to be included in this membership if they are still being counted as a dependent child on the parent(s) income tax.

Individual + 2 or more – $80/month ($80 activation fee)
A month-to-month membership for three or more people living in the same household and they must be considered immediate family.   Immediate Family:  A married couple and/or single parent and their children.  The children must be 19 years of age or younger and living at home.  If a child is between the ages of 20-24 they are eligible to be included in this membership if they are still being counted as a dependent child on the parent(s) income tax.

Senior (65 yrs+) – $37.50/month (no activation fee)
A month-to-month membership for any individual 65 years of age or older. (Senior discount:  The activation fee is waived when joining or rejoining)

Senior + 1 – $55/month (no activation fee)
A month-to-month membership for any married couple who are 65 years of age or older.  (Senior discount:  The activation fee is waived when joining or rejoining)

Benefits of Membership

  • Access to gated member parking lot
  • Access to member locker room (Click here for locker room policies.)
  • Up to 75 minutes of free child care for kids ages 6 months to 7 years while parents are using The W
  • Special rates on group fitness classes and personal training
  • Free admission to all Wartburg home athletic events

Current members can check their accounts online and check their member visits. Please click to view this information. Any questions regarding usernames or passwords, contact Carrie Gleason at 352-8716.

Join the W Today!
New members must stop by the Welcome Desk to register. You will need to get a photo ID and complete paperwork. If you have question prior to joining, call 319-352-8311.

Reduced-Price Memberships

Reduced-price programs are available. Families or members of families are eligible for reduced-price programs if they are currently receiving food stamps. Youth are eligible for reduced-price programs if they receive reduced-price or free hot lunches. All activation fees are waived.

  • Individual – $37.50/month 
  • Individual + 1 – $55/month 
  • Individual + 2 or more – $65/month 

Participants will need to provide verification of assistance, due to privacy laws, The W will no longer verify assistance with the schools or federal/state agencies.  A letter is mailed or emailed to each family receiving free or reduced lunches at the beginning of each school year.  A copy of this letter may submitted to qualify for reduced programming. Other forms of assistance may be submitted to qualify for reduced programming. Examples: food stamp letter of eligibility. Please call 319-352-8716 for questions concerning qualifications.

Other Membership Types

Area College Students on Breaks:  A membership that allows college students who visit the area during the summer months or breaks during the academic year.  (The activation fee is waived)

1 week=$30
2 weeks=$40
3 week=$50
4 weeks or a full calendar month=$60
More than 4 weeks= prorated dues for the current month then $50/month thereafter. 

Temporary Membership:  A membership that allows people who are temporarily working in the area or are here for an extended visit with family and friends.  These people must reside outside of Bremer, Butler, Blackhawk, Buchanan, Chickasaw, Floyd, and Fayette Counties. (The activation fee is waived)

1 week=$30
2 weeks=$40
3 week=$50
4 weeks or a full calendar month=$60
