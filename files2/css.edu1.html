Undergraduate Applications

Scholastica App

Common App

The College of St. Scholastica offers first-time and international students two options to apply. The Scholastica App is a tool that we have long offered. Students are also welcome to apply using the Common App. It's FREE to apply!

St. Scholastica offers test-optional admission for first-year, undergraduate students. Applicants can choose whether or not to submit test scores (ACT or SAT) with their application. If applicants do not feel their standardized test scores reflect their academic ability, they can submit an essay describing their academic accomplishments.
