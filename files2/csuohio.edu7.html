ADMITTED STUDENTS: REGISTER FOR ORIENTATION!

Register for orientation using CampusNet, Cleveland State’s online student portal, call 216-523-7416, or email orientation@csuohio to reserve your spot as a CSU Viking!

At Cleveland State University we continue to closely monitor the spread and impact of the COVID-19 virus. All Orientation sessions will be held in an online format. You’ll still receive a Viking welcome, an opportunity to connect with current CSU students, faculty and staff, and advice on registering for classes, all from the comfort of your own home.

Incoming Summer and Fall 2021 students that are registered for Orientation will be receiving additional details about their online Orientation program as we get closer to their scheduled date. 
​​​​​​​

Who We Are...

Orientation is designed for students who have made their decision to attend Cleveland State University. At Orientation, we will provide you with all the information you need in a convenient and precise way. Expect to meet new people and learn about the University.
