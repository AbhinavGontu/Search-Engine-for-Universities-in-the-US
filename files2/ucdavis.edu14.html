Welcome to UC Davis Orientation

Welcome New Aggies!

Congratulations on your admission to UC Davis, where the most innovative ideas come from the most diverse minds. Here, you will be welcomed like family—and challenged to think about a future that hasn’t been thought of before.

To help you prepare for a great first year, we have planned a comprehensive UC Davis Orientation experience in three mandatory parts. Be sure to read through the information about all three, including pricing information and financial assistance, in order to be prepared for your first quarter at UC Davis!

Step 1

Aggie 101

Learn about UC Davis resources, explore your Aggie community, and prepare to arrive on campus - all online. Aggie 101 is now live for all new students!

Start now!

Step 2

Aggie Advising

Receive academic Aggie Advising remotely in preparation for course schedule planning and registration.

Make a Plan!

Step 3

Aggie Orientation

Get excited about being an Aggie! Build community networks, find resources, and begin your life at UC Davis during our five day Aggie Orientation program.
