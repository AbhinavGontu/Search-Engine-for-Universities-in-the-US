Donations

We try to help out as many people as possible with our donations, but when it comes down to it, there are a few areas that we tend to support more than others.

Here at Ian’s, we like to help the local music scene in any way possible. We are also heavily committed to being as friendly to the environment as we can, so if that’s your shtick, then we might help you out too. We also like to support organizations that deal with issues surrounding children, because, after all, children are the future (and future pizza fanatics). We also enjoy having a strong connection to the Universities that have helped build our cult following, so we’re happy to work with University Orgs.

If you’ve read the guidelines and feel like you fit into any of these areas, then, by all means, please fill out a Dough-Nation request form by clicking the link below and we will get back to you. Thanks for thinking of us!
