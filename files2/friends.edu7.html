Apply – Undergraduate

Apply to Friends’ Undergraduate Program

Most high school juniors and seniors, as well as students considering transferring from one college or university to another, will apply to several universities during the college search process. This practice ensures that you do not limit your options or have to play catch-up once you’ve made a decision.

Applying to Friends is easy and convenient, and you can choose how you wish to apply for admission.

  • Complete the online application
  • Download the fillable PDF application and email it back to us at admissions@friends.edu.

Requirements for International Applicants

As an international student, there are a few more requirements for admission, but we’ll walk you through them step by step. Get all the information you need to be admitted to Friends University as an international student.
