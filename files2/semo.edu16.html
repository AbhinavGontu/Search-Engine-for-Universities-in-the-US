Complete Your Application
  1. Create your online account with Southeast Missouri State University.
  2. Select “Web Only” as your location of interest.
  3. Fill out your application to the University.

NOTE: If you are a current Southeast student and wish to change to an online program, contact us at southeastonline@semo.edu.

Apply Now to start your online program

Build your Success Plan

Once you’re admitted, build your plan for success—we’ll help!

  1. Look over your advising resources.
  2. Select “Recently Admitted.”
  3. Activate your SE Key—this will launch your dashboard, where you can plan for and keep track of your success.
  4. Read the Online Guide to Success—it’s a fast overview of making the most of an online course and your online program.
  5. Take a demo course, test your system, and prepare to succeed. (Optional)
