Dance NOW! Miami to Perform at Coker University

Internationally renowned dance company Dance NOW! Miami will be performing at Coker University on Thursday, March 18, 2021, at 7:30 PM in Watson Theatre. Coker faculty, staff, and students have the option to attend in-person as there are a limited number of in-person tickets available on a first-come, first-served basis at coker.edu/vpa-tickets. Tickets for virtual attendees are also available at coker.edu/vpa-tickets.

Grady B. Jones Joins Coker University as Interim Vice President of Institutional Advancement

Coker University recently appointed Grady B. Jones as Interim Vice President of Institutional Advancement. 

Coker University Recognizes 230 Students for Academic Achievement

230 Coker University students were recognized on the President’s List or Dean’s List for earning a 4.0 or 3.75 GPA respectively for the 2020 fall semester.
