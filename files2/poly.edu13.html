Technology

Helpdesk

ITS provides computing supports for all faculty, administrators, staff, and students at NYU Law.

Make Helpdesk your first call for any of your technology-related issues. If we can’t resolve your issue or answer your question, we’ll find the right group to do so or put you in their capable hands.

Please contact us via phone or email, details are in the sidebar. You can also open a ticket for your request if you know what you need.

While we hope you don’t have to call, we look forward to assisting you when you do.
