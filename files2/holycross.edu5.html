    Webcams

    Get a bird’s-eye view of Holy Cross via our webcams. The cameras provide live, streaming video 24/7.

    Hogan Courtyard

    A busy "crossroads" of campus, the Hogan Courtyard is a popular spot for outdoor gatherings and student events. The buildings, from left, are O’Kane (with clock tower), Fenwick and Smith halls. The Worcester skyline is in the background.

    View the webcam »

    Linden Lane

    Linden Lane, the beloved leafy entrance to the College, serves as the first point of contact for many visitors. To the right is O’Kane Hall and its landmark clock tower. The Worcester skyline is in the background.

    View the webcam »

    Prior Center for Performing Arts

    Situated between the Hogan Campus Center and the Luth Athletic Complex, the performing arts center, will include a 400-seat concert hall and a 200-seat flexible performance space, plus technology to support creative interactions between the arts and other disciplines.  

    View the webcam »
    Learn more about the Prior Center for Performing Arts »

    Recreation and Wellness Center

    Watch construction of the Joanne Chouinard-Luth Recreation and Wellness Center, which will replace the Field House. The new facility will be home for all recreation, fitness, and wellness programming for students, faculty, and staff.

    View the webcam »
    Learn more about the Recreation and Wellness Center »
