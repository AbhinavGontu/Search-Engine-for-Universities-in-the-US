Be Kept Up to Date on How to Stay Safe.

Both the current UW-Green Bay Policy and the Governors order require that face coverings be used at all times while an individual is inside common areas of buildings or in situations where physical distancing is not possible. All the details are here.

Our faculty are not only experts in their fields, but they have also continued their professional development in the areas of virtual learning to be ready to give you the quality classroom experience you deserve. Learn how to find the mode of your courses on the Learning Options webpage.

Transformation

On campus, every day is different from the last. From the people you meet, to the conversations you have, to the way you look at life, its always new and exciting. Opportunities surround you and possibilities are endless. At UW-Green Bay, thousands of students compete in intramural sports, go to concerts, or volunteer in the community, and the list goes on. Not a Phoenix yet? Come visit or even apply!

Get Involved!

See the many options to enjoy Student Life at UW-Green Bay.

Make a Difference

Explore, take part, and share your interests with other like-minded students in over 100 Student Orgs or guide the student experience with Student Government.

Find Your Fit

Meet friends and compete in Intramurals. Work out, do yoga, or swim a lap at the Kress Events Center. Root for the team at a home game! Go Phoenix!

Your University Union

Grab a cup of coffee or split a pizza with UW-Green Bay's Dining options or enjoy the pool tables, video games, good friends and great events at the University Union.

You're Accepted

Find like-minded peers at the Pride Center, Multi-Ethnic Student Affairs (MESA Office) or find a Student Org that interests you.

Still Haven't Picked a Major?

This is a big decision and we have help if you need it! If you dont have a major picked out yet, its okay. Our Career Services office has advisers who can also help you explore different career options or ask Academic Advising during their drop in sessions to explore new class options.

Student Services (GBOSS)

GBOSS - Green Bay One Stop Shop is here to help. We offer many services that are available on campus free to students, so take advantage of them! From getting books for class at the Phoenix Bookstore, helping you register for classes or graduate, we have you covered - all in one place.

Financial Aid, Scholarships & Jobs

Learn how to file for FAFSA and loans at Financial Aid. Explore Scholarships. Earn cash as a Student Employee on campus.

Housing & myResLink

Looking to live on campus, change rooms, or find out your room assignment? Did you forget your mail combination or need a dorm repair? Visit myResLink.

Tutoring, Writing Center & Library

Get an extra hand at Tutoring Services, help on your paper at the Writing Center or find the perfect reference at the Cofrin Library.

Pass Points & Parking

Fill up on Pass Points to spend on campus or off. Don't forget to register your vehicle or guest at Virtual Parking Permit.
