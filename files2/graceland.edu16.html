Welcome to Graceland University!

Graceland was founded in 1895 on a liberal arts foundation. Since then, the university has grown and adapted with the world and we would love for you to become a part of our community. We offer undergraduate and graduate degrees at two campuses and in an online environment. Interested? Check out our admission requirements, available financial aid and much more.

Undergraduate

Are you planning to attend Graceland directly from high school? Check out the admissions information for undergraduate programs.

Graduate

Have you already completed your undergraduate degree and you are now interested in advancing your career? Check out our graduate programs in nursing, data science, religion and nutrition.

Online

Graceland offers undergraduate and graduate programs in a convenient online environment. We have been at the forefront of distance learning since before the internet. Learn more about our online offerings.

Transfer

Are you planning to transfer to Graceland for your undergraduate degree from another college? We would love for you to join us. View our transfer admissions steps.

International

Graceland's positioning in both academics and geography offers a unique opportunity to international students to pursue studies in a university environment while enjoying the best of the American heartland.

Learn more

Check out the successes our students are experiencing during their time at Graceland, and imagine the possibilities for your own success!
