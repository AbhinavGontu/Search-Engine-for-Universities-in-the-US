Central Savings Bank Community Calendar

 

 

CSB Burger Bash

CSB Pancake Breakfast 

For 22 years Central Savings Bank has been providing a free pancake breakfast to the Sault Ste Marie Community in early August. 

 

Lake State Classic 

A weekend for everyone, regardless of golf skills, it is about reuniting with fellow Lakers and friends from years past with the common goal of supporting LSSU. The proceeds from the tournament go to helping LSSU students pursue their academic and athletic dreams. The Lake State Classic is a way for Lakers, family and friends to gather and celebrate being a part of the Laker experience.

https://foundation.lssu.edulake-state-classic

 

Bud Cooper Classic 

The annual Bud Cooper Memorial Golf Classic is held at Wild Bluff Golf Course. Proceeds help cover expenses related to the basketball team’s operations. Members of the team will be on-hand to meet with participants.

https://foundation.lssu.edu/cooper-classic/
 

Men's Basketball Reverse Raffle 

The Reverse Raffle is the premier fundraiser for Men’s Basketball. This event includes a social hour and dinner, followed by the reverse raffle. Tickets are $100 the winner has a chance walk away with $5,000. This event takes place in the beginning of each basketball season.

 

 

Great Lake State Weekend 

Women’s Walk is the marquee fundraiser for women’s athletics at Lake Superior State University. During the past eight years, over $350,000 has been raised to help elevate our teams and give them the means to compete at the NCAA Division II national level. Donations help offset the costs of equipment, increased travel expenses and improving the overall student-athlete experience.

 

 

Laker Club

The Laker Club is the official support group for the entire Lake Superior State University athletics program, and contributions to Laker Club go toward scholarships for LSSU athletes.. Contributions to Laker Club assist individual athletes and empower Lake Superior State's Division I and II sports teams to remain competitive on a national scale.Click on the LSSU Foundation link below to learn the variety of member benefits to joining Laker Club. They include tax deductions, parking privileges, priority tickets and special recognition. You can also contact the LSSU Foundation, 906-635-2665, or Tom Coates, tcoates@lssu.edu or 906-635-6670.

https://foundation.lssu.edu/laker-club/

 

Women's Walk 

Women’s Walk is the marquee fundraiser for women’s athletics at Lake Superior State University. During the past eight years, over $350,000 has been raised to help elevate our teams and give them the means to compete at the NCAA Division II national level. Donations help offset the costs of equipment, increased travel expenses and improving the overall student-athlete experience.
