50th Anniversary

The Fond du Lac campus celebrates its 50th Anniversary year!

The Fond du Lac campus celebrated its 50th anniversary on Friday, November 2, 2018. See photos from the event here!

The Fond du Lac campus has a rich history worth celebrating. While the buildings, landscape and programs have evolved over five decades, its mission and vision have held strong: to be a premier educational institution dedicated to developing creative, responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our anniversary celebration included:

  • Special musical performances by the Fond du Lac campus alumni and friends
  • Cash bar and hors d’oeuvres
  • Opportunity to connect and share stories with fellow alumni, students, staff and community members

Over the last 50 years the Fond du Lac campus has provided student-centered education that is inspired by creativity and backed by the quality of the University of Wisconsin System.
