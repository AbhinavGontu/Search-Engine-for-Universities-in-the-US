Ways to Give

Why give?

Montana State University is on the rise -- in enrollment, size and prestige. Our university's continued excellence is reliant on the generosity of alumni, friends, parents, faculty and staff. Every gift, no matter the size, helps Montana State fulfill its land grant mission and provides unparalleled educational opportunities for future generations.

Annual giving

Annual giving is the university’s annual fundraising effort specifically seeking recurring gifts from alumni, parents, families, friends, faculty and staff in support of the university’s colleges and units.

This category of gifts fall in a range between $1 to $25,000, so every gift, no matter the amount, makes a difference by supporting our outstanding students and faculty, groundbreaking research and exceptional programs. When you participate in our annual fundraising campaigns, you help fulfill our land-grant mission and are to ensure that a great MSU experience is available for generations to come.

Annual gifts of $2,500* are placed in a special category of giving called the President’s Circle. Donors at this level receive invitations to special events and insider communications. (*faculty and staff enter the President's Circle with an annual gift of $1,200).

Alumni and friends who have given $1,000 or more in support to MSU throughout their lifetime, are recognized as part of the Blue & Gold Forever giving society. Donors at this level receive insider communications, and a variety of perks. *If you are a lifetime member of the Alumni Association, your membership will be credited as $1,000 of support to MSU no matter what you paid for that membership.

Gift Planning

Making a planned gift is a wonderful way to show your support and appreciation for Montana State University and its mission while accommodating your own personal, financial, estate-planning and philanthropic goals. Thoughtful planning can maximize your estate and can reduce the tax burden on your heirs and leaves a lasting legacy for MSU.

Our gift planning officers are here to help you determine the best gift vehicles to meet your financial needs and make a lasting impact at MSU. We can work with you, your family and your advisers to help you create a plan that meets both your financial and charitable goals.

Learn about gift planning options

Endowments

Donor endowed funds are Montana State University’s steadiest and most reliable source of funding. Endowed gifts can be designated and  are permanently invested to generate an annual return and provide continuing financial support.

Endowments provide a unique opportunity to eternally support what you care about most. These gifts may serve a specific purpose or may be directed for unrestricted general support. MSU’s endowment is made up of more than 1,200 individual funds that support faculty and students, including professorships and financial aid for undergraduates, graduate fellowships, academic programs and centers, and student life and activities.

Major gifts

Along with our mission of raising private support for the university, the Alumni Foundation’s efforts rely on the work of development officers to grow our potential for private support. These professionals work collaboratively with academic leadership and university administrators to secure gifts for university initiatives and campus priorities. By establishing and growing relationships with members of the MSU community, including alumni, parents, faculty, administrators and friends of the university, the development officers elevate our donors’ sights and affirm that Montana State University is a philanthropic priority.
