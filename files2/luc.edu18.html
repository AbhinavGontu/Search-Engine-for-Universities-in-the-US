News

Here is a sampling of where you can find timely updates about your alma mater, favorite professors, and former classmates.

Loyola News

Loyola News is a monthly eNewsletter that is sent the first week of the month to all alumni. If you aren't receiving Loyola News, update your communication preferences by logging into the online alumni directory.

Class notes

New home? New job? New baby? It's news to us so share it with the rest of the alumni community! Class notes submitted online or by mail will be included in a future issue of Loyola Magazine. Learn More

Inside Loyola

Inside Loyola online is the Loyola community’s primary source for University news. The site features daily updates of campus news and Loyola-related feature stories. It also highlights upcoming events and showcases campus photography and videos. Read Online

Loyola news room

This online portal is the home of Loyola University's comprehensive news site, brought to you by University Marketing and Communications. University press releases, lists of Loyola's subject experts, as well as contacts for media inquires can be found here. Read Online

Loyola Magazine

Loyola magazine has a circulation of roughly 150,000 and is published three times a year. The magazine website gets about 2,000 visitors a month. The mission of Loyola magazine is to engage and build connections among Loyola’s large external community, including alumni, parents, corporations, and friends; and to deepen their relationships and commitments to the University. The magazine features University news, as well as alumni calendars, class notes, and feature stories. If you aren't receiving Loyola Magazine, update your address by logging into the online alumni directory.  Read Online
