SMC ATHLETIC CAMPS

SMC ATHLETIC CAMPS | The Saint Mary's Experience

 

Nestled in the plush Moraga Valley, Saint Mary’s College is one of the most picturesque campuses on the West Coast. Saint Mary’s College provides outstanding Athletic Camps to children between the ages of 4-18 years. Our Athletic Camps are all offered in a variety of ways to accommodate all skill levels. 

Please note that residential camps are reserved for high school students only.

Refund Policy & Cancellation Plan
Request for a refund must be made 24 hours prior to the start of camp. In the event you must cancel your reservation,  a refund will be determined by the policy outlined below:

  • Camp credit can be applied to the same camp on a future date (expires one year from the start date of the originally purchased camp).
  • Cancellation Protection can be purchased during check out. If purchased, participants will receive a full refund minus the cancellation protection fee. Cancellation Protection can only be purchased on initial registration.

Questions About Our Summer Camps?
Our Athletic Camps Office would love to hear from you.
They can be reached at (925) 631-4FUN or smccamps@stmarys-ca.edu.

All Saint Mary’s Athletic camps are open to any and all entrants (limited only by number, age, grade level and/or gender).
