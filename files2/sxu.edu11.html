The Legends Fund

 

 

Reaching legendary status starts right here.

 

 

Join the team that’s elevating SXU Athletics.

You’re as passionate as we are about helping SXU Athletics reach the top of our game. In joining The Legends Fund, you’re on the team that’s creating sustainable support for next-level elite. Our student-athletes are doing their part — now it’s time to dig in and take a giant leap forward.

 

You’re up.

The Legends Fund gives everyone a way to make a difference. Get on board because a new era of Cougar Athletics tradition starts now.

 

Be part of the beginning.

Join us before December 31, 2020, and you’ll be a member of the Legends Fund starting lineup, The Founders Club. This inner circle entitles you to special exclusive benefits designed to recognize your dedication far into the future.
