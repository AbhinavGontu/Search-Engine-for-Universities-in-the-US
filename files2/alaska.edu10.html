Announcements

University of Alaska response to the Covid-19 virus:

For information concerning the Covid-19 virus from the University of Alaska, please refer to the University of Alaska coronavirus information page. The information on that page will be updated, as necessary, as the situation changes.

Students and Employees:

Select direct deposit for refunds and reimbursements today!  Direct Deposit is processed daily, but because of COVID, campuses are printing checks less frequently. Look under Student Services & Account Information or Financial Information to get your refund or other payment the fastest way possible.

Students:

Payment plans are open for enrollment. Divide your bill into equal payments due monthly. Go to Student Services & Account Information > Student Account > Pay Term Balance/Payment Plan Setup to get started.

Employees:

Direct Deposit for Payroll is now available through a new electronic form. Please click here to access the new form. Paper forms will not be accepted after April 6th.

Students* are required to complete UA Safe Title IX training annually. The deadline for Spring 2021 training is March 26, 2021. Click here to log in and access the student training.    
 *degree seeking, living in campus housing, middle college students enrolled at the university, or students on national/international exchange who have not completed the training since July 1, 2020

Employees are required to complete Title IX training each fiscal year by October 1. New employees are expected to complete the training within 30 days of hire.  Click here to log in and access the employee training.   

Students:

Some official notifications will be sent to your preferred email address, while others will be sent only to your UA email address.

To set up a forward from your UA email and designate your preferred email address, on the UAOnline Personal Information menu go to Manage Your Address, Email and Phone Information

Check your Class Schedule & Grades on your mobile!
