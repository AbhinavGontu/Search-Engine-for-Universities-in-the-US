FRIB status in COVID-19 situation

In compliance with the Michigan Occupational Safety and Health Administration’s Emergency Rules issued 14 October 2020, the FRIB Laboratory continues to limit on-site functions. Access to the facility is limited to specially-designated employees, contractors, and scientific users. Access and permitted activities are subject to stringent precautionary measures.
