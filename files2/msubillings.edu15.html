The MSUB Athletics office of communications provides coverage of the Yellowjackets' 16 varsity sports, including web and social media presence, gameday promotions and materials, and local media and community outreach. The office is responsible for maintenance of statistics, scheduling, and all forms of media associated with all sports. All inquiries regarding information about student-athletes and coaches, including requests for interviews, should be directed to the office of communications.

Preferred Nomenclature
When referring to MSUB Athletics, please refrain from using a 'dash' before Billings in the title of the university. Refer to the table below for the proper and improper nomenclature.
