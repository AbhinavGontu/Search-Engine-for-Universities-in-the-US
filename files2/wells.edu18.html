There are many ways for you to participate in this year's I Heart Wells Week!

Monday, Feb. 8, 2021

Print or make your own heart and write WHY you "heart" Wells and post or upload your photo to be part of our fun Valentine's Day post!

Click here to download the pdf

Tuesday, Feb. 9, 2021

SIP and WIP Tea Time with alums and students--all are invited! Bring your current projects, wear your Wells swag and be entered to win a prize!

Register here 

Wednesday, Feb. 10, 2021

Virtual Learning by the Lake with Linda Schwab '73 | Register here

Show us that you're wearing Wells red on Facebook, Instagram or Twitter to be entered into a drawing to win a prize!

Lakeside Lounge event for prospective students: Wells Traditions

Share your favorite trinket in the comments on the WCA Facebook #TBT post

Thursday, Feb. 11, 2021

Today we will ring the bells in honor of our community's essential workers.

Friday, Feb. 12, 2021

Remember to print or make your own heart and write WHY you "heart" Wells and post or upload your photo to be part of our fun Valentine's Day post!

Click here to download the pdf

Sunday, Feb. 14, 2021

Happy Valentine's Day! Be sure to check out all the reasons why our community "hearts" Wells on our Facebook page.
