Athletics

Ready to bleed purple? Whether it’s a D-I varsity match or a pickup game, Holy Cross students come together to compete, excel and cheer one another on. 

Strong Bodies. Strong Minds.

Our students’ achievements on the playing field are matched by their success in the classroom:

  • Holy Cross’ overall Graduation Success Rate for all student-athletes is 99 percent — tied for the first best mark in the country out of 353 Division I schools. Twenty-three of a possible 26 Holy Cross athletic teams achieved a perfect 100 percent graduation rate.

  • Over the past two decades, three Holy Cross student-athletes won Patriot League Scholar-Athlete of the Year and 20 were named Academic All-Americans.
Division I Varsity Sports
Holy Cross’ 27 NCAA Division I teams continue the Crusaders’ storied athletics tradition.
Athletics Website
Keep up to date on the latest scores, schedules and statistics from this year’s varsity teams at the official home of Holy Cross athletics.
Patriot League
Holy Cross is a founding member of the Patriot League, an NCAA Division I league that prizes academic excellence as well as athletic achievement.
Club Sports and Intramurals
You don’t need a 90 mph fastball or lightning speed to play sports at Holy Cross. Our club and intramural sports teams allow students to compete in a wide range of activities without the commitment required by full-time varsity athletics.
Athletic and Recreational Facilities
From our 23,000-seat football stadium to our new, first-class indoor complex, Holy Cross boasts impressive athletic facilities.
Athletics Store
Let the world know you bleed purple by coming to the game in the latest Holy Cross athletic apparel, available year-round from our bookstore.
