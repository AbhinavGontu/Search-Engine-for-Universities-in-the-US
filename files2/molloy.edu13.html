Alumni Association



 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

All graduates of Molloy College are members of The Alumni Association. Graduates of associate's, bachelor's, master's and doctoral degrees, from all years, are encouraged to participate in Alumni Association events and meetings.

The Alumni Association is governed by an Executive Board and an Administration Board.  You can read about the members of the Board here.  The Executive Board directs the business of the Alumni Association and determines the future direction of the association.  The Administrative Board contributes to the growth of the Alumni Association and the well-being of your fellow graduates.  The Association meets at least 6x a year, usually on the first Monday of the month. Members can call in to the meetings if they are unable to physically be present.During the COVID-19 pandemic, while we social distance, we are holding our meetings on ZOOM.

If you are interested in finding out more about becoming a Board member, or you want to attend our next meeting, please contact Mary Jane Reilly, Director of Alumni Relations, at mreilly@molloy.edu or 516.323.4702.
