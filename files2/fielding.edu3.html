Academic Catalog 2020-2021

 

Browser

Firefox is the recommended browser.

Searching the Catalog 

Use the keyword search (directly above the catalog menu on your right) to find specific items. Use the Advanced Search feature below the search box to limit your search to specific areas of the catalog (courses, programs, etc.).

My Catalog 

This feature allows you to save catalog information that interests you into a password-protected account, so that you can return to easily (think of it as adding items into an online shopping cart). Simply click on “My Catalog” and follow the instructions to create an account. Once you create your account, you can access your catalog favorites at any time by clicking on “My Catalog” and logging in. if you choose not to create an account, you can still save favorite items into your Catalog throughout your browsing session, but they won’t be retained once your browsing session ends.

Quick Icons 

At the top and bottom right of each catalog page you’ll see these four icons:

 Saving When viewing any page or course description, clicking on the star icon will add that page or course description to your catalog favorites.

Sharing Content Touching your cursor on the “Share” icon will give you the option to share the page you are viewing on Facebook or Twitter. 

 Printing Content Clicking on the printer icon creates a printer-friendly version of the page.

Help clicking on the question mark icon will open the help menu.

Switching Between Catalogs 

As we publish each academic year’s catalog, the previous year’s catalog will still be available online. Archived catalogs are available from the dropdown menu at the top right of each page, right beneath the main Fielding banner.

Mobile Site

The fully functional mobile site allows you to access the catalog using your mobile device.

Questions or Comments? 

We’re happy to get your comments and/or answer any questions. Please email them to registrar@fielding.edu.
