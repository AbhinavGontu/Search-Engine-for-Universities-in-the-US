Communications and Public Affairs

Supporting Rockefeller scientists and the greater Rockefeller community.

The Office of Communications and Public Affairs promotes and disseminates research news and other information about The Rockefeller University.

Among its diverse responsibilities, the office oversees relations between Rockefeller scientists and the media; manages the content and design of the universityâs publications, website, and social media channels; and develops the strategic positioning and visual identity for the university.

The office also offers support for Rockefeller scientists and administrators communicating with key audiences. All general inquiries for Communications and Public Affairs can be directed to requests@rockefeller.edu or 212-327-7900.
