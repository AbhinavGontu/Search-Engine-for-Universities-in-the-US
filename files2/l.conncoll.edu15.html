Cooking Up Culture 
(Spring 2021)
  • March 8: Joyce Bennet (Anthropology) and Celestino Sajvin (Dining Services) are preparing a Guatemalan dish, pepian;
  • March 22: Matteo Pace (Italian) is making baccalà alla romana, a classic of Roman cuisine;
  • April 5: Mariko Moher will introduce us to gyoza;
  • April 19: Laura Little and Petko Ivanon (Slavic) will bring a recipe from Anya von Bremzen's Mastering the Art of Soviet Cooking;
  • May 3: Rachel Black (Anthropology) will conclude in style with French crêpes.
The events will be held on Zoom on Monday nights at 7PM, at the following link: https://conncoll-edu.zoom.us/j/9140789167
