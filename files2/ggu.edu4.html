Your Future Can’t Wait

With degrees and certificates designed and taught by industry leaders in the heart of San Francisco, GGU is the right place to take the next step towards your goals.

Not only are we pioneers in online education (20 years and counting!) but all of our programs and services are developed for busy, working adults like you.

Data Analytics

Basic Proficiency in
Data Visualization with Tableau

Tableau is an essential data visualization tool which provides businesses with insights about customers, sales, inventory, and distribution.

Basic Proficiency in
Programming with HQL

HQL is an advanced tool that helps enterprises to target ads to consumers based on analysis of social media content and other types of big data.

Basic Proficiency in
Programming with Python

Python is used to develop large websites, operating systems, and applications and is essential for any role that involves data analysis for every industry.

Basic Proficiency in
Programming with R

R was originally developed for statistical analysis and can be used to analyze any type or volume of unstructured data including video, images, audio, and text.

Basic Proficiency in
Programming with SAS

SAS is the default platform used across different industries for data collection, clinical research, risk analysis, and statistical analysis of data.

Basic Proficiency in
Programming with SQL

SQL is the primary language used to interact with databases and enables you to generate reports that provide meaningful insights and answers.

Leadership

Leading and Managing
through Change and Disruption

A certificate for established and aspiring leaders and managers who want to gain practical and operational insight that can help employees find meaning and value in their work.

Building and Sustaining Relationships
for Organizational Success

A program designed to help employees at all levels build a strong portfolio of skills, experience, and knowledge that lead to strong relationships in any organizational setting.

GGU is proud to partner with Pathstream to help our students learn in-demand skills with some of the most-used tools in the job market today.

Pathstream collaborates with leading technology companies to build branded digital skills career programs delivered through college and university partnerships. Our programs are for anyone who wants to learn the digital skills needed to succeed in the modern economy.

Project Management

The Asana Project Management Certificate will help you stand out in the job market by equipping you with hands-on experience in project management and preparing you for the Project Management Institute’s CAPM® exam.

Digital Marketing

The Facebook Digital Marketing Certificate is an online six-course program that will help you gain hands-on experience with digital marketing tools such as Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Mailchimp and more.

Salesforce for Business

This online three-course certificate will help you gain hands-on experience in customer relationship management with access to practice your skills in the Salesforce platform.
