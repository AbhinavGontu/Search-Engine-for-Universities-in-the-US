The Housing Application for Current,New, Transfer and returning students is now open! 

Log into your MyHousing portal to fill out your contract and application to secure your housing application for the 2021-2022 academic year.

To find more instructions on how to fill out your application, please click on Residence Hall Home and find important links at the bottom of the page.  There you will see Application Process. 

*Reminder there is a First Year live on requirement for the 2021-2022 academic year* 

 

Housing Operations and Residence Life is open
Monday through Friday from 8 am to 5 pm

Call 509.963.1831 or email Housing@cwu.edu

 

   
Covid-19 Updates and FAQS

New Students

New First Year students, Transfer Students and Current CWU students who do not currently live on campus, can apply for Housing starting January 25, 2021.

Current Residence Hall Students

Current students living in the residence halls can apply for housing during Housing Priority Application from January 6-19, 2021.
