Admissions and Aid
  1. Home
  2. Admissions and Aid

Find your direction and follow it. Do North.

At Northern Vermont University, North is more than just where we are. It's who we are. It's what we do. Our friendly admissions and aid staff members at our Johnson and Lyndon campuses are here to help you determine your next steps on your path to becoming a college student and to help you discover how you'll make your impact with a degree from NVU. Feel free to email us any time with questions at Admissions@NorthernVermont.edu.

You can also contact us directly at our campus offices.

Complete your application.

NVU accepts applications via our own secure, web-based form. You will apply to either Johnson campus or our Lyndon campus.  

Pay your application fee.

You can pay your $50 application fee online or via check made payable to Northern Vermont University. 

Send your official transcripts.

Submit your official transcript(s) to the campus at which you intend to enroll. 

Optional: 
Submit your SAT/ACT scores.

NVU does not require test scores. If you choose to submit your scores, use SAT code: 3766; ACT code: 4316

Optional: 
Submit an essay/personal statement.

Choose one of three topics to write about, to help us get to know you better. 

Optional: 
Submit an audio-visual audition.

If you are applying as a music, musical theater, or music education major, please submit an audio-visual audition. Learn more about audition guidelines here.
