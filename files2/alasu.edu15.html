Your gateway to the world of information and a partner in your success!

The Levi Watkins Learning Center extends a warm welcome to new and returning students of Alabama State University. We invite you to come to the Library for a chat with our Librarians and other Library staff. The librarians are available and eager to meet you. Come by and learn more about the resources available to students, take a tour of our Library, and find out about student jobs in the Library. 

Students are at the center of what we do in the Library. We are passionate about student learning, and are dedicated to creating a welcoming, engaging, and dynamic place for study, collaboration, and innovation. We can't wait to meet you and welcome you to campus! We're so glad you're here!
