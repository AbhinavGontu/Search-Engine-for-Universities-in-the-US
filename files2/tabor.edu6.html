Directions to Tabor College – Hillsboro, Kansas

From the West (Colorado)

  • Take I-70 East
  • Merge onto I-135 South
  • Take I-135 South to US-56 East
  • Follow US-56 East to North Ash Street and turn right
  • Turn left onto West D Street
  • Go four blocks and campus will be on your left

From the East (Kansas City)

  • Take I-70 West to I-470 West
  • Merge onto I-335 South
  • Merge onto US-50 West
  • Take US-50 West to US-150/US-56 and turn right
  • Take US-56 to North Adams Street
  • Turn left on North Adams Street to East D Street
  • Turn right onto East D Street
  • Campus will be on your right

From the North (Nebraska)

  • Take US-81 South to US-56 East
  • Follow that to North Ash Street and turn right
  • Turn left onto West D Street
  • Go four blocks and campus will be on your left

From the South (Wichita)

  • Take I-135 North to US-50 East
  • Take US-50 East to CR-835
  • Turn left on CR-835, which becomes Indigo Road, then becomes South Ash Street
  • Turn right onto West D Street
  • Go four blocks and campus will be on your left
