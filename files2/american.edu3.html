WCL Welcomes Congresswoman Norton March 18The movement to achieve statehood for Washington, DC has never been closer to achieving its goal. On Thursday, March 18, join American University Washington College of Law’s Program on Law and Government for “The Road to 51: The Past, Present, and Future Of The Struggle For DC Statehood.” The discussion will feature insights from Congresswoman Eleanor Holmes Norton.
JD Admitted Students Month: March 15-April 15March 15-April 15, AUWCL JD Admissions will host an extensive series of virtual sessions during Virtual Admitted Students Month. The series of online events will introduce admitted students to faculty, current students and alumni, and provide overview sessions on academic success, experiential learning, financial aid, career and professional development, and moving to DC.

Nationally Ranked Specialty Programs

American University Washington College of Law’s top specialty programs, nationally ranked by U.S. News and World Report – #2 Clinical Program, #4 International Law, #4 Trial Advocacy, #10 Intellectual Property, #11 Health Law – are just a few of our areas of excellence. We provide our students with the critical skills and values to have an immediate impact in the Washington, D.C. region, our nation, and around the world.
