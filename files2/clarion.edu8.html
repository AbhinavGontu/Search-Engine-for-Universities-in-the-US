Golden Eagles Rising

Our Mission:
The Clarion University Athletic Department works collaboratively with others to support our students’ pursuit of excellence by providing them with challenging opportunities to develop their athletic, academic, professional and social skills, and to represent the Clarion community through athletic involvement and competition.

Past, Present, Future

Athletics at Clarion University has a strong tradition and a bright future. Together, we have faced challenges, and now, coupling the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania’s $42 million investment in Tippin Gymnasium with this campaign, we are poised to achieve greater competitive excellence within the PSAC and nationally.

Together, we will create the athletic facilities that our student-athletes deserve and our alumni and fans want; together, we will establish scholarships which will position our coaches to recruit and retain the most athletically talented and academically driven student-athletes; and, together, we will ensure our athletic excellence.

We are grateful for your past support, and we ask you to invest in our future success.

Goals


  • Facility Projects

    Having a place for student athletes to train, build, and hone their skills is important. Reaching our goal will allow us to develop facilities that put us on the cutting edge of collegiate athletics.
    Facility Projects

  • Scholarships

    Your support of our teams is essential to our ability to recruit and retain students who will be successful in the classroom and on their teams. 

    Scholarships

  • Annual Support

    Annual operating support allows the athletic department and individual sports the flexibility they need for recruiting and training expenses, travel support to enhance the experience of student-athletes, and necessary training and development equipment. 

    Annual Support

Laying the Foundation

This campaign is about laying the foundation for our programs to develop into the best the conference has to offer. When the quality of our facilities matches the drive of our coaches, and the amounts and types of scholar- ships that we can provide meet the determination of our student-athletes, Clarion University will once again be recognized as a program that develops champions across all of our athletic programs.

Wendy Snodgrass, D.Ed. Director of Athletics

Support the GOlden Eagles

To build on our athletic legacy, we have launched Golden Eagles Rising, a campaign to  support our student-athletes, the programs in which they participate and the facilities where they train, practice and compete. To learn more about the campaign and how you can join the effort, call 814-393-2637 or email supportgoldeneagles@clarion.edu.

Now is your chance to own a piece of this venerable building’s main gymnasium floor while, at the same time, helping our student-athletes rise up and take on the best of their competition.
