Orientation

ATTENTION: Information about the Summer 2021 Orientation program will be available soon. Questions? Contact New Student and Family Programs at orientu@gmu.edu or 703-993-2475. Welcome to Mason!

Welcome to Mason Nation! The Office of New Student and Family Programs serves as the home for new students and families during your first year at Mason. It’s our job to help you connect your excitement about being a new Patriot to the endless opportunities that we have for you here in our academic community.

If you have questions, contact New Student and Family Programs at (703) 993-2475 or orientu@gmu.edu.
When contacting our office, please provide your name and G number.
